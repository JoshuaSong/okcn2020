φωνή
a sound, tone
φωνή a tone (articulate, bestial or artificial); by implication, an address (for any purpose), saying or language
Derivation: probably akin to G5316 through the idea of disclosure;
KJV Usage: noise, sound, voice. G5316
Thayer:
1) a sound, a tone
1a) of inanimate things, as musical instruments
2) a voice
2a) of the sound of uttered words
3) speech
3a) of a language, tongue
