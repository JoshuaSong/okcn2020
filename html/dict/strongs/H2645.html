חפה
châphâh
khaw-faw'
A primitive root (compare H2644, H2653); to cover; by implication to veil, to incase, protect
KJV Usage: ceil, cover, overlay.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חפה

1. to cover, overlay, wainscotted, covered with boards or panelling
a. (Qal) to cover
b. (Niphal) to be covered
c. (Piel) to cover, overlay
Origin: a primitive root (compare H2644, H2653)
