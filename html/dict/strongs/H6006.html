עמשׂ עמס
‛âmas ‛âmaś
aw-mas', aw-mas'
A primitive root; to load, that is, impose a burden (or figuratively infliction)
KJV Usage: be borne, (heavy) burden (self), lade, load, put.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
