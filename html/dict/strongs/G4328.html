προσδοκάω
to expect
προσδοκάω to anticipate (in thought, hope or fear); by implication, to await
Derivation: from G4314 and dokeuo (to watch);
KJV Usage: (be in) expect(-ation), look (for), when looked, tarry, wait for. G4314
Thayer:
1) to expect (whether in thought, in hope, or in fear)
2) to look for, wait for

  • Look (for), Looking (after, for)
  • Wait
