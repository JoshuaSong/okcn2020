ἀτιμάζω
to hold in no honour, to esteem lightly, dishonour, slight
ἀτιμάζω to render infamous, i.e. (by implication) contemn or maltreat
Derivation: from G820;
KJV Usage: despise, dishonour, suffer shame, entreat shamefully. G820
Thayer:
1) to dishonour, insult, treat with contempt
1a) whether in word, deed or thought
