ἐμπίπτω
to fall in
ἐμπίπτω to fall on, i.e. (literally) to be entrapped by, or (figuratively) be overwhelmed with
Derivation: from G1722 and G4098;
KJV Usage: fall among (into). G1722 G4098
Thayer:
1) to fall into
1a) to fall among robbers
1b) fall into one's power
