τεκμήριον
a sure signs.
τεκμήριον a token (as defining a fact), i.e. criterion of certainty
Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of τεκμάρ (a goal or fixed limit);
KJV Usage: infallible proof.
Thayer:
1) that from which something is surely and plainly known
2) on indubitable evidence, a proof
