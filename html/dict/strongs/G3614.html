οἰκία
a building, house, dwelling
οἰκία properly, residence (abstractly), but usually (concretely) an abode (literally or figuratively); by implication, a family (especially domestics)
Derivation: from G3624;
KJV Usage: home, house(-hold). G3624
Thayer:
1) a house
1a) an inhabited edifice, a dwelling
1b) the inmates of a house, the family
1c) property, wealth, goods
For Synonyms see entry G5867
