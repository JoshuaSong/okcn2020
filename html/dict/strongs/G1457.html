to renovate, consecrate
ἐγκαινίζω to renew, i.e. inaugurate
Derivation: from G1456;
KJV Usage: consecrate, dedicate. G1456
Thayer:
1) to renew
2) to do anew, again
3) to initiate, consecrate, dedication
