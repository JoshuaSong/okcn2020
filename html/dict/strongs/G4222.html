ποτίζω
to give to drink
ποτίζω to furnish drink, irrigate
Derivation: from a derivative of the alternate of G4095;
KJV Usage: give (make) to drink, feed, water. G4095
Thayer:
1) to give to drink, to furnish drink
2) to water, irrigate (plants, fields etc.)
3) metaph. to imbue, saturate one's mind
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Drink (-eth, -er, -ing), Drank
  • Feed, Fed
  • Water (Noun and Verb) Watering, Waterless
