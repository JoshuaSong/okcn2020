κληρόω
to appoint
κληρόω to allot, i.e. (figuratively) to assign (a privilege)
Derivation: from G2819;
KJV Usage: obtain an inheritance. G2819
Thayer:
1) to cast lots, determine by lot
2) to choose by lot
3) to allot, assign by lot
3a) on to another as a possession
4) in NT: to make a lot, i.e. a heritage, private possession
