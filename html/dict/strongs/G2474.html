Ἰσραήλ Israel (i.e. Jisrael), the adopted name of Jacob, including his descendants (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H3478);
KJV Usage: Israel. H3478
Thayer:
1) the name given to the patriarch Jacob (and borne by him in addition to his former name)
2) the family or descendants of Israel, the nation of Israel
3) Christians, the Israel of God (Gal
6:16), for not all those who draw their bodily descent from Israel are true Israelites, i.e. are those whom God pronounces to be Israelites and has chosen to salvation
Literal: Israel = "he shall be a prince of God"
