ὀλιγωρέω
to esteem little
ὀλιγωρέω to have little regard for, i.e. to disesteem
Derivation: from a compound of G3641 and ὤρα ("care");
KJV Usage: despise. G3641
Thayer:
1) to care little for, regard lightly, make small account
