εἰδωλόθυτον an image-sacrifice, i.e. part of an idolatrous offering
Derivation: neuter of a compound of G1497 and a presumed derivative of G2380;
KJV Usage: (meat, thing that is) offered (in sacrifice, sacrificed) to (unto) idols. G1497 G2380
Thayer:
1) sacrificed to idols, the flesh left over from the heathen sacrifices
1a) it was either eaten at the feasts or sold (by the poor and the miserly) in the market
