ῥύμη
the force, swing, rush
ῥύμη an alley or avenue (as crowded)
Derivation: prolongation from G4506 in its original sense;
KJV Usage: lane, street. G4506
Thayer:
1) a swing, rush, force, trail, of a body in motion
2) a tract of way in a town shut in by buildings on both sides
3) a street, a lane
