κατήγορος
an accuser
κατήγορος against one in the assembly, i.e. a complainant at law; specially, Satan
Derivation: from G2596 and G58;
KJV Usage: accuser. G2596 G58
Thayer:
1) an accuser
1a) a name given to the devil by the rabbis
