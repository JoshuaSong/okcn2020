παραδέχομαι
to receive from
παραδέχομαι to accept near, i.e. admit or (by implication) delight in
Derivation: from G3844 and G1209;
KJV Usage: receive. G3844 G1209
Thayer:
1) to receive, take up, take upon one's self
2) to admit i.e. not to reject, to accept, receive
2a) of a son: to acknowledge as one's own
