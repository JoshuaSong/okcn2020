νομοθετέω
to make law
νομοθετέω to legislate, i.e. (passively) to have (the Mosaic) enactments injoined, be sanctioned (by them)
Derivation: from G3550;
KJV Usage: establish, receive the law. G3550
Thayer:
1) to enact laws
1a) laws are enacted or prescribed for one, to be legislated for, furnished with laws
2) to sanction by law, enact
