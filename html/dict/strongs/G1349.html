δίκη
custom, usage
δίκη right (as self-evident), i.e. justice (the principle, a decision, or its execution)
Derivation: probably from G1166;
KJV Usage: judgment, punish, vengeance. G1166
Thayer:
1) custom, usage
2) right, just
3) a suit at law
4) a judicial hearing, judicial decision, esp. sentence of condemnation
5) execution of a sentence, punishment
5a) to suffer punishment
6) the goddess Justice, avenging justice
