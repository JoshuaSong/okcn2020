כּתת
kâthath
kaw-thath'
A primitive root; to bruise or violently strike
KJV Usage: beat (down, to pieces), break in pieces, crushed, destroy, discomfit, smite, stamp.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּתת

1. to beat, crush by beating, crush to pieces, crush fine
a. (Qal)
1. to beat or crush fine
2. to beat, hammer
b. (Piel)
1. to beat or crush fine
2. to beat, hammer
c. (Pual) to be beaten
d. (Hiphil) to beat in pieces, shatter
e. (Hophal) to be beaten, be crushed
Origin: a primitive root
