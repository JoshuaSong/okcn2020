προαιρέομαι to choose for oneself before another thing (prefer), i.e. (by implication) to propose (intend)
Derivation: from G4253 and G138;
KJV Usage: purpose. G4253 G138
Thayer:
1) to bring forward, bring forth from one's stores
2) to bring forth for one's self, to choose for one's self before another, to prefer
3) to purpose
