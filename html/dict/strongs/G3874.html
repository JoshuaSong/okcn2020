παράκλησις
a calling to one's aid, summons
παράκλησις imploration, hortation, solace
Derivation: from G3870;
KJV Usage: comfort, consolation, exhortation, intreaty. G3870
Thayer:
1) a calling near, summons, (esp. for help)
2) importation, supplication, entreaty
3) exhortation, admonition, encouragement
4) consolation, comfort, solace; that which affords comfort or refreshment
4a) thus of the Messianic salvation (so the Rabbis call the Messiah the consoler, the comforter)
5) persuasive discourse, stirring address
5a) instructive, admonitory, conciliatory, powerful hortatory discourse
