παράγω
to lead by
παράγω to lead near, i.e. (reflexively or intransitively) to go along or away
Derivation: from G3844 and G71;
KJV Usage: depart, pass (away, by, forth). G3844 G71
Thayer:
1) pass by
1a) to lead past, lead by
1b) to lead aside, mislead
1b1) to lead away
1c) to lead to
1c1) to lead forth, bring forward
2) to pass by, go past
2a) to depart, go away
3) metaph. disappear
