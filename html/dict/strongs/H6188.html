ערל
‛ârêl
aw-rale'
A primitive root; properly to strip; but used only as denominative from H6189; to expose or remove the prepuce, whether literally (to go naked) or figuratively (to refrain from using)
KJV Usage: count uncircumcised, foreskin to be uncovered.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ערל

1. to remain uncircumcised, count uncircumcised, count as foreskin
a. (Qal)
1. to regard as uncircumcised
2. to remain unharvested (fig.)
b. (Niphal) to be counted as uncircumcised
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1695
Parts of Speech: Verb
