ἐκδημέω
to be abroad, to be on one's travels
ἐκδημέω to emigrate, i.e. (figuratively) vacate or quit
Derivation: from a compound of G1537 and G1218;
KJV Usage: be absent. G1537 G1218
Thayer:
1) to go abroad
2) emigrate, depart
3) to be or live abroad
