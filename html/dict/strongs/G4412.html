πρῶτον firstly (in time, place, order, or importance)
Derivation: neuter of G4413 as adverb (with or without G3588);
KJV Usage: before, at the beginning, chiefly (at, at the) first (of all). G4413 G3588
Thayer:
1) first in time or place
1a) in any succession of things or persons
2) first in rank
2a) influence, honour
2b) chief
2c) principal
3) first, at the first
