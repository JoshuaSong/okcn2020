δαπανάω
to spend
δαπανάω to expend, i.e. (in a good sense) to incur cost, or (in a bad one) to waste
Derivation: from G1160;
KJV Usage: be at charges, consume, spend. G1160
Thayer:
1) to incur expense, expend, spend
2) in a bad sense: to waste, squander, consume
