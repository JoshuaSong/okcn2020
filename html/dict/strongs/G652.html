ἀπόστολος
a messenger, ambassador, envoy
ἀπόστολος a delegate; specially, an ambassador of the Gospel; officially a commissioner of Christ ("apostle") (with miraculous powers)
Derivation: from G649;
KJV Usage: apostle, messenger, he that is sent. G649
Thayer:
1) a delegate, messenger, one sent forth with orders
1a) specifically applied to the twelve apostles of Christ
1b) in a broader sense applied to other eminent Christian teachers
1b1) of Barnabas
1b2) of Timothy and Silvanus
