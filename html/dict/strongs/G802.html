ἀσύνθετος
uncompounded
ἀσύνθετος properly, not agreed, i.e. treacherous to compacts
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4934;
KJV Usage: covenant-breaker. G1 G4934
Thayer:
1) uncompounded, simple
2) covenant breaking, faithless
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Covenant-breaker, implacable
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Covenant Breaker
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Covenant-breakers
