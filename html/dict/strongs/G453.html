ἀνόητος

ἀνόητος unintelligent; by implication, sensual
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3539;
KJV Usage: fool(-ish), unwise. G1 G3539
Thayer:
1) not understood, unintelligible
2) not understanding, unwise, foolish
