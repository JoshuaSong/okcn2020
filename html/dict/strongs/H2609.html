Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חנס
Hanes = "grace has fled"
1. a place in Egypt, site uncertain but may be either of the following
a. Tahpanhes, a fortified town on the eastern frontier
b. a place in lower Egypt, on an island in the Nile south of Memphis
Origin: of Egyptian derivation
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Location
