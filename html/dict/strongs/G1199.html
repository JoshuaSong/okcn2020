δεσμόν or δεσμός a band, i.e. ligament (of the body) or shackle (of a prisoner); figuratively, an impediment or disability
Derivation: neuter and masculine respectively from G1210;
KJV Usage: band, bond, chain, string. G1210
Thayer:
1) a band or bond
