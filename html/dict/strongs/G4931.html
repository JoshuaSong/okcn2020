συντελέω
to bring quite to an end, complete, accomplish
συντελέω to complete entirely; generally, to execute (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and G5055;
KJV Usage: end, finish, fulfil, make. G4862 G5055
Thayer:
1) to end together or at the same time
2) to end completely
2a) bring to an end, finish, complete
3) to accomplish, bring to fulfilment
3a) to come to pass
4) to effect, make, (conclude)
5) to finish
5a) to make an end of
5b) to bring to an end
5c) destroy
