שׁאה שׁואה שׁוא
shô' shô'âh shô'âh
sho, sho-aw', sho-aw'
From an unused root meaning to rush over; a tempest; by implication devastation
KJV Usage: desolate (-ion), destroy, destruction, storm, wasteness.
