παρακούω
to hear beside
παρακούω to mishear, i.e. (by implication) to disobey
Derivation: from G3844 and G191;
KJV Usage: neglect to hear. G3844 G191
Thayer:
1) to hear aside
1a) causally or carelessly or amiss
2) to be unwilling to hear
2a) on hearing to neglect, to pay no heed to
2b) to refuse to hear, pay no regard to, disobey
