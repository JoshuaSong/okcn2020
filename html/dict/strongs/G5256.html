ὑπηρετέω
to do service on board ship, to do rower's service
ὑπηρετέω to be a subordinate, i.e. (by implication) subserve
Derivation: from G5257;
KJV Usage: minister (unto), serve. G5257
Thayer:
1) to act as a rower, to row
2) to minister, render services
