deficiency, need, want
ὑστέρημα a deficit; specially, poverty
Derivation: from G5302;
KJV Usage: that which is behind, (that which was) lack(-ing), penury, want. G5302
Thayer:
1) deficiency, that which is lacking
2) in reference to property and resources, poverty, want, destitution
