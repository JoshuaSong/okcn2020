yechdı̂yâhû

From H3162 and H3050; unity of Jah; Jechdijah, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Jehdeiah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יחדּיּהוּ
Jehdeiah = "Jehovah is unity"
1. a Levite in the time of David
2. a Meronothite, officer of David in charge of the she-asses
Origin: from H3162 and H3050
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
