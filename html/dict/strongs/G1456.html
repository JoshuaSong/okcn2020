ἐγκαίνια
a feast of renovation
ἐγκαίνια innovatives, i.e. (specially) renewal (of religious services after the Antiochian interruption)
Derivation: neuter plural of a presumed compound from G1722 and G2537;
KJV Usage: dedication. G1722 G2537
Thayer:
1) dedication, consecration
1a) in particular the annual feast celebrated eight days beginning in the
25th of Chislev (middle of our December), instituted by Judas Maccabaeus [164 BC] in memory of the cleansing of the temple from the pollution of Antiochus Epiphanes
