ἀπόβλητος
to be thrown away
ἀπόβλητος cast off, i.e. (figuratively) such as to be rejected
Derivation: from G577;
KJV Usage: be refused. G577
Thayer:
1) thrown away, to be thrown away, rejected, despised, abominated
1a) as unclean
