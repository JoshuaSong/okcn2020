θύω properly, to rush (breathe hard, blow, smoke), i.e. (by implication) to sacrifice (properly, by fire, but genitive case); by extension to immolate (slaughter for any purpose)
Derivation: a primary verb;
KJV Usage: kill, (do) sacrifice, slay.
Thayer:
1) to sacrifice, immolate
2) to slay, kill
2a) of the paschal lamb
3) slaughter
