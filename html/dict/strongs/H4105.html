me hêyṭab'êl
meh-hay-tab-ale'
From H3190 (augmented) and H410; bettered of God; Mehetabel, the name of an Edomitish man and woman
KJV Usage: Mehetabeel, Mehetabel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מהיטבאל
Mehetabeel or Mehetabel = "favoured of God" n pr m
1. the ancestor of Shemaiah, the false prophet who was hired against Nehemiah by Tobiah and Sanballat n pr f
2. the daughter of Matred and wife of king Hadad of Edom
Origin: from H3190 (augmented) and H410
TWOT: None
