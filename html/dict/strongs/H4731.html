מקּלה מקּל
maqqêl maqqe lâh
mak-kale', mak-kel-aw'
From an unused root meaning apparently to germinate; a shoot, that is, stick (with leaves on, or for walking, striking, guiding, divining)
KJV Usage: rod, ([hand-]) staff.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
