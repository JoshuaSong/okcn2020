γόης
one who howls out enchantments, a sorcerer, enchanter
γόης properly, a wizard (as muttering spells), i.e. (by implication) an imposter
Derivation: from γοάω (to wail);
KJV Usage: seducer.
Thayer:
1) a wailer, a howler
2) a juggler, enchanter (because incantations used to be uttered in a kind of howl)
3) a deceiver, imposter
