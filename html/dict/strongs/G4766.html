στρώννυμι , or simpler στρωννύω ; to "strew," i.e. spread (as a carpet or couch)
Derivation: prolongation from a still simpler στρόω , (used only as an alternate in certain tenses) (probably akin to G4731 through the idea of positing);
KJV Usage: make bed, furnish, spread, strew. G4731
Thayer:
1) to spread
2) furnish
3) to spread with couches or divans
