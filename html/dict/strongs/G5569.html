ψευδάδελφος
a false brother
ψευδάδελφος a spurious brother, i.e. pretended associate
Derivation: from G5571 and G80;
KJV Usage: false brethren. G5571 G80
Thayer:
1) a false brother
2) one who ostentatiously professes to be a Christian, but is destitute of Christian knowledge and piety
