θεραπεύω
to be an attendant, do service
θεραπεύω to wait upon menially, i.e. (figuratively) to adore (God), or (specially) to relieve (of disease)
Derivation: from the same as G2324;
KJV Usage: cure, heal, worship. G2324
Thayer:
1) to serve, do service
2) to heal, cure, restore to health
