שׁושׁנּים עדוּת שׁוּשׁן עדוּת
shûshan ‛êdûth shôshannı̂ym ‛êdûth
shoo-shan' or sho-shan-neem' (ay-dooth')
The second form being plural; from H7799 and H5715; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage; Shushan Eduth or Shoshannim Eduth, the title of a popular song
KJV Usage: Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.
