ἐσθίω
to eat
ἐσθίω used only in certain tenses, the rest being supplied by G5315; to eat (usually literal)
Derivation: strengthened for a primary ἔδω (to eat);
KJV Usage: devour, eat, live. G5315
Thayer:
1) to eat
2) to eat (consume) a thing
2a) to take food, eat a meal
3) metaph. to devour, consume
