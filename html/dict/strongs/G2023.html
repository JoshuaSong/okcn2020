ἐπιχορηγέω
to supply besides
ἐπιχορηγέω to furnish besides, i.e. fully supply, (figuratively) aid or contribute
Derivation: from G1909 and G5524;
KJV Usage: add, minister (nourishment, unto). G1909 G5524
Thayer:
1) to supply, furnish, present
2) to be supplied, ministered to, assisted
