Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּעלת בּאר
Baalath-beer = "mistress of the well"
1. a town in the south part of Judah, given to Simeon, which also bore the name of 'Ramath-negeb'
Origin: from H1172 and H875
