קנה
qânâh
kaw-naw'
Feminine of H7070; reediness; Kanah, the name of a stream and of a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Kanah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קנה
Kanah = "reed"
1. a stream flowing into the Mediterranean Sea that marked the boundary between Ephraim on the south and Manasseh on the north
2. a town marking the boundary of Asher
Origin: fem. of H7070
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Location
