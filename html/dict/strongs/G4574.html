σέβασμα
an object of awe
σέβασμα something adored, i.e. an object of worship (god, altar, etc)
Derivation: from G4573;
KJV Usage: devotion, that is worshipped. G4573
Thayer:
1) whatever is religiously honoured, an object of worship
1a) of temples, altars, statues, idolatrous images
