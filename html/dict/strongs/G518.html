ἀπαγγέλλω
to bring tidings, report, announce
ἀπαγγέλλω to announce
Derivation: from G575 and the base of G32;
KJV Usage: bring word (again), declare, report, shew (again), tell. G575 G32
Thayer:
1) to bring tidings (from a person or a thing), bring word, report
2) to proclaim, to make known openly, declare
