ἐμπνέω
to blow
ἐμπνέω to inhale, i.e. (figuratively) to be animated by (bent upon)
Derivation: from G1722 and G4154;
KJV Usage: breathe. G1722 G4154
Thayer:
1) to breathe in or on
2) to inhale
2a) threatenings and slaughter were so to speak the element from which he drew his breath
