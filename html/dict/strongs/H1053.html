Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּית שׁמשׁ
Beth-shemesh = "house of the sun" or "sun-temple"
1. a town in southwest Judah
2. a town in Naphtali
3. a town in Issachar
4. a town in Egypt
Origin: from H1004 and H8121
TWOT: None
