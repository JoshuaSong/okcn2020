ἀσπίς
a round shield
ἀσπίς a buckler (or round shield); used of a serpent (as coiling itself), probably the "asp"
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: asp.
Thayer:
1) an asp, a small and most venomous serpent, the bite of which is fatal unless the bitten part be immediately cut away
