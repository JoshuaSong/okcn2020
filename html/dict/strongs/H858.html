אתא אתה
'âthâh 'âthâ'
aw-thaw', aw-thaw'
(Chaldee); corresponding to H857
KJV Usage: (be-) come, bring.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אתא אתה

1. to come, arrive
a. (P'al) to come
b. (Aphel) to bring
c. (Hophal) to be brought
2. used in the NT in the phrase "maranatha" - "Lord come"
Origin: corresponding to H857
TWOT: 2618
Parts of Speech: Verb
