ἀναδέχομαι
to take up, catch, receive
ἀναδέχομαι to entertain (as a guest)
Derivation: from G303 and G1209;
KJV Usage: receive. G303 G1209
Thayer:
1) to take up, take upon's self, undertake, assume
2) to receive, entertain anyone hospitably
