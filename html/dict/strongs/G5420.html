φράσσω
to fence in, hedge round
φράσσω to fence or inclose, i.e. (specially), to block up (figuratively, to silence)
Derivation: apparently a strengthening form of the base of G5424;
KJV Usage: stop. G5424
Thayer:
1) to fence in, block up, stop up, close up
2) to put to silence
