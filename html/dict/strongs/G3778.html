οὗτος , including nominative masculine plural οὗτοι , nominative feminine singular αὕτη , and nominative feminine plural αὕται the he (she or it), i.e. this or that (often with article repeated)

KJV Usage: he (it was that), hereof, it, she, such as, the same, these, they, this (man, same, woman), which, who. G846 G3588
