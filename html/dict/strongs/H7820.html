שׁחט
shâchaṭ
shaw-khat'
A primitive root (rather identical with H7819 through the idea of striking); to hammer out
KJV Usage: beat.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁחט

1. to beat, hammer
a. (Qal) beaten (participle)
2. (TWOT) to kill, slaughter
Origin: a primitive root [identical with H7819 through the idea of striking]
TWOT: 2362
Parts of Speech: Verb
