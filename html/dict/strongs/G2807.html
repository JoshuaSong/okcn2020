κλείς
that which serves for closing
κλείς a key (as shutting a lock), literally or figuratively
Derivation: from G2808;
KJV Usage: key. G2808
Thayer:
1) a key
1a) since the keeper of the keys has the power to open and to shut
1b) metaph. in the NT to denote power and authority of various kinds
