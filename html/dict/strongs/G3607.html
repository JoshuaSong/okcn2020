ὀθόνη
fine linen
ὀθόνη a linen cloth, i.e. (especially) a sail
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: sheet.
Thayer:
1) linen (i.e. fine white linen for women's clothing)
2) linen clothes (sheet or sail)
