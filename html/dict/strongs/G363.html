ἀναμιμνήσκω
to remind
ἀναμιμνήσκω to remind; (reflexively) to recollect
Derivation: from G303 and G3403;
KJV Usage: call to mind, (bring to , call to, put in), remember(-brance). G303 G3403
Thayer:
1) to call to remembrance, to remind, to admonish
2) to remember, to remember and weigh well and consider
