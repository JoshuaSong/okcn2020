זרד
zered
zeh'-red
From an unused root meaning to be exuberant in growth; lined with shrubbery; Zered, a brook East of the Dead Sea
KJV Usage: Zared, Zered.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

זרד
Zered = "osier brook"
1. a river east of the Jordan, in the region of Moab and Edom, a source of the Arnon river
Origin: from an unused root meaning to be exuberant in growth
TWOT: None
