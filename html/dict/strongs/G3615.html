οἰκιακός
of
οἰκιακός familiar, i.e. (as noun) relatives
Derivation: from G3614;
KJV Usage: they (them) of (his own) household. G3614
Thayer:
1) one belonging to the house
2) one under control of the master of a house, whether a son or a servant
