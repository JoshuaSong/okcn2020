διαγινώσκω to know thoroughly, i.e. ascertain exactly
Derivation: from G1223 and G1097;
KJV Usage: (would) enquire, know the uttermost. G1223 G1097
Thayer:
1) to distinguish, i.e. to know accurately, ascertain exactly
2) in a legal sense, to examine, determine, decide
