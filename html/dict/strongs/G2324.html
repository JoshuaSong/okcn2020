θεράπων
a waiting-man, attendant

Derivation: apparently a participle from an otherwise obsolete derivative of the base of G2330;
KJV Usage: servant. G2330
Thayer:
1) an attendant, servant: of God
1a) spoken of Moses discharging the duties committed to him by God
