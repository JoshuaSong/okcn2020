προπετής
falling forwards, inclined forward
προπετής falling forward, i.e. headlong (figuratively, precipitate)
Derivation: from a compound of G4253 and G4098;
KJV Usage: heady, rash(-ly). G4253 G4098
Thayer:
1) to fall forwards, headlong, sloping, precipitously
2) precipitate, rash, reckless
