ῥᾳδιουργία
ease in doing, facility
ῥᾳδιουργία recklessness, i.e. (by extension) malignity
Derivation: from the same as G4467;
KJV Usage: mischief. G4467
Thayer:
1) ease in doing, faculty
2) levity or easiness in thinking or acting
2a) love of a lazy effeminate life
3) unscrupulous, cunning, mischief
