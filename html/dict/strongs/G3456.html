μυκτηρίζω
to turn up the nose
μυκτηρίζω to make mouths at, i.e. ridicule
Derivation: from a derivative of the base of G3455 (meaning snout, as that whence lowing proceeds);
KJV Usage: mock. G3455
Thayer:
1) to turn up the nose or sneer at
2) to mock, deride
