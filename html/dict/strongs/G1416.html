δύνω or δῦμι to go "down"
Derivation: prolonged forms of an obsolete primary δύο (to sink);
KJV Usage: set.
Thayer:
1) to go into, enter
2) go under, be plunged into, sink in
2a) used in the NT of the setting of the sun
