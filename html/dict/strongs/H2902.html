טוּח
ṭûach
too'-akh
A primitive root; to smear, especially with lime
KJV Usage: daub, overlay, plaister, smut.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

טוּח

1. to spread over, overlay, plaster, cover over, coat, besmear
a. (Qal) to overspread, coat, overlay
b. (Niphal) to be coated, be plastered
2. (Qal) to be besmeared
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 795
Parts of Speech: Verb
