συγκοινωνέω
to have a joint share of
συγκοινωνέω to share in company with, i.e. co-participate in
Derivation: from G4862 and G2841;
KJV Usage: communicate (have fellowship) with, be partaker of. G4862 G2841
Thayer:
1) to become a partaker together with others, or to have fellowship with a thing
