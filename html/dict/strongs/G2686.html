κατασοφίζομαι
to conquer by sophisms
κατασοφίζομαι to be crafty against, i.e. circumvent
Derivation: middle voice from G2596 and G4679;
KJV Usage: deal subtilly with. G2596 G4679
Thayer:
1) to circumvent by artifice or fraud, conquer by subtle devices
2) to outwit
3) overreach
4) to deal craftily with
