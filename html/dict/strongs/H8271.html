שׁרא
she rê'
sher-ay'
(Chaldee); a root corresponding to that of H8293; to free, separate; figuratively to unravel, commence; by implication (of unloading beasts) to reside
KJV Usage: begin dissolve, dwell, loose.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁרא

1. to loosen, abide, begin
a. (P'al)
1. to loosen
2. to abide (from loosing girths for camp)
b. (Pael) to begin, open
c. (Ithpael) to be loosened
Origin: a root corresponding to that of H8293
TWOT: 3048
Parts of Speech: Verb
