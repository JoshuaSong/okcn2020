καθοράω
to look down
καθοράω to behold fully, i.e. (figuratively) distinctly apprehend
Derivation: from G2596 and G3708;
KJV Usage: clearly see. G2596 G3708
Thayer:
1) to look down, see from above, view from on high
2) to see thoroughly, perceive clearly, understand
