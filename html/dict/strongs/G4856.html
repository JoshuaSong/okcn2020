συμφωνέω
to agree in sound, be in harmony
συμφωνέω to be harmonious, i.e. (figuratively) to accord (be suitable, concur) or stipulate (by compact)
Derivation: from G4859;
KJV Usage: agree (together, with). G4859
Thayer:
1) to agree together
2) to agree with one in making a bargain, to make an agreement, to bargain
