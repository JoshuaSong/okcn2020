Δρούσιλλα Drusilla, a member of the Herodian family
Derivation: a feminine diminutive of Drusus (a Roman name);
KJV Usage: Drusilla.
Thayer:
1) the daughter of Agrippa the elder, wife of Felix, the governor of Judaea, a most licentious woman
Literal: Drusilla = "watered by the dew"
