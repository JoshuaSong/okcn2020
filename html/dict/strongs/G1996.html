ἐπισυνάγω
to collect and bring to
ἐπισυνάγω to collect upon the same place
Derivation: from G1909 and G4863;
KJV Usage: gather (together). G1909 G4863
Thayer:
1) to gather together besides, to bring together to others already assembled
2) to gather together against
3) to gather together in one place
