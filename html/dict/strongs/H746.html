Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אריוך
Arioch = "lion-like"
1. the ancient king of Ellasar, ally of Chedorlaomer
2. the chief of the executioners for Nebuchadnezzar
Origin: of foreign origin
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
