נפג
nepheg
neh'-feg
From an unused root probably meaning to spring forth; a sprout; Nepheg, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Nepheg.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נפג
Nepheg = "sprout"
1. one of sons of Izhar and grandson of Kohath of the tribe of Levi in the time of the exodus
2. one of the sons of David born to him at Jerusalem
Origin: from an unused root probably meaning to spring forth
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
