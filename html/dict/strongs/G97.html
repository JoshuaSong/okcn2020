ἄδολος
without fraud, guileless
ἄδολος and G1388; undeceitful, i.e. (figuratively) unadulterated
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle);
KJV Usage: sincere. G1 G1388
Thayer:
1) guileless
1a) in things: unmixed, unadulterated, pure
1b) in persons: without dishonest intent, guileless
