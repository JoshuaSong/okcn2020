ἀναπολόγητος indefensible
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G626;
KJV Usage: without an excuse, inexcusable. G1 G626
Thayer:
1) without defense or excuse
2) that which cannot be defended, inexcusable
