קבץ
qâbats
kaw-bats'
A primitive root; to grasp, that is, collect
KJV Usage: assemble (selves), gather (bring) (together, selves together, up), heap, resort, X surely, take up.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קבץ

1. to gather, assemble

b. (Niphal)
1. to assemble, gather
2. to be gathered
c. (Piel) to gather, gather together, take away
d. (Pual) to be gathered together
e. (Hithpael) to gather together, be gathered together
