ὁλοκαύτωμα
a whole burnt-offering, holocaust
ὁλοκαύτωμα a wholly-consumed sacrifice ("holocaust")
Derivation: from a derivative of a compound of G3650 and a derivative of G2545;
KJV Usage: (whole) burnt offering. G3650 G2545
Thayer:
1) a whole burnt offering
1a) a victim the whole (and not like other victims only a part) of which was burned
