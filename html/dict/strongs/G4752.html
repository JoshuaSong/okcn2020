στρατεία
an expedition, campaign
στρατεία military service, i.e. (figuratively) the apostolic career (as one of hardship and danger)
Derivation: from G4754;
KJV Usage: warfare. G4754
Thayer:
1) an expedition, campaign, military service, warfare
2) metaph. Paul likens his contest with the difficulties that oppose him in the discharge of his apostolic duties, as warfare
