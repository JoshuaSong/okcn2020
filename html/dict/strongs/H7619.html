שׁוּבאל שׁבוּאל
she bû'êl shûbâ'êl
sheb-oo-ale', shoo-baw-ale'
From H7617 (abbreviated) or H7725 and H410; captive (or returned) of God; Shebuel or Shubael, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Shebuel, Shubael.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁוּבאל שׁבוּאל
Shebuel or Shubael = "captive of God"
1. son of Gershom and grandson of Moses
2. one of the 14 sons of Heman the musician
Origin: from H7617 (abbrev) or H7725 and H410
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
