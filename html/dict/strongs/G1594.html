ἐκνήφω
to sleep off a drunken fit, become sober again
ἐκνήφω (figuratively) to rouse (oneself) out of stupor
Derivation: from G1537 and G3525;
KJV Usage: awake. G1537 G3525
Thayer:
1) to return to one's self from drunkenness, become sober
2) metaph. to return to soberness of mind
