μεταπέμπω
to send after
μεταπέμπω to send from elsewhere, i.e. (middle voice) to summon or invite
Derivation: from G3326 and G3992;
KJV Usage: call (send) for. G3326 G3992
Thayer:
1) to send one after another
2) like our send after i.e. to send for
3) to send after for one's self, cause to be sent
