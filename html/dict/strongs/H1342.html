גּאה
gâ'âh
gaw-aw'
A primitive root; to mount up; hence in general to rise, (figuratively) be majestic
KJV Usage: gloriously, grow up, increase, be risen, triumph.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּאה

1. to rise up, grow up, be exalted in triumph
a. (Qal)
1. to rise up
2. to grow up
3. to be lifted up, be raised up, be exalted
