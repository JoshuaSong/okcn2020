συγκατατίθεμαι to deposit (one's vote or opinion) in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to accord with
Derivation: middle from G4862 and G2698;
KJV Usage: consent. G4862 G2698
Thayer:
1) to deposit together with another
2) to deposit one's vote in the urn with another
3) to consent to, vote for, agree with
