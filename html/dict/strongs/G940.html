βασκαίνω
to slander, malign, belie, disparage
βασκαίνω to malign, i.e. (by extension) to fascinate (by false representations)
Derivation: akin to G5335;
KJV Usage: bewitch. G5335
Thayer:
1) to speak ill of one, to slander, traduce him
2) to bring evil on one by feigning praise or an evil eye
3) to charm, to bewitch
