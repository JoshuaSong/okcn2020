רבלה
riblâh
rib-law'
From an unused root meaning to be fruitful; fertile; Riblah, a place in Syria
KJV Usage: Riblah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רבלה
Riblah = "fertility"
1. a place on the eastern boundary of Israel
2. a town in the land of Hamath on the great road between Babylon and Palestine
Origin: from an unused root meaning to be fruitful
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Location
