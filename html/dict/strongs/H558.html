אמציהוּ אמציה
'ămatsyâh 'ămatsyâhû
am-ats-yaw', am-ats-yaw'-hoo
From H553 and H3050; strength of Jah; Amatsjah, the name of four Israelites
KJV Usage: Amaziah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אמציהוּ אמציה
Amaziah = "Jehovah is mighty"
1. a king of Judah, son Joash, father of Azariah
2. a priest of Bethel under Jeroboam II
3. father of Joshah, of the tribe of Simeon
4. a Levite tabernacle singer in David's day
Origin: from H553 and H3050
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
