deeply grieved
περίλυπος grieved all around, i.e. intensely sad
Derivation: from G4012 and G3077;
KJV Usage: exceeding (very) sorry(-owful). G4012 G3077
Thayer:
1) very sad, exceedingly sorrowful
2) overcome with sorrow so much as to cause one's death
