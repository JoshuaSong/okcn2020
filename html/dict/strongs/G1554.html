ἐκδίδωμι
to give up, surrender
ἐκδίδωμι to give forth, i.e. (specially) to lease
Derivation: from G1537 and G1325;
KJV Usage: let forth (out). G1537 G1325
Thayer:
1) to give out of one's house, power, hand, stores
2) to give out, give up, give over
3) to let out for hire
4) to farm out
5) to let out for one's advantage
