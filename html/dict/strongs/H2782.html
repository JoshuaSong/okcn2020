חרץ
chârats
khw-rats'
A prim root; properly to point sharply, that is, (literally) to wound; figuratively to be alert, to decide
KJV Usage: bestir self, decide, decree, determine, maim, move.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חרץ

1. to cut, sharpen, decide, decree, determine, maim, move, be decisive, be mutilated
a. (Qal)
1. to cut, mutilate
2. to sharpen
3. to decide
b. (Niphal) to be decisive
Origin: a primitive root
