ὀργίζω
to make angry, provoke to anger, irritate
ὀργίζω to provoke or enrage, i.e. (passively) become exasperated
Derivation: from G3709;
KJV Usage: be angry (wroth). G3709
Thayer:
1) to provoke, to arouse to anger
2) to be provoked to anger, be angry, be wroth
