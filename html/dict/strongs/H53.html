אבשׁלום אבישׁלום
'ăbı̂yshâlôm 'abshâlôm
ab-ee-shaw-lome', ab-shaw-lome'
From H1 and H7965; father of peace (that is, friendly); Abshalom, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite
KJV Usage: Abishalom, Absalom.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אבשׁלום אבישׁלום
Absalom or Abishalom = "my father is peace"
1. father-in-law of Rehoboam
2. third son of David, killer of first-born son Amnon, also leader of revolt against his father - David
Origin: from H1 and H7965
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
