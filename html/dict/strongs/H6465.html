Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּעור
Peor = "cleft" n pr loc
1. a mountain peak in Moab belonging to the Abarim range and near Pisgah n pr deity
2. a false god worshipped in Moab; corresponds to Baal
Origin: from H6473
TWOT: None
