φυσικός
natural, native
φυσικός "physical", i.e. (by implication) instinctive
Derivation: from G5449;
KJV Usage: natural. Compare G5591. G5449 G5591
Thayer:
1) produced by nature, inborn
2) agreeable to nature
3) governed by (the instincts of) nature
