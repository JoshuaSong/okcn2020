προσμένω
to bide
προσμένω to stay further, i.e. remain in a place, with a person; figuratively, to adhere to, persevere in
Derivation: from G4314 and G3306;
KJV Usage: abide still, be with, cleave unto, continue in (with). G4314 G3306
Thayer:
1) to remain with, to continue with one
2) to hold fast to: the grace of God received in the Gospel
3) to remain still, tarry, stay
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words

  • Cleave, Clave
  • Continue, Continuance
  • Tarry
