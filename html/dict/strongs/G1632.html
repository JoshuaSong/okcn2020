ἐκχέω
to pour out
ἐκχέω , or (by variation) ἐκχύνω to pour forth; figuratively, to bestow
Derivation: from G1537 and χέω (to pour);
KJV Usage: gush (pour) out, run greedily (out), shed (abroad, forth), spill. G1537
Thayer:
1) to pour out, shed forth
2) metaph. to bestow or distribute largely
