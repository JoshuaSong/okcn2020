נביאה
ne bı̂y'âh
neb-ee-yaw'
Feminine of H5030; a prophetess or (generally) inspired woman; by implication a poetess; by association a prophet's wife
KJV Usage: prophetess.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נביאה

1. prophetess
a. ancient type endowed with gift of song (Miriam)
b. later type consulted for a word (Huldah)
c. false prophetess (Noadiah)
d. wife of Isaiah the prophet
Origin: from H5030
TWOT: 1277c
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
