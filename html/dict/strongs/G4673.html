σορός
a vessel for holding
σορός a funereal receptacle (urn, coffin), i.e. (by analogy) a bier
Derivation: probably akin to the base of G4987;
KJV Usage: bier. G4987
Thayer:
1) an urn or receptacle for keeping the bones of the dead
2) the funeral couch or bier on which the Jews carried their dead forth to burial
