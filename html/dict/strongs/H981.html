בּטה בּטא
bâṭâ' bâṭâh
baw-taw', baw-taw'
A primitive root; to babble; hence to vociferate angrily
KJV Usage: pronounce, speak (unadvisedly).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּטה בּטא

1. to speak rashly or angrily, speak thoughtlessly
a. (Qal) one that babbles, speaks rashly (part.)
b. (Piel) to speak rashly, unadvisedly
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 232
Parts of Speech: Verb
