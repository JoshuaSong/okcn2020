ἐπιτυγχάνω
to hit the mark
ἐπιτυγχάνω to chance upon, i.e. (by implication) to attain
Derivation: from G1909 and G5177;
KJV Usage: obtain. G1909 G5177
Thayer:
1) to light or hit upon any person or thing
2) to attain to, obtain
