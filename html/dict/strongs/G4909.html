συνευδοκέω
to consent to
συνευδοκέω to think well of in common, i.e. assent to, feel gratified with
Derivation: from G4862 and G2106;
KJV Usage: allow, assent, be pleased, have pleasure. G4862 G2106
Thayer:
1) to be pleased together with, to approve together (with others)
2) to be pleased at the same time with, consent, agree to
2a) to applaud
