ἀποπλανάω
to lead astray
ἀποπλανάω to lead astray (figuratively); passively, to stray (from truth)
Derivation: from G575 and G4105;
KJV Usage: err, seduce. G575 G4105
Thayer:
1) to cause to go astray
2) to lead away from the truth to error
3) to go astray, stray away from
