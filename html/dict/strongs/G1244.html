διαιρέω
to take one from another, to cleave in twain, to divide into parts
διαιρέω to separate, i.e. distribute
Derivation: from G1223 and G138;
KJV Usage: divide. G1223 G138
Thayer:
1) to divide into parts, to part, to tear, cleave or cut asunder
2) to distribute
