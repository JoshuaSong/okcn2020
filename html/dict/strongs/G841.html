αὐτάρκεια
sufficiency in oneself, independence
αὐτάρκεια self-satisfaction, i.e. (abstractly) contentedness, or (concretely) a competence
Derivation: from G842;
KJV Usage: contentment, sufficiency. G842
Thayer:
1) a perfect condition of life in which no aid or support is needed
2) sufficiency of the necessities of life
3) a mind contented with its lot, contentment
