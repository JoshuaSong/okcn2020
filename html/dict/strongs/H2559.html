חמק
châmaq
khaw-mak'
A primitive root; properly to enwrap; hence to depart (that is, turn about)
KJV Usage: go about, withdraw self.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חמק

1. to withdraw, turn around, turn away
a. (Qal) to turn away
b. (Hithpael) to turn about, vacillate, turn hither and thither
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: TWOT- 682
Parts of Speech: Verb
