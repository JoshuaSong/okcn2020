ἀχρεῖος
useless, unprofitable, good for nothing
ἀχρεῖος useless, i.e. (euphemistically) unmeritorious
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5534 (compare G5532);
KJV Usage: unprofitable. G1 G5534 G5532
Thayer:
1) useless, good for nothing
