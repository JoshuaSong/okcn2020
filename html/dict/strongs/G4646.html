σκολιός
curved, winding, twisted, tangled
σκολιός warped, i.e. winding; figuratively, perverse
Derivation: from the base of G4628;
KJV Usage: crooked, froward, untoward. G4628
Thayer:
1) crooked, curved
2) metaph.
2a) perverse, wicked
2b) unfair, surly, froward
