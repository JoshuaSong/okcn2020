נפך
nôphek
no'-fek
From an unused root meaning to glisten; shining; a gem, probably the garnet
KJV Usage: emerald.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נפך

1. a precious stone in the high priest's breastplate
a. perhaps an emerald, turquoise, ruby, or carbuncle
2. jewels imported from Tyre
Origin: from an unused root meaning to glisten
TWOT: 1391
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
