κατακόπτω
to cut down, cut in pieces, cut up
κατακόπτω to chop down, i.e. mangle
Derivation: from G2596 and G2875;
KJV Usage: cut. G2596 G2875
Thayer:
1) to cut up in pieces
2) to slay
3) to beat, bruise
4) to cut, gash, mangle
