καταχθόνιος
subterranean
καταχθόνιος subterranean, i.e. infernal (belonging to the world of departed spirits)
Derivation: from G2596 and χθών (the ground);
KJV Usage: under the earth. G2596
Thayer:
1) subterranean
1a) refers to those who dwell in the world below, departed souls
