ὑποδέω
to bind
ὑποδέω to bind under one's feet, i.e. put on shoes or sandals
Derivation: from G5259 and G1210;
KJV Usage: bind on, (be) shod. G5259 G1210
Thayer:
1) to underbind
2) to bind under one's self, bind on
