ἄθεος
without God, denying the gods
ἄθεος godless
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G2316;
KJV Usage: without God. G1 G2316
Thayer:
1) without God, knowing and worshipping no God
2) denying the gods, esp. the recognised gods of the state
3) godless, ungodly
4) abandoned by the gods
