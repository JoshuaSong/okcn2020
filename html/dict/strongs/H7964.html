שׁלּח שׁלּוּח
shillûach shillûach
shil-loo'-akh, shil-loo'-akh
From H7971; (only in plural) a dismissal, that is, (of a wife) divorce (especially the document); also (of a daughter) dower
KJV Usage: presents, have sent back.
