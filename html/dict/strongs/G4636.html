σκῆνος
the body
σκῆνος a hut or temporary residence, i.e. (figuratively) the human body (as the abode of the spirit)
Derivation: from G4633;
KJV Usage: tabernacle. G4633
Thayer:
1) a tabernacle, a tent
2) metaph. of the human body, in which the soul dwells as in a tent, and which is taken down at death
