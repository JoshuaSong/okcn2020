συγχράομαι
to make joint use of, avail oneself of
συγχράομαι to use jointly, i.e. (by implication) to hold intercourse in common
Derivation: from G4862 and G5530;
KJV Usage: have dealings with. G4862 G5530
Thayer:
1) to use with anyone, use jointly
2) to associate with, to have dealings with
