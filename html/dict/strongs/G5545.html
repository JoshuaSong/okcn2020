χρῖσμα
anything smeared on
χρῖσμα an unguent or smearing, i.e. (figuratively) the special endowment ("chrism") of the Holy Spirit
Derivation: from G5548;
KJV Usage: anointing, unction. G5548
Thayer:
1) anything smeared on, unguent, ointment, usually prepared by the Hebrews from oil and aromatic herbs. Anointing was the inaugural ceremony for priests
