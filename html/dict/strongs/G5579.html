ψεῦδος a falsehood
Derivation: from G5574;
KJV Usage: lie, lying. G5574
Thayer:
1) a lie
2) conscious and intentional falsehood
3) in a broad sense, whatever is not what it seems to be
3a) of perverse, impious, deceitful precepts
