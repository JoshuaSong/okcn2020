מגנּה מגן
mâgên me ginnâh
maw-gane', meg-in-naw'
From H1598; a shield (that is, the small one or buckler); figuratively a protector; also the scaly hide of the crocodile
KJV Usage: X armed, buckler, defence, ruler, + scale, shield.
