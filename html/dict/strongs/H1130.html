ben - hădad
ben-had-ad'
From H1121 and H1908; son of Hadad; Ben Hadad, the name of several Syrian kings
KJV Usage: Ben-hadad.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּן־הדד
Ben-hadad = "son of [the false god] Hadad"
1. the king of Syria, contemporary with Asa of Judah
2. the son of Hazael, also king of Syria
Origin: from H1121 and H1908
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
