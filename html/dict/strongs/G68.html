ἀγρός
fields, lands
ἀγρός a field (as a drive for cattle); genitive case, the country; specially, a farm, i.e. hamlet
Derivation: from G71;
KJV Usage: country, farm, piece of ground, land. G71
Thayer:
1) land
1a) the field, the country
1b) a piece of land, bit of tillage
1c) the farms, country seats, neighbouring hamlets
