στόμαχος
a mouth, opening
στόμαχος an orifice (the gullet), i.e. (specially), the "stomach"
Derivation: from G4750;
KJV Usage: stomach. G4750
Thayer:
1) the throat
2) an opening, orifice, esp. of the stomach
3) the stomach
