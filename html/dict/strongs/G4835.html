συμπαθής
sympathizing with
συμπαθής having a fellow-feeling ("sympathetic"), i.e. (by implication) mutually commiserative
Derivation: from G4841;
KJV Usage: having compassion one of another. G4841
Thayer:
1) suffering or feeling the like with another, sympathetic
