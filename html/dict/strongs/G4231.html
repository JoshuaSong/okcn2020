πραγματεύομαι
to busy
πραγματεύομαι to busy oneself with, i.e. to trade
Derivation: from G4229;
KJV Usage: occupy. G4229
Thayer:
1) to be occupied in anything
2) to carry on a business
3) to carry on the business of a banker or a trader
