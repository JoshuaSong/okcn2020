τάγμα
that which has been ordered
τάγμα something orderly in arrangement (a troop), i.e. (figuratively) a series or succession
Derivation: from G5021;
KJV Usage: order. G5021
Thayer:
1) that which has been arranged, thing placed in order
2) a body of soldiers, a corps
3) band, troop, class
