מתוּשׁאל
me thûshâ'êl
meth-oo-shaw-ale'
From H4962 and H410, with the relative interposed; man who (is) of God; Methushael, an antediluvian patriarch
KJV Usage: Methusael.

מתוּשׁאל
Methusael = "who is of God"
1. son of Mehujael, 4th in descent from Cain, and father of Lamech
