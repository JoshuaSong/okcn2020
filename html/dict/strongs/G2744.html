καυχάομαι
to speak loud, be loud-tongued
καυχάομαι to vaunt (in a good or a bad sense)
Derivation: from some (obsolete) base akin to that of αὐχέω (to boast) and G2172;
KJV Usage: (make) boast, glory, joy, rejoice. G2172
Thayer:
1) to glory (whether with reason or without)
2) to glory on account of a thing
3) to glory in a thing

  • Joy (Noun and Verb), Joyfulness, Joyfully, Joyous
  • Rejoice
