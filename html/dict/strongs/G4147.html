πλουτέω
to be rich, wealthy
πλουτέω to be (or become) wealthy (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4148;
KJV Usage: be increased with goods, (be made, wax) rich. G4148
Thayer:
1) to be rich, to have abundance
1a) of outward possessions
2) metaph. to be richly supplied
2a) is affluent in resources so that he can give blessings of salvation to all
