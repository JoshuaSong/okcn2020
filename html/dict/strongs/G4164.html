ποικίλος
many-coloured, spotted, mottled, pied, dappled
ποικίλος motley, i.e. various in character
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: divers, manifold.
Thayer:
1) a various colours, variegated
2) of various sorts
