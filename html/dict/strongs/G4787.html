συγκινέω
to stir up together
συγκινέω to move together, i.e. (specially), to excite as a mass (to sedition)
Derivation: from G4682 and G2795;
KJV Usage: stir up. G4682 G2795
Thayer:
1) to move together with others
2) to throw into commotion, excite, stir up
