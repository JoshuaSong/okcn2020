νέκρωσις
a state of death, deadness: death
νέκρωσις decease; figuratively, impotency
Derivation: from G3499;
KJV Usage: deadness, dying. G3499
Thayer:
1) putting to death, killing
2) being put to death
3) the dead state, utter sluggishness
3a) of bodily members and organs
