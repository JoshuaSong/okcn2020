יעוּשׁ
ye ‛ûsh
yeh-oosh'
From H5789; hasty; Jeush, the name of an Edomite and of four Israelites
KJV Usage: Jehush, Jeush. Compare H3274.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יעוּשׁ
Jehush or Jeush = "He hurries to aid"
1. son of Eshek, a remote descendant of king Saul
2. a Gershonite Levite of the house of Shimei
3. son of king Rehoboam of Judah
Origin: from H5789
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
