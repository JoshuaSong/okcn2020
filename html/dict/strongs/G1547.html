ἐκγαμίζω
to give in marriage
ἐκγαμίζω to marry off a daughter
Derivation: from G1537 and a form of G1061 (compare G1548);
KJV Usage: give in marriage. G1537 G1061 G1548
Thayer:
1) to give away in marriage: a daughter
2) to marry, to be given in marriage
