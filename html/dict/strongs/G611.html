ἀποκρίνομαι to conclude for oneself, i.e. (by implication) to respond; by Hebraism (compare H6030) to begin to speak (where an address is expected)
Derivation: from G575 and κρίνω;
KJV Usage: answer. G575 H6030
Thayer:
1) to give an answer to a question proposed, to answer
2) to begin to speak, but always where something has preceded (either said or done) to which the remarks refer
