ῥᾳδιούργημα
a reckless act, crime
ῥᾳδιούργημα easy-going behavior, i.e. (by extension) a crime
Derivation: from a comparative of ῥᾴδιος (easy, i.e. reckless) and G2041;
KJV Usage: lewdness. G2041
Thayer:
1) a piece of knavery, rascality, villainy
