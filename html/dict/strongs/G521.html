ἀπαίδευτος
uneducated
ἀπαίδευτος uninstructed, i.e. (figuratively) stupid
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3811;
KJV Usage: unlearned. G1 G3811
Thayer:
1) without instruction, and disciple, uneducated, ignorant, rude
