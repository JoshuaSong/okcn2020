σκοπός
one that watches, one that looks after
σκοπός perhaps akin to G4626 through the idea of concealment; compare G4629); a watch (sentry or scout), i.e. (by implication) a goal
Derivation: from σκέπτομαι (to peer about ("skeptic");
KJV Usage: mark. G4626 G4629
Thayer:
1) an observer, a watchman
2) the distant mark looked at, the goal or end one has in view
