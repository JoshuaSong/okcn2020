Γόμοῤῥα Gomorrha (i.e. Amorah), a place near the Dead Sea
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H6017);
KJV Usage: Gomorrha. H6017
Thayer:
1) a city in eastern part of Judah that was destroyed when the Lord rained fire and brimstone on it; now covered by the Dead Sea
Literal: Gomorrha = "submersion"
