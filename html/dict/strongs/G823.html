ἄτομος
uncut, unmown
ἄτομος uncut, i.e. (by implication) indivisible (an "atom" of time)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and the base of G5114;
KJV Usage: moment. G1 G5114
Thayer:
1) that cannot be cut in two, or divided, indivisible
1a) of a moment of time
