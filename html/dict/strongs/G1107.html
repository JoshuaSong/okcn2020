γνωρίζω
to make known, point out, explain
γνωρίζω to make known; subjectively, to know
Derivation: from a derivative of G1097;
KJV Usage: certify, declare, make known, give to understand, do to wit, wot. G1097
Thayer:
1) to make known
1a) to become known, be recognised
2) to know, to gain knowledge of, have thorough knowledge of
2a) in earlier Greek it means "to gain a knowledge of" or "have thorough knowledge of"
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Know, Known, Knowledge, Unknown
  • Understand, Understood
  • Wit (to)
