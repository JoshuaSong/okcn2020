ἅλλομαι
to spring, leap, bound
ἅλλομαι to jump; figuratively, to gush
Derivation: middle voice of apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: leap, spring up.
Thayer:
1) to leap
2) to spring up, gush up: of water
