ἐπιλύω
to loose, untie
ἐπιλύω to solve further, i.e. (figuratively) to explain, decide
Derivation: from G1909 and G3089;
KJV Usage: determine, expound. G1909 G3089
Thayer:
1) to unloose, untie
2) to clear (a controversy), decide, settle
3) to explain (what is obscure and hard to understand)
