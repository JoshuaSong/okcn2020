Ἑρμῆς
Hermes
Ἑρμῆς Hermes, the name of the messenger of the Greek deities; also of a Christian
Derivation: perhaps from G2046;
KJV Usage: Hermes, Mercury. G2046
Thayer:
1) a Greek deity called by the Romans Mercurius (Mercury)
2) a certain Christian
Literal: Mercurius or Hermes = "herald of the gods"
