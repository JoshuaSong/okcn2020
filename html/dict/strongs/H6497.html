פּקע
peqa‛
peh'-kah
From an unused root meaning to burst; only used as an architectural term of an ornament similar to H6498, a semi-globe
KJV Usage: knop.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּקע

1. knob-shaped or ball-shaped or gourd-shaped carved wood or metal ornament
Origin: from an unused root meaning to burst
TWOT: 1804a
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
