διαχωρίζομαι to remove (oneself) wholly, i.e. retire
Derivation: from G1223 and the middle voice of G5563;
KJV Usage: depart. G1223 G5563
Thayer:
1) to separate thoroughly or wholly
2) to separate one's self, depart
