καύχημα
a boast, vaunt
καύχημα a boast (properly, the object; by implication, the act) in a good or a bad sense
Derivation: from G2744;
KJV Usage: boasting, (whereof) to glory (of), glorying, rejoice(-ing). G2744
Thayer:
1) that of which one glories or can glory, matter or ground of glorying
2) a glorying or boasting
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Glory (to boast), Glorying
