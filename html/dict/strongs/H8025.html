שׁלף
shâlaph
shaw-laf'
A primitive root; to pull out, up or off
KJV Usage: draw (off), grow up, pluck off.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁלף

1. to draw out or off, take off
a. (Qal)
1. to draw out
2. to draw off
3. to shoot up, draw out (of blade of grass on roof)
