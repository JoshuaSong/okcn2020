αἰσθητήριον
an organ of sense
αἰσθητήριον properly, an organ of perception, i.e. (figuratively) judgment
Derivation: from a derivative of G143;
KJV Usage: senses. G143
Thayer:
1) faculty of the mind for perceiving, understanding, judging
