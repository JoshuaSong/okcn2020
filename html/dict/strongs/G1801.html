ἐνωτίζομαι
to hearken to
ἐνωτίζομαι to take in one's ear, i.e. to listen
Derivation: middle voice from a compound of G1722 and G3775;
KJV Usage: hearken. G1722 G3775
Thayer:
1) to receive into the ear, to give ear to, listen
