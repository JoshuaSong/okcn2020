καταπονέω
to subdue after a hard struggle
καταπονέω to labor down, i.e. wear with toil (figuratively, harass)
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of G4192;
KJV Usage: oppress, vex. G2596 G4192
Thayer:
1) to tire down with toil, exhaust with labour
1a) to afflict or oppress with evils
1b) to make trouble for
1c) to treat roughly
