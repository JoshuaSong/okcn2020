אין
'ayin
ay'-yin
As if from a primitive root meaning to be nothing or not exist; a non-entity; generally used as a negative particle
KJV Usage: else, except, fail [father-] less, be gone, in [-curable], neither, never, no (where), none, nor (any, thing), not, nothing, to nought, past, un [-searchable], well-nigh, without, Compare H370.
