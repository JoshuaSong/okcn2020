ὀρφανός
an orphan
ὀρφανός bereaved ("orphan"), i.e. parentless
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: comfortless, fatherless.
Thayer:
1) bereft (of a father, of parents)
1a) of those bereft of a teacher, guide, guardian
1b) orphaned
