φλύαρος
silly talk, foolery, nonsense
φλύαρος a garrulous person, i.e. prater
Derivation: from φλύω (to bubble);
KJV Usage: tattler.
Thayer:
1) of persons uttering or doing silly things, garrulous, babbling
2) of things, foolish, trifling, vain
