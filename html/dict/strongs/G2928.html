κρύπτω
to hide, cover, cloak
κρύπτω to conceal (properly, by covering)
Derivation: a primary verb;
KJV Usage: hide (self), keep secret, secret(-ly).
Thayer:
1) to hide, conceal, to be hid
2) escape notice
3) metaph. to conceal (that it may not become known)
