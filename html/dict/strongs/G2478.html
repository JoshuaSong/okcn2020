ἰσχυρός
strong, mighty
ἰσχυρός forcible (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2479;
KJV Usage: boisterous, mighty(-ier), powerful, strong(-er, man), valiant. G2479
Thayer:
1) strong, mighty
1a) of living beings
1a1) strong either in body or in mind
1a2) of one who has strength of soul to sustain the attacks of Satan, strong and therefore exhibiting many excellences
1b) on inanimate things
1b1) strong, violent, forcibly uttered, firm, sure

  • Powerful, Powerfully
  • Strong, Stronger
