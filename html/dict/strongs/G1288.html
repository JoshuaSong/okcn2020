διασπάω
to tear asunder, part forcibly
διασπάω to draw apart, i.e. sever or dismember
Derivation: from G1223 and G4685;
KJV Usage: pluck asunder, pull in pieces. G1223 G4685
Thayer:
1) to rend asunder, break asunder
1a) of a man, to tear in pieces
