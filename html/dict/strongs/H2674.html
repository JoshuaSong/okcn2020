חצור
châtsôr
khaw-tsore'
A collective form of H2691; village; Chatsor, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine and of one in Arabia
KJV Usage: Hazor.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חצור
Hazor = "castle"
1. a royal city of northern Palestine allotted to Naphtali
2. one of the cities of Judah in the extreme south
3. a town north of Jerusalem in which the Benjamites resided after the return from exile
4. a site in Arabia
Origin: a collective form of H2691
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Location
