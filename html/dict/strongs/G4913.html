συνήδομαι
to rejoice together
συνήδομαι to rejoice in with oneself, i.e. feel satisfaction concerning
Derivation: middle voice from G4862 and the base of G2237;
KJV Usage: delight. G4862 G2237
Thayer:
1) to rejoice together with (another or others)
2) in the NT once to rejoice or delight with one's self or inwardly
