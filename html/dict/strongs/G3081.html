λυσιτελεῖ impersonally, it answers the purpose, i.e. is advantageous
Derivation: third person singular present indicative active of a derivative of a compound of G3080 and G5056;
KJV Usage: it is better. G3080 G5056
Thayer:
1) to pay the taxes
2) to return expenses
3) to be useful, advantageous, it profits
