ἐμπρήθω
to blow up, inflate
ἐμπρήθω to enkindle, i.e. set on fire
Derivation: from G1722 and πρήθω (to blow a flame);
KJV Usage: burn up. G1722
Thayer:
1) to burn
2) destroy by fire
