גּזז
gâzaz
gaw-zaz'
A primitive root (akin to H1468); to cut off; specifically to shear a flock, or shave the hair; figuratively to destroy an enemy
KJV Usage: cut off (down), poll, shave, ([sheep-]) shear (-er).
