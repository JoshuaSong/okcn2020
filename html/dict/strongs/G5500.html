χειροτονέω
to stretch out the hand
χειροτονέω to be a hand-reacher or voter (by raising the hand), i.e. (generally) to select or appoint
Derivation: from a comparative of G5495 and τείνω (to stretch);
KJV Usage: choose, ordain. G5495
Thayer:
1) to vote by stretching out the hand
2) to create or appoint by vote: one to have charge of some office or duty
3) to elect, create, appoint
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words

  • Choice, Choose, Chosen
