θροέω
to cry aloud
θροέω to clamor, i.e. (by implication) to frighten
Derivation: from θρέομαι to wail;
KJV Usage: trouble.
Thayer:
1) to cry aloud, make a noise by outcry
1a) in the NT, to trouble, frighten
1b) to be troubled in mind, to be frightened, alarmed
