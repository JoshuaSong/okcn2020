ἐνδυναμόω
to strengthen
ἐνδυναμόω to empower
Derivation: from G1722 and G1412;
KJV Usage: enable, (increase in) strength(-en), be (make) strong. G1722 G1412
Thayer:
1) to be strong, endue with strength, strengthen
2) to receive strength, be strengthened, increase in strength
3) in a bad sense
3a) be bold, headstrong
