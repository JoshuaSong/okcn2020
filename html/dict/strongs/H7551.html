רקם
râqam
raw-kam'
A primitive root; to variegate color, that is, embroider; by implication to fabricate
KJV Usage: embroiderer, needlework, curiously work.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רקם

1. to variegate, mix colours
a. (Qal) variegator (worker in colours) (participle)
b. (Pual) to be skilfully wrought or woven
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2216
Parts of Speech: Verb
