par‛ôh ne kôh par‛ôh ne kô

Of Egyptian derivation; Paroh Nekoh (or Neko), an Egyptian king
KJV Usage: Pharaoh-necho, Pharaoh-nechoh.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּרעה נכו פּרעה נכה
Pharaoh-necho = "great house-he is smitten"
1. the Pharaoh of Egypt who fought king Josiah of Judah at Megiddo and killed him
Origin: of Egyptian derivation
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
