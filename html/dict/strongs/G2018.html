ἐπιφέρω
to bring, put
ἐπιφέρω to bear upon (or further), i.e. adduce (personally or judicially (accuse, inflict)), superinduce
Derivation: from G1909 and G5342;
KJV Usage: add, bring (against), take. G1909 G5342
Thayer:
1) to bring upon, bring forward
1a) used of accusers
2) to lay upon, to inflict
3) to bring upon, i.e. in addition, to add, increase
4) to put upon, cast upon, impose
