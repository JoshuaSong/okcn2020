σκήνωμα an encampment, i.e. (figuratively) the Temple (as God's residence), the body (as a tenement for the soul)
Derivation: from G4637;
KJV Usage: tabernacle. G4637
Thayer:
1) a tent, a tabernacle
1a) of the temple as God's habitation
1b) of the tabernacle of the covenant
1c) metaph. of the human body as the dwelling of the soul
