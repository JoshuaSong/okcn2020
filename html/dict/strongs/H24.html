אביב
'âbı̂yb
aw-beeb'
From an unused root (meaning to be tender); green, that is a young ear of grain; hence the name of the month Abib or Nisan
KJV Usage: Abib, ear, green ears of corn.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אביב

1. fresh, young barley ears, barley
2. month of ear-forming, of greening of crop, of growing green Abib, month of exodus and passover (March or April)
Origin: from an unused root (meaning to be tender)
TWOT: 1b
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
