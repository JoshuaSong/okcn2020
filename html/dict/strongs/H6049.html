ענן
‛ânan
aw-nan'
A primitive root; to cover; used only as denominative from H6051, to cloud over; figuratively to act covertly, that is, practise magic
KJV Usage: X bring, enchanter, Meonemin, observe (-r of) times, soothsayer, sorcerer.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ענן

1. (Piel) to make appear, produce, bring (clouds)
2. (Poel) to practise soothsaying, conjure
a. to observe times, practice soothsaying or spiritism or magic or augury or witchcraft
b. soothsayer, enchanter, sorceress, diviner, fortuneteller, barbarian, Meonenim (participle)
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1655,1656
Parts of Speech: Verb
