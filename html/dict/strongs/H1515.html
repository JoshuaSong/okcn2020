Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּחר
Gahar = "hiding place"
1. the head of a family of temple slaves returning from exile with Zerubbabel
Origin: from an unused root meaning to hide
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
