פּזז
pâzaz
paw-zaz'
A primitive root (rather identical with H6338); to solidify (as if by refining); also to spring (as if separating the limbs)
KJV Usage: leap, be made strong.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּזז

1. to bound, be agile, be supple
a. (Qal) to be nimble
b. (Piel) to leap, show agility
Origin: a primitive root [identical with H6338]
TWOT: 1754
Parts of Speech: Verb
