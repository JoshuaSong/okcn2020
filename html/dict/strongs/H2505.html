חלק
châlaq
khaw-lak'
A primitive root; to be smooth (figuratively); by implication (as smooth stones were used for lots) to apportion or separate
KJV Usage: deal, distribute, divide, flatter, give, (have, im-) part (-ner), take away a portion, receive, separate self, (be) smooth (-er).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חלק

1. to divide, share, plunder, allot, apportion, assign
a. (Qal)
1. to divide, apportion
2. to assign, distribute
3. to assign, impart
4. to share
5. to divide up, plunder
b. (Niphal)
1. to divide oneself
2. to be divided
3. to assign, distribute
c. (Piel)
1. to divide, apportion
2. to assign, distribute
3. to scatter
d. (Pual) to be divided
e. (Hiphil) to receive a portion or part
f. (Hithpael) to divide among themselves
2. to be smooth, slippery, deceitful
a. (Qal) to be smooth, slippery
b. (Hiphil)
1. to be smooth
2. to flatter
Origin: a primitive root
