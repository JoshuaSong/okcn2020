θεμελιόω
to lay the foundation of, found firmly
θεμελιόω to lay a basis for, i.e. (literally) erect, or (figuratively) consolidate
Derivation: from G2310;
KJV Usage: (lay the) found(- ation), ground, settle. G2310
Thayer:
1) to lay the foundation, to found
2) to make stable, establish
