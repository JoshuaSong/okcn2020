βραχίων
the arm
βραχίων the arm, i.e. (figuratively) strength
Derivation: properly, comparative of G1024, but apparently in the sense of βράσσω (to wield);
KJV Usage: arm. G1024
Thayer:
1) the arm
1a) the arm of God is a Hebrew idiom for the might and the power of God
