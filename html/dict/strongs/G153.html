αἰσχύνομαι to feel shame (for oneself)
Derivation: from αἶσχος (disfigurement, i.e. disgrace);
KJV Usage: be ashamed.
Thayer:
1) to disfigure
2) to dishonour
3) to suffuse with shame, make ashamed, be ashamed
