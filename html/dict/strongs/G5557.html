χρυσός
gold
χρυσός gold; by extension, a golden article, as an ornament or coin
Derivation: perhaps from the base of G5530 (through the idea of the utility of the metal);
KJV Usage: gold. G5530
Thayer:
1) precious things made of gold, golden ornaments
1a) an image made of gold
1b) stamped gold, gold coin
