ἀπερισπάστως undistractedly, i.e. free from (domestic) solicitude
Derivation: adverb from a compound of G1 (as a negative participle) and a presumed derivative of G4049;
KJV Usage: without distraction. G1 G4049
Thayer:
1) without distraction, without solicitude or anxiety or care
