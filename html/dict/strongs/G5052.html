τελεσφορέω
to bring fruit to perfection
τελεσφορέω to be a bearer to completion (maturity), i.e. to ripen fruit (figuratively)
Derivation: from a compound of G5056 and G5342;
KJV Usage: bring fruit to perfection. G5056 G5342
Thayer:
1) to bring to (perfection or) maturity
1a) of fruits
1b) of pregnant women
1c) of animals bringing their young to maturity
