πρόσκλισις
inclination, proclivity
πρόσκλισις a leaning towards, i.e. (figuratively) proclivity (favoritism)
Derivation: from a compound of G4314 and G2827;
KJV Usage: partiality. G4314 G2827
Thayer:
1) an inclination or proclivity of mind
2) a joining the party of one
3) partiality
