κοδράντης a quadrans, i.e. the fourth part of an as
Derivation: of Latin origin;
KJV Usage: farthing.
Thayer:
1) a quadrans (about the fourth part of an "as"); in the NT a coin equal to one half the Attic chalcus worth about 3/8 of a cent
