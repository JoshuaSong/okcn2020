פּרשׂ
pâraś
paw-ras'
A primitive root; to break apart, disperse, etc.
KJV Usage: break, chop in pieces, lay open, scatter, spread (abroad, forth, selves, out), stretch (forth, out).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּרשׂ

1. to spread, spread out, stretch, break in pieces
a. (Qal)
1. to spread out, display
2. to spread over
b. (Niphal) to be scattered, be spread out
c. (Piel)
1. to spread out
2. to scatter
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1832
Parts of Speech: Verb
