πυγμή
a fist
πυγμή the clenched hand, i.e. (only in dative case as adverb) with the fist (hard scrubbing)
Derivation: from a primary πύξ (the fist as a weapon);
KJV Usage: oft.
Thayer:
1) the fist, clenched hand
2) up to the elbow
