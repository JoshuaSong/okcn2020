מעל
mâ‛al
maw-al'
A primitive root; properly to cover up; used only figuratively to act covertly, that is, treacherously
KJV Usage: transgress, (commit, do a) tresspass (-ing).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מעל

1. to act unfaithfully, act treacherously, transgress, commit a trespass
a. (Qal) to act unfaithfully or treacherously
1. against man
2. against God
3. against devoted thing
4. against husband
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1230
Parts of Speech: Verb
