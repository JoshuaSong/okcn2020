ἐγκρύπτω
to hide
ἐγκρύπτω to conceal in, i.e. incorporate with
Derivation: from G1722 and G2928;
KJV Usage: hid in. G1722 G2928
Thayer:
1) to conceal in something
2) to mingle one thing with another
