Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁמרון
Shimron = "watch-height" n pr m
1. 4th son of Issachar and the progenitor of the family of Shimronites n pr loc
2. a Canaanite royal city allotted to Zebulun
Origin: from H8105 in its original sense
