συμπαρακαλέω
to invite together
συμπαρακαλέω to console jointly
Derivation: from G4862 and G3870;
KJV Usage: comfort together. G4862 G3870
Thayer:
1) to call upon or invite or exhort at the same time or together
2) to strengthen (comfort) with others
