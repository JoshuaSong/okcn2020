ἄνομος
without law, lawless
ἄνομος lawless, i.e. (negatively) not subject to (the Jewish) law; (by implication, a Gentile), or (positively) wicked
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G3551;
KJV Usage: without law, lawless, transgressor, unlawful, wicked. G1 G3551
Thayer:
1) destitute of (the Mosaic) law
1a) of the Gentiles
2) departing from the law, a violator of the law, lawless, wicked
