προσωπολήπτης
a respecter of persons
προσωπολήπτης an accepter of a face (individual), i.e. (specially), one exhibiting partiality
Derivation: from G4383 and G2983;
KJV Usage: respecter of persons. G4383 G2983
Thayer:
1) an acceptor of persons
2) one who discriminates
