כּעס
ka‛as
kaw-as'
A primitive root; to trouble; by implication to grieve, rage, be indignant
KJV Usage: be angry, be grieved, take indignation, provoke (to anger, unto wrath), have sorrow, vex, be wroth.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּעס

1. to be angry, be vexed, be indignant, be wroth, be grieved, provoke to anger and wrath
a. (Qal)
1. to be vexed, be indignant
2. to be angry
b. (Piel) to provoke to anger
c. (Hiphil)
1. to vex
2. to vex, provoke to anger
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1016
Parts of Speech: Verb
