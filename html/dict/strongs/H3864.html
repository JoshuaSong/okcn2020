לבּי לוּבי
lûbı̂y lûbbı̂y
loo-bee', loob-bee'
Patrial from a name probably derived from an unused root meaning to thirst, that is, a dry region; apparently a Libyan or inhabitant of interior Africa (only in plural)
KJV Usage: Lubim (-s), Libyans.
