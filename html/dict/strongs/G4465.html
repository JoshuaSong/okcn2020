ῥαβδοῦχος
one who carries a rod
ῥαβδοῦχος a rod- (the Latin fasces) holder, i.e. a Roman lictor (constable or executioner)
Derivation: from G4464 and G2192;
KJV Usage: serjeant. G4464 G2192
Thayer:
1) one who carries the rods i.e. the fasces
2) a lictor, a public official who bore the fasces or staff and other insignia of office before a magistrate
