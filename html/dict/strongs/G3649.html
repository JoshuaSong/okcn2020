ὀλολύζω
to cry to the gods with a loud voice, cry aloud
ὀλολύζω to "howl" or "halloo", i.e. shriek
Derivation: a reduplicated primary verb;
KJV Usage: howl.
Thayer:
1) to howl, wail, lament
1a) of a loud cry whether for joy or of grief
