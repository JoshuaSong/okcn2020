ἀρέσκω
to make good, make up
ἀρέσκω to be agreeable (or by implication, to seek to be so)
Derivation: probably from G142 (through the idea of exciting emotion);
KJV Usage: please. G142
Thayer:
1) to please
2) to strive to please
2a) to accommodate one's self to the opinions desires and interests of others
