ἔμπορος
one who goes on shipboard as a passenger
ἔμπορος a (wholesale) tradesman
Derivation: from G1722 and the base of G4198;
KJV Usage: merchant. G1722 G4198
Thayer:
1) one on a journey, whether by sea or by land, esp. for trade
2) a merchant as opposed to a retailer or petty tradesman
