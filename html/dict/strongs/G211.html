ἀλάβαστρον
a box
ἀλάβαστρον properly, an "alabaster" box, i.e. (by extension) a perfume vase (of any material)
Derivation: neuter of ἀλάβαστρος (of uncertain derivation), the name of a stone;
KJV Usage: (alabaster) box.
Thayer:
1) a box made of alabaster in which unguents are preserved
