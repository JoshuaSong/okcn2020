Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ינוחה ינוח
Janoah or Janohah = "He rests"
1. a place apparently in the north of Galilee in Naphtali, taken by Tiglath-pileser in his first incursion into Palestine
Origin: from H3240
TWOT: None
