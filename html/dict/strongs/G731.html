ἄῤῥητος unsaid, i.e. (by implication) inexpressible
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and the same as G4490;
KJV Usage: unspeakable. G1 G4490
Thayer:
1) unsaid, unspoken
2) unspeakable (on account of its sacredness)
