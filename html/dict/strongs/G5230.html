ὑπέρακμος
past the bloom of youth
ὑπέρακμος beyond the "acme", i.e. figuratively (of a daughter) past the bloom (prime) of youth
Derivation: from G5228 and the base of G188;
KJV Usage: + pass the flower of (her) age. G5228 G188
Thayer:
1) beyond the bloom or prime of life
2) overripe, plump and ripe, (and so in a greater danger of defilement)
2a) of a virgin
