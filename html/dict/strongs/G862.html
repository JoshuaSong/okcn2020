ἄφθαρτος
uncorrupted, incorruptible
ἄφθαρτος undecaying (in essence or continuance)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5351;
KJV Usage: not (in-, un-)corruptible, immortal. G1 G5351
Thayer:
1) uncorrupted, not liable to corruption or decay, imperishable
1a) of things
2) immortal
2a) of the risen dead

  • Incorruptible, unfading
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
