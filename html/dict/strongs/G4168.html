ποίμνιον
a flock
ποίμνιον a flock, i.e. (figuratively) group (of believers)
Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G4167;
KJV Usage: flock. G4167
Thayer:
1) a flock (esp.) of sheep
2) a group of Christ's disciples
3) bodies of Christian (churches) presided over by elders
