שׁם
shâm
shawm
A primitive particle (rather from the relative H834); there (transfered to time) then; often thither, or thence
KJV Usage: in it, + thence, there (-in, + of, + out), + thither, + whither.

c. from there, thence
d. then (as an adverb of time)
