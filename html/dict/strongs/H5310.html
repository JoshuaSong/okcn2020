נפץ
nâphats
naw-fats'
A primitive root; to dash to pieces, or scatter
KJV Usage: be beaten in sunder, break (in pieces), broken, dash (in pieces), cause to be discharged, dispersed, be overspread, scatter.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נפץ

1. to shatter, break, dash, beat in pieces
a. (Qal)
1. to shatter
2. shattering (infinitive)
b. (Piel) to dash to pieces
c. (Pual) to pulverise
2. to scatter, disperse, overspread, be scattered
a. (Qal)
1. to be scattered
2. dispersed (participle)
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1394
Parts of Speech: Verb
