συλλυπέω
to hurt
συλλυπέω to afflict jointly, i.e. (passive) sorrow at (on account of) someone
Derivation: from G4862 and G3076;
KJV Usage: be grieved. G4862 G3076
Thayer:
1) to affect with grief together
2) give with one's self
