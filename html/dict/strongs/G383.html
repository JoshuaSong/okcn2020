ἀνασείω
to shake back, swing to and fro, move up and down
ἀνασείω figuratively, to excite
Derivation: from G303 and G4579;
KJV Usage: move, stir up. G303 G4579
Thayer:
1) to shake up
2) to stir up, excite, rouse
