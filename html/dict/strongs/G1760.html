ἐνθυμέομαι
to lay to heart, consider well, reflect on, ponder
ἐνθυμέομαι to be inspirited, i.e. ponder
Derivation: from a compound of G1722 and G2372;
KJV Usage: think. G1722 G2372
Thayer:
1) to bring to mind, revolve in mind, ponder
2) to think, to deliberate
