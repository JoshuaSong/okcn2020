Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חותם
Hotham = "seal"
1. a man of Asher, son of Heber, of the family of Beriah
2. father of two of David's mighty warriors
Origin: the same as H2368
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
