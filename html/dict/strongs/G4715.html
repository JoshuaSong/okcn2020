στατήρ
gold stater
στατήρ a stander (standard of value), i.e. (specially), a stater or certain coin
Derivation: from the base of G2746;
KJV Usage: piece of money. G2746
Thayer:
1) a stater, a coin
1a) in the NT a silver stater equal to four Attic or two Alexandrian drachmas, a Jewish shekel
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
