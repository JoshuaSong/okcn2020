ἦχος a loud or confused noise ("echo"), i.e. roar; figuratively, a rumor
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: fame, sound.
Thayer:
1) a sound, noise
1a) spoken of the roar of the sea waves
2) rumour, report
