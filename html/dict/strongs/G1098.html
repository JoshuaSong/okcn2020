γλεῦκος sweet wine, i.e. (properly) must (fresh juice), but used of the more saccharine (and therefore highly inebriating) fermented wine
Derivation: akin to G1099;
KJV Usage: new wine. G1099
Thayer:
1) the sweet juice pressed the grape, sweet wine
