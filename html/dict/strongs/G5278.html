ὑπομένω
to stay behind, survive
ὑπομένω to stay under (behind), i.e. remain; figuratively, to undergo, i.e. bear (trials), have fortitude, persevere
Derivation: from G5259 and G3306;
KJV Usage: abide, endure, (take) patient(-ly), suffer, tarry behind. G5259 G3306
Thayer:
1) to remain
1a) to tarry behind
2) to remain i.e. abide, not recede or flee
2a) to preserve: under misfortunes and trials to hold fast to one's faith in Christ
2b) to endure, bear bravely and calmly: ill treatments
