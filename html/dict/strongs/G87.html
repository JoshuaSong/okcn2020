ἀδιάκριτος
undecided
ἀδιάκριτος properly, undistinguished, i.e. (actively) impartial
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G1252;
KJV Usage: without partiality. G1 G1252
Thayer:
1) undistinguished, unintelligible
2) without dubiousness, ambiguity or uncertainty
