συμπόσιον
a drinking-party, symposium
συμπόσιον a drinking-party ("symposium"), i.e. (by extension) a room of guests
Derivation: neuter of a derivative of the alternate of G4844;
KJV Usage: company. G4844
Thayer:
1) a drinking party, entertainment
1a) of the party itself, the guests
1b) rows of guests
