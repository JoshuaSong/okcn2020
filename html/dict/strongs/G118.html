ἀθλέω
having contended with
ἀθλέω to contend in the competitive games
Derivation: from ἄθλος (a contest in the public lists);
KJV Usage: strive.
Thayer:
1) to engage in a contest, contend in public games, contend for a prize
2) to endure, suffer
