בּכר
bâkar
baw-kar'
A primitive root; properly to burst the womb, that is, (causatively) bear or make early fruit (of woman or tree); also (as denominatively from H1061) to give the birthright
KJV Usage: make firstborn, be firstling, bring forth first child (new fruit).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּכר

1. to be born first
a. (Piel)
1. to bear early, new fruit
2. to give the right of the firstborn 1a
b. to make as firstborn 1a
c. to constitute as firstborn
d. (Pual)
1. to be born a firstling
2. to be made a firstling
e. (Hiphil) one bearing her first child
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 244
Parts of Speech: Verb
