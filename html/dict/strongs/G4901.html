συνεπιμαρτυρέω to testify further jointly, i.e. unite in adding evidence
Derivation: from G4862 and G1957;
KJV Usage: also bear witness. G4862 G1957
Thayer:
1) at attest together with
2) to join in bearing witness, to unite in adding testimony
