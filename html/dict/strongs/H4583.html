מעין מעון o
mâ‛ôn mâ‛ı̂yn
maw-ohn', maw-een'
From the same as H5772; an abode, of God (the Tabernacle or the Temple), men (their home) or animals (their lair); hence a retreat (asylum)
KJV Usage: den, dwelling ([-]) place), habitation.
