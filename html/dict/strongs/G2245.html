ἡλίκος
as big as
ἡλίκος as big as, i.e. (interjectively) how much
Derivation: from ἧλιξ (a comrade, i.e. one of the same age);
KJV Usage: how (what) great.
Thayer:
1) as old as, as tall as
1a) how great
1b) how small
