רוח
râvach
raw-vakh'
A primitive root (rather identical with H7306); properly to breathe freely, that is, revive; by implication to have ample room
KJV Usage: be refreshed, large.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רוח

1. to be wide, be spacious, breathe
a. (Qal) to breathe easily, be relieved
b. (Pual) spacious (participle)
Origin: a primitive root [identical with H7306]
TWOT: 2132
