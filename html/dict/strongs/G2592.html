καρποφορέω
to bear fruit
καρποφορέω to be fertile (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2593;
KJV Usage: be (bear, bring forth) fruit(-ful). G2593
Thayer:
1) to bear fruit
2) to bear, bring forth, deeds
