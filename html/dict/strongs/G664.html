ἀποτόμως abruptly, i.e. peremptorily
Derivation: adverb from a derivative of a comparative of G575 and τέμνω (to cut);
KJV Usage: sharply(-ness). G575
Thayer:
1) abruptly, precipitously
2) sharply, severely, curtly
