כּמו כּמו
ke mô kâmô
kem-o', kaw-mo'
A form of the prefix K, but used separately (compare H3651); as, thus, so
KJV Usage: according to, (such) as (it were, well as), in comparison of, like (as, to, unto), thus, when, worth.
