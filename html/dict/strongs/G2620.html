κατακαυχάομαι
to boast against
κατακαυχάομαι to exult against (i.e. over)
Derivation: from G2596 and G2744;
KJV Usage: boast (against), glory, rejoice against. G2596 G2744
Thayer:
1) to glory against, to exult over, to boast one's self to the injury (of a person or thing)
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Glory (to boast), Glorying
