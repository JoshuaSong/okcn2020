Σωσθένης of safe strength; Sosthenes, a Christian
Derivation: from the base of G4982 and that of G4599;
KJV Usage: Sosthenes. G4982 G4599
Thayer:
1) a Jew at Corinth who was seized and beaten in the presence of Gallio
2) a certain Christian associate of Paul
Literal: Sosthenes = "saviour of his nation"
