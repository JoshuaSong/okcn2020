εὐπροσωπέω
to make a fair show
εὐπροσωπέω to be of good countenance, i.e. (figuratively) to make a display
Derivation: from a compound of G2095 and G4383;
KJV Usage: make a fair show. G2095 G4383
Thayer:
1) to make a fair show
2) to please
