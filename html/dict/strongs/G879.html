ἀφυπνόω
to wake from sleep
ἀφυπνόω properly, to become awake, i.e. (by implication) to drop (off) in slumber
Derivation: from a compound of G575 and G5258;
KJV Usage: fall asleep. G575 G5258
Thayer:
1) to awaken from sleep
2) to fall asleep, to fall off to sleep
