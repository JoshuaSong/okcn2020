Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּמנה
Timnath or Timnah or Thimnathah = "portion"
1. a town on the northern boundary of Judah later assigned to Dan
2. a town in the hill country of Judah
Origin: from H4487
TWOT: None
