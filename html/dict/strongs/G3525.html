νήφω
to drink no wine
νήφω to abstain from wine (keep sober), i.e. (figuratively) be discreet
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: be sober, watch.
Thayer:
1) to be sober, to be calm and collected in spirit
2) to be temperate, dispassionate, circumspect
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Sober, Soberly, Soberminded
  • Watch (Noun and Verb), Watchers, Watchful, Watchings
