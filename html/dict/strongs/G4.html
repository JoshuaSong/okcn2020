ἀβαρής
without weight: not burdensome
ἀβαρής weightless, i.e. (figuratively) not burdensome
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G922;
KJV Usage: from being burdensome. G1 G922
Thayer:
1) not burdensome
2) not heavy
3) light without weight
