Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יעישׁ
Jeush = "assembler"
1. son of Esau by Aholibamah the daughter of Anah, son of Zibeon the Hivite
2. a Benjamite, son of Bilhan
Origin: from H5789
