ἐποπτεύω
to look over, overlook, watch
ἐποπτεύω to inspect, i.e. watch
Derivation: from G1909 and a derivative of G3700;
KJV Usage: behold. G1909 G3700
Thayer:
1) to be an overseer
2) to look upon, view attentively
3) to watch
