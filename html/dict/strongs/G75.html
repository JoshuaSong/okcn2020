ἀγωνίζομαι
to contend for a prize
ἀγωνίζομαι to struggle, literally (to compete for a prize), figuratively (to contend with an adversary), or genitive case (to endeavor to accomplish something)
Derivation: from G73;
KJV Usage: fight, labor fervently, strive. G73
Thayer:
1) to enter a contest: contend in the gymnastic games
2) to contend with adversaries, fight
3) metaph. to contend, struggle, with difficulties and dangers
4) to endeavour with strenuous zeal, strive: to obtain something
