ψευδομαρτυρέω
to be a false witness, bear false witness
ψευδομαρτυρέω to be an untrue testifier, i.e. offer falsehood in evidence
Derivation: from G5575;
KJV Usage: be a false witness. G5575
Thayer:
1) to utter falsehoods in giving testimony, to testify falsely, to bear false witness
