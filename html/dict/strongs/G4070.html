πέρυσι
a year ago, last year
πέρυσι the by-gone, i.e. (as noun) last year
Derivation: adverb from G4009;
KJV Usage: + a year ago. G4009
Thayer:
1) last year
2) the year just past
2a) for a year past, a year ago
