περιέρχομαι
to go round, go about
περιέρχομαι to come all around, i.e. stroll, vacillate, veer
Derivation: from G4012 and G2064 (including its alternate);
KJV Usage: fetch a compass, vagabond, wandering about. G4012 G2064
Thayer:
1) to go about
1a) of strollers
1b) of wanderers
1c) of navigators (making a circuit)
