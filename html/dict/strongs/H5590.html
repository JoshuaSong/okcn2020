סער
sâ‛ar
saw-ar'
A primitive root; to rush upon; by implication to toss (transitively or intransitively, literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: be (toss with) tempest (-uous), be sore troubled, come out as a (drive with the, scatter with a) whirlwind.
