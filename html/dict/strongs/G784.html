ἄσπιλος
without spot, spotless
ἄσπιλος unblemished (physically or morally)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G4695;
KJV Usage: without spot, unspotted. G1 G4695
Thayer:
1) spotless
2) metaph.
2a) free from censure, irreproachable
2b) free from vice, unsullied
