ὑπολείπω
to leave remaining
ὑπολείπω to leave under (behind), i.e. (passively) to remain (survive)
Derivation: from G5295 and G3007;
KJV Usage: be left. G5295 G3007
Thayer:
1) to leave behind
2) to be left behind, left remaining
2a) of a survivor
