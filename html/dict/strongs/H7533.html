רצץ
râtsats
raw-tsats'
A primitive root; to crack in pieces, literally or figuratively
KJV Usage: break, bruise, crush, discourage, oppress, struggle together.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רצץ

1. to crush, oppress
a. (Qal)
1. to crush, get crushed, be crushed
2. to crush, oppress (fig)
3. crushed (participle passive)
b. (Niphal) to be crushed, be broken
c. (Piel)
1. to crush in pieces
2. to grievously oppress (fig)
d. (Poel) to oppress (fig)
e. (Hiphil) to crush
f. (Hithpoel) to crush each other
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2212
Parts of Speech: Verb
