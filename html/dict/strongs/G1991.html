ἐπιστηρίζω
to make to lean on
ἐπιστηρίζω to support further, i.e. reestablish
Derivation: from G1909 and G4741;
KJV Usage: confirm, strengthen. G1909 G4741
Thayer:
1) to establish besides, strengthen more
2) to render more firm, confirm
