משׁפּחה
mishpâchâh
mish-paw-khaw'
From H8192 (compare H8198); a family, that is, circle of relatives; figuratively a class (of persons), a species (of animals) or sort (of things); by extension a tribe or people
KJV Usage: family, kind (-red).
