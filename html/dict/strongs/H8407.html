תּלגּת פּלנאסר תּגלת פּלאסר
Tiglath-pileser or Tilgath-pilneser = "thou will uncover the wonderful bond"
1. an Assyrian king who attacked Samaria or northern kingdom of Israel in the reign of Pekah
