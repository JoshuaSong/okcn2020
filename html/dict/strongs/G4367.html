προστάσσω
to place
προστάσσω to arrange towards, i.e. (figuratively) enjoin
Derivation: from G4314 and G5021;
KJV Usage: bid, command. G4314 G5021
Thayer:
1) to assign or ascribe to, join to
2) to enjoin, order, prescribe, command
2a) to appoint, to define
