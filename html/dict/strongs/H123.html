אדום אדם
'ĕdôm 'ĕdôm
ed-ome', ed-ome'
From H122; red (see Genesis 25:25); Edom, the elder twin-brother of Jacob; hence the region (Idumaea) occuped by him
KJV Usage: Edom, Edomites, Idumea.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אדום אדם
Edom = "red"
1. Edom
2. Edomite, Idumean - descendants of Esau
3. land of Edom, Idumea - land south and south east of Palestine
Origin: from H122
TWOT: 26e
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
