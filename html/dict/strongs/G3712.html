ὀργυιά a stretch of the arms, i.e. a fathom
Derivation: from G3713;
KJV Usage: fathom. G3713
Thayer:
1) fathom, the distance across the breast from the tip of one middle finger to the tip of the other when the arms are outstretched,
5 to 6 feet (2 m)
