γενεαλογέω
to trace by way of pedigree
γενεαλογέω to reckon by generations, i.e. trace in genealogy
Derivation: from G1074 and G3056;
KJV Usage: count by descent. G1074 G3056
Thayer:
1) to recount a family's origin and lineage, trace ancestry
2) to draw one's origin, derive one's pedigree
