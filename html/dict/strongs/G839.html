αὔριον
to-morrow
αὔριον properly, fresh, i.e. (adverb with ellipsis of G2250) to-morrow
Derivation: from a derivative of the same as G109 (meaning a breeze, i.e. the morning air);
KJV Usage: (to-)morrow, next day. G109 G2250
Thayer:
1) tomorrow
