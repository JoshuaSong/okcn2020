עולה עלה
‛ôlâh ‛ôlâh
o-law', o-law'
Feminine active participle of H5927; a step or (collectively stairs, as ascending); usually a holocaust (as going up in smoke)
KJV Usage: ascent, burnt offering (sacrifice), go up to. See also H5766.
