ἐκδαπανάω to expend (wholly), i.e. (figuratively) exhaust
Derivation: from G1537 and G1159;
KJV Usage: spend. G1537 G1159
Thayer:
1) to exhaust by expending, to spend wholly, use up,
2) to spend one's self wholly
