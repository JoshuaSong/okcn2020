ἀντίτυπον corresponding ("antitype"), i.e. a representative, counterpart
Derivation: neuter of a compound of G473 and G5179;
KJV Usage: (like) figure (whereunto). G473 G5179
Thayer:
1) a thing formed after some pattern
2) a thing resembling another, its counterpart
2a) something in the Messianic times which answers to the type, as baptism corresponds to the deluge (
1 Pet 3:21)
