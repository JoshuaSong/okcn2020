ἐξαιρέω
to take out of
ἐξαιρέω actively, to tear out; middle voice, to select; figuratively, to release
Derivation: from G1537 and G138;
KJV Usage: deliver, pluck out, rescue. G1537 G138
Thayer:
1) to pluck out, draw out, i.e. root out
2) to choose out (for one's self), select, one person from many
3) to rescue, deliver
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Deliver, Deliverance, Deliverer
  • Pluck (out)
  • Rescue
