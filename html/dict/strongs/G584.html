ἀποδείκνυμι
to point away from
ἀποδείκνυμι to show off, i.e. exhibit; figuratively, to demonstrate, i.e. accredit
Derivation: from G575 and G1166;
KJV Usage: (ap-)prove, set forth, shew. G575 G1166
Thayer:
1) to point away from one's self, to point out, show forth, to expose to view, exhibit
2) to declare, to show, to prove what kind of person anyone is, to prove by arguments, demonstrate
