κορβᾶν
a gift
κορβᾶν and κορβανᾶς a votive offering and the offering; a consecrated present (to the Temple fund); by extension (the latter term) the Treasury itself, i.e. the room where the contribution boxes stood
Derivation: of Hebrew and Chaldee origin respectively (H7133);
KJV Usage: Corban, treasury. H7133
Thayer:
1) a gift offered (or to be offered) to God
2) the sacred treasury
