אלף
'eleph
eh'-lef
Properly the same as H504; hence (an ox’s head being the first letter of the alphabet, and this eventually used as a numeral) a thousand
KJV Usage: thousand.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אלף

1. a thousand
a. as numeral
2. a thousand, company
a. as a company of men under one leader, troops
Origin: prop, the same as H504
TWOT: 109a
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
