לוּ לא לוּא
lû' lû' lû
loo, loo, loo
A conditional particle; if; by implication (interjectionally as a wish) would that!
KJV Usage: if (haply), peradventure, I pray thee, though, I would, would God (that).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

לוּ לא לוּא

1. if, oh that!, if only!
a. if (unlikely condition)
b. if only!, oh that!, would that!
Origin: a conditional particle
TWOT: 1085
Parts of Speech: Conjunction
