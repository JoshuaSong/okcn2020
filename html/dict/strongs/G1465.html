ἐγκόπτω
to hinder, thwart
ἐγκόπτω to cut into, i.e. (figuratively) impede, detain
Derivation: from G1722 and G2875;
KJV Usage: hinder, be tedious unto. G1722 G2875
Thayer:
1) to cut into, to impede one's course by cutting off his way
2) hinder
