θέλημα
will
θέλημα a determination (properly, the thing), i.e. (actively) choice (specially, purpose, decree; abstractly, volition) or (passively) inclination
Derivation: from the prolonged form of G2309;
KJV Usage: desire, pleasure, will. G2309
Thayer:
1) what one wishes or has determined shall be done
1a) of the purpose of God to bless mankind through Christ
1b) of what God wishes to be done by us
1b1) commands, precepts
2) will, choice, inclination, desire, pleasure
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Desire (Noun and Verb), Desirous
  • Will, Would
