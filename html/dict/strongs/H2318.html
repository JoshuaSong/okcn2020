חדשׁ
châdash
khaw-dash'
A primitive root; to be new; causatively to rebuild
KJV Usage: renew, repair.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חדשׁ

1. to be new, renew, repair
a. (Piel)
1. to renew, make anew
2. to repair
b. (Hithpael) to renew oneself
