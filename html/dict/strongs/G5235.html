ὑπερβάλλω
to throw over
ὑπερβάλλω to throw beyod the usual mark, i.e. (figuratively) to surpass (only active participle supereminent)
Derivation: from G5228 and G906;
KJV Usage: exceeding, excel, pass. G5228 G906
Thayer:
1) to surpass in throwing, to throw over or beyond any thing
2) to transcend, surpass, exceed, excel
3) excelling, exceeding
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Exceed, Exceeding, Exceedingly
  • Excel, Excellency, Excellent
  • Pass, come to Pass
