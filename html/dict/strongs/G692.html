ἀργός
shining, bright, glistening
not working the ground, living without labour
ἀργός inactive, i.e. unemployed; (by implication) lazy, useless
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G2041;
KJV Usage: barren, idle, slow. G1 G2041
Thayer:
1) free from labour, at leisure
2) lazy, shunning the labour which one ought to perform
For Synonyms see entry G5814
