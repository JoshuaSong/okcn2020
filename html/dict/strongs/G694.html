ἀργύριον
a piece of silver, a silver coin
ἀργύριον silvery, i.e. (by implication) cash; specially, a silverling (i.e. drachma or shekel)
Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G696;
KJV Usage: money, (piece of) silver (piece). G696
Thayer:
1) silver
2) money
3) a silver coin, silver piece, a shekel
