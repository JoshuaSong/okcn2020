εὐνοέω
to be well-inclined
εὐνοέω to be well- minded, i.e. reconcile
Derivation: from a compound of G2095 and G3563;
KJV Usage: agree. G2095 G3563
Thayer:
1) to wish (one) well
2) to be well disposed, of a peaceable spirit
