μαρτυρία evidence given (judicially or genitive case)
Derivation: from G3144;
KJV Usage: record, report, testimony, witness. G3144
Thayer:
1) a testifying
1a) the office committed to the prophets of testifying concerning future events
2) what one testifies, testimony, i.e. before a judge
