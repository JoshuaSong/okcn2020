שׁאה
shâ'âh
shaw-aw'
A primitive root; to rush; by implication to desolate
KJV Usage: be desolate, (make a) rush (-ing), (lay) waste.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁאה

1. crash, make a din or crash, crash into ruins, be in ruins, be desolated
a. (Qal) to crash into ruins
b. (Niphal)
1. to be ruined
2. to crash
3. to be left (a desolation)
c. (Hiphil)
1. to cause to crash
2. to cause to be desolated
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2301
Parts of Speech: Verb
