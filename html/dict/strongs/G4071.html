πετεινόν a flying animal, i.e. bird
Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G4072;
KJV Usage: bird, fowl. G4072
Thayer:
1) flying, winged
2) flying or winged animals, birds
2a) the birds of the heaven, i.e. flying in the heaven (air)
