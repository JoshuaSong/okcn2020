נסג
nâsag
naw-sag'
A primitive root; to retreat
KJV Usage: departing away, remove, take (hold), turn away.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נסג

1. to move away, backslide, move, go, turn back
a. (Qal) to backslide, prove recreant to
b. (Hiphil)
1. to displace, move back
2. to remove, carry away
c. (Hophal) to be driven back, be moved back
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1469
Parts of Speech: Verb
