ממזר
mamzêr
mam-zare'
From an unused root mian. to alienate; a mongrel, that is, born of a Jewish father and a heathen mother
KJV Usage: bastard.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

b. mixed population (fig.)
c. born of a Jewish father and a heathen mother or visa versa
