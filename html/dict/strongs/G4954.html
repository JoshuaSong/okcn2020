σύσσωμος
united in one body
σύσσωμος of a joint body, i.e. (figuratively) a fellow-member of the Christian community
Derivation: from G4862 and G4983;
KJV Usage: of the same body. G4862 G4983
Thayer:
1) belonging to the same body
2) metaph. to the same church
