χαλιναγωγέω
to guide with
χαλιναγωγέω to be a bit-leader, i.e. to curb (figuratively)
Derivation: from a compound of G5469 and the reduplicated form of G71;
KJV Usage: bridle. G5469 G71
Thayer:
1) to lead by a bridle, to guide
2) to bridle, hold in check, restrain
