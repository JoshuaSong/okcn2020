Λυδία
Lydia
Λυδία Lydia, a Christian woman
Derivation: properly, feminine of Ludios (of foreign origin) (a Lydian, in Asia Minor);
KJV Usage: Lydia.
Thayer:
1) a woman of Thyatira, a seller of purple, the first European convert of Paul, and afterward his hostess during his first stay at Philippi
Literal: Lydia = "travail"
