ἐπισύστασις
a gathering together against, a riotous meeting
ἐπισύστασις a conspiracy, i.e. concourse (riotous or friendly)
Derivation: from the middle voice of a compound of G1909 and G4921;
KJV Usage: that which cometh upon, + raising up. G1909 G4921
Thayer:
1) a hostile banding together or concourse
1a) to excite to a riotous gathering of the people to make a mob
1b) a troublesome throng of persons seeking help, counsel, comfort
1c) throng to one
