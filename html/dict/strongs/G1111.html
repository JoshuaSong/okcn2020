γογγύζω
to mutter, murmur
γογγύζω to grumble
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: murmur.
Thayer:
1) to murmur, mutter, grumble, say anything against in a low tone
1a) of the cooing of doves
1b) of those who confer secretly together
1c) of those who discontentedly complain
