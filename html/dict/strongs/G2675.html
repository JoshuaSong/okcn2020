καταρτίζω
to adjust
καταρτίζω to complete thoroughly, i.e. repair (literally or figuratively) or adjust
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of G739;
KJV Usage: fit, frame, mend, (make) perfect(-ly join together), prepare, restore. G2596 G739
Thayer:
1) to render, i.e. to fit, sound, complete
1a) to mend (what has been broken or rent), to repair
1a1) to complete
1b) to fit out, equip, put in order, arrange, adjust
1b1) to fit or frame for one's self, prepare
1c) ethically: to strengthen, perfect, complete, make one what he ought to be
