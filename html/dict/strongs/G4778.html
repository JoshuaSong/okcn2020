συγκακουχέω to maltreat in company with, i.e. (passively) endure persecution together
Derivation: from G4862 and G2558;
KJV Usage: suffer affliction with. G4862 G2558
Thayer:
1) to treat ill with another
2) to be ill treated in company with, share persecutions or come into a fellowship of ills
