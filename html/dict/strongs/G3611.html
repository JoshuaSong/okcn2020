οἰκέω
to inhabit, occupy
οἰκέω to occupy a house, i.e. reside (figuratively, inhabit, remain, inhere); by implication, to cohabit
Derivation: from G3624;
KJV Usage: dwell. See also G3625. G3624 G3625
Thayer:
