אפרסתכי אפרסכי
'ăpharse kay 'ăpharsathkay
af-ar-sek-ah'ee, af-ar-sath-kah'ee
(Chaldee); of foreign origin (only in the plural); an Apharsekite or Apharsathkite, an unknown Assyrian tribe
KJV Usage: Apharsachites, Apharsathchites.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אפרסתכי אפרסכי
Apharsachites = "as causers of division (?)" Apharsathchites = "I will divide the deceivers (?)"
1. (CLBL) an unknown office
2. (BDB) perhaps an office or an official
3. (TWOT)
a. a people
b. perhaps a title of an official
Origin: of foreign origin
