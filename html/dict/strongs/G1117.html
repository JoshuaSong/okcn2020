γόμος
a ship's freight, burden, tonnage
γόμος a load (as filling), i.e. (specially) a cargo, or (by extension) wares
Derivation: from G1073;
KJV Usage: burden, merchandise. G1073
Thayer:
1) a lading or freight of a ship, cargo, merchandise conveyed in a ship
2) any merchandise
