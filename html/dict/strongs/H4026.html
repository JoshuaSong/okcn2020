מגדּלה מגדּל
migdâl migdâlâh
mig-dawl', mig-daw-law'
From H1431; a tower (from its size or height); by analogy a rostrum; figuratively a (pyramidal) bed of flowers
KJV Usage: castle, flower, pulpit, tower. Compare the names following.
