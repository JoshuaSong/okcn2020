to drop evil
βλασφημέω to vilify; specially, to speak impiously
Derivation: from G989;
KJV Usage: (speak) blaspheme(-er, -mously, -my), defame, rail on, revile, speak evil. G989
Thayer:
1) to speak reproachfully, rail at, revile, calumniate, blaspheme
2) to be evil spoken of, reviled, railed at
