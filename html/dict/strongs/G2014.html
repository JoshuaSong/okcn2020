ἐπιφαίνω
to shew forth, display, shew off
ἐπιφαίνω to shine upon, i.e. become (literally) visible or (figuratively) known
Derivation: from G1909 and G5316;
KJV Usage: appear, give light. G1909 G5316
Thayer:
1) to show to or upon
1a) to bring to light
2) to appear, become visible
2a) of stars
3) to become clearly known, to show one's self
