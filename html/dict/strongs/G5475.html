χαλκός
copper
χαλκός copper (the substance, or some implement or coin made of it)
Derivation: perhaps from G5465 through the idea of hollowing out as a vessel (this metal being chiefly used for that purpose);
KJV Usage: brass, money. G5465
Thayer:
1) brass
2) what is made of brass, money, coins of brass (also of silver and gold)
