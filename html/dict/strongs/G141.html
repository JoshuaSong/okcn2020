αἱρετικός
able to choose
αἱρετικός a schismatic
Derivation: from the same as G140;
KJV Usage: heretic (the Greek word itself). G140
Thayer:
1) fitted or able to take or choose a thing
2) schismatic, factious, a follower of a false doctrine
3) heretic
