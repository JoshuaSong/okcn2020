משׁח
mâshach
maw-shakh'
A primitive root; to rub with oil, that is, to anoint; by implication to consecrate; also to paint
KJV Usage: anoint, paint.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

משׁח

1. to smear, anoint, spread a liquid
a. (Qal)
1. to smear
2. to anoint (as consecration)
3. to anoint, consecrate
b. (Niphal) to be anointed
Origin: a primitive root
