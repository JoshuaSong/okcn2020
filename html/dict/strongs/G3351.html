μετοικίζω
to lead settlers to another abode
μετοικίζω to transfer as a settler or captive, i.e colonize or exile
Derivation: from the same as G3350;
KJV Usage: carry away, remove into. G3350
Thayer:
1) to transfer settlers
2) to cause to remove into another land
