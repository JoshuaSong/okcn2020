κνήθω
to scratch, tickle
κνήθω to scratch, i.e. (by implication) to tickle
Derivation: from a primary κνάω (to scrape);
KJV Usage: X itching.
Thayer:
1) to scratch, tickle, make to itch
2) to itch
3) desirous of hearing something pleasant
