ἀρτύω
to arrange, devise, prepare
ἀρτύω to prepare, i.e. spice (with stimulating condiments)
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G142;
KJV Usage: season. G142
Thayer:
1) to prepare, arrange, with respect to food
2) to season, make savory
