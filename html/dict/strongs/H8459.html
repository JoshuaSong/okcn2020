Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּחוּ
Tohu = "lowly"
1. Levite, son of Zuph, father of Elihu, and great grandfather of Samuel
Origin: from an unused root meaning to depress
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
