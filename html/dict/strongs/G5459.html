φωσφόρος light-bearing ("phosphorus"), i.e. (specially), the morning-star (figuratively)
Derivation: from G5457 and G5342;
KJV Usage: day star. G5457 G5342
Thayer:
1) light bringing, giving light
2) the planet Venus, the morning star, day star
3) metaph. Christ
