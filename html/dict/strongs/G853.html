ἀφανίζω
to make unseen, hide from sight
ἀφανίζω to render unapparent, i.e. (actively) consume (becloud), or (passively) disappear (be destroyed)
Derivation: from G852;
KJV Usage: corrupt, disfigure, perish, vanish away. G852
Thayer:
1) to snatch out of sight, to put out of view, to make unseen
2) to cause to vanish away, to destroy, consume
3) to deprive of lustre, render unsightly
3a) to disfigure
