תּודה
tôdâh
to-daw'
From H3034; properly an extension of the hand, that is, (by implication) avowal, or (usually) adoration; specifically a choir of worshippers
KJV Usage: confession, (sacrifice of) praise, thanks (-giving, offering).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּודה

1. confession, praise, thanksgiving
a. give praise to God
b. thanksgiving in songs of liturgical worship, hymn of praise
c. thanksgiving choir or procession or line or company
d. thank-offering, sacrifice of thanksgiving
e. confession
Origin: from H3034
