λογομαχέω
to war about words
λογομαχέω to be disputatious (on trifles)
Derivation: from a compound of G3056 and G3164;
KJV Usage: strive about words. G3056 G3164
Thayer:
1) to contend about words
2) to wrangle about empty and trifling matters
