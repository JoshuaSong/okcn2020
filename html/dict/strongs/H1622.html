גּרגּשׁי
girgâshı̂y
ghir-gaw-shee'
Patrial from an unused name (of uncertain derivation); a Girgashite, one of the native tribes of Canaan
KJV Usage: Girgashite, Girgasite.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּרגּשׁי
Girgashite or Girgasite = "dwelling on a clayey soil"
1. descendants of Canaan and one of the nations living east of the sea of Galilee when the Israelites entered the promised land
Origin: patrial from an unused name [of uncertain derivation]
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Adjective
