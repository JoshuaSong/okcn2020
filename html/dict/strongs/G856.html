ἀφεδρών
a privy
ἀφεδρών a place of sitting apart, i.e. a privy
Derivation: from a compound of G575 and the base of G1476;
KJV Usage: draught. G575 G1476
Thayer:
1) a place where the human waste discharges are dumped
1a) a privy, sink, toilet
