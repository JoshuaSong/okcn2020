Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אלזבד
Elzabad = "God has given"
1. a Gadite warrior who aided David
2. a Korhite Levite
Origin: from H410 and H2064
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
