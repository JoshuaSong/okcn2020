פּלה
pâlâh
paw-law'
A primitive root; to distinguish (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: put a difference, show marvellous, separate, set apart, sever, make wonderfully.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּלה

1. to be distinct, marked out, be separated, be distinguished
a. (Niphal)
1. to be distinct, be separated, be distinguished
2. to be wonderful
b. (Hiphil) to make separate, set apart
