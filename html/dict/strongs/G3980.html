πειθαρχέω
to obey one in authority
πειθαρχέω to be persuaded by a ruler, i.e. (genitive case) to submit to authority; by analogy, to conform to advice
Derivation: from a compound of G3982 and G757;
KJV Usage: hearken, obey (magistrates). G3982 G757
Thayer:
1) to obey (a ruler or a superior)
