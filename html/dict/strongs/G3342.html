μεταξύ
betwixt, between
μεταξύ betwixt (of place or person); (of time) as adjective, intervening, or (by implication) adjoining
Derivation: from G3326 and a form of G4862;
KJV Usage: between, mean while, next. G3326 G4862
Thayer:
1) between
1a) meanwhile, in the mean time
2) after, afterwards
