ὕψιστος
highest, loftiest
ὕψιστος highest, i.e. (masculine singular) the Supreme (God), or (neuter plural) the heavens
Derivation: superlative from the base of G5311;
KJV Usage: most high, highest. G5311
Thayer:
1) highest, most high
1a) of place: the highest regions
1b) of rank: the most high God
