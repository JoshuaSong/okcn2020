νέος
young, youthful
νέος "new", i.e. (of persons) youthful, or (of things) fresh; figuratively, regenerate
Derivation: including the comparative νεότερος ; a primary word;
KJV Usage: new, young.
Thayer:
1) recently born, young, youthful
2) new

νέος, νεώτερος
neos neōteros
neh'-os, neh-o'-ter-os
A primary word, including the comparative (second form); “new”, that is, (of persons) youthful, or (of things) fresh; figuratively regenerate
