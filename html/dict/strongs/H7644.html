שׁבנה שׁבנא
shebnâ' shebnâh
sheb-naw', sheb-naw'
From an unused root meaning to grow; growth; Shebna or Shebnah, an Israelite
KJV Usage: Shebna, Shebnah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁבנה שׁבנא
Shebna = "vigour"
1. a person of high position in the court of king Hezekiah of Judah; subsequently the secretary of Hezekiah
Origin: from an unused root meaning to grow
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
