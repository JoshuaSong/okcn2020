נוּס
nûs
noos
A primitive root; to flit, that is, vanish away (subside, escape; causatively chase, impel, deliver)
KJV Usage: X abate, away, be displayed, (make to) flee (away, -ing), put to flight, X hide, lift up a standard.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נוּס

1. to flee, escape
a. (Qal)
1. to flee
2. to escape
3. to take flight,m depart, disappear
4. to fly (to the attack) on horseback
b. (Polel) to drive at
c. (Hithpolel) to take flight
d. (Hiphil)
1. to put to flight
2. to drive hastily
3. to cause to disappear, hide
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1327
Parts of Speech: Verb
