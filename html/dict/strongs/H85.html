אברהם
'abrâhâm
ab-raw-hawm'
Contracted from H1 and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous); father of a multitude; Abraham, the later name of Abram
KJV Usage: Abraham.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אברהם
Abraham = "father of a multitude" or "chief of multitude"
1. friend of God and founder of Hebrew nation via God's elective covenant
Origin: contracted from H1 and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous)
TWOT: 4b
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
