נשׁים אשּׁה
'ishshâh nâshı̂ym
ish-shaw', naw-sheem'
The first form is the feminine of H376 or H582; the second form is an irregular plural; a woman (used in the same wide sense as H582).
KJV Usage: [adulter]ess, each, every, female, X many, + none, one, + together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נשׁים אשּׁה

1. woman, wife, female
a. woman (opposite of man)
b. wife (woman married to a man)
c. female (of animals)
d. each, every (pronoun)
Origin: from H376 or H582
TWOT: 137a
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
