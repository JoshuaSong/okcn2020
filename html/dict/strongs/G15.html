ἀγαθοποιέω
to do good
ἀγαθοποιέω to be a well-doer (as a favor or a duty)
Derivation: from G17;
KJV Usage: (when) do good (well). G17
Thayer:
1) to do good, do something which profits others
1a) to be a good help to someone
1b) to do someone a favour
1c) to benefit
2) to do well, do right
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Good, Goodly, Goodness
  • Well (do), Well-doing
