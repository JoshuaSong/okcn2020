ἀῤῥαβών a pledge, i.e. part of the purchase-money or property given in advance as security for the rest
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H6162);
KJV Usage: earnest. H6162
Thayer:
1) an earnest
1a) money which in purchases is given as a pledge or downpayment that the full amount will subsequently be paid
