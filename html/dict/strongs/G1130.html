γυμνητεύω
to be light-armed
γυμνητεύω to strip, i.e. (reflexively) go poorly clad
Derivation: from a derivative of G1131;
KJV Usage: be naked. G1131
Thayer:
1) to be lightly or poorly clad
2) to be a light armed soldier
