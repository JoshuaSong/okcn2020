στήκω
to stand
στήκω to be stationary, i.e. (figuratively) to persevere
Derivation: from the perfect tense of G2476;
KJV Usage: stand (fast). G2476
Thayer:
1) to stand firm
2) to persevere, to persist
3) to keep one's standing
