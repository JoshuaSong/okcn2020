חרץ חריץ
chârı̂yts chârits
khaw-reets', khaw-reets'
From H2782; properly incisure or (passive) incised (compare H2742); hence a threshing sledge (with sharp teeth); also a slice (as cut)
KJV Usage: + cheese, harrow.
