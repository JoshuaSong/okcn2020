καταντάω to meet against, i.e. arrive at (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of G473;
KJV Usage: attain, come. G2596 G473
Thayer:
1) to come to, arrive
1a) to come to a place over against, opposite another
1b) metaph. to attain to a thing
