συναγωνίζομαι
to contend along with, to share in a contest
συναγωνίζομαι to struggle in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to be a partner (assistant)
Derivation: from G4862 and G75;
KJV Usage: strive together with. G4862 G75
Thayer:
1) to strive together with one, to help one in striving
