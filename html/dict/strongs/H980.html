Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּחוּרים בּחרים
Bahurim = "young men's village"
1. a town in Benjamin between Jerusalem and Jericho beyond the Mount of Olives on the way to Jericho
Origin: masculine plural of H970
