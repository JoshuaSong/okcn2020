כּנּרת כּנּרות
kinne rôth kinnereth
kin-ner-oth', kin-neh'-reth
Respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as H3658; perhaps harp shaped; Kinneroth or Kinnereth, a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּנּרת כּנּרות
Chinneroth or Cinneroth or Chinnereth = "harps"
1. the early name of the Sea of Galilee
2. a town and district in Naphtali near the Sea of Galilee
Origin: respectively pl. and sing. fem. from the same as H3658
TWOT: None
