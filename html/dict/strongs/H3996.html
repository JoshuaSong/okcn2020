מבוא
mâbô'
maw-bo'
From H935; an entrance (the place or the act); specifically (with or without H8121) sunset or the west; also (adverbially with preposition) towards
KJV Usage: by which came, as cometh, in coming, as men enter into, entering, entrance into, entry, where goeth, going down, + westward. Compare H4126.
