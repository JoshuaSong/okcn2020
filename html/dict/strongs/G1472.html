ἐγχρίω
to rub, anoint
ἐγχρίω to rub in (oil), i.e. besmear
Derivation: from G1722 and G5548;
KJV Usage: anoint. G1722 G5548
Thayer:
1) to rub in, besmirch, anoint
2) to anoint one's self
