קרה
qârâh
kaw-raw'
A primitive root; to light upon (chiefly by accident); causatively to bring about; specifically to impose timbers (for roof or floor)
KJV Usage: appoint, lay (make) beams, befall, bring, come (to pass unto), floor, [hap] was, happen (unto), meet, send good speed.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קרה

1. to encounter, meet, befall, happen, come to meet
a. (Qal)
1. to encounter, meet
2. to befall
b. (Niphal)
1. to encounter, meet (without pre-arrangement)
2. to chance to be present
3. to come to meet
c. (Hiphil) to cause to meet, appoint
2. to build with beams
a. (Piel) to lay the beams of, furnish with beams
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2068,2068e
Parts of Speech: Verb
