δανείζω
to put out money at usury, to lend
δανείζω to loan on interest; reflexively, to borrow
Derivation: from G1156;
KJV Usage: borrow, lend. G1156
Thayer:
1) to lend money
2) to have money lent to one's self
3) to take a loan, borrow
For Synonyms see entry G5827
