χαλκολίβανον
fine brass.
χαλκολίβανον burnished copper, an alloy of copper (or gold) and silver having a brilliant lustre
Derivation: neuter of a compound of G5475 and G3030 (in the implied mean of whiteness or brilliancy);
KJV Usage: fine brass. G5475 G3030
Thayer:
1) some metal like gold if not more precious
