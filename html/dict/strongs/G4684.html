σπαταλάω
to live lewdly, to run riot
σπαταλάω to be voluptuous
Derivation: from σπατάλη (luxury);
KJV Usage: live in pleasure, be wanton.
Thayer:
1) to live luxuriously, lead a voluptuous life, (give one's self to pleasure)
