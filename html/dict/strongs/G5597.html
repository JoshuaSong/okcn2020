ψώχω
to rub out
ψώχω to triturate, i.e. (by analogy) to rub out (kernels from husks with the fingers or hand)
Derivation: prolongation from the same base as G5567;
KJV Usage: rub. G5567
Thayer:
1) to rub, to rub to pieces
