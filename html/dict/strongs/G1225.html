διαβάλλω
to throw over
διαβάλλω (figuratively) to traduce
Derivation: from G1223 and G906;
KJV Usage: accuse. G1223 G906
Thayer:
1) to throw over or across, to send over
2) to traduce, calumniate, slander, accuse, defame
