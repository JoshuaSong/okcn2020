ἀγορά
an assembly of the people
ἀγορά probably akin to G1453); properly, the town-square (as a place of public resort); by implication, a market or thoroughfare
Derivation: from ἀγείρω (to gather;
KJV Usage: market(-place), street. G1453
Thayer:
1) any assembly, especially of the people
2) the place of assembly
2a) for public debating,
2b) for elections
2c) for trials
2d) for buying and selling
2e) for all kinds of business
3) market place, street
