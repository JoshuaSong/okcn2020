ἀποφορτίζομαι to unload
Derivation: from G575 and the middle voice of G5412;
KJV Usage: unlade. G575 G5412
Thayer:
1) to lay down a load, unlade, discharge
1a) of sailors lightening ship during a storm to avoid shipwreck
