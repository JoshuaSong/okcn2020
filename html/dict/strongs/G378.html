ἀναπληρόω
to fill up
ἀναπληρόω to complete; by implication, to occupy, supply; figuratively, to accomplish (by coincidence ot obedience)
Derivation: from G303 and G4137;
KJV Usage: fill up, fulfill, occupy, supply. G303 G4137
Thayer:
1) to fill up, make full, e.g. a ditch
2) to supply
