בּעל צפון
ba‛al tse phôn
bah'-al tsef-one'
From H1168 and H6828 (in the sense of cold) (according to others as Egyptian form of Typhon, the destroyer); Baal of winter; Baal Tsephon, a place in Egypt
KJV Usage: Baal-zephon.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּעל צפון
Baal Tsphon or Baal-zephon = "lord of the north"
1. a place in Egypt near the Red Sea where Pharaoh and his army were destroyed during the Exodus
Origin: from H1168 and H6828
TWOT: None
