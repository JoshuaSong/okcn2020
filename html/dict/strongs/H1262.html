בּרה
bârâh
baw-raw'
A primitive root; to select; also (as denominative from H1250) to feed; also (as equivalent to H1305) to render clear (Ecclesiastes 3:18)
KJV Usage: choose, (cause to) eat, manifest, (give) meat.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּרה

1. to eat, consume
a. (Qal) to eat
b. (Piel) for eating, devouring
c. (Hiphil) to cause to eat
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 281
Parts of Speech: Verb
