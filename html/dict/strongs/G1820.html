ἐξαπορέομαι to be utterly at a loss, i.e. despond
Derivation: middle voice from G1537 and G639;
KJV Usage: (in) despair. G1537 G639
Thayer:
1) to be utterly at loss, be utterly destitute of measures or resources, to renounce all hope, be in despair
