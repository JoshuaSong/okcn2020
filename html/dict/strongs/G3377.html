μηνύω
to disclose what is secret, reveal, betray
μηνύω to disclose (through the idea of mental effort and thus calling to mind), i.e. report, declare, intimate
Derivation: probably from the same base as G3145 and G3415 (i.e. μάω, to strive);
KJV Usage: shew, tell. G3145 G3415
Thayer:
1) to disclose or make known something secret
1a) in a forensic sense, to inform, report
2) to declare, tell, make known
3) to indicate, intimate
3a) of a teacher
