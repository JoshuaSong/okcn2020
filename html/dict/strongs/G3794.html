ὀχύρωμα
a stronghold, fortress
ὀχύρωμα a castle (figuratively, argument)
Derivation: from a remote derivative of G2192 (meaning to fortify, through the idea of holding safely);
KJV Usage: stronghold. G2192
Thayer:
1) a castle, stronghold, fortress, fastness
2) anything on which one relies
2a) of the arguments and reasonings by which a disputant endeavours to fortify his opinion and defend it against his opponent
