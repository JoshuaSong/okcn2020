εὐλάβεια
discretion, caution, circumspection
εὐλάβεια properly, caution, i.e. (religiously) reverence (piety); by implication, dread (concretely)
Derivation: from G2126;
KJV Usage: fear(-ed). G2126
Thayer:
1) caution, circumspection, discretion
1a) avoidance
1b) a reasonable shunning
2) reverence, veneration
2a) reverence toward God, godly fear, piety
3) fear anxiety, dread
For Synonyms see entry G5835
