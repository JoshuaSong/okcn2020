σεληνιάζομαι
to be moonstruck
σεληνιάζομαι to be moon-struck, i.e. crazy
Derivation: middle voice or passive from a presumed derivative of G4582;
KJV Usage: be a lunatic. G4582
Thayer:
1) to be moon-struck or lunatic
2) to be epileptic
2a) epilepsy being supposed to return and increase with the increase of the moon. This meaning is doubtful as the Greeks knew nothing of epilepsy.
