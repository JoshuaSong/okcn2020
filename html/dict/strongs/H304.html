Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אחלי
Ahlai = "O! would that!"
1. daughter of Sheshan
2. father of one of David's heroes (mighty men, warriors)
Origin: the same as H305
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
