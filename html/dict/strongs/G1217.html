δημιουργός
one who works for the people, a skilled workman, handicraftsman
δημιουργός a worker for the people, i.e. mechanic (spoken of the Creator)
Derivation: from G1218 and G2041;
KJV Usage: maker. G1218 G2041
Thayer:
1) a workman for the public
2) the author of any work, an artisan, framer, builder
