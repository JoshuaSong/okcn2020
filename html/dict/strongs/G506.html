ἀνυπότακτος
not made subject
ἀνυπότακτος unsubdued, i.e. insubordinate (in fact or temper)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5293;
KJV Usage: disobedient, that is not put under, unruly. G1 G5293
Thayer:
1) not made subject, unsubjected
2) that cannot be subjected to control, disobedient, unruly, refractory
