עקּבה עקב
‛âqêb ‛iqqe bâh
aw-kabe', ik-keb-aw'
From H6117; a heel (as protuberant); hence a track; figuratively the rear (of an army). (lier in wait is by mistake for H6120.)
KJV Usage: heel, [horse-] hoof, last, lier in wait [by mistake for H6120], (foot-) step.
