διαλογισμός
a balancing of accounts
διαλογισμός discussion, i.e. (internal) consideration (by implication, purpose), or (external) debate
Derivation: from G1260;
KJV Usage: dispute, doubtful(-ing), imagination, reasoning, thought. G1260
Thayer:
1) the thinking of a man deliberating with himself
1a) a thought, inward reasoning
1b) purpose, design
2) a deliberating, questioning about what is true
2a) hesitation, doubting
2b) disputing, arguing
