דּשׁן
deshen
deh'-shen
From H1878; the fat; abstractly fatness, that is, (figuratively) abundance; specifically the (fatty) ashes of sacrifices
KJV Usage: ashes, fatness.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

דּשׁן

1. fat ashes, fatness
a. fatness
1. of fertility
2. of blessing (fig.)
b. fat ashes (ashes of victims, mixed with the fat)
Origin: from H1878
