תּפארת תּפארה
tiph'ârâh tiph'ereth
tif-aw-raw', tif-eh'-reth
From H6286; ornament (abstractly or concretely, literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: beauty (-iful), bravery, comely, fair, glory (-ious), honour, majesty.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּפארת תּפארה

1. beauty, splendour, glory
a. beauty, finery (of garments, jewels)
b. glory
1. of rank, renown
2. as attribute of God
c. honour (or nation Israel)
d. glorying, boasting (of individual)
Origin: from H6286
TWOT: 1726b
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
