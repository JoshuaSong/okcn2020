ὀρθός
straight
ὀρθός right (as rising), i.e. (perpendicularly) erect (figuratively, honest), or (horizontally) level or direct
Derivation: probably from the base of G3735;
KJV Usage: straight, upright. G3735
Thayer:
1) straight, erect
1a) upright
1b) straight, not crooked
