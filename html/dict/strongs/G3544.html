νομικός
resting on law, conventional
νομικός according (or pertaining) to law, i.e. legal (ceremonially); as noun, an expert in the (Mosaic) law
Derivation: from G3551;
KJV Usage: about the law, lawyer. G3551
Thayer:
1) pertaining to the law, one learned in the law
2) in the NT an interpreter and teacher of the Mosaic law
