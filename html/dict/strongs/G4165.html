ποιμαίνω
to be shepherd
ποιμαίνω to tend as a shepherd of (figuratively, superviser)
Derivation: from G4166;
KJV Usage: feed (cattle), rule. G4166
Thayer:
1) to feed, to tend a flock, keep sheep
1a) to rule, govern
1a1) of rulers
1a2) to furnish pasture for food
1a3) to nourish
1a4) to cherish one's body, to serve the body
1a5) to supply the requisites for the soul's need
For Synonyms see entry G5824
