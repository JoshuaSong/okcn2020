ἐμμένω
to abide in
ἐμμένω to stay in the same place, i.e. (figuratively) persevere
Derivation: from G1722 and G3306;
KJV Usage: continue. G1722 G3306
Thayer:
1) to remain in, continue
2) to persevere in anything, a state of mind etc.
3) to hold fast, to be true to, abide by, keep
