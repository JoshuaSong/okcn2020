παράπτωμα
a false step, a transgression, trespass
παράπτωμα a side-slip (lapse or deviation), i.e. (unintentional) error or (wilful) transgression
Derivation: from G3895;
KJV Usage: fall, fault, offence, sin, trespass. G3895
Thayer:
1) to fall beside or near something
2) a lapse or deviation from truth and uprightness
2a) a sin, misdeed
