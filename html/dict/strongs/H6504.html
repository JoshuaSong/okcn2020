פּרד
pârad
paw-rad'
A primitive root; to break through, that is, spread or separate (oneself)
KJV Usage: disperse, divide, be out of joint, part, scatter (abroad), separate (self), sever self, stretch, sunder.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

1. to separate, divide
a. (Qal) to divide
b. (Niphal)
1. to divide, separate
2. to be divided, be separated
c. (Piel) to be separated
d. (Pual) to be divided
e. (Hiphil)
1. to divide, separate
2. to make a division, make a separation
f. (Hithpael) to be divided, be separated, get separated
