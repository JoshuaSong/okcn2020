λαλέω
to talk, chat, prattle, babble
λαλέω to talk, i.e. utter words
Derivation: a prolonged form of an otherwise obsolete verb;
KJV Usage: preach, say, speak (after), talk, tell, utter. Compare G3004. G3004
Thayer:
1) to utter a voice or emit a sound
2) to speak
2a) to use the tongue or the faculty of speech
2b) to utter articulate sounds
3) to talk
4) to utter, tell
5) to use words in order to declare one's mind and disclose one's thoughts
5a) to speak
