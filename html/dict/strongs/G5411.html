φόρος
that which is brought in, tribute
φόρος a load (as borne), i.e. (figuratively) a tax (properly, an individual assessment on persons or property; whereas G5056 is usually a general toll on goods or travel)
Derivation: from G5342;
KJV Usage: tribute. G5342 G5056
Thayer:
1) tribute, esp. the annual tax levied upon houses, lands, and persons
