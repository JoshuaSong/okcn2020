Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

צוּר
Zur = "rock"
1. father of Cozbi and one of the 5 Midianite princes who were slain when Balaam fell
2. son of Jehiel, the father or leader of Gibeon
Origin: the same as H6697
TWOT: None
