πωλέω
to exchange
πωλέω to barter (as a pedlar), i.e. to sell
Derivation: probably ultimately from πέλομαι (to be busy, to trade);
KJV Usage: sell, whatever is sold.
Thayer:
1) to barter, to sell
2) sellers
