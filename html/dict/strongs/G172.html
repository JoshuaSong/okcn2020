ἄκακος
unknowing of ill, guileless
ἄκακος not bad, i.e. (objectively) innocent or (subjectively) unsuspecting
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G2556;
KJV Usage: harmless, simple. G1 G2556
Thayer:
1) without guile or fraud, harmless, free from guilt
2) fearing no evil from others, distrusting no one
