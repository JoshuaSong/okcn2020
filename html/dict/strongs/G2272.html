ἡσύχιος
still, quiet, at rest, at ease
ἡσύχιος properly, keeping one's seat (sedentary), i.e. (by implication) still (undisturbed, undisturbing)
Derivation: a prolonged form of a compound probably of a derivative of the base of G1476 and perhaps G2192;
KJV Usage: peaceable, quiet. G1476 G2192
Thayer:
1) quiet, tranquil
