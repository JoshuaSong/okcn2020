παραλέγομαι (specially), to lay one's course near, i.e. sail past
Derivation: from G3844 and the middle voice of G3004 (in its original sense);
KJV Usage: pass, sail by. G3844 G3004
Thayer:
1) to sail past, coast along
2) work past, weather
