συζάω
to live with
συζάω to continue to live in common with, i.e. co-survive (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and G2198;
KJV Usage: live with. G4862 G2198
Thayer:
1) to live together with one
1a) of physical life on earth
1b) to live a new life in union with Christ, i.e. dedicated to God
