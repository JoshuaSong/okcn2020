ἀνάκρισις
examination of parties concerned in a suit, a preparation
ἀνάκρισις a (judicial) investigation
Derivation: from G350;
KJV Usage: examination. G350
Thayer:
1) an examination
2) as a law term among the Greeks, the preliminary investigation held for the purposes of gathering evidences for the information of the judges
