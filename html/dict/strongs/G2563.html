κάλαμος
a reed
κάλαμος a reed (the plant or its stem, or that of a similar plant); by implication, a pen
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: pen, reed.
Thayer:
1) a reed
2) a staff made of a reed, a reed staff
3) a measuring reed or rod
4) a writer's reed, a pen
