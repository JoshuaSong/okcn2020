συστέλλω
to draw together, draw in: to shorten sail
συστέλλω to send (draw) together, i.e. enwrap (enshroud a corpse for burial), contract (an interval)
Derivation: from G4862 and G4724;
KJV Usage: short, wind up. G4862 G4724
Thayer:
1) to place together
1a) to draw together, contact
1a1) to diminish
1a2) to shorten, abridge
1a3) the time has been drawn together into a brief compass, is shortened
1b) to roll together, wrap up, wrap around with bandages, etc., to enshroud
