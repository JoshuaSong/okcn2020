διερμηνεύω
to interpret, expound
διερμηνεύω to explain thoroughly, by implication, to translate
Derivation: from G1223 and G2059;
KJV Usage: expound, interpret(-ation). G1223 G2059
Thayer:
1) to unfold the meaning of what is said, explain, expound
2) to translate into one's native language
