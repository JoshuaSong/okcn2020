βρέφος
the babe in the womb
βρέφος an infant (properly, unborn) literally or figuratively
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: babe, (young) child, infant.
Thayer:
1) an unborn child, embryo, a foetus
2) a new-born child, an infant, a babe
