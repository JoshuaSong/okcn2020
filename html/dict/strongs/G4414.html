πρωτοστάτης
one who stands first, on the right, the right-hand man
πρωτοστάτης one standing first in the ranks, i.e. a captain (champion)
Derivation: from G4413 and G2476;
KJV Usage: ringleader. G4413 G2476
Thayer:
1) one who stands in the front rank
1a) a leader, chief, champion
