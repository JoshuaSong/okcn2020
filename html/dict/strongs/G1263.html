διαμαρτύρομαι
to protest solemnly
διαμαρτύρομαι to attest or protest earnestly, or (by implication) hortatively
Derivation: from G1223 and G3140;
KJV Usage: charge, testify (unto), witness. G1223 G3140
Thayer:
1) to testify
1a) earnestly, religiously to charge
2) to attest, testify to, solemnly affirm
2a) to give solemn testimony to one
2b) to confirm a thing by testimony, to testify, cause it to be believed
