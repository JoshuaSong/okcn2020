מסּכה
massêkâh
mas-say-kaw'
From H5258; properly a pouring over, that is, fusion of metal (especially a cast image); by implication a libation, that is, league; concretely a coverlet (as if poured out)
KJV Usage: covering, molten (image), vail.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מסּכה

1. a pouring, libation, molten metal, cast image, drink offering
a. libation (with covenant sacrifice)
b. molten metal, molten image, molten gods
2. web, covering, veil, woven stuff
Origin: from H5258
TWOT: 1375c,1376a
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
