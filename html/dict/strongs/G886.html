ἀχειροποίητος
not wrought by hands
ἀχειροποίητος unmanufactured, i.e. inartificial
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G5499;
KJV Usage: made without (not made with) hands. G1 G5499
Thayer:
1) not made with hands
