βίος
life
βίος life, i.e. (literally) the present state of existence; by implication, the means of livelihood
Derivation: a primary word;
KJV Usage: good, life, living.
Thayer:
1) life
1a) life extensively
1a1) the period or course of life
1b) that by which life is sustained, resources, wealth, goods
For Synonyms see entry G5821
