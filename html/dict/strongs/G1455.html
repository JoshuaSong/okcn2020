ἐγκάθετος subinduced, i.e. surreptitiously suborned as a lier-in-wait
Derivation: from G1722 and a derivative of G2524;
KJV Usage: spy. G1722 G2524
Thayer:
1) secretly to lie in wait, a spy
2) one who is bribed by others to entrap a man by crafty words
