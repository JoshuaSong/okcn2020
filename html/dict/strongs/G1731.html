ἐνδείκνυμι
to mark, point out
ἐνδείκνυμι to indicate (by word or act)
Derivation: from G1722 and G1166;
KJV Usage: do, show (forth). G1722 G1166
Thayer:
1) to point out
1a) to show, demonstrate, prove, whether by arguments or by acts
2) to manifest, display, put forth
