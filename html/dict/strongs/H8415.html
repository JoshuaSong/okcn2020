תּהם תּהום
te hôm te hôm
teh-home', teh-home'
(Usually feminine) from H1949; an abyss (as a surging mass of water), especially the deep (the main sea or the subterranean water supply)
KJV Usage: deep (place), depth.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּהם תּהום

1. deep, depths, deep places, abyss, the deep, sea
a. deep (of subterranean waters)
b. deep, sea, abysses (of sea)
c. primeval ocean, deep
d. deep, depth (of river)
e. abyss, the grave
Origin: from H1949
TWOT: 2495a
Parts of Speech: Noun
