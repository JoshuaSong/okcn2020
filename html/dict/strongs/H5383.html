נשׁה
nâshâh
naw-shaw'
A primitive root (rather identical with H5382, in the sense of H5378); to lend or (by reciprocity) borrow on security or interest
KJV Usage: creditor, exact, extortioner, lend, usurer, lend on (taker of) usury.
