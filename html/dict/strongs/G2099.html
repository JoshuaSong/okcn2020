εὐαγγελιστής
the bringer of good tidings, an evangelist, preacher of the gospel
εὐαγγελιστής a preacher of the gospel
Derivation: from G2097;
KJV Usage: evangelist. G2097
Thayer:
1) a bringer of good tidings, an evangelist
2) the name given to the NT heralds of salvation through Christ who are not apostles
