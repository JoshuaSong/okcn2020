ἀντιτάσσομαι to range oneself against, i.e. oppose
Derivation: from G473 and the middle voice of G5021;
KJV Usage: oppose themselves, resist. G473 G5021
Thayer:
1) to range in battle against
2) to oppose one's self, resist
