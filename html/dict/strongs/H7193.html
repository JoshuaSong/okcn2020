קשׂקשׂת
qaśqeśeth
kas-keh'-seth
By reduplication from an unused root meaning to shale off as bark; a scale (of a fish); hence a coat of mail (as composed of or covered with jointed plates of metal)
KJV Usage: mail, scale.
