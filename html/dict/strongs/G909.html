βαπτισμός
a dipping in water, ablution
βαπτισμός ablution (ceremonial or Christian)
Derivation: from G907;
KJV Usage: baptism, washing. G907
Thayer:
1) a washing, purification effected by means of water
1a) of washing prescribed by the Mosaic law (Heb
9:10) which seems to mean an exposition of the difference between the washings prescribed by the Mosaic law and Christian baptism
