διασαφέω
to make quite clear, shew plainly
διασαφέω to clear thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) declare
Derivation: from G1223 and σαφής (clear);
KJV Usage: tell unto. G1223
Thayer:
1) to make clear or plain, to explain, unfold, declare
2) of things done, to declare i.e. to tell, announce, narrate
