חרסה חרס
cheres charsâh
kheh'-res, khar'-saw
From an unused root meaning to scrape; the itch; also (perhaps from the mediating idea of H2777) the sun
KJV Usage: itch, sun.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חרסה חרס

1. itch, an eruptive disease
2. sun

TWOT: 759b,748a
