ἀνεψιός
a first-cousin, cousin
ἀνεψιός properly, akin, i.e. (specially) a cousin
Derivation: from G1 (as a particle of union) and an obsolete νέπος (a brood);
KJV Usage: sister's son. G1
Thayer:
1) a cousin
