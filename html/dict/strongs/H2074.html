זבוּלן זבלוּן זבוּלוּן
Zebulun = "exalted"
1. the 10th of the sons of Jacob, 6th and last of Leah; progenitor of Zebulun
2. the tribe descended from Zebulun n pr loc
3. the land allocated to the tribe of Zebulun
