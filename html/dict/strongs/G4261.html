to throw before, throw
προβάλλω to throw forward, i.e. push to the front, germinate
Derivation: from G4253 and G906;
KJV Usage: put forward, shoot forth. G4253 G906
Thayer:
1) to throw forward
1a) of trees, to shoot forth, put out leaves
1b) to germinate
1c) to push forward, thrust forward, put forward
