Ἰακώβ Jacob (i.e. Ja`akob), the progenitor of the Israelites
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H3290);
KJV Usage: also an Israelite:--Jacob. H3290
Thayer:
1) was the second son of Isaac
2) the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary
Literal: Jacob = "heel-catcher or supplanter"
