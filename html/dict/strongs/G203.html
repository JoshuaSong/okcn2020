ἀκροβυστία
the foreskin
ἀκροβυστία the prepuce; by implication, an uncircumcised (i.e. gentile, figuratively, unregenerate) state or person
Derivation: from G206 and probably a modified form of πόσθη (the penis or male sexual organ);
KJV Usage: not circumcised, uncircumcised (with G2192), uncircumcision. G206 G2192
Thayer:
1) having the foreskin, uncircumcised
2) a Gentile
3) a condition in which the corrupt desires rooted in the flesh were not yet extinct
