εὐχάριστος
winning
εὐχάριστος well favored, i.e. (by implication) grateful
Derivation: from G2095 and a derivative of G5483;
KJV Usage: thankful. G2095 G5483
Thayer:
1) mindful of favours, grateful, thankful
2) pleasing, agreeable
3) acceptable to others, winning, liberal, beneficent
