בּרא
bârâ'
baw-raw'
A primitive root; (absolutely) to create; (qualified) to cut down (a wood), select, feed (as formative processes)
KJV Usage: choose, create (creator), cut down, dispatch, do, make (fat).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּרא

1. to create, shape, form
a. (Qal) to shape, fashion, create (always with God as subject)
1. of heaven and earth
2. of individual man
3. of new conditions and circumstances
4. of transformations
b. (Niphal) to be created
1. of heaven and earth
2. of birth
3. of something new
4. of miracles
c. (Piel)
1. to cut down
2. to cut out
2. to be fat
a. (Hiphil) to make yourselves fat
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 278
Parts of Speech: Verb
