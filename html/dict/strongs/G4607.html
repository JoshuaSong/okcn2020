σικάριος a dagger-man or assassin; a freebooter (Jewish fanatic outlawed by the Romans)
Derivation: of Latin origin;
KJV Usage: murderer. Compare G5406. G5406
Thayer:
1) an assassin
1a) one who carries a dagger or short sword under his clothing, that he may kill secretly and treacherously any one he wishes to
1b) a cutthroat
For Synonyms see entry G5811
