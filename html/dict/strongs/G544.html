ἀπειθέω
to be disobedient, refuse compliance
ἀπειθέω to disbelieve (wilfully and perversely)
Derivation: from G545;
KJV Usage: not believe, disobedient, obey not, unbelieving. G545
Thayer:
1) not to allow one's self to be persuaded
1a) to refuse or withhold belief
1b) to refuse belief and obedience
2) not to comply with
