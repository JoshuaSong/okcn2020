φρίσσω
to be rough
φρίσσω to "bristle" or chill, i.e. shudder (fear)
Derivation: apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: tremble.
Thayer:
1) to bristle, stiffen stand up
2) to shudder, to be struck with extreme fear, to be horrified
