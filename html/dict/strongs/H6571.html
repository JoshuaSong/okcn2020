פּרשׁ
pârâsh
paw-rawsh'
From H6567; a steed (as stretched out to a vehicle, not single nor for mounting (compare H5483)); also (by implication) a driver (in a chariot), that is, (collectively) cavalry
KJV Usage: horseman.
