ἐγκρίνω
to reckon in
ἐγκρίνω to judge in, i.e. count among
Derivation: from G1722 and G2919;
KJV Usage: make of the number. G1722 G2919
Thayer:
1) to reckon among, judge among
2) to judge one worthy of being admitted to a certain class
