רבקה
ribqâh
rib-kaw'
From an unused root probably meaning to clog by tying up the fetlock; fettering (by beauty); Ribkah, the wife of Isaac
KJV Usage: Rebekah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רבקה
Rebekah = "ensnarer"
1. daughter of Bethuel, sister of Laban, wife of Isaac, and mother of Esau and Jacob
Origin: from an unused root probably meaning to clog by tying up the fetlock
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
