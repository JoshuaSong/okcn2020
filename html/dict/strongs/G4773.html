συγγενής
born with, congenital, natural, in-born
συγγενής a relative (by blood); by extension, a fellow countryman
Derivation: from G4862 and G1085;
KJV Usage: cousin, kin(-sfolk, -sman). G4862 G1085
Thayer:
1) of the same kin, akin to, related by blood
2) in a wider sense, of the same nation, a fellow countryman
