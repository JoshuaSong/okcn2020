בּלה
bâlâh
baw-law'
A primitive root; to fail; by implication to wear out, decay (causatively consume, spend)
KJV Usage: consume, enjoy long, become (make, wax) old, spend, waste.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּלה

1. to wear out, become old
a. (Qal) to wear out
b. (Piel)
1. to wear out
2. to wear out by use, use up completely
3. to enjoy, use to the full
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 246
Parts of Speech: Verb
