פּכרת צביים
pôkereth tse bâyı̂ym
po-keh'-reth tseb-aw-yeem'
From the active participle (of the same form as the first word) feminine of an unused root (meaning to entrap) and plural of H6643; trap of gazelles; Pokereth Tsebajim, one of the servants of Solomon
KJV Usage: Pochereth of Zebaim.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּכרת צביים
Pochereth of Zebaim = "here the cutting off"
1. a servant of Solomon whose descendants returned from exile with Zerubbabel
Origin: from the act. part. (of the same form as the first word) fem. of an unused root (meaning to entrap) and pl. of H6643
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
