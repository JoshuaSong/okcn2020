חמס
châmas
khaw-mas'
A primitive root; to be violent; by implication to maltreat
KJV Usage: make bare, shake off, violate, do violence, take away violently, wrong, imagine wrongfully.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חמס

1. to wrong, do violence to, treat violently, do wrongly
a. (Qal) to treat violently, do wrong
1. of physical wrong
2. of ethical wrong
3. of physical and ethical wrong
b. (Niphal) to be treated violently
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: TWOT- 678
