ὀρθοποδέω
to walk uprightly
ὀρθοποδέω to be straight-footed, i.e. (figuratively) to go directly forward
Derivation: from a compound of G3717 and G4228;
KJV Usage: walk uprightly. G3717 G4228
Thayer:
1) to walk in a straight course
2) metaph. to act uprightly
