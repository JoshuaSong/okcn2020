μαθητεύω
to be pupil
μαθητεύω intransitively, to become a pupil; transitively, to disciple, i.e. enrol as scholar
Derivation: from G3101;
KJV Usage: be disciple, instruct, teach. G3101
Thayer:
1) to be a disciple of one
1a) to follow his precepts and instructions
2) to make a disciple
2a) to teach, instruct
