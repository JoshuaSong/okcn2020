νήπιος
not yet speaking
νήπιος not speaking, i.e. an infant (minor); figuratively, a simple-minded person, an immature Christian
Derivation: from an obsolete particle νή- (implying negation) and G2031;
KJV Usage: babe, child (+ -ish). G2031
Thayer:
1) an infant, little child
2) a minor, not of age
3) metaph. childish, untaught, unskilled
