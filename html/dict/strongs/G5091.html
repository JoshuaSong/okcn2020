τιμάω
to pay honour to, hold in honour, to honour, revere, reverence
τιμάω to prize, i.e. fix a valuation upon; by implication, to revere
Derivation: from G5093;
KJV Usage: honour, value. G5093
Thayer:
1) to estimate, fix the value
1a) for the value of something belonging to one's self
2) to honour, to have in honour, to revere, venerate
