διαλλάσσω
to change one with another, interchange
διαλλάσσω to change thoroughly, i.e. (mentally) to conciliate
Derivation: from G1223 and G236;
KJV Usage: reconcile. G1223 G236
Thayer:
1) to change
2) to change the mind of anyone, to reconcile
3) to be reconciled, to renew friendship with one
