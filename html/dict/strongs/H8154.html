שׁשׂה שׁסה
shâsâh shâśâh
shaw-saw', shaw-saw'
(The second form being used in Isaiah 10:13); a primitive root; to plunder
KJV Usage: destroyer, rob, spoil (-er).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
