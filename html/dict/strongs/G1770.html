ἐννεύω
to make signs to, to ask by signs
ἐννεύω to nod at, i.e. beckon or communicate by gesture
Derivation: from G1722 and G3506;
KJV Usage: make signs. G1722 G3506
Thayer:
1) to nod to, signify or express by a nod or a sign
