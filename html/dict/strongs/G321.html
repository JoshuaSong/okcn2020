ἀνάγω
to lead up
ἀνάγω to lead up; by extension to bring out; specially, to sail away
Derivation: from G303 and G71;
KJV Usage: bring (again, forth, up again), depart, launch (forth), lead (up), loose, offer, sail, set forth, take up. G303 G71
Thayer:
1) to lead up, to lead or bring into a higher place
2) of navigators: launch out, set sail, put to sea
