οἰκτείρω
to pity, feel pity for, have pity upon
οἰκτείρω also (in certain tenses) prolonged οἰκτερέω to exercise pity
Derivation: from οἶκτος (pity);
KJV Usage: have compassion on.
Thayer:
1) to pity, have compassion on
