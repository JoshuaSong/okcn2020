ארם
'ărâm
arawm'
From the same as H759; the highland; Aram or Syria, and its inhabitants; also the name of a son of Shem, a grandson of Nahor, and of an Israelite
KJV Usage: Aram, Mesopotamia, Syria, Syrians.
