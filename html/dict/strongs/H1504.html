גּזר
gâzar
gaw-zar'
A primitive root; to cut down or off; (figuratively) to destroy, divide, exclude or decide
KJV Usage: cut down (off), decree, divide, snatch.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּזר

1. to cut, divide, cut down, cut off, cut in two, snatch, decree
a. (Qal)
1. to cut in two, divide
2. to cut down
3. to cut off, destroy, exterminate
4. to decree
b. (Niphal)
1. to be cut off, separated, excluded
2. to be destroyed, cut off
3. to be decreed
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 340
Parts of Speech: Verb
