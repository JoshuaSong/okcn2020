φρόνιμος
in one's right mind, in one's senses
φρόνιμος thoughtful, i.e. sagacious or discreet (implying a cautious character; while G4680 denotes practical skill or acumen; and G4908 indicates rather intelligence or mental acquirement); in a bad sense conceited (also in the comparative)
Derivation: from G5424;
KJV Usage: wise(-r). G5424 G4680 G4908
Thayer:
1) intelligent, wise
2) prudent, i.e. mindful of one's interests
For Synonyms see entry G5872
