Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּמר
Gomer = "complete" n pr m
1. the eldest son of Japheth and grandson of Noah; the progenitor of the early Cimmerians and other branches of the Celtic family n pr f
2. the unfaithful wife of the prophet Hosea; Hosea's relationship with her was symbolic of God's relationship with wayward Israel
Origin: from H1584
TWOT: 363a
