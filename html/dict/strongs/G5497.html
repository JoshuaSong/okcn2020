χειραγωγός
one that leads by the hand, a leader, guide
χειραγωγός a hand-leader, i.e. personal conductor (of a blind person)
Derivation: from G5495 and a reduplicated form of G71;
KJV Usage: some to lead by the hand. G5495 G71
Thayer:
1) leading one by the hand
