μεσουράνημα
mid-heaven, mid-air
μεσουράνημα mid-sky
Derivation: from a presumed compound of G3319 and G3772;
KJV Usage: midst of heaven. G3319 G3772
Thayer:
1) mid-heaven
2) the highest point in the heavens, which the sun occupies at noon, where what is done can be seen and heard by all
