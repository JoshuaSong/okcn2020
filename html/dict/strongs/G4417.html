πταίω
to make to stumble
πταίω to trip, i.e. (figuratively) to err, sin, fail (of salvation)
Derivation: a form of G4098;
KJV Usage: fall, offend, stumble. G4098
Thayer:
1) to cause one to stumble or fall
2) to stumble
2a) to err, make a mistake, to sin
2b) to fall into misery, become wretched
