ἐλάχιστος
the smallest, least
ἐλάχιστος used as equivalent to G3398; least (in size, amount, dignity, etc.)
Derivation: superlative of ἔλαχυς (short);
KJV Usage: least, very little (small), smallest. G3398
Thayer:
1) smallest least
1a) in size
1b) in amount: of management of affairs
1c) in importance: what is the least moment
1d) in authority: of commandments
1e) in the estimation of men: of persons
1f) in rank and excellence: of persons
