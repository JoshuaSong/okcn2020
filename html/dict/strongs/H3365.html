יקר
yâqar
yaw-kar'
A primitive root; properly apparently to be heavy, that is, (figuratively) valuable; causatively to make rare (figuratively to inhibit)
KJV Usage: be (make) precious, be prized, be set by, withdraw.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יקר

1. to esteem, be prized, be valuable, be precious, be costly, be appraised
a. (Qal)
1. to be precious, be highly valued, be esteemed, be costly
2. to be appraised
b. (Hiphil) to make something precious
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 905
Parts of Speech: Verb
