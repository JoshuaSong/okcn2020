ἀστοχέω
to miss the mark, to miss, fail
ἀστοχέω to miss the mark, i.e. (figuratively) deviate from truth
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a negative particle) and στοίχος (an aim);
KJV Usage: err, swerve. G1
Thayer:
1) to deviate from, miss (the mark)
