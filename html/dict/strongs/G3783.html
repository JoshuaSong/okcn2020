ὀφείλημα
that which is owed, a debt
ὀφείλημα something owed, i.e. (figuratively) a due; morally, a fault
Derivation: from (the alternate of) G3784;
KJV Usage: debt. G3784
Thayer:
1) that which is owed
1a) that which is justly or legally due, a debt
2) metaph. offence, sin
