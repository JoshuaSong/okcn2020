χάραξ
a pointed stake
χάραξ akin to G1125 through the idea of scratching); a stake, i.e. (by implication) a palisade or rampart (military mound for circumvallation in a siege)
Derivation: from χαράσσω (to sharpen to a point;
KJV Usage: trench. G1125
Thayer:
1) a pale or stake, a palisade
2) a palisade or rampart
2a) pales between which earth, stones, trees, and timbers are heaped and packed together
