ἱκανός
becoming, befitting, sufficing
ἱκανός competent (as if coming in season), i.e. ample (in amount) or fit (in character)
Derivation: from ἵκω (ἱκάνω or ἱκνέομαι, akin to G2240) (to arrive);
KJV Usage: able, + content, enough, good, great, large, long (while), many, meet, much, security, sore, sufficient, worthy. G2240
Thayer:
1) sufficient
1a) many enough, enough
1b) sufficient in ability, i.e. meet, fit
