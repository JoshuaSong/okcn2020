προχειρίζομαι to handle for oneself in advance, i.e. (figuratively) to purpose
Derivation: middle voice from G4253 and a derivative of G5495;
KJV Usage: choose, make. G4253 G5495
Thayer:
1) to put into the hand, to deliver into the hands
2) to take into one's hands
3) to set before one's self, to prostrate, to determine
4) to choose, to appoint
4a) for one's use
4b) for one's salvation
