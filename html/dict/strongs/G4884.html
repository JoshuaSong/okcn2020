συναρπάζω
to seize and carry clean away
συναρπάζω to snatch together, i.e. seize
Derivation: from G4862 and G726;
KJV Usage: catch. G4862 G726
Thayer:
1) to seize by force
2) to catch or lay hold of (one so that he is no longer his own master)
3) to seize by force and carry away
