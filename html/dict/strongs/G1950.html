ἐπιλανθάνομαι
to forget
ἐπιλανθάνομαι to lose out of mind; by implication, to neglect
Derivation: middle voice from G1909 and G2990;
KJV Usage: (be) forget(-ful of). G1909 G2990
Thayer:
1) to forget
2) neglecting, no longer caring for
2) forgotten, given over to oblivion, i.e. uncared for
