θήρα
a hunting of wild beasts, the chase
θήρα hunting, i.e. (figuratively) destruction
Derivation: from θήρ (a wild animal, as game);
KJV Usage: trap.
Thayer:
1) a hunting of wild beasts to destroy them
2) metaph. of preparing destruction for men
