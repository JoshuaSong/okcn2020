Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּרא
Gera = "a grain"
1. a son of Benjamin
2. a son of Bela and grandson of Benjamin
3. any member of the Gera family of the Benjamite tribe
Origin: perhaps from H1626
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
