παράκειμαι
to lie beside
παράκειμαι to lie near, i.e. be at hand (figuratively, be prompt or easy)
Derivation: from G3844 and G2749;
KJV Usage: be present. G3844 G2749
Thayer:
1) to lie beside, to be near
2) to be present, at hand
