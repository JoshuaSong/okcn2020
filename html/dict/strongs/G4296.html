προκηρύσσω
to proclaim by herald, proclaim publicly
προκηρύσσω to herald (i.e. proclaim) in advance
Derivation: from G4253 and G2784;
KJV Usage: before (first) preach. G4253 G2784
Thayer:
1) to announce or proclaim be herald beforehand
2) to announce beforehand (of the herald himself)
