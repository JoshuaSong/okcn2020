ὑποδείκνυμι
to shew secretly
ὑποδείκνυμι to exhibit under the eyes, i.e. (figuratively) to exemplify (instruct, admonish)
Derivation: from G5259 and G1166;
KJV Usage: show, (fore-)warn. G5259 G1166
Thayer:
1) to show by placing under (i.e. before) the eyes
2) to show by words and arguments, i.e. to teach
3) to show by make known future things
