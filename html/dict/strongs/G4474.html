ῥαπίζω
to strike with a stick, to cudgel, flog, bastinado
ῥαπίζω to slap
Derivation: from a derivative of a primary ῥέπω (to let fall, "rap");
KJV Usage: smite (with the palm of the hand). Compare G5180. G5180
Thayer:
1) to smite with a rod or staff
2) to smite in the face with the palm of the hand, to box the ear
