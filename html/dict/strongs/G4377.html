προσφωνέω
to call
προσφωνέω to sound towards, i.e. address, exclaim, summon
Derivation: from G4314 and G5455;
KJV Usage: call unto, speak (un-)to. G4314 G5455
Thayer:
1) to call to, to address by calling
2) to call to one's self, summon
