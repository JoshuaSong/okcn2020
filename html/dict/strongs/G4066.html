περίχωρος
round about a place
περίχωρος around the region, i.e. circumjacent (as noun, with G1093 implied vicinity)
Derivation: from G4012 and G5561;
KJV Usage: country (round) about, region (that lieth) round about. G4012 G5561 G1093
Thayer:
1) lying round about, neighbouring
1a) the region round about
1b) the region of Jordan
