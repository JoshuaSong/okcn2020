דּבר דּביר
de bı̂yr debir
deb-eer', deb-eer'
From H1696 (apparently in the sense of oracle); the shrine or innermost part of the sanctuary
KJV Usage: oracle.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

דּבר דּביר

1. the holy of holies, the innermost room of the temple or tabernacle
a. hindmost chamber, innermost room of the temple of Solomon, most holy place, holy of holies
2. (TWOT) oracle
Origin: from H1696 (apparently in the sense of oracle)
TWOT: 399g
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
