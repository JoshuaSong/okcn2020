פּגּל פּגּוּל
piggûl piggûl
pig-gool', pig-gool'
From an unused root meaning to stink; properly fetid, that is, (figuratively) unclean (ceremonially)
KJV Usage: abominable (-tion, thing).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
