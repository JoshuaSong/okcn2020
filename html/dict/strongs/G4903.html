συνεργέω
to work together with, help in work, cooperate
συνεργέω to be a fellow-worker, i.e. co-operate
Derivation: from G4904;
KJV Usage: help (work) with, work(-er) together. G4904
Thayer:
1) to work together, help in work, be partner in labour
2) to put forth power together with and thereby to assist
