רדנים דּדנים
dôdânı̂ym rôdânı̂ym
do-daw-neem', ro-daw-neem'
The second form is used by orthographical error in 1 Chronicles 1:7. A plural of uncertain derivation; Dodanites, or descendants of a son of Javan
KJV Usage: Dodanim.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רדנים דּדנים
Dodanim or Rodanim = "leaders"
1. sons or descendants of Javan
Origin: a plural of uncertain derivation
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
