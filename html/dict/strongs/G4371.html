προσφάγιον
anything eaten with other food
προσφάγιον something eaten in addition to bread, i.e. a relish (specially, fish; compare G3795)
Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of a compound of G4314 and G5315;
KJV Usage: meat. G4314 G5315 G3795
Thayer:
1) anything eaten with bread
1a) spoken of fish boiled or broiled
