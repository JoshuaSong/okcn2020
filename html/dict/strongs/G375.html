ἀναπέμπω
to send up
ἀναπέμπω to send up or back
Derivation: from G303 and G3992;
KJV Usage: send (again). G303 G3992
Thayer:
1) to send up
1a) to a higher place
1b) to a person higher in office, authority, or power
2) to send back
