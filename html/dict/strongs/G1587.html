ἐκλείπω
to leave out, omit, pass over
ἐκλείπω to omit, i.e. (by implication) cease (die)
Derivation: from G1537 and G3007;
KJV Usage: fail. G1537 G3007
Thayer:
1) fail
1a) to leave out, omit, pass by
1b) to leave, quit
2) to fail
2a) to leave off, cease, stop
2b) of the failing or eclipse of the light of the sun and the moon
