κατήφεια
dejection, sorrow, shame
κατήφεια demureness, i.e. (by implication) sadness
Derivation: from a compound of G2596 and perhaps a derivative of the base of G5316 (meaning downcast in look);
KJV Usage: heaviness. G2596 G5316
Thayer:
1) a downcast look expressive of sorrow
2) shame, dejection, gloom
