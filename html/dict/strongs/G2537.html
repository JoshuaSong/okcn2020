καινός
Derivation: of uncertain affinity; new (especially in freshness; while G3501 is properly so with respect to age
KJV Usage: new. G3501
Thayer:
1) new
1a) as respects form
1a1) recently made, fresh, recent, unused, unworn
1b) as respects substance
1b1) of a new kind, unprecedented, novel, uncommon, unheard of
For Synonyms see entry G5852
