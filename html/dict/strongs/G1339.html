διΐστημι to stand apart, i.e. (reflexively) to remove, intervene
Derivation: from G1223 and G2476;
KJV Usage: go further, be parted, after the space of. G1223 G2476
Thayer:
1) to place separately, put asunder, disjoin
2) to stand apart, to part, depart
