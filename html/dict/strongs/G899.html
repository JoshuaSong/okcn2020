βάθος
depth
βάθος profundity, i.e. (by implication) extent; (figuratively) mystery
Derivation: from the same as G901;
KJV Usage: deep(-ness, things), depth. G901
Thayer:
1) depth, height
1a) of "the deep" sea
1b) metaph.
1b1) deep, extreme, poverty
1b2) of the deep things of God
