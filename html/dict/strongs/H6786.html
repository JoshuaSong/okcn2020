צמרי
tse mârı̂y
tsem-aw-ree'
Patrial from an unused name of a place in Palestine; a Tsemarite or branch of the Canaanites
KJV Usage: Zemarite.

צמרי
Zemarite = see Zemaraim "double woolens"
