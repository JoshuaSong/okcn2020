συλλογίζομαι
to collect and bring at once before the mind, to compute fully, sum up
συλλογίζομαι to reckon together (with oneself), i.e. deliberate
Derivation: from G4862 and G3049;
KJV Usage: reason with. G4862 G3049
Thayer:
1) to bring together accounts, reckon up, compute
2) to reckon with one's self, to reason
