διαπορεύομαι to travel through
Derivation: from G1223 and G4198;
KJV Usage: go through, journey in, pass by. G1223 G4198
Thayer:
1) to cause to pass through a place
2) to carry across
3) to journey through a place, go through
4) to travel through
