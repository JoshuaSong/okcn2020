ὑποστρέφω
to turn round about, turn back
ὑποστρέφω to turn under (behind), i.e. to return (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G5259 and G4762;
KJV Usage: come again, return (again, back again), turn back (again). G5259 G4762
Thayer:
1) to turn back
1a) to turn about
2) to return
