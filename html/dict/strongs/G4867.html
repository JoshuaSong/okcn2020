συναθροίζω
to gather together, assemble
συναθροίζω to convene
Derivation: from G4862 and ἀθροίζω (to hoard);
KJV Usage: call (gather) together. G4862
Thayer:
1) to gather together with others
2) to assemble
3) to be gathered together i.e. to come together
