Ἀβιληνή Abilene, a region of Syria
Derivation: of foreign origin (compare H58);
KJV Usage: Abilene. H58
Thayer:
1) a region of Syria between Lebanon and Hermon towards Phoenicia,
18 miles (29 km) from Damascus and Heliopolis in a remarkable gorge called "Suk Wadi Barada".
Literal: Abilene = "grassy meadow"
