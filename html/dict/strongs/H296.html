אחיקם
'ăchı̂yqâm
akh-ee-kawm'
From H251 and H6965; brother of rising (that is, high); Achikam, an Israelite
KJV Usage: Ahikam.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אחיקם
Ahikam = "my brother has risen (arisen)"
1. son of Shaphan, an officer in Josiah's reign
2. protector of Jeremiah
3. father of Gedaliah
Origin: from H251 and H6965
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
