εὔχομαι
to pray, offer prayers, pay one's vows, make a vow
εὔχομαι to wish; by implication, to pray to God
Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;
KJV Usage: pray, will, wish.
Thayer:
1) to pray to God
2) to wish, to pray, to pray for
