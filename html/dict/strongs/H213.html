אוּץ
'ûts
oots
A primitive root; to press; (by implication) to be close, hurry, withdraw
KJV Usage: (make) haste (-n, -y), labor, be narrow.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אוּץ

1. to press, be pressed, make haste, urge, be narrow
a. (Qal)
1. to press, hasten
2. to be pressed, confined, narrow
3. to hasten, make haste
4. be narrow
b. (Hiphil)
1. to urge, insist
2. to hasten
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 51
Parts of Speech: Verb
