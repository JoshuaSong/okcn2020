ῥομφαία
a large sword, scymitar
ῥομφαία a sabre, i.e. a long and broad cutlass (any weapon of the kind, literally or figuratively)
Derivation: probably of foreign origin;
KJV Usage: sword.
Thayer:
1) a large sword
2) properly a long Thracian javelin, also a kind of long sword wont to be worn on the right shoulder
