ὄγκος
the barb
bulk, size, mass
ὄγκος a mass (as bending or bulging by its load), i.e. burden (hindrance)
Derivation: probably from the same as G43;
KJV Usage: weight. G43
Thayer:
1) whatever is prominent, protuberance, bulk, mass
1a) hence a burden, weight, encumbrance
For Synonyms see entry G5819
