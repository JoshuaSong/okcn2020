καταποντίζω
to throw into the sea, drown therein
καταποντίζω to plunge down, i.e. submerge
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of the same as G4195;
KJV Usage: drown, sink. G2596 G4195
Thayer:
1) to plunge or sink into the sea
1a) to sink, to go down
1b) a grievous offender for the purpose of killing him, to drown
