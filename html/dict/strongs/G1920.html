ἐπιγίνομαι to arrive upon, i.e. spring up (as a wind)
Derivation: from G1909 and G1096;
KJV Usage: blow. G1909 G1096
Thayer:
1) to become or happen afterwards
1a) to be born after
2) to come to, arrive: of time
3) to arise, spring up, come on
