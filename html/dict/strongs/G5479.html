χαρά
joy, delight

Derivation: from G5463;
KJV Usage: gladness, X greatly, (X be exceeding) joy(-ful, -fully, -fulness, -ous). G5463
Thayer:
1) joy, gladness
1a) the joy received from you
1b) the cause or occasion of joy
1b1) of persons who are one's joy
