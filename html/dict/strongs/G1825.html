ἐξεγείρω
to awaken
ἐξεγείρω to rouse fully, i.e. (figuratively) to resuscitate (from death), release (from infliction)
Derivation: from G1537 and G1453;
KJV Usage: raise up. G1537 G1453
Thayer:
1) to arouse, raise up (from sleep)
2) to rouse up, stir up, incite
