καταστρώννυμι
to lay low
καταστρώννυμι to strew down, i.e. (by implication) to prostrate (slay)
Derivation: from G2596 and G4766;
KJV Usage: overthrow. G2596 G4766
Thayer:
1) to strew over (the ground)
2) to prostrate, slay, (cf to lay low)
