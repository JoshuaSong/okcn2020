μάλιστα (adverbially) most (in the greatest degree) or particularly
Derivation: neuter plural of the superlative of an apparently primary adverb μάλα (very);
KJV Usage: chiefly, most of all, (e-)specially.
Thayer:
1) especially, chiefly, most of all, above all
