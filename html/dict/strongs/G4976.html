σχῆμα
form, figure, appearance
σχῆμα a figure (as a mode or circumstance), i.e. (by implication) external condition
Derivation: from the alternate of G2192;
KJV Usage: fashion. G2192
Thayer:
1) the habitus, as comprising everything in a person which strikes the senses, the figure, bearing, discourse, actions, manner of life etc.
