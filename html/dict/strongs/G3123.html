μᾶλλον (adverbially) more (in a greater degree)) or rather
Derivation: neuter of the comparative of the same as G3122;
KJV Usage: + better, X far, (the) more (and more), (so) much (the more), rather. G3122
Thayer:
1) more, to a greater degree, rather
1a) much, by far
1b) rather, sooner
1c) more willingly, more readily, sooner
