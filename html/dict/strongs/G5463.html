χαίρω
to rejoice, be glad, be delighted
χαίρω to be "cheer"ful, i.e. calmly happy or well-off; impersonally, especially as salutation (on meeting or parting), be well
Derivation: a primary verb;
KJV Usage: farewell, be glad, God speed, greeting, hall, joy(- fully), rejoice.
Thayer:
1) to rejoice, be glad
2) to rejoice exceedingly
3) to be well, thrive
4) in salutations, hail!
5) at the beginning of letters: to give one greeting, salute
