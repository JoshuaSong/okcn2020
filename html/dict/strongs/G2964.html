κυρόω
to make valid, confirm, ratify, determine
κυρόω to make authoritative, i.e. ratify
Derivation: from the same as G2962;
KJV Usage: confirm. G2962
Thayer:
1) to make valid
2) to confirm publicly or solemnly, to ratify
