ἰάομαι
to heal, cure
ἰάομαι to cure (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: middle voice of apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: heal, make whole.
Thayer:
1) to cure, heal
2) to make whole
2a) to free from errors and sins, to bring about (one's) salvation

  • Whole (made), Wholly, Wholesome
