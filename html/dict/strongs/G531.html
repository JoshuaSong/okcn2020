ἀπαράβατος
not passing over to
ἀπαράβατος not passing away, i.e. untransferable (perpetual)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3845;
KJV Usage: unchangeable. G1 G3845
Thayer:
1) unviolated, not to be violated, inviolable
2) unchangeable and therefore not liable to pass to a successor
