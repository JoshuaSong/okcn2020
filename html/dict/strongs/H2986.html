יבל
yâbal
yaw-bal'
A primitive root; properly to flow; causatively to bring (especially with pomp)
KJV Usage: bring (forth), carry, lead (forth).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יבל

1. to bring, lead, carry, conduct, bear along
a. (Hiphil)
1. to bear along, bring
2. to carry away, lead away
3. to lead, conduct
b. (Hophal)
1. to be borne along
2. to be borne (to the grave)
3. to be brought, be led, be conducted
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 835
Parts of Speech: Verb
