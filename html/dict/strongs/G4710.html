σπουδή
haste, speed
σπουδή "speed", i.e. (by implication) despatch, eagerness, earnestness
Derivation: from G4692;
KJV Usage: business, (earnest) care(-fulness), diligence, forwardness, haste. G4692
Thayer:
1) haste, with haste
2) earnestness, diligence
2a) earnestness in accomplishing, promoting, or striving after anything
2b) to give all diligence, interest one's self most earnestly
