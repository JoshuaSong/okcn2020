διδακτός
taught, learnt
διδακτός (subjectively) instructed, or (objectively) communicated by teaching
Derivation: from G1321;
KJV Usage: taught, which … teacheth. G1321
Thayer:
1) that can be taught
2) taught, instructed by one
3) teachings, precepts
