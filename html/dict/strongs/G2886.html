κοσμικός
of the world
κοσμικός terrene ("cosmic"), literally (mundane) or figuratively (corrupt)
Derivation: from G2889 (in its secondary sense);
KJV Usage: worldly. G2889
Thayer:
1) of or belonging to the world
1a) relating to the universe
1b) earthly
1c) worldly, i.e. having the character of this present corrupt age
