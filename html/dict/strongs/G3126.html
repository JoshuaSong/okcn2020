μαμμωνᾶς mammonas, i.e. avarice (deified)
Derivation: of Chaldee origin (confidence, i.e. wealth, personified);
KJV Usage: mammon.
Thayer:
1) mammon
2) treasure
3) riches (where it is personified and opposed to God)
