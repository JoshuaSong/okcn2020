μετοικεσία
the Removal
μετοικεσία a change of abode, i.e. (specially), expatriation
Derivation: from a derivative of a compound of G3326 and G3624;
KJV Usage: X brought, carried(-ying) away (in-)to. G3326 G3624
Thayer:
1) removal from one abode to another, esp. a forced removal
