Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּמישׁ כּמושׁo
Chemosh = "subduer"
1. the national deity of the Moabites and a god of the Ammonites
a. also identified with 'Baal-peor', 'Baal-zebub', 'Mars' and 'Saturn'
b. worship of this god was introduced into Jerusalem by Solomon and abolished by king Josiah of Judah
Origin: from an unused root meaning to subdue
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name
