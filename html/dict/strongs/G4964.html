συσχηματίζω
to conform
συσχηματίζω to fashion alike, i.e. conform to the same pattern (figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and a derivative of G4976;
KJV Usage: conform to, fashion self according to. G4862 G4976
Thayer:
1) to conform one's self (i.e. one's mind and character) to another's pattern, (fashion one's self according to)
