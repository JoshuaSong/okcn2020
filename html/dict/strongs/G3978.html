πεζεύω
to go
πεζεύω to foot a journey, i.e. travel by land
Derivation: from the same as G3979;
KJV Usage: go afoot. G3979
Thayer:
1) to travel on foot (not on horseback or in carriage), or (if opp. to going by sea) by land
