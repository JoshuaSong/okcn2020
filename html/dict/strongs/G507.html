ἄνω upward or on the top
Derivation: adverb from G473;
KJV Usage: above, brim, high, up. G473
Thayer:
1) up, upwards, above, on high
2) of the quarters of the heaven, northward
3) of countries, inland, up from the coast
4) of time, formerly
