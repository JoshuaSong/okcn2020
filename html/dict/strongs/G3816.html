παῖς
a child
παῖς a boy (as often beaten with impunity), or (by analogy), a girl, and (genitive case) a child; specially, a slave or servant (especially a minister to a king; and by eminence to God)
Derivation: perhaps from G3817;
KJV Usage: child, maid(-en), (man) servant, son, young man. G3817
Thayer:
1) a child, boy or girl
1a) infants, children
2) servant, slave
2a) an attendant, servant, spec. a king's attendant, minister
For Synonyms see entry G5868
