Βοανεργές sons of commotion; Boanerges, an epithet of two of the apostles
Derivation: of Chaldee origin (H1123 and H7266);
KJV Usage: Boanerges. H1123 H7266
Thayer:
1) a nickname given to James and John, the sons of Zebedee, by the Lord. The name seems to denote fiery and destructive zeal that may be likened to a thunder storm.
Literal: Boanerges = "sons of thunder"
