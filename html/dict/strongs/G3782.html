ὀφειλή
a debt
ὀφειλή indebtedness, i.e. (concretely) a sum owed; figuratively, obligation, i.e. (conjugal) duty
Derivation: from G3784;
KJV Usage: debt, due. G3784
Thayer:
1) that which is owed
2) a debt
3) metaph. dues: specifically of conjugal duty
