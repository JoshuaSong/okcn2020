δημηγορέω
to speak in the assembly
δημηγορέω to be a people-gatherer, i.e. to address a public assembly
Derivation: from a compound of G1218 and G58;
KJV Usage: make an oration. G1218 G58
Thayer:
1) to address a public assembly, make a speech to the people
