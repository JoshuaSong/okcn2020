κόπος
a striking, beating
κόπος a cut, i.e. (by analogy) toil (as reducing the strength), literally or figuratively; by implication, pains
Derivation: from G2875;
KJV Usage: labour, + trouble, weariness. G2875
Thayer:
1) a beating
2) a beating of the breast with grief, sorrow
3) labour
3a) trouble
3a1) to cause one trouble, make work for him
3b) intense labour united with trouble and toil
For Synonyms see entry G5860
