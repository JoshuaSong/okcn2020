αἰχμάλωτος
taken by the spear, captive to one's spear, taken prisoner
αἰχμάλωτος properly, a prisoner of war, i.e. (genitive case) a captive
Derivation: from αἰχμή (a spear) and a derivative of the same as G259;
KJV Usage: captive. G259
Thayer:
1) a captive
