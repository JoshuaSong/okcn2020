αἰφνίδιος
unforeseen, sudden
αἰφνίδιος unexpected, i.e. (adverbially) suddenly
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a negative particle) and G5316 (compare G1810) (meaning non-apparent);
KJV Usage: sudden, unawares. G1 G5316 G1810
Thayer:
1) unexpected, sudden, unforeseen
