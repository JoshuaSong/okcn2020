ἐρημόω
to strip bare, to desolate, lay waste
ἐρημόω to lay waste (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2048;
KJV Usage: (bring to, make) desolate(-ion), come to nought. G2048
Thayer:
1) to make desolate, lay waste
2) to ruin, bring to desolation
3) to despoil one, strip her of her treasures
