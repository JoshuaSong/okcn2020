ἐξίστημι
to put out of its place, to change
ἐξίστημι to put (stand) out of wits, i.e. astound, or (reflexively) become astounded, insane
Derivation: from G1537 and G2476;
KJV Usage: amaze, be (make) astonished, be beside self (selves), bewitch, wonder. G1537 G2476
Thayer:
1) to throw out of position, displace
1a) to amaze, to astonish, throw into wonderment
1b) to be amazed, astounded
1c) to be out of one's mind, besides one's self, insane

  • Beside oneself (to be)
  • Bewitch
