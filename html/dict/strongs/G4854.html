σύμφυτος
born with one, congenital, innate, natural, inborn, inbred
σύμφυτος grown along with (connate), i.e. (figuratively) closely united to
Derivation: from G4862 and a derivative of G5453;
KJV Usage: planted together. G4862 G5453
Thayer:
1) born together with, of joint origin
1a) connate, congenital, innate, implanted by birth or nature
2) grown together, united with
3) kindred
