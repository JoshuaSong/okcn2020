εὐκαιρέω
to devote one's leisure
εὐκαιρέω to have good time, i.e. opportunity or leisure
Derivation: from G2121;
KJV Usage: have leisure (convenient time), spend time. G2121
Thayer:
1) to have opportunity
2) to have leisure
3) to do something
4) to give one's time to a thing
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Leisure (to have)
  • Opportunity (lack)
  • Spend, Spent
