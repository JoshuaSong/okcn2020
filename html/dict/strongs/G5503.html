χήρα
bereft of a husband, a widow
χήρα a widow (as lacking a husband), literally or figuratively
Derivation: feminine of a presumed derivative apparently from the base of G5490 through the idea of deficiency;
KJV Usage: widow. G5490
Thayer:
1) a widow
2) metaph. a city stripped of its inhabitants and riches is represented under the figure of a widow
