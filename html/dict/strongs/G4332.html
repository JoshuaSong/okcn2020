προσεδρεύω
to sit near, be always at
προσεδρεύω to sit near, i.e. attend as a servant
Derivation: from a compound of G4314 and the base of G1476;
KJV Usage: wait at. G4314 G1476
Thayer:
1) to sit near
2) to attend assiduously
3) to be in attendance upon, not to quit one's side
