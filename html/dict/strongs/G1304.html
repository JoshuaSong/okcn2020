διατρίβω
to rub between, rub hard, rub away, consume, waste
διατρίβω to wear through (time), i.e. remain
Derivation: from G1223 and the base of G5147;
KJV Usage: abide, be, continue, tarry. G1223 G5147
Thayer:
1) to rub between, rub hard
2) to wear away, consume
3) to spend, pass time
