פּוּה פּוּאה
pû'âh pûvvâh
poo-aw', poov-vaw'
From H6284; a blast; Puah or Puvvah, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Phuvah, Pua, Puah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּוּה פּוּאה
Puah or Pua or Phuvah = "splendid"
1. son of Issachar
a. also 'Pua' and 'Phuvah'
2. man of Issachar, father of Tola, the judge of Israel after Abimelech
Origin: from H6284
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
