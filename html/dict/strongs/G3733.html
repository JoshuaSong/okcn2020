a bird
ὄρνις a bird (as rising in the air), i.e. (specially), a hen (or female domestic fowl)
Derivation: probably from a prolonged form of the base of G3735;
KJV Usage: hen. G3735
Thayer:
1) a bird
2) a cock, a hen
