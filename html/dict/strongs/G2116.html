εὐθύνω
to guide straight, direct
εὐθύνω to straighten (level); technically, to steer
Derivation: from G2117;
KJV Usage: governor, make straight. G2117
Thayer:
1) to make straight, level, plain
2) to lead or guide straight, to keep straight or direct
2a) of the steersman or helmsman of a ship
2b) of a charioteer
