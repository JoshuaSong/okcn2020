κωλύω
to let, hinder, check, prevent
κωλύω to estop, i.e. prevent (by word or act)
Derivation: from the base of G2849;
KJV Usage: forbid, hinder, keep from, let, not suffer, withstand. G2849
Thayer:
1) to hinder, prevent forbid
2) to withhold a thing from anyone
3) to deny or refuse one a thing
