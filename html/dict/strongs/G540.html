ἀπάτωρ
without father, fatherless
ἀπάτωρ fatherless, i.e. of unrecorded paternity
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G3962;
KJV Usage: without father. G1 G3962
Thayer:
1) whose father is not recorded in the genealogies
