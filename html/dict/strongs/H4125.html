mô'âbı̂y mô'âbı̂yâh mô'âbı̂yth
mo-aw-bee', mo-aw-bee-yaw', mo-aw-beeth'
Patronymic from H4124; a Moabite or Moabitess, that is, a descendant from Moab
KJV Usage: (woman) of Moab, Moabite (-ish, -ss).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מואבית מואביּה מואבי
Moabite = "from father: what father?"
1. an citizen of Moab
2. an inhabitant of the land of Moab
Origin: patronymical from H4124
