κατέρχομαι
to go down from
κατέρχομαι to come (or go) down (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2596 and G2064 (including its alternate);
KJV Usage: come (down), depart, descend, go down, land. G2596 G2064
Thayer:
1) to come down, go down
1a) of one who goes from a higher to a lower locality
1b) of those who come to a place by a ship
