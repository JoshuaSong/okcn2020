ἀντλέω
to bale out bilge-water, bale the ship
ἀντλέω to bale up (properly, bilge water), i.e. dip water (with a bucket, pitcher, etc.)
Derivation: from ἄντλος (the hold of a ship);
KJV Usage: draw (out).
Thayer:
1) to draw out of a ship's bilge-water, to bale or pump out
2) to draw water
