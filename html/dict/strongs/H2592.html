חנּיאל
channı̂y'êl
khan-nee-ale'
From H2603 and H410; favor of God; Channiel, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Hanniel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חנּיאל
Hanniel = "favoured of God"
1. son of Ephod and a prince of the tribe of Manasseh
2. son of Ulla and a chief of the tribe of Asher; also 'Haniel'
Origin: from H2603 and H410
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
