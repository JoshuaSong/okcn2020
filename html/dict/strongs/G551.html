ἀπείραστος
incapable of being tempted by
ἀπείραστος untried, i.e. not temptable
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G3987;
KJV Usage: not to be tempted. G1 G3987
Thayer:
1) that can not be tempted by evil, not liable to temptation to sin
