ἀπολείπω
to leave over
ἀπολείπω to leave behind (passively, remain); by implication, to forsake
Derivation: from G575 and G3007;
KJV Usage: leave, remain. G575 G3007
Thayer:
1) to leave, to leave behind
2) to desert or forsake
