μιμνήσκω
to remind, put
μιμνήσκω to remind, i.e. (middle voice) to recall to mind
Derivation: a prolonged form of G3415 (from which some of the tenses are borrowed);
KJV Usage: be mindful, remember. G3415
Thayer:
1) to remind
1a) to be recalled or to return to one's mind, to remind one's self of, to remember
1b) to be recalled to mind, to be remembered, had in remembrance
1c) to remember a thing
1d) be mindful of
