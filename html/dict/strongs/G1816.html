ἐξανατέλλω
to spring up from
ἐξανατέλλω to start up out of the ground, i.e. germinate
Derivation: from G1537 and G393;
KJV Usage: spring up. G1537 G393
Thayer:
1) to make spring up, cause to shoot forth
2) to spring up
