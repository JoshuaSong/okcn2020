νομοδιδάσκαλος
a teacher of the law
νομοδιδάσκαλος an expounder of the (Jewish) law, i.e. a Rabbi
Derivation: from G3551 and G1320;
KJV Usage: doctor (teacher) of the law. G3551 G1320
Thayer:
1) a teacher and interpreter of the law: among the Jews
1a) of those who among Christians went about as champions and interpreters of the Mosaic law
