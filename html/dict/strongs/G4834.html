συμπαθέω
to sympathise
συμπαθέω to feel "sympathy" with, i.e. (by implication) to commiserate
Derivation: from G4835;
KJV Usage: have compassion, be touched with a feeling of. G4835
Thayer:
1) to be affected with the same feeling as another, to sympathise with
2) to feel for, have compassion on
