κατασύρω
to pull down, lay waste, ravage
κατασύρω to drag down, i.e. arrest judicially
Derivation: from G2596 and G4951;
KJV Usage: hale. G2596 G4951
Thayer:
1) to draw down, pull down
2) to draw along, drag forcibly
