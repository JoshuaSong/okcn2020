ἀναζάω
to return to life, be alive again
ἀναζάω to recover life (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G303 and G2198;
KJV Usage: (be a-)live again, revive. G303 G2198
Thayer:
1) live again, recover life
1a) to be restored to a correct life
1a1) of one who returns to a better moral state
1b) to revive, regain strength and vigour
