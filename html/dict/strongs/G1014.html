βούλομαι
to will, wish, be willing
βούλομαι to "will," i.e. (reflexively) be willing
Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;
KJV Usage: be disposed, minded, intend, list, (be, of own) will (-ing). Compare G2309. G2309
Thayer:
1) to will deliberately, have a purpose, be minded
2) of willing as an affection, to desire
