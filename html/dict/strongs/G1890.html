ἐπαφρίζω
to foam up
ἐπαφρίζω to foam upon, i.e. (figuratively) to exhibit (a vile passion)
Derivation: from G1909 and G875;
KJV Usage: foam out. G1909 G875
Thayer:
1) to foam up
2) to cast out as foam, foam out
