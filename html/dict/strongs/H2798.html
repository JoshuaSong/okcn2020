Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חרשׁים
Charashim or Harashim = "valley of craftsmen"
1. a valley near Lydda, a few miles east of Joppa, founded by Joab of Othniel's family
Origin: pl. of H2796
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
