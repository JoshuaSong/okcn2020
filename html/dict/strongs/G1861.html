ἐπαγγέλλω
to tell, proclaim, announce
ἐπαγγέλλω to announce upon (reflexively), i.e. (by implication) to engage to do something, to assert something respecting oneself
Derivation: from G1909 and the base of G32;
KJV Usage: profess, (make) promise. G1909 G32
Thayer:
1) to announce that one is about to do or furnish something
1a) to promise (of one's own accord) to engage voluntarily
2) to profess
2a) an art, to profess one's skill in something
