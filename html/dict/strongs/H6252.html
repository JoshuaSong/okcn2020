Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עשׁתּרת עשׁתּרות
Ashtaroth or Astaroth = "star" n pr f deity
1. false goddesses in the Canaanite religion, usually related to fertility cult n pr loc
2. a city in Bashan east of the Jordan given to Manasseh
a. same as 06255
