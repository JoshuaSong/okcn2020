συμπληρόω
to help to fill
συμπληρόω to implenish completely, i.e. (of space) to swamp (a boat), or (of time) to accomplish (passive, be complete)
Derivation: from G4862 and G4137;
KJV Usage: (fully) come, fill up. G4862 G4137
Thayer:
1) to fill completely
1a) of the hold of a ship
2) to complete entirely, be fulfilled: of time
