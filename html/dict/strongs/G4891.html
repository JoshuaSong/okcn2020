συνεγείρω
to help in raising
συνεγείρω to rouse (from death) in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to revivify (spirtually) in resemblance to
Derivation: from G4862 and G1453;
KJV Usage: raise up together, rise with. G4862 G1453
Thayer:
1) to raise together, to cause to raise together
2) to raise up together from mortal death to a new and blessed life dedicated to God
