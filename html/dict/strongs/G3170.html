μεγαλύνω
to make great
μεγαλύνω to make (or declare) great, i.e. increase or (figuratively) extol
Derivation: from G3173;
KJV Usage: enlarge, magnify, shew great. G3173
Thayer:
1) to make great, magnify
1a) metaph. to make conspicuous
2) to deem or declare great
2a) to esteem highly, to extol, laud, celebrate
3) to get glory and praise
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words

  • Magnify
