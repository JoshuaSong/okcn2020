תּבוּאה
te bû'âh
teb-oo-aw'
From H935; income, that is, produce (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: fruit, gain, increase, revenue.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּבוּאה

1. produce, product, revenue
a. product, yield, crops (of the earth usually)
b. income, revenue
c. gain (of wisdom) (fig)
d. product of lips (fig)
Origin: from H935
TWOT: 212c
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
