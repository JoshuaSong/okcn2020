ἐπιζητέω
to seek after, wish for, miss
ἐπιζητέω to search (inquire) for; intensively, to demand, to crave
Derivation: from G1909 and G2212;
KJV Usage: desire, enquire, seek (after, for). G1909 G2212
Thayer:
1) to enquire for, seek for, search for, seek diligently
2) to wish for, crave
3) to demand, clamour for
