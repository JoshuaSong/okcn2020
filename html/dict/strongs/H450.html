'elyâdâ‛

From H410 and H3045; God (is) knowing; Eljada, the name of two Israelites and of an Aramaean
KJV Usage: Eliada.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אלידע
Eliadah or Eliada = "God knows"
1. a son of David
2. a Benjamite warrior chief
3. an Aramean, the father of an enemy of Solomon
Origin: from H410 and H3045
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
