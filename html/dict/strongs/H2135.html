זכה
zâkâh
zaw-kaw'
A primitive root (compare H2141); to be translucent; figuratively to be innocent
KJV Usage: be (make) clean, cleanse, be clear, count pure.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

זכה

1. to be clean, be pure, be clear
a. (Qal)
1. to be clean, be pure
2. to be clear, be justified
b. (Piel)
1. to make clean, make pure, keep clean, keep pure
c. (Hithpael)
1. to cleanse
2. to make yourself clean, purify oneself
Origin: a primitive root [compare H2141]; to be translucent
TWOT: 549
Parts of Speech: Verb
