γυμνάζω
to train naked, train in gymnastic exercise
γυμνάζω to practise naked (in the games), i.e. train (figuratively)
Derivation: from G1131;
KJV Usage: exercise. G1131
Thayer:
1) to exercise naked (in a palaestra or school of athletics)
2) to exercise vigorously, in any way, either the body or the mind
