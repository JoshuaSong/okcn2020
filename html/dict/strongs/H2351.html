חץ חוּץ
chûts chûts
khoots, khoots
(Both forms feminine in the plural); from an unused root meaning to sever; properly separate by a wall, that is, outside, outdoors
KJV Usage: abroad, field, forth, highway, more, out (-side, -ward), street, without.
