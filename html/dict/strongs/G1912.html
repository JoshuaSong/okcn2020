ἐπιβαρέω to be heavy upon, i.e. (pecuniarily) to be expensive to; figuratively, to be severe towards
Derivation: from G1909 and G916;
KJV Usage: be chargeable to, overcharge. G1909 G916
Thayer:
1) to put a burden upon, to load
2) to be burdensome
