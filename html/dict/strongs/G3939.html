παροικέω
to dwell beside
παροικέω to dwell near, i.e. reside as a foreigner
Derivation: from G3844 and G3611;
KJV Usage: sojourn in, be a stranger. G3844 G3611
Thayer:
1) to dwell beside (one) or in one's neighbourhood, to live near
2) in the NT, to be or dwell in a place as a stranger, to sojourn
For Synonyms see entry G5854
