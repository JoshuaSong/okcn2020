μακαρισμός
a pronouncing happy, blessing
μακαρισμός beatification, i.e. attribution of good fortune
Derivation: from G3106;
KJV Usage: blessedness. G3106
Thayer:
1) declaration of blessedness
2) to utter a declaration of blessedness upon one
3) to pronounce one blessed
