ἀγκάλη
the bent arm
ἀγκάλη an arm (as curved)
Derivation: from ἄγκος (a bend, "ache");
KJV Usage: arm.
Thayer:
1) the curve or inner angle of the arm, the bent arm
2) anything closely enfolding, as the arms of the sea, etc.
