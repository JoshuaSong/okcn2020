κτίζω
to people
κτίζω to fabricate, i.e. found (form originally)
Derivation: probably akin to G2932 (through the idea of proprietorship of the manufacturer);
KJV Usage: create, Creator, make. G2932
Thayer:
1) to make habitable, to people, a place, region, island
1a) to found a city, colony, state
2) to create
2a) of God creating the worlds
2b) to form, shape, i.e. to completely change or transform
