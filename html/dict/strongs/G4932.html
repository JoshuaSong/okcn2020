συντέμνω
to cut in pieces: to cut down, cut short
συντέμνω to contract by cutting, i.e. (figuratively) do concisely (speedily)
Derivation: from G4862 and the base of G5114;
KJV Usage: (cut) short. G4862 G5114
Thayer:
1) to cut in pieces
2) to cut short
3) metaph.
3a) to dispatch briefly, execute or finish quickly
3b) to hasten
3c) a short word i.e. an expedited prophecy or decree
