ἐξέραμα
a vomit, thing vomited
ἐξέραμα vomit, i.e. food disgorged
Derivation: from a comparative of G1537 and a presumed ἐράω (to spue);
KJV Usage: vomit. G1537
Thayer:
1) vomit
2) what is cast out by vomiting
