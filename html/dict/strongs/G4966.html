Συχέμ Sychem (i.e. Shekem), the name of a Canaanite and of a place in Palestine
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H7927);
KJV Usage: Sychem. H7927

2) the son of Emmor
Literal: Shechem = "shoulder (literal early rising): diligence"
