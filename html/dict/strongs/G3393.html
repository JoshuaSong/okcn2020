μίασμα
stain, defilement, the taint of guilt
μίασμα (morally) foulness (properly, the effect)
Derivation: from G3392 ("miasma");
KJV Usage: pollution. G3392
Thayer:
1) that which defiles, defilement
2) metaph., vices the foulness of which contaminates one in his intercourse with the ungodly mass of mankind
