פּגשׁ
pâgash
paw-gash'
A primitive root; to come in contact with, whether by accident or violence; figuratively to concur
KJV Usage: meet (with, together).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּגשׁ

1. to meet, join, encounter
a. (Qal) to meet, encounter
b. (Niphal) to meet together, meet each other
c. (Piel) to meet, encounter
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1733
Parts of Speech: Verb
