διασώζω
to preserve through
διασώζω to save thoroughly, i.e. (by implication or analogy) to cure, preserve, rescue, etc.
Derivation: from G1223 and G4982;
KJV Usage: bring safe, escape (safe), heal, make perfectly whole, save. G1223 G4982
Thayer:
1) to preserve through danger, to bring safely through
1a) to save, i.e. cure one who is sick, bring him through
2) to save, keep from perishing
3) to save out of danger, rescue

  • Heal, Healing
  • Safe, Safely, Safety
  • Save, Saving
  • Whole (made), Wholly, Wholesome
