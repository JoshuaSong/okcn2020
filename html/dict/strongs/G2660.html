κατανύσσω to pierce thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) to agitate violently ("sting to the quick")
Derivation: from G2596 and G3572;
KJV Usage: prick. G2596 G3572
Thayer:
1) to prick, pierce
2) metaph. to pain the mind sharply, agitate it vehemently
2a) esp. of the emotion of sorrow
