ערבּה
‛ărûbbâh
ar-oob-baw'
Feminine passive participle of H6048 in the sense of a bargain or exchange; something given as security, that is, (literally) a token (of safety) or (metaphorically) a bondsman
KJV Usage: pledge, surety.
