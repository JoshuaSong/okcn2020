חפצי בּהּ
chephtsı̂y bâhh
khef-tsee' baw
From H2656 with suffixes; my delight (is) in her; Cheptsibah, a fanciful name for Palestine
KJV Usage: Hephzi-bah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חפצי בּהּ
Hephzi-bah = "my delight is in her"
1. the queen of King Hezekiah and mother of Manasseh
2. a name for Jesusalem (fig.)
Origin: from H2656 with suffixes
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
