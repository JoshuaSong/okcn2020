כּהן
kâhan
kaw-han'
A primitive root, apparently meaning to mediate in religious services; but used only as denominative from H3548; to officiate as a priest; figuratively to put on regalia
KJV Usage: deck, be (do the office of a, execute the, minister in the) priest (‘s office).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּהן

1. to act as a priest, minister in a priest's office
a. (Piel)
1. to minister as a priest, serve as a priest
2. to be or become a priest
3. to play the priest
Origin: a primitive root, apparently meaning to mediate in religious services
