ספן
sâphan
saw-fan'
A primitive root; to hide by covering; specifically to roof (passive participle as noun, a roof) or wainscot; figuratively to reserve
KJV Usage: cieled, cover, seated.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

a. (Qal)
1. to cover in, panel
