נתוּן נתין o
nâthı̂yn nâthûn
naw-theen', naw-thoon'
The second form is the proper form, as passive participle; from H5414; one given, that is, (in the plural only) the Nethinim, or Temple Servants (as given up to that duty)
KJV Usage: Nethinims.
