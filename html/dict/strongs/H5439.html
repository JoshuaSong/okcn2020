סביבה סביב
sâbı̂yb sebı̂ybâh
saw-beeb', seb-ee-baw'
From H5437; (as noun) a circle, neighbor, or environs; but chiefly (as adverb, with or without preposition) around
KJV Usage: (place, round) about, circuit, compass, on every side.
