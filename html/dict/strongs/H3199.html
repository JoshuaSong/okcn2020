יכין
yâkı̂yn
yaw-keen'
From H3559; he (or it) will establish; Jakin, the name of three Israelites and of a temple pillar
KJV Usage: Jachin.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יכין
Jachin = "He will establish" n pr m
1. the 4th son of Simeon and founder of the family of Jachinites
2. a priest and the head of the 21st course in the time of David
3. a post-exilic priest in the time of Nehemiah n pr
4. name of the right-hand pillar before the temple
Origin: from H3559
TWOT: None
