πηγή
running waters, streams
πηγή a fount (literally or figuratively), i.e. source or supply (of water, blood, enjoyment) (not necessarily the original spring)
Derivation: probably from G4078 (through the idea of gushing plumply);
KJV Usage: fountain, well. G4078
Thayer:
1) fountain, spring
2) a well fed by a spring
