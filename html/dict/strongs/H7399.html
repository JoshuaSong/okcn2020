Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רכשׁ רכוּשׁ

1. property, goods, possessions
a. property, goods
1. general term for all movable goods
2. of livestock
3. of stores, utensils, etc
Origin: from pass part of H7408
TWOT: 2167b
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
