ὑδροποτέω
to drink water
ὑδροποτέω to be a water-drinker, i.e. to abstain from vinous beverages
Derivation: from a compound of G5204 and a derivative of G4095;
KJV Usage: drink water. G5204 G4095
Thayer:
1) to drink water, be a drinker of water
