περιάγω
to lead
περιάγω to take around (as a companion); reflexively, to walk around
Derivation: from G4012 and G71;
KJV Usage: compass, go (round) about, lead about. G4012 G71
Thayer:
1) to lead around, to lead about with one's self
2) to go about, walk about
