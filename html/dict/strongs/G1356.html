διοπετής sky-fallen (i.e. an aerolite)
Derivation: from the alternate of G2203 and the alternate of G4098;
KJV Usage: which fell down from Jupiter. G2203 G4098
Thayer:
1) fallen from Zeus, i.e. from heaven
2) an image of the Ephesian Artemis which was supposed to have fallen from heaven
