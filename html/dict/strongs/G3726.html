ὁρκίζω
to make
ὁρκίζω to put on oath, i.e. make swear; by analogy, to solemnly enjoin
Derivation: from G3727;
KJV Usage: adjure, charge. G3727
Thayer:
1) to force to take an oath, to administer an oath to
2) to adjure (solemnly implore)
