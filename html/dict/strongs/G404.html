ἀναψύχω
to cool, to revive by fresh air, to refresh
ἀναψύχω properly, to cool off, i.e. (figuratively) relieve
Derivation: from G303 and G5594;
KJV Usage: refresh. G303 G5594
Thayer:
1) to cool again, to cool off, recover from the effects of heat
1a) to refresh (one's spirit)
2) to recover breath, take the air, cool off, revive, refresh one's self
