Βηθεσδά house of kindness; Beth-esda, a pool in Jerusalem
Derivation: of Chaldee origin (compare H1004 and H2617);
KJV Usage: Bethesda. H1004 H2617
Thayer:
1) the name of a pool near the sheep-gate at Jerusalem, whose waters had curative powers
Literal: Bethesda = "house of mercy" or "flowing water"
