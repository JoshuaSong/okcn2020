ἀλλά
otherwise, but
ἀλλά properly, other things, i.e. (adverbially) contrariwise (in many relations)
Derivation: neuter plural of G243;
KJV Usage: and, but (even), howbeit, indeed, nay, nevertheless, no, notwithstanding, save, therefore, yea, yet. G243
Thayer:
1) but
1a) nevertheless, notwithstanding
1b) an objection
1c) an exception
1d) a restriction
1e) nay, rather, yea, moreover
1f) forms a transition to the cardinal matter
