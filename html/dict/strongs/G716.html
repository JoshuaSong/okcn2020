ἅρμα
a chariot
ἅρμα a chariot (as raised or fitted together (compare G719))
Derivation: probably from G142 (perhaps with G1 (as a particle of union) prefixed);
KJV Usage: chariot. G142 G1 G719
Thayer:
1) a chariot
2) of war chariots (i.e. armed with scythes)
3) chariots (drawn with many horses)
