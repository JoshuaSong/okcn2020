κατανεύω
to nod assent
κατανεύω to nod down (towards), i.e. (by analogy) to make signs to
Derivation: from G2596 and G3506;
KJV Usage: beckon. G2596 G3506
Thayer:
1) to nod to, make a sign
2) to indicate to another by a nod or sign what one wishes him to do
