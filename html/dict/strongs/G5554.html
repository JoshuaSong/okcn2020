χρυσοδακτύλιος
with ring of gold
χρυσοδακτύλιος gold-ringed, i.e. wearing a golden finger-ring or similar jewelry
Derivation: from G5557 and G1146;
KJV Usage: with a gold ring. G5557 G1146
Thayer:
