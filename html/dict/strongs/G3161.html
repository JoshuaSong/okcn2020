Ματταθίας Mattathias (i.e. Mattithjah), an Israelite and a Christian
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H4993);
KJV Usage: Mattathias. H4993
Thayer:
1) the son of Amos, in the genealogy of Christ
2) Mattathias was the son of Semei in the genealogy of Christ
Literal: Mattathias = "gift of Jehovah"
