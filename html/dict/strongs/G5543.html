χρηστός
useful, good of its kind, serviceable
χρηστός employed, i.e. (by implication) useful (in manner or morals)
Derivation: from G5530;
KJV Usage: better, easy, good(-ness), gracious, kind. G5530
Thayer:
1) fit, fit for use, useful
1a) virtuous, good
2) manageable
2a) mild, pleasant (as opp. to harsh, hard sharp, bitter)
2b) of things: more pleasant, of people, kind, benevolent
