ὑπόδικος
brought to trial
ὑπόδικος under sentence, i.e. (by implication) condemned
Derivation: from G5259 and G1349;
KJV Usage: guilty. G5259 G1349
Thayer:
1) under judgment, one who lost his suit
2) debtor to one, owing satisfaction to
2a) of liable to punishment from God
