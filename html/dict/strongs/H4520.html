מנשּׁי
me nashshı̂y
men-ash-shee'
From H4519; a Menashshite or descendant of Menashsheh
KJV Usage: of Manasseh, Manassites.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מנשּׁי
Manassites = Manasseh "causing to forget"
1. descendants of Manasseh, son of Joseph and grandson of Jacob
a. specifically used only of that half that lived east of the Jordan
Origin: from H4519
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Adjective
