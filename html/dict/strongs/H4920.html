Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

משׁלמיהוּ משׁלמיה
Meshelemiah = "whom Jehovah repays"
1. a Korhite Levite porter or gatekeeper of the house of God in the time of David
Origin: from H7999 and H3050
TWOT: None
