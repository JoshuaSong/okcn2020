בּהרת
bôhereth
bo-heh'-reth
Feminine active participle of the same as H925; a whitish spot on the skin
KJV Usage: bright spot.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּהרת

1. white patch of skin, brightness, bright spot (on skin)
2. (CLBL) bright spot, scar, blister, boil
a. resulting from fire
b. resulting from inflammation of the skin
c. possible sign of leprosy
Origin: active participle of the same as H925
TWOT: 211a
Parts of Speech: Noun Feminine
