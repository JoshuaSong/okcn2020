אהלות אהלים
'ăhâlı̂ym 'ăhâlôth
a-haw-leem', a-haw-loth'
(The second form, which is feminine, is used only in the plural); of foreign origin; aloe wood (that is, sticks)
KJV Usage: (tree of lign-) aloes.
