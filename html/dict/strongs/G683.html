ἀπωθέομαι or ἀπώθομαι to push off, figuratively, to reject
Derivation: from G575 and the middle voice of ὠθέω or ὤθω (to shove);
KJV Usage: cast away, put away (from), thrust away (from). G575
Thayer:
1) to thrust away, push away, repel
2) to thrust away from one's self, to drive away from one's self
2a) repudiate, reject, refuse
