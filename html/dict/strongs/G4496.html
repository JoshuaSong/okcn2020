ῥίπτω
to throw, cast, hurl
ῥίπτω to fling (properly, with a quick toss, thus differing from G906, which denotes a deliberate hurl; and from τείνω (see in G1614), which indicates an extended projection); by qualification, to deposit (as if a load); by extension, to disperse
Derivation: a primary verb (perhaps rather akin to the base of G4474, through the idea of sudden motion);
KJV Usage: cast (down, out), scatter abroad, throw. G4474 G906 G1614
Thayer:
1) to cast, throw
2) throw down
3) to cast forward or before
4) to set down (with the suggestion of haste and want of care)
5) to throw to the ground, prostrate
