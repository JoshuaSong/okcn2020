νόημα
that which is perceived, a perception, thought
νόημα a perception, i.e. purpose, or (by implication) the intellect, disposition, itself
Derivation: from G3539;
KJV Usage: device, mind, thought. G3539
Thayer:
1) a mental perception, thought
2) an evil purpose
3) that which thinks, the mind, thoughts or purposes
