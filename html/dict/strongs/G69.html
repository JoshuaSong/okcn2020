ἀγρυπνέω
to lie awake, be wakeful
ἀγρυπνέω to be sleepless, i.e. keep awake
Derivation: ultimately from G1 (as negative particle) and G5258;
KJV Usage: watch. G1 G5258
Thayer:
1) to be sleepless, keep awake, watch
2) to be circumspect, attentive, ready
