συστρέφω
to twist up into a ball
συστρέφω to twist together, i.e. collect (a bundle, a crowd)
Derivation: from G4862 and G4762;
KJV Usage: gather. G4862 G4762
Thayer:
1) to twist together, roll together
2) to collect, combine, unite
2a) of men, to (gather themselves together) assemble
