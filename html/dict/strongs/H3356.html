Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יקים
Jakim = "He will raise"
1. a Benjamite and descendant of Shimhi
2. a Levite priest in charge of the 12th course in the time of David
Origin: from H6965
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
