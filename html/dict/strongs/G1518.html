εἰρηνοποιός
a peace-maker
εἰρηνοποιός pacificatory, i.e. (subjectively) peaceable
Derivation: from G1515 and G4160;
KJV Usage: peacemaker. G1515 G4160
Thayer:
1) a peacemaker
2) pacific, loving peace
