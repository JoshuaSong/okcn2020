πηλίκος
how great
πηλίκος how much (as an indefinite), i.e. in size or (figuratively) dignity
Derivation: a quantitative form (the feminine) of the base of G4225;
KJV Usage: how great (large). G4225
Thayer:
1) how great, how large
1a) in a material reference (denoting geometrical magnitude as distinguished from arithmetical)
1b) in an ethical sense: how distinguished
