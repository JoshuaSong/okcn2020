κραταιόω to empower, i.e. (passively) increase in vigor
Derivation: from G2900;
KJV Usage: be strengthened, be (wax) strong. G2900
Thayer:
1) to strengthen, make strong
2) to be made strong, to increase in strength, to grow strong
