καταβραβεύω
to give judgment against
καταβραβεύω to award the price against, i.e. (figuratively) to defraud (of salvation)
Derivation: from G2596 and G1018 (in its original sense);
KJV Usage: beguile of reward. G2596 G1018
Thayer:
1) to decide as umpire against someone
2) to defraud or beguile of the prize of victory
3) metaph. to deprive of salvation
