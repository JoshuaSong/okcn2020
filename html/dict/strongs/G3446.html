μόρφωσις
form, semblance
μόρφωσις formation, i.e. (by implication), appearance (semblance or (concretely) formula)
Derivation: from G3445;
KJV Usage: form. G3445
Thayer:
1) a forming, shaping
2) form
2a) the mere form, semblance
2b) the form befitting a thing or truly expressing the fact, the very form
