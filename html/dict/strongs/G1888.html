ἐπαυτοφώρῳ in theft itself, i.e. (by analogy) in actual crime
Derivation: from G1909 and G846 and (the dative case singular of) a derivative of φώρ (a thief);
KJV Usage: in the very act. G1909 G846
Thayer:
1) in the act
2) caught in the act of theft
3) caught in the act of perpetuating any other crime
