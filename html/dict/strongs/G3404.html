μισέω
to hate
μισέω to detest (especially to persecute); by extension, to love less
Derivation: from a primary μῖσος (hatred);
KJV Usage: hate(-ful).
Thayer:
1) to hate, pursue with hatred, detest
2) to be hated, detested
