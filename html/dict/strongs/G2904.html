κράτος
strength, might
κράτος vigor ("great") (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: perhaps a primary word;
KJV Usage: dominion, might(-ily), power, strength.
Thayer:
1) force, strength
2) power, might: mighty with great power
2a) a mighty deed, a work of power
3) dominion
For Synonyms see entry G5820
