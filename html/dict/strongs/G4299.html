πρόκριμα
prejudgment, prejudice
πρόκριμα a prejudgment (prejudice), i.e. prepossession
Derivation: from a compound of G4253 and G2919;
KJV Usage: prefer one before another. G4253 G2919
Thayer:
1) an opinion formed before the facts are known
2) a prejudgment, a prejudice
