πρεσβεύω
to be the elder
πρεσβεύω to be a senior, i.e. (by implication) act as a representative (figuratively, preacher)
Derivation: from the base of G4245;
KJV Usage: be an ambassador. G4245
Thayer:
1) to be older, prior by birth or in age
2) to be an ambassador, act as an ambassador
