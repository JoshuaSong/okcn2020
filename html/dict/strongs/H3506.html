KJV Usage: Ithran.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יתרן
Ithran = "advantage"
1. an Edomite, son of Dishon, a Horite, and probably a phylarch of a tribe of the Horim
2. an descendant of Asher
Origin: from H3498
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
