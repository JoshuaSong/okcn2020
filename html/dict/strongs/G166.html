αἰώνιος
lasting for an age
αἰώνιος perpetual (also used of past time, or past and future as well)
Derivation: from G165;
KJV Usage: eternal, for ever, everlasting, world (began). G165
Thayer:
1) without beginning and end, that which always has been and always will be
2) without beginning
3) without end, never to cease, everlasting
For Synonyms see entry G5801
