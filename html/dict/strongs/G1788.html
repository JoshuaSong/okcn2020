ἐντρέπω
to turn about
ἐντρέπω to invert, i.e. (figuratively and reflexively) in a good sense, to respect; or in a bad one, to confound
Derivation: from G1722 and the base of G5157;
KJV Usage: regard, (give) reference, shame. G1722 G5157
Thayer:
1) to shame one
2) to be ashamed
3) to reverence a person
4) to turn about
