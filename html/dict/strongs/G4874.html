συναναμίγνυμι
to mix up together
συναναμίγνυμι to mix up together, i.e. (figurative) associate with
Derivation: from G4862 and a compound of G303 and G3396;
KJV Usage: (have, keep) company (with). G4862 G303 G3396
Thayer:
1) to mix up together
2) to keep company with, be intimate with one
