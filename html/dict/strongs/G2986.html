λαμπρός
bright, brilliant, radiant
λαμπρός radiant; by analogy, limpid; figuratively, magnificent or sumptuous (in appearance)
Derivation: from the same as G2985;
KJV Usage: bright, clear, gay, goodly, gorgeous, white. G2985
Thayer:
1) shining
1a) brilliant
1b) clear, transparent
2) splendid, magnificent
2a) splendid things i.e. luxuries or elegancies in dress or style

  • Gorgeous, Gorgeously
  • Sumptuous, Sumptuously
