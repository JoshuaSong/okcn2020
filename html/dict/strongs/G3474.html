μωρός
dull, sluggish, stupid
μωρός dull or stupid (as if shut up), i.e. heedless, (morally) blockhead, (apparently) absurd
Derivation: probably from the base of G3466;
KJV Usage: fool(-ish, X -ishness). G3466
Thayer:
1) foolish
2) impious, godless
