διαλέγομαι to say thoroughly, i.e. discuss (in argument or exhortation)
Derivation: middle voice from G1223 and G3004;
KJV Usage: dispute, preach (unto), reason (with), speak. G1223 G3004
Thayer:
1) to think different things with one's self, mingle thought with thought
1a) to ponder, revolve in mind
2) to converse, discourse with one, argue, discuss
