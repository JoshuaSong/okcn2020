Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

לעדּן
Laadan = "put in order"
1. an Ephraimite ancestor of Joshua, son of Nun
2. a Levite son of Gershom; also 'Libni'
Origin: from the same as H3935
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
