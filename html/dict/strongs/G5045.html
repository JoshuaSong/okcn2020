τέκτων
any worker in wood
τέκτων an artificer (as producer of fabrics), i.e. (specially), a craftsman in wood
Derivation: from the base of G5098;
KJV Usage: carpenter. G5098
Thayer:
1) a worker in wood, a carpenter, joiner, builder
1a) a ship's carpenter or builder
2) any craftsman, or workman
2a) the art of poetry, maker of songs
3) a planner, contriver, plotter
3a) an author
