φαρμακεύς
a poisoner, sorcerer
φαρμακεύς a druggist ("pharmacist") or poisoner, i.e. (by extension) a magician
Derivation: from φάρμακον (a drug, i.e. spell-giving potion);
KJV Usage: sorcerer.
Thayer:
1) one who prepares or uses magical remedies
2) sorcerer
