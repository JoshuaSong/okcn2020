נבית נביות
ne bâyôth nebâyôth
neb-aw-yoth', neb-aw-yoth'
Feminine plural from H5107; fruitfulnesses; Nebajoth, a son of Ishmael, and the country settled by him
KJV Usage: Nebaioth, Nebajoth.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נבית נביות
Nebaioth or Nebajoth = "heights"
1. a son of Ishmael
2. people descended from 1 called the Nabateans with their capital at Petra
Origin: from H5107
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
