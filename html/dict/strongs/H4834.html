מרץ
mârats
maw-rats'
A primitive root; properly to press, that is, (figuratively) to be pungent or vehement; to irritate
KJV Usage: embolden, be forcible, grievous, sore.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מרץ

1. to be or make sick
a. (Niphal) to be grievous or painful
b. (Hiphil) to make sick
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1245
Parts of Speech: Verb
