ἀσχήμων
misshapen, unseemly, shameful
ἀσχήμων properly, shapeless, i.e. (figuratively) inelegant
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G2192 (in the sense of its congener G4976);
KJV Usage: uncomely. G1 G2192 G4976
Thayer:
1) deformed
2) indecent, unseemly
