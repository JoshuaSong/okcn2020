רבּה
rabbâh
rab-baw'
Feminine of H7227; great; Rabbah, the name of two places in Palestine, East and West
KJV Usage: Rabbah, Rabbath.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

רבּה
Rabbath = "great"
1. the capital city of the Ammonites located east of the Jordan
2. a town in Judah; site uncertain
Origin: fem. of H7227
TWOT: None
