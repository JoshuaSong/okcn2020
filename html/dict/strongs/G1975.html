ἐπιπορεύομαι
to travel, march to, march over
ἐπιπορεύομαι to journey further, i.e. travel on (reach)
Derivation: from G1909 and G4198;
KJV Usage: come. G1909 G4198
Thayer:
1) to go or journey to
2) to go to, traverse
2a) regions, cities
3) to make a hostile inroad, overrun, march over
