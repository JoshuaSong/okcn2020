ἐξανίστημι
to raise up: to make one rise
ἐξανίστημι objectively, to produce, i.e. (figuratively) beget; subjectively, to arise, i.e. (figuratively) object
Derivation: from G1537 and G450;
KJV Usage: raise (rise) up. G1537 G450
Thayer:
1) to make to rise up, to rise up, to produce
2) to rise (in an assembly to speak)
