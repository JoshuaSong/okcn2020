בּזז
bâzaz
baw-zaz'
A primitive root; to plunder
KJV Usage: catch, gather, (take) for a prey, rob (-ber), spoil, take (away, spoil), X utterly.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּזז

1. to spoil, plunder, prey upon, seize
a. (Qal) to spoil, plunder, despoil
b. (Niphal) to be spoiled, plundered
c. (Pual) to be taken as spoil
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 225
Parts of Speech: Verb
