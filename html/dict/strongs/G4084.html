πιάζω to squeeze, i.e. seize (gently by the hand (press), or officially (arrest), or in hunting (capture))
Derivation: probably another form of G971;
KJV Usage: apprehend, catch, lay hand on, take. Compare G4085. G971 G4085
Thayer:
1) to lay hold of
2) to take, capture
2a) of fishes
3) to apprehend
3a) of a man, in order to imprison him
