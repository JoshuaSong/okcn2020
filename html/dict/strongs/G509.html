ἄνωθεν
from above, from on high
ἄνωθεν from above; by analogy, from the first; by implication, anew
Derivation: from G507;
KJV Usage: from above, again, from the beginning (very first), the top. G507
Thayer:
1) from above, from a higher place
1a) of things which come from heaven or God
2) from the first, from the beginning, from the very first
3) anew, over again
