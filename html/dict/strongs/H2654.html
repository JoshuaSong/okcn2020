חפץ
châphêts
khaw-fates'
A primitive root; properly to incline to; by implication (literally but rarely) to bend; figuratively to be pleased with, desire
KJV Usage: X any at all, (have, take) delight, desire, favour, like, move, be (well) pleased, have pleasure, will, would.
