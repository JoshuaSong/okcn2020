אבל
'ăbâl
ab-awl'
Apparently from H56 through the idea of negation; nay, that is, truly or yet
KJV Usage: but, indeed, nevertheless, verily.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אבל

1. truly, verily, surely
2. but, however, howbeit
3. contrariwise, nay rather (neg.)
Origin: apparently from H56 through the idea of negation
TWOT: 8
Parts of Speech: Adverb
