ὑγιής
sound, healthy, hearty, sound in
ὑγιής healthy, i.e. well (in body); figuratively, true (in doctrine)
Derivation: from the base of G837;
KJV Usage: sound, whole. G837
Thayer:
1) sound
1a) of a man who is sound in body
2) to make one whole i.e. restore him to health
3) metaph. teaching which does not deviate from the truth
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Sound (Adjective), be Sound
  • Whole (made), Wholly, Wholesome
