Ματθάτ Matthat (i.e. Mattithjah), the name of two Israelites
Derivation: probably a shortened form of G3161;
KJV Usage: Mathat. G3161
Thayer:
1) a son of Levi, in the genealogy of Christ
2) grandfather of the Mary
Literal: Matthat = "gift of God"
