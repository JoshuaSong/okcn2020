συνείδησις
self-consciousness: conscience
συνείδησις co-perception, i.e. moral consciousness
Derivation: from a prolonged form of G4894;
KJV Usage: conscience. G4894
Thayer:
1) the consciousness of anything
2) the soul as distinguishing between what is morally good and bad, prompting to do the former and shun the latter, commending one, condemning the other
2a) the conscience
