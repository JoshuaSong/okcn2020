ζημιόω
to cause loss
ζημιόω to injure, i.e. (reflexively or passively) to experience detriment
Derivation: from G2209;
KJV Usage: be cast away, receive damage, lose, suffer loss. G2209
Thayer:
1) to affect with damage, do damage to
2) to sustain damage, to receive injury, suffer loss
