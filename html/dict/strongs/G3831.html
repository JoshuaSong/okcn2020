πανήγυρις
a general
πανήγυρις a mass-meeting, i.e. (figuratively) universal companionship
Derivation: from G3956 and a derivative of G58;
KJV Usage: general assembly. G3956 G58
Thayer:
1) a festal gathering of the whole people to celebrate public games or other solemnities
2) a public festal assembly
