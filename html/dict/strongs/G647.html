ἀποστάσιον properly, something separative, i.e. (specially) divorce
Derivation: neuter of a (presumed) adjective from a derivative of G868;
KJV Usage: (writing of) divorcement. G868
Thayer:
1) divorce, repudiation
2) a bill of divorce
