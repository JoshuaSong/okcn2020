עקשׁ
‛âqash
aw-kash'
A primitive root; to knot or distort; figuratively to pervert (act or declare perverse)
KJV Usage: make crooked, (prove, that is) perverse (-rt).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עקשׁ

1. to be perverse, twist, pervert, make crooked, prove perverse, declare perverse
a. (Niphal) to be crooked
b. (Piel) to twist, distort, pervert, make crooked
c. (Hiphil) to declare crooked
d. (Qal) perverse
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1684
Parts of Speech: Verb
