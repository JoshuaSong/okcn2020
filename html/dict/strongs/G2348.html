θνήσκω
to die, be dying
θνήσκω to die (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: a strengthened form of a simpler primary θάνω (which is used for it only in certain tenses);
KJV Usage: be dead, die.
Thayer:
1) to die, to be dead
2) metaph. to be spiritually dead
