διαλαλέω
to talk over
διαλαλέω to talk throughout a company, i.e. converse or (genitive case) publish
Derivation: from G1223 and G2980;
KJV Usage: commune, noise abroad. G1223 G2980
Thayer:
1) to converse together, to talk with
