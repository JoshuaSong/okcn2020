συνεφίστημι
to set on the watch together, make attentive
συνεφίστημι to stand up together, i.e. to resist (or assault) jointly
Derivation: from G4862 and G2186;
KJV Usage: rise up together. G4862 G2186
Thayer:
1) to place over or appoint together
2) to rise up together
2a) against one
