ἄσβεστος
unquenchable, inextinguishable
ἄσβεστος not extinguished, i.e. (by implication) perpetual
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4570;
KJV Usage: not to be quenched, unquenchable. G1 G4570
Thayer:
1) unquenched, unquenchable
1a) of eternal hell fire to punish the damned
