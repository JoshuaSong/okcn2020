καταστέλλω
to put in order, arrange
καταστέλλω to put down, i.e. quell
Derivation: from G2596 and G4724;
KJV Usage: appease, quiet. G2596 G4724
Thayer:
1) to send or put down, to lower
2) to put or keep down one who is roused or incensed, to repress, restrain, appease, quiet
