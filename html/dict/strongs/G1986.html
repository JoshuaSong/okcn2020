ἐπισπάομαι to draw over, i.e. (with G203 implied) efface the mark of circumcision (by recovering with the foreskin)
Derivation: from G1909 and G4685;
KJV Usage: become uncircumcised. G1909 G4685 G203
Thayer:
1) to draw on (let him not draw on his foreskin)From the days of Antiochus Epiphanes [B.C. 175-164] down, there had been Jews who, in order to conceal from heathen persecutors or scoffers the external sign of their nationality, circumcision, sought artificially to compel nature to reproduce the prepuce, by extending or drawing forward with an iron instrument the remnant of it still left, so as to cover the glands.
