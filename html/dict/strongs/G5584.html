ψηλαφάω
to feel
ψηλαφάω to manipulate, i.e. verify by contact; figuratively, to search for
Derivation: from the base of G5567 (compare G5586);
KJV Usage: feel after, handle, touch. G5567 G5586
Thayer:
1) to handle, touch and feel
2) metaph. mentally to seek after tokens of a person or a thing
