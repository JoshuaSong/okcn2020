εὐσεβέω
to live
εὐσεβέω to be pious, i.e. (towards God) to worship, or (towards parents) to respect (support)
Derivation: from G2152;
KJV Usage: show piety, worship. G2152
Thayer:
1) to act piously or reverently
1a) towards God, one's country, magistrates, relations, and all to whom dutiful regard or reverence is due
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Piety (to shew)
  • Worship (Verb and Noun), Worshiping
