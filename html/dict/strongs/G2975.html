λαγχάνω
to obtain by lot, by fate, by the will of the gods
λαγχάνω to lot, i.e. determine (by implication, receive) especially by lot
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary verb, which is only used as an alternate in certain tenses;
KJV Usage: his lot be, cast lots, obtain.
Thayer:
1) to obtain by lot
1a) to receive by divine allotment, obtain
2) to cast lots, determine by lot
