αὐξάνω
to make large, increase, augment
αὐξάνω to grow ("wax"), i.e. enlarge (literal or figurative, active or passive)
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary verb;
KJV Usage: grow (up), (give the) increase.
Thayer:
1) to cause to grow, augment
2) to increase, become greater
3) to grow, increase
3a) of plants
3b) of infants
3c) of a multitude of people
3d) of inward Christian growth
