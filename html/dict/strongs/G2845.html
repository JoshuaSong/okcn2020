κοίτη
the marriage-bed
κοίτη a couch; by extension, cohabitation; by implication, the male sperm
Derivation: from G2749;
KJV Usage: bed, chambering, X conceive. G2749
Thayer:
1) a place for laying down, resting, sleeping in
1a) a bed, couch
2) the marriage bed
2a) of adultery
3) cohabitation, whether lawful or unlawful
3a) sexual intercourse
