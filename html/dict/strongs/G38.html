ἁγιασμός
consecration, sanctification
ἁγιασμός properly, purification, i.e. (the state) purity; concretely (by Hebraism) a purifier
Derivation: from G37;
KJV Usage: holiness, sanctification. G37
Thayer:
1) consecration, purification
2) the effect of consecration
2a) sanctification of heart and life
