יעבּץ
ya‛bêts
yah-bates'
From an unused root probably meaning to grieve; sorrowful; Jabets, the name of an Israelite, and also of a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Jabez.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יעבּץ
Jabez = "sorrow" n pr m
1. the head of a Calebite family n pr loc
2. a town in Judah apparently near Bethlehem
Origin: from an unused root probably meaning to grieve
TWOT: None
