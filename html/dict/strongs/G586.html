ἀποδεκατόω
to tithe, pay tithes of
ἀποδεκατόω to tithe (as debtor or creditor)
Derivation: from G575 and G1183;
KJV Usage: (give, pay, take) tithe. G575 G1183
Thayer:
1) to give, pay a tithe of anything
2) to exact receive a tenth from anyone
