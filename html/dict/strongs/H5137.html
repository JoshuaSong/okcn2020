נזה
nâzâh
naw-zaw'
A primitive root; to spirt, that is, besprinkle (especially in expiation)
KJV Usage: sprinkle.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נזה

1. to spurt, spatter, sprinkle
a. (Qal) to spurt, spatter
b. (Hiphil) to cause to spurt, sprinkle upon
2. to spring, leap
a. (Hiphil) to cause to leap, startle
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1335,1336
Parts of Speech: Verb
