late
ὄψιος late; feminine (as noun) afternoon (early eve) or nightfall (later eve)
Derivation: from G3796;
KJV Usage: even(-ing, (-tide)). G3796
Thayer:
1) late
2) evening
2a) either from three to six o'clock p.m.
2b) from six o'clock p.m. to the beginning of night
