σωτήριον defender or (by implication) defence
Derivation: neuter of the same as G4991 as (properly, concretely) noun;
KJV Usage: salvation. G4991
Thayer:
1) saving, bringing salvation
2) he who embodies this salvation, or through whom God is about to achieve it
3) the hope of (future) salvation
