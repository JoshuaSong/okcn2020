ἀποδιορίζω
to mark off by dividing, to separate
ἀποδιορίζω to disjoin (by a boundary, figuratively, a party)
Derivation: from G575 and a compound of G223 and G3724;
KJV Usage: separate. G575 G223 G3724
Thayer:
1) to disjoin, part, separate from another
2) making divisions or separations
