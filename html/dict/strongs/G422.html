ἀνεπαίσχυντος
having no cause for shame
ἀνεπαίσχυντος not ashamed, i.e. irreprehensible:
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a compound of G1909 and G153;
KJV Usage: that needeth not to be ashamed. G1 G1909 G153
Thayer:
1) having no cause to be ashamed
