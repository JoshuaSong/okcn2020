ἀλλοτριεπίσκοπος overseeing others' affairs, i.e. a meddler (specially, in Gentile customs)
Derivation: from G245 and G1985;
KJV Usage: busybody in other men's matters. G245 G1985
Thayer:
1) one who takes the supervision of affairs pertaining to others and in no wise to himself, a meddler in other men's affairs
