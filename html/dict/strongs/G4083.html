πῆχυς
the fore-arm
πῆχυς the fore-arm, i.e. (as a measure) a cubit
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: cubit.
Thayer:
1) a cubit
2) a measure of length equal to distance from the joint of the elbow to the tip of the middle finger (i.e. about
18 inches, (.5 m) but its precise length varied and is disputed)
