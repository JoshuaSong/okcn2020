Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּמן תּימן
Teman = "south" n pr m
1. son of Eliphaz, grandson of Esau, and one of the dukes of Edom
2. the tribe descended from 1 noted for the wisdom of its people n pr loc
3. the region occupied by the descendants of 1, located east of Idumea
Origin: the same as H8486
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech:
