θυμομαχέω
to fight desperately
θυμομαχέω to be in a furious fight, i.e. (figuratively) to be exasperated
Derivation: from a presumed compound of G2372 and G3164;
KJV Usage: be highly displeased. G2372 G3164
Thayer:
1) to carry on war with great animosity
2) to be very angry, be exasperated
