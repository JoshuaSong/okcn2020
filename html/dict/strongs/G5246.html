ὑπέρογκος
of excessive bulk, swollen to a great size
ὑπέρογκος bulging over, i.e. (figuratively) insolent
Derivation: from G5228 and G3591;
KJV Usage: great swelling. G5228 G3591
Thayer:
1) overswollen
2) metaph. immoderate, extravagant
