חזה חזא
chăzâ' chăzâh
khaz-aw', khaz-aw'
(Chaldee); corresponding to H2372; to gaze upon; mentally to dream, be usual (that is, seem)
KJV Usage: behold, have [a dream], see, be wont.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חזה חזא

1. to see, behold
a. (P'al)
1. to see
2. to see, behold, witness
3. to behold (in a dream or vision)
4. customary, seemly (passive)
Origin: corresponding to H2372
TWOT: 2725
Parts of Speech: Verb
