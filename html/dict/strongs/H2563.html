חמר
chômer
kho'-mer
From H2560; properly a bubbling up, that is, of water, a wave; of earth, mire or clay (cement); also a heap; hence a chomer or dry measure
KJV Usage: clay, heap, homer, mire, motion, mortar.

c. mire
2. heap
a. swelling, surging (of water)
