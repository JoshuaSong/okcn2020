ἀφικνέομαι
to come to
ἀφικνέομαι to go (i.e. spread) forth (by rumor)
Derivation: from G575 and the base of G2425;
KJV Usage: come abroad. G575 G2425
Thayer:
1) to come from a place
2) to come to, arrive
