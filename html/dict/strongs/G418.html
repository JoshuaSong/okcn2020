ἀνένδεκτος
impossible
ἀνένδεκτος unadmitted, i.e. (by implication) not supposable
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of the same as G1735;
KJV Usage: impossible. G1 G1735
Thayer:
1) that cannot be admitted, inadmissible, unallowable, improper
