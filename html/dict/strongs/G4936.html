συντρέχω
to run together so as to meet, to encounter
συντρέχω to rush together (hastily assemble) or headlong (figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and G5143 (including its alternate);
KJV Usage: run (together, with). G4862 G5143
Thayer:
1) to run together
1a) of the gathering of a multitude
2) to run along with others
3) metaph.
3a) to rush with, to cast one's self, plunge
