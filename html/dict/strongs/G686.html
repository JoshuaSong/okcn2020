ἄρα a particle denoting an inference more or less decisive (as follows)
Derivation: probably from G142 (through the idea of drawing a conclusion);
KJV Usage: haply, (what) manner (of man), no doubt, perhaps, so be, then, therefore, truly, wherefore. Often used in connection with other particles, especially G1065 or G3767 (after) or G1487 (before). Compare also G687. G142 G1065 G3767 G1487 G687
Thayer:
1) therefore, so then, wherefore
