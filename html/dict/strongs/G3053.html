λογισμός
a counting, reckoning, calculation, computation
λογισμός computation, i.e. (figuratively) reasoning (conscience, conceit)
Derivation: from G3049;
KJV Usage: imagination, thought. G3049
Thayer:
1) a reckoning, computation
2) a reasoning: such as is hostile to the Christian faith
3) a judgment, decision: such as conscience passes
