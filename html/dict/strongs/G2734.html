κατοπτρίζομαι to mirror oneself, i.e. to see reflected (figuratively)
Derivation: middle voice from a compound of G2596 and a derivative of G3700 (compare G2072);
KJV Usage: behold as in a glass. G2596 G3700 G2072
Thayer:
1) to show in a mirror, to make to reflect, to mirror
2) to look at one's self in a mirror
3) to behold one's self in a mirror
