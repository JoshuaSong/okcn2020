πλήθω
to be
πλήθω ; to "fill" (literally or figuratively (imbue, influence, supply)); specially, to fulfil (time)
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary πλέω (which appears only as an alternate in certain tenses and in the reduplicated form pimplemi);
KJV Usage: accomplish, full (…come), furnish.
Thayer:
1) to fill
2) to be fulfilled, to be filled
