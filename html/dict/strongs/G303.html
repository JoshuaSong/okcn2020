ἀνά
up, upon
ἀνά properly, up; but (by extension) used (distributively) severally, or (locally) at (etc.)
Derivation: a primary preposition and adverb;
KJV Usage: and, apiece, by, each, every (man), in, through. In compounds (as a prefix) it often means (by implication) repetition, intensity, reversal, etc.
Thayer:
1) into the midst, in the midst, amidst, among, between
