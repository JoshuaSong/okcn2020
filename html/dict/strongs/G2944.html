κυκλόω
to encircle, surround
κυκλόω to encircle, i.e. surround
Derivation: from the same as G2945;
KJV Usage: compass (about), come (stand) round about. G2945
Thayer:
1) to go around, lead around
2) to surround, encircle, encompass
2a) of persons standing around
2b) of besiegers
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words

  • Compass
  • Stand (Noun and Verb), Standing, Stood
