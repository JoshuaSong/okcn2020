κατακρίνω
to give as sentence against
κατακρίνω to judge against, i.e. sentence
Derivation: from G2596 and G2919;
KJV Usage: condemn, damn. G2596 G2919
Thayer:
1) to give judgment against, to judge worthy of punishment
1a) to condemn
1b) by one's good example to render another's wickedness the more evident and censurable
