εὐεργέτης
a well-doer, benefactor
εὐεργέτης a worker of good, i.e. (specially) a philanthropist
Derivation: from G2095 and the base of G2041;
KJV Usage: benefactor. G2095 G2041
Thayer:
1) benefactor
2) a title of honour, conferred on such as had done their country service, and upon princes, equivalent to Soter, Pater Patriae
