μεθύω
to be drunken with wine
μεθύω to drink to intoxication, i.e. get drunk
Derivation: from another form of G3178;
KJV Usage: drink well, make (be) drunk(-en). G3178
Thayer:
1) to be drunken
2) metaph. of one who has shed blood or murdered profusely
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Drink (-eth, -er, -ing), Drank
  • Drunk, (-en, be), Drunkard, Drunkenness
