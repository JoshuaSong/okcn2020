ἀναπίπτω
to fall back
ἀναπίπτω to fall back, i.e. lie down, lean back
Derivation: from G303 and G4098;
KJV Usage: lean, sit down (to meat). G303 G4098
Thayer:
1) to lie back, lie down
2) to recline at a table, to sit back
