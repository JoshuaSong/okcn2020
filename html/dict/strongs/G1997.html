ἐπισυναγωγή
a gathering
ἐπισυναγωγή a complete collection; especially a Christian meeting (for worship)
Derivation: from G1996;
KJV Usage: assembling (gathering) together. G1996
Thayer:
1) a gathering together in one place
2) the (religious) assembly (of Christians)
