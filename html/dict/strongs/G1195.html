δεσμεύω
to fetter, put in chains
δεσμεύω to be a binder (captor), i.e. to enchain (a prisoner), to tie on (a load)
Derivation: from a (presumed) derivative of G1196;
KJV Usage: bind. G1196
Thayer:
1) to put in chains
2) to bind up, bind together
