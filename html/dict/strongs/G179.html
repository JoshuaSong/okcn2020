ἀκατάλυτος
indissoluble
ἀκατάλυτος indissoluble, i.e. (figuratively) permanent
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2647;
KJV Usage: endless. G1 G2647
Thayer:
1) indissoluble, not subject to destruction
