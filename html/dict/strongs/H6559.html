Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּרצים
Perazim = "breaches"
1. a mountain in Palestine
a. possibly the same as 'Baal-perazim' which was the scene of a victory of David over the Philistines located in the valley of Rephaim, south of Jerusalem, on the road to Bethlehem
Origin: pl. of H6556
TWOT: None
