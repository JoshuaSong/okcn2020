βέβηλος
allowable to be trodden, permitted to human use
βέβηλος accessible (as by crossing the door-way), i.e. (by implication, of Jewish notions) heathenish, wicked
Derivation: from the base of G939 and βηλός (a threshold);
KJV Usage: profane (person). G939
Thayer:
1) accessible, lawful to be trodden
1a) of places
2) profane
2a) unhallowed, common, public place
2b) of men, ungodly
