ἐνδόμησις
structure
ἐνδόμησις a housing in (residence), i.e. structure
Derivation: from a compound of G1722 and a derivative of the base of G1218;
KJV Usage: building. G1722 G1218
Thayer:
1) that which is build in
1a) the material built into a wall, i.e. of which the wall is composed
1b) a mole built into the sea to form a breakwater, and so construct a harbour
