ἀποτρέπω
to turn
ἀποτρέπω to deflect, i.e. (reflexively) avoid
Derivation: from G575 and the base of G5157;
KJV Usage: turn away. G575 G5157
Thayer:
1) to turn one's self away from
2) to shun, avoid
