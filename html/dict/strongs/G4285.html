προηγέομαι
to go first and lead the way, to be the leader
προηγέομαι to lead the way for others, i.e. show deference
Derivation: from G4253 and G2233;
KJV Usage: prefer. G4253 G2233
Thayer:
1) to go before and show the way, to go before and lead, to go before as a leader
