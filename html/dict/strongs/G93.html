ἀδικία
wrong-doing, injustice
ἀδικία (legal) injustice (properly, the quality, by implication, the act); morally, wrongfulness (of character, life or act)
Derivation: from G94;
KJV Usage: iniquity, unjust, unrighteousness, wrong. G94
Thayer:
1) injustice, of a judge
2) unrighteousness of heart and life
3) a deed violating law and justice, act of unrighteousness

  • Wrong (Noun and Verb), Wrongdoer, Wrongdoing
