ἐπιδίδωμι
to give besides
ἐπιδίδωμι to give over (by hand or surrender)
Derivation: from G1909 and G1325;
KJV Usage: deliver unto, give, let (+ (her drive)), offer. G1909 G1325
Thayer:
1) to hand, give by hand
2) to give over
2a) give up to the power or will of one
