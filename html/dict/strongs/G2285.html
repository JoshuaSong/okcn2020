θάμβος
astonishment, amazement
θάμβος stupefaction (by surprise), i.e. astonishment
Derivation: akin to an obsolete τάφω (to dumbfound);
KJV Usage: X amazed, + astonished, wonder.
Thayer:
1) to render immovable
2) amazement
