καταγγέλλω to proclaim, promulgate
Derivation: from G2596 and the base of G32;
KJV Usage: declare, preach, shew, speak of, teach. G2596 G32
Thayer:
1) to announce, declare, promulgate, make known
2) to proclaim publicly, publish
3) to denounce, report, betray
