εὐγενής
well-born, of noble race, of high descent
εὐγενής well born, i.e. (literally) high in rank, or (figuratively) generous
Derivation: from G2095 and G1096;
KJV Usage: more noble, nobleman. G2095 G1096
Thayer:
1) well born, of a noble family
2) noble minded
