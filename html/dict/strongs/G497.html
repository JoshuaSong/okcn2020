ἀντιστρατεύομαι
to make war against
ἀντιστρατεύομαι (figuratively) to attack, i.e. (by implication) destroy
Derivation: from G473 and G4754;
KJV Usage: war against. G473 G4754
Thayer:
1) to make a military expedition, or take the field, against anyone
2) to oppose, war against
