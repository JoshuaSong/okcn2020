μυκάομαι
to grate, creak
μυκάομαι to bellow (roar)
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of μύζω ( to "moo");
KJV Usage: roar.
Thayer:
1) to low, bellow, properly of horned cattle
2) to roar, of a lion
