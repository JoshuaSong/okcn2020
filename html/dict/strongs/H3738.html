כּרה
kârâh
kaw-raw'
A primitive root; properly to dig; figuratively to plot; generally to bore or open
KJV Usage: dig, X make (a banquet), open.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּרה

1. to dig, excavate, dig through
a. (Qal) to dig
b. (Niphal) to be dug
2. (Qal) to give a banquet or feast
3. (Qal) to get by trade, trade, buy
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1033,1034,1035
Parts of Speech: Verb
