ἀπώλεια
destruction
ἀπώλεια ruin or loss (physical, spiritual or eternal)
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G622;
KJV Usage: damnable(-nation), destruction, die, perdition, X perish, pernicious ways, waste. G622
Thayer:
1) destroying, utter destruction
1a) of vessels
2) a perishing, ruin, destruction
2a) of money
2b) the destruction which consists of eternal misery in hell
