בּעל חנן
ba‛al chânân
bah'-al khaw-nawn'
From H1167 and H2608; possessor of grace; Baal Chanan, the name of an Edomite, also of an Israelite
KJV Usage: Baal-hanan.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּעל חנן
Baal-hanan = "Baal is gracious"
1. an early king of Edom
2. one of David's officers, a Gederite, who had the superintendence of his olive and sycamore plantations
Origin: from H1167 and H2603
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
