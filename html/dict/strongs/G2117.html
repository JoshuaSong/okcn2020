straight
εὐθύς straight, i.e. (literally) level, or (figuratively) true; adverbially (of time) at once
Derivation: perhaps from G2095 and G5087;
KJV Usage: anon, by and by, forthwith, immediately, straightway. G2095 G5087
Thayer:
1) straight, level
2) straight forward, upright, true, sincere
3) straightway, immediately, forthwith
