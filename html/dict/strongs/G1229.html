διαγγέλλω
to give notice by a messenger, to send as a message
διαγγέλλω to herald thoroughly
Derivation: from G1223 and the base of G32;
KJV Usage: declare, preach, signify. G1223 G32
Thayer:
1) to carry a message through, announce everywhere, through places, through assemblies of men etc.
2) to publish abroad, declare

  • Publish
