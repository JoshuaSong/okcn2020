περιΐστημι to stand all around, i.e. (near) to be a bystander, or (aloof) to keep away from
Derivation: from G4012 and G2476;
KJV Usage: avoid, shun, stand by (round about). G4012 G2476
Thayer:
1) to place around one
2) to stand around
2a) to turn one's self about for the purpose of avoiding something
2b) to avoid, shun
