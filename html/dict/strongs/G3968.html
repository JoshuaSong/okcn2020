πατρίς
of one's fathers
πατρίς a father-land, i.e. native town; (figuratively) heavenly home
Derivation: from G3902;
KJV Usage: (own) country. G3902
Thayer:
1) one's native country
1a) one's fatherland, one's own country, a fixed abode or home
1b) one's own native place i.e. a city
