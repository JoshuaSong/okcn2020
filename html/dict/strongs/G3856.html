παραδειγματίζω
to make an example of
παραδειγματίζω to show alongside (the public), i.e. expose to infamy
Derivation: from G3844 and G1165;
KJV Usage: make a public example, put to an open shame. G3844 G1165
Thayer:
1) to set forth as a public example, make an example of
1a) in a bad sense
1a1) to hold up to infamy
1a2) to expose to public disgrace
