συζεύγνυμι
to yoke together, couple
συζεύγνυμι to yoke together, i.e. (figuratively) conjoin (in marriage)
Derivation: from G4862 and the base of G2201;
KJV Usage: join together. G4862 G2201
Thayer:
1) to fasten to one yoke, yoke together
2) to join together unite
2a) of the marriage tie
