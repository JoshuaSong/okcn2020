ἀναπαύω
to make to cease, to stop
ἀναπαύω (reflexively) to repose (literally or figuratively (be exempt), remain); by implication, to refresh
Derivation: from G303 and G3973;
KJV Usage: take ease, refresh, (give, take) rest. G303 G3973
Thayer:
1) to cause or permit one to cease from any movement or labour in order to recover and collect his strength
2) to give rest, refresh, to give one's self rest, take rest
3) to keep quiet, of calm and patient expectation
