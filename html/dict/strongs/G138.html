αἱρέομαι to take for oneself, i.e. to prefer
Derivation: probably akin to G142;
KJV Usage: choose. Some of the forms are borrowed from a cognate ἕλλομαι ; which is otherwise obsolete. G142
Thayer:
1) to take for oneself, to prefer, choose
2) to choose by vote, elect to office
