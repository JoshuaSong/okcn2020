ἑτεροζυγέω
to be yoked in unequal partnership
ἑτεροζυγέω to yoke up differently, i.e. (figuratively) to associate discordantly
Derivation: from a compound of G2087 and G2218;
KJV Usage: unequally yoke together with. G2087 G2218
Thayer:
1) to come under an unequal or different yoke, to be unequally yoked
1a) to have fellowship with one who is not an equal: 2Cor 6:14, where the apostle is forbidding Christians to have intercourse with idolaters
