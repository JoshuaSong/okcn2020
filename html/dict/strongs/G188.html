ἀκμήν adverbially, just now, i.e. still
Derivation: accusative case of a noun ("acme") akin to ἀκή (a point) and meaning the same;
KJV Usage: yet.
Thayer:
1) a point
2) extremity, climax, acme, highest degree
3) the present time
