ὑπογραμμός an underwriting, i.e. copy for imitation (figuratively)
Derivation: from a compound of G5259 and G1125;
KJV Usage: example. G5259 G1125
Thayer:
1) a writing copy, including all the letters of the alphabet, given to beginners as an aid in learning to draw them
2) an example set before one
