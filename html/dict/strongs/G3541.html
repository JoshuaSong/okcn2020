νόθος
a bastard, baseborn child
νόθος a spurious or illegitimate son
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: bastard.
Thayer:
1) illegitimate, bastard
2) one born, not in lawful wedlock, but of a concubine or female slave
