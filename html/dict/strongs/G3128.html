Μανασσῆς Mannasses (i.e. Menashsheh), an Israelite
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H4519);
KJV Usage: Manasses. H4519
Thayer:
1) the first born son of Joseph
2) the son of Hezekiah, king of Judah
Literal: Manasseh = "forgetting"
