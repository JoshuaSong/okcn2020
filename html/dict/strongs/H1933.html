הוה הוא
hâvâ' hâvâh
haw-vaw', haw-vaw'
A primitive root (compare H183, H1961) supposed to mean properly to breathe; to be (in the sense of existence)
KJV Usage: be, X have.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

הוה הוא

1. Qal)
a. to fall
b. to be, become, exist, happen
