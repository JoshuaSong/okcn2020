בּעה
bâ‛âh
baw-aw
A primitive root; to gush over, that is, to swell; (figuratively) to desire earnestly; by implication to ask
KJV Usage: cause, inquire, seek up, swell out, boil.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּעה

1. to seek out, swell, cause to swell, boil up, enquire
a. (Qal)
1. to seek, enquire
2. to cause to boil up
b. (Niphal)
1. to be searched out
2. to be swelling, bulging, swelling out
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 259
Parts of Speech: Verb
