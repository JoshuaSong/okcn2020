δάκνω
to bite
δάκνω to bite, i.e. (figuratively) thwart
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary root;
KJV Usage: bite.
Thayer:
1) to bite with the teeth
2) metaph., to wound the soul, cut lacerate, rend with reproaches
