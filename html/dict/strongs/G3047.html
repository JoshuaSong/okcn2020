λίψ the south(- west) wind (as bringing rain, i.e. (by extension) the south quarter)
Derivation: probably from λείβω (to pour a "libation");
KJV Usage: southwest.
Thayer:
1) the south west wind
2) the quarter of the heavens whence the south west wind blows
