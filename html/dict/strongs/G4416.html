πρωτότοκος
first-born
πρωτότοκος first-born (usually as noun, literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4413 and the alternate of G5088;
KJV Usage: firstbegotten(-born). G4413 G5088
Thayer:
1) the firstborn
1a) of man or beast
1b) of Christ, the first born of all creation
