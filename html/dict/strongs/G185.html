ἀκέραιος
unmixed, pure in blood
ἀκέραιος unmixed, i.e. (figuratively) innocent
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G2767;
KJV Usage: harmless, simple. G1 G2767
Thayer:
1) unmixed, pure as in wines or metals
2) of the mind, without a mixture of evil, free from guile, innocent, simple
