τρίβολος
a caltrop
τρίβολος properly, a crow-foot (three-pronged obstruction in war), i.e. (by analogy) a thorny plant (caltrop)
Derivation: from G5140 and G956;
KJV Usage: brier, thistle. G5140 G956
Thayer:
1) a thistle, a prickly wild plant, hurtful to other plants
