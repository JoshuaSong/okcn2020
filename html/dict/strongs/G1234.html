διαγογγύζω
to murmur among themselves
διαγογγύζω to complain throughout a crowd
Derivation: from G1223 and G1111;
KJV Usage: murmur. G1223 G1111
Thayer:
1) to murmur
1a) either of a whole crowd, or among one another
1b) always used of many indignantly complaining
