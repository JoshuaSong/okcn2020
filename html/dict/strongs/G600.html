ἀποκαθίστημι
to re-establish, restore, reinstate
ἀποκαθίστημι to reconstitute (in health, home or organization)
Derivation: from G575 and G2525;
KJV Usage: restore (again). G575 G2525
Thayer:
1) to restore to its former state
2) to be in its former state
