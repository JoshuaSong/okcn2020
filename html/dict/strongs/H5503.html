סחר
sâchar
saw-khar'
A primitive root; to travel round (specifically as a pedlar); intensively to palpitate
KJV Usage: go about, merchant (-man), occupy with, pant, trade, traffick.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

סחר

1. to go around, go about, travel about in, go about in trade
a. (Qal)
1. to go about to and fro (in business)
2. trader, trafficker (participle)
b. (Pilpel) to palpitate
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1486
Parts of Speech: Verb
