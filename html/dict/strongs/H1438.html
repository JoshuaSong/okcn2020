גּדע
gâda‛
gaw-dah'
A primitive root; to fell a tree; generally to destroy anything
KJV Usage: cut (asunder, in sunder, down, off), hew down.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּדע

1. to cut, hew, chop, cut down, hew down, hew off, cut off, cut in two, shave off
a. (Qal) to hew, chop in two
b. (Niphal) to be chopped off, be hewn off
c. (Piel) to cut off or down in two, hew off or down in two
d. (Pual) to chop down, hew down
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 316
Parts of Speech: Verb
