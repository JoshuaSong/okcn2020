Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּלהה
Bilhah = "troubled" n pr f
1. Rachel's handmaid whom she gave to Jacob as a concubine, mother to two of Jacob's children, Dan and Naphtali n pr loc
2. a city in Simeon
Origin: from H1089
