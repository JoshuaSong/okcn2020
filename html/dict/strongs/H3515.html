כּבד
kâbêd
kaw-bade'
From H3513; heavy; figuratively in a good sense (numerous) or in a bad sense (severe, difficult, stupid)
KJV Usage: (so) great, grievous, hard (-ened), (too) heavy (-ier), laden, much, slow, sore, thick.
