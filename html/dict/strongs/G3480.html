Ναζωραῖος a Nazoræan, i.e. inhabitant of Nazareth; by extension, a Christian
Derivation: from G3478;
KJV Usage: Nazarene, of Nazareth. G3478
Thayer:
1) an inhabitant of Nazareth
2) a title given to Jesus in the NT
3) a name given to Christians by the Jews, Ac. 24:5
Literal: Nazarite = "one separated"
