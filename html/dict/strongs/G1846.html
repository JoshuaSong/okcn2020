ἐξορύσσω
to dig out
ἐξορύσσω to dig out, i.e. (by extension) to extract (an eye), remove (roofing)
Derivation: from G1537 and G3736;
KJV Usage: break up, pluck out. G1537 G3736
Thayer:
1) to dig out, to pluck out (the eyes)
2) to dig through
