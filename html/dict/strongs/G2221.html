ζωγρέω
to take alive, take captive
ζωγρέω to take alive (make a prisoner of war), i.e. (figuratively) to capture or ensnare
Derivation: from the same as G2226 and G64;
KJV Usage: take captive, catch. G2226 G64
Thayer:
1) to take alive
2) to take, catch, capture
