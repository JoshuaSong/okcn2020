τρίζω
to utter a shrill cry, to scream, cry
τρίζω to creak (squeak), i.e. (by analogy) to grate the teeth (in frenzy)
Derivation: apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: gnash.
Thayer:
1) to squeak, make a shrill cry
2) to gnash or grind one's teeth
