συλαγωγέω
to carry off as booty, lead captive
συλαγωγέω to lead away as booty, i.e. (figuratively) seduce
Derivation: from the base of G4813 and (the reduplicated form of) G71;
KJV Usage: spoil. G4813 G71
Thayer:
1) to carry off booty
1a) to carry one off as a captive (and slave)
1b) to lead away from the truth and subject to one's sway
