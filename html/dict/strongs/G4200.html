πορισμός
a providing, procuring
πορισμός furnishing (procuring), i.e. (by implication) money-getting (acquisition)
Derivation: from a derivative of πόρος (a way, i.e. means);
KJV Usage: gain.
Thayer:
1) acquisition, gain
2) source of gain
