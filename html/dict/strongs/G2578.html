to bend, curve
κάμπτω to bend
Derivation: apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: bow.
Thayer:
1) to bend, bow, the knee (the knees)
1a) to one
1a1) in honour of one
1a2) in religious veneration
1b) used of worshippers
2) to bow one's self
