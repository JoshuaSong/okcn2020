ἀπειπόμην to say off for oneself, i.e. disown
Derivation: reflexive past of a compound of G575 and G2036;
KJV Usage: renounce. G575 G2036
Thayer:
1) to speak out, set forth, declare
2) to forbid
3) to give up, renounce
