עפל
‛âphal
aw-fal'
A primitive root; to swell; figuratively be elated
KJV Usage: be lifted up, presume.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עפל

1. to lift up, swell, be lifted up
a. (Pual) to swell
b. (Hiphil) to be bold, be swelled
2. to presume, be heedless
a. (Hiphil) to be heedless, show heedlessness
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1662,1663
Parts of Speech: Verb
