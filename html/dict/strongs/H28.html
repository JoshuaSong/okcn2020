אבידע
'ăbı̂ydâ‛
ab-ee-daw'
From H1 and H3045; father of knowledge (that is knowing); Abida, a son of Abraham by Keturah
KJV Usage: Abida, Abidah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אבידע
Abida or Abidah = "my father knows"
1. fourth son of Midian and grandson of Abraham by his wife Keturah (after Sarah died)
Origin: from H1 and H3045
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
