περίκειμαι
to lie round about
περίκειμαι to lie all around, i.e. inclose, encircle, hamper (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4012 and G2749;
KJV Usage: be bound (compassed) with, hang about. G4012 G2749
Thayer:
1) to lie around
2) to be compassed with, have round one
