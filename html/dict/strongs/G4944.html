συνωδίνω
to be in travail together
συνωδίνω to have (parturition) pangs in company (concert, simultaneously) with, i.e. (figuratively) to sympathize (in expectation of relief from suffering)
Derivation: from G4862 and G5605;
KJV Usage: travail in pain together. G4862 G5605
Thayer:
1) to feel the pains of travail with, be in travail together
2) metaph. to undergo agony (like a woman in childbirth) along with
