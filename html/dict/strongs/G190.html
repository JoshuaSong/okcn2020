ἀκολουθέω
to follow
ἀκολουθέω properly, to be in the same way with, i.e. to accompany (specially, as a disciple)
Derivation: from G1 (as a particle of union) and κέλευθος (a road);
KJV Usage: follow, reach. G1
Thayer:
1) to follow one who precedes, join him as his attendant, accompany him
2) to join one as a disciple, become or be his disciple
2a) side with his party
