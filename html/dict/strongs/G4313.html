προπορεύομαι
to go before
προπορεύομαι to precede (as guide or herald)
Derivation: from G4253 and G4198;
KJV Usage: go before. G4253 G4198
Thayer:
1) to send before, to make to precede
2) to go before one
2a) of a leader
2b) of a messenger or a herald
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Go (went), Go onward, etc.
