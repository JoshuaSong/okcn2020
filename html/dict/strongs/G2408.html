Ἱερεμίας Hieremias (i.e. Jermijah), an Israelite
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H3414);
KJV Usage: Jeremiah. H3414
Thayer:
1) the son of Hilkiah of the priests that were in Anathoth. He was called very young (B.C.
626) to the prophetic office, and prophesied 42 years. He probably died in Egypt.
Literal: Jeremiah = "whom Jehovah has appointed"
