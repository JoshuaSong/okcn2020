ἀμήτωρ
without mother, motherless
ἀμήτωρ motherless, i.e. of unknown maternity
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G3384;
KJV Usage: without mother. G1 G3384
Thayer:
1) born without a mother
2) bereft of a mother
3) born of a base or unknown mother
4) unmotherly, not worthy of the name of mother
