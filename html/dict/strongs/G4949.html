Συροφοίνισσα a Syro-phœnician woman, i.e. a female native of Phœnicia in Syria
Derivation: feminine of a compound of G4948 and the same as G5403;
KJV Usage: Syrophenician. G4948 G5403
Thayer:
1) the name of a mixed nation, half Phoenicians and half Syrians
Literal: Syrophenician = "exalted palm"
