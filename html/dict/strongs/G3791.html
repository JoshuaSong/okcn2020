ὀχλέω
to move, disturb
ὀχλέω to mob, i.e. (by implication) to harass
Derivation: from G3793;
KJV Usage: vex. G3793
Thayer:
1) to excite a mob against one
2) to disturb, roll away
3) to trouble, molest
4) be in confusion, in an uproar
5) to be vexed, molested, troubled: by demons
