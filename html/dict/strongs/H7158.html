קרית ספר קרית סנּה
qiryath sannâh qiryath sêpher
keer-yath' san-naw', keer-yath' say'-fer
From H7151 and a simpler feminine from the same as H5577, or (for the second form) H5612; city of branches, or of a book; Kirjath Sannah or Kirjath Sepher, a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Kirjath-sannah, Kirjath-sepher.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קרית ספר קרית סנּה
Kirjath-sepher or Kirjath-sannah = "city of the book"
1. a town in the mountains of Judah to the west of Hebron
Origin: from H7151 and a simpler fem. from the same as H5577, or (for the latter name) H5612
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Neuter
