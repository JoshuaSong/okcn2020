ὑποκρίνομαι
to reply, make answer, answer
ὑποκρίνομαι to decide (speak or act) under a false part, i.e. (figuratively) dissemble (pretend)
Derivation: middle voice from G5259 and G2919;
KJV Usage: feign. G5259 G2919
Thayer:
1) to take up another's statements in reference to what one has decided for one's self
1a) to reply, answer
2) to make answer (speak) on the stage
2a) to impersonate anyone, play a part
3) to simulate, feign, pretend
