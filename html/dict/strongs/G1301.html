διατηρέω
to watch closely, observe
διατηρέω to watch thoroughly, i.e. (positively and transitively) to observe strictly, or (negatively and reflexively) to avoid wholly
Derivation: from G1223 and G5083;
KJV Usage: keep. G1223 G5083
Thayer:
1) to keep continually or carefully
