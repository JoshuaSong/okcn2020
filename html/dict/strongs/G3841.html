παντοκράτωρ
almighty
παντοκράτωρ the all-ruling, i.e. God (as absolute and universal sovereign)
Derivation: from G3956 and G2904;
KJV Usage: Almighty, Omnipotent. G3956 G2904
Thayer:
1) he who holds sway over all things
2) the ruler of all
3) almighty: God
