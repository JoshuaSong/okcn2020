ἐντυγχάνω
to light upon, fall in with, meet with
ἐντυγχάνω to chance upon, i.e. (by implication) confer with; by extension to entreat (in favor or against)
Derivation: from G1722 and G5177;
KJV Usage: deal with, make intercession. G1722 G5177
Thayer:
1) to light upon a person or a thing, fall in with, hit upon, a person or a thing
2) to go to or meet a person, esp. for the purpose of conversation, consultation, or supplication
3) to pray, entreat
4) make intercession for any one
