אזרח
'ezrâch
ez-rawkh'
From H2224 (in the sense of springing up); a spontaneous growth, that is, native (tree or persons)
KJV Usage: bay tree, (home-) born (in the land), of the (one’s own) country (nation).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אזרח

1. a native (one rising from the soil)
a. of man, native Israelites
b. of tree, native (to Israel)
Origin: from H2224 (in the sense of springing up)
TWOT: 580b
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
