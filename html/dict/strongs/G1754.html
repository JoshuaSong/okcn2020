ἐνεργέω
to be in action, to operate
ἐνεργέω to be active, efficient
Derivation: from G1756;
KJV Usage: do, (be) effectual (fervent), be mighty in, shew forth self, work (effectually in). G1756
Thayer:
1) to be operative, be at work, put forth power
1a) to work for one, aid one
2) to effect
3) to display one's activity, show one's self operative
