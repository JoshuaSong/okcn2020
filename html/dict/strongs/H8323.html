שׂרר
śârar
saw-rar'
A primitive root; to have (transitively exercise; reflexively get) dominion
KJV Usage: X altogether, make self a prince, (bear) rule.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׂרר

1. to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over, reign, govern
a. (Qal) to rule over, govern
b. (Hithpael) to lord it over
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2295
Parts of Speech: Verb
