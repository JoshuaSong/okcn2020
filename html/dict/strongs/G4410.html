πρωτοκαθεδρία
the first seat
πρωτοκαθεδρία a sitting first (in the front row), i.e. preeminence in council
Derivation: from G4413 and G2515;
KJV Usage: chief (highest, uppermost) seat. G4413 G2515
Thayer:
1) to sit in the first seat, the first or chief seat
