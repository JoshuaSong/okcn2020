κυλλός
crooked, crippled
κυλλός rocking about, i.e. crippled (maimed, in feet or hands)
Derivation: from the same as G2947;
KJV Usage: maimed. G2947
Thayer:
1) crooked
1a) of the members of the body
1b) injured, disabled
2) maimed, mutilated
