או או
'ô 'av
o, av
The first form is presumed to be the “constructive” or genitival form of the second form which is short for H185; desire (and so probably in Proverbs 31:4); hence (by way of alternative) or, also if
KJV Usage: also, and, either, if, at the least, X nor, or, otherwise, then, whether.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

או או

1. or, rather
a. implying that the latter choice is preferred
b. or if, introducing an example to be seen under a particular principle
c. (in series) either...or, whether...or
d. if perchance
e. except, or else
2. whether, not the least, if, otherwise, also, and, then
Origin: presumed to be the "constructive" or genitival form of 'av {av}; short for H185
