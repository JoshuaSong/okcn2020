Ἔραστος beloved; Erastus, a Christian
Derivation: from ἐράω (to love);
KJV Usage: Erastus.
Thayer:
1) a companion of the apostle Paul
2) the chamberlain or rather the public treasurer, of Corinth
Literal: Erastus = "beloved"
