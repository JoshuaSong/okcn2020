γλωσσόκομον
a case for the mouthpiece of a pipe
γλωσσόκομον properly, a case (to keep mouthpieces of wind-instruments in) i.e. (by extension) a casket or (specially) purse
Derivation: from G1100 and the base of G2889;
KJV Usage: bag. G1100 G2889
Thayer:
1) a case in which to keep mouth-pieces of wind instruments
2) a small box for other uses
2a) esp. a casket, a purse to keep money in
