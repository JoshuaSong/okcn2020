ποταμοφόρητος
carried away by a river
ποταμοφόρητος river-borne, i.e. overwhelmed by a stream
Derivation: from G4215 and a derivative of G5409;
KJV Usage: carried away of the flood. G4215 G5409
Thayer:
1) carried away by a stream (i.e. overwhelmed, drowned in the waters)
