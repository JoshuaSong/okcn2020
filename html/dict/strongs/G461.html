ἀνορθόω
to set up again, restore, rebuild
ἀνορθόω to straighten up
Derivation: from G303 and a derivative of the base of G3717;
KJV Usage: lift (set) up, make straight. G303 G3717
Thayer:
1) to set up, make erect
1a) of a deformed person
2) to rear again, build anew
