συγκεράννυμι
to mix up with, commingle
συγκεράννυμι to commingle, i.e. (figuratively) to combine or assimilate
Derivation: from G4862 and G2767;
KJV Usage: mix with, temper together. G4862 G2767
Thayer:
1) to mix together, commingle
2) to unite
2a) caused the several parts to combine into an organic structure, which is the body
2b) to unite one thing to another
