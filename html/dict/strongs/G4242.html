πρεσβεία
age, seniority
πρεσβεία seniority (eldership), i.e. (by implication) an embassy (concretely, ambassadors)
Derivation: from G4243;
KJV Usage: ambassage, message. G4243
Thayer:
1) age, dignity, right of the first born
2) the business normally intrusted to elders, spec. the office of an ambassador, an embassy
