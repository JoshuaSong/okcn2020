ἀνακαθίζω
to set up
ἀνακαθίζω properly, to set up, i.e. (reflexively) to sit up
Derivation: from G303 and G2523;
KJV Usage: sit up. G303 G2523
Thayer:
1) to raise one's self and sit upright, to sit up, erect
