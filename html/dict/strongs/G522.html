ἀπαίρω
to lift off, carry off, take away, to remove from
ἀπαίρω to lift off, i.e. remove
Derivation: from G575 and G142;
KJV Usage: take (away). G575 G142
Thayer:
1) to lift off, take or carry away
2) to be taken away from anyone
