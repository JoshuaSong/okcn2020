πρόσκαιρος
for a season, temporary
πρόσκαιρος for the occasion only, i.e. temporary
Derivation: from G4314 and G2540;
KJV Usage: dur-(eth) for awhile, endure for a time, for a season, temporal. G4314 G2540
Thayer:
1) for a season
2) enduring only for a while
3) temporary
