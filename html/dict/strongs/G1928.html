ἐπιδιατάσσομαι
to add an order
ἐπιδιατάσσομαι to appoint besides, i.e. supplement (as a codicil)
Derivation: middle voice from G1909 and G1299;
KJV Usage: add to. G1909 G1299
Thayer:
1) to ordain besides, to add something to what has been ordained
