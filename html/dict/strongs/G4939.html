σύντροφος
brought up together with
σύντροφος a fellow-nursling, i.e. comrade
Derivation: from G4862 and G5162 (in a passive sense);
KJV Usage: brought up with. G4862 G5162
Thayer:
1) nourished with one
2) brought up with one
3) companion of one's childhood and youth
