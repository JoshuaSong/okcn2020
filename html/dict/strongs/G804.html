ἀσφαλής
not liable to fall, immoveable, steadfast, firm
ἀσφαλής secure (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and sphallo (to "fail");
KJV Usage: certain(-ty), safe, sure. G1
Thayer:
1) firm (that which can be relied on)
2) certain, true
3) suited to confirm
