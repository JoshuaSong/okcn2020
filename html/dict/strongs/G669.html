ἀποφθέγγομαι
to speak one's opinion plainly
ἀποφθέγγομαι to enunciate plainly, i.e. declare
Derivation: from G575 and G5350;
KJV Usage: say, speak forth, utterance. G575 G5350
Thayer:
1) to speak out, speak forth, pronounce
1a) not a word of everyday speech but one "belonging to dignified and elevated discourse"
