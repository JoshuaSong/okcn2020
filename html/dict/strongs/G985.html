βλαστάνω
to bud, sprout, grow
βλαστάνω to germinate; by implication, to yield fruit
Derivation: from βλαστός (a sprout);
KJV Usage: bring forth, bud, spring (up).
Thayer:
1) to sprout, bud, put forth new leaves
2) to produce
