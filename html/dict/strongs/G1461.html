ἐγκεντρίζω to prick in, i.e. ingraft
Derivation: from G1722 and a derivative of G2759;
KJV Usage: graff in(-to). G1722 G2759
Thayer:
1) to cut into for the sake of inserting a scion
2) to inoculate, ingraft, graft in
