ἀνυπόκριτος
without dissimulation
ἀνυπόκριτος undissembled, i.e. sincere
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5271;
KJV Usage: without dissimulation (hypocrisy), unfeigned. G1 G5271
Thayer:
1) unfeigned, undisguised, sincere
