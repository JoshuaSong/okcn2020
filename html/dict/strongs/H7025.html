קיר חרשׂת קיר חרשׂ
qı̂yr chereś qı̂yr chăreśeth
(keer) kheh'-res, khar-eh'-seth
From H7023 and H2789; fortress of earthenware; Kir-Cheres or Kir-Chares-eth, a place in Moab
KJV Usage: Kir-haraseth, Kir-hareseth, Kir-haresh, Kir-heres.
