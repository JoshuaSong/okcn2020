δημόσιος
belonging to the people
δημόσιος public; (feminine singular dative case as adverb) in public
Derivation: from G1218;
KJV Usage: common, openly, publickly. G1218
Thayer:
1) belonging to the people or state, public
2) publicly, in public places, in full view of all
