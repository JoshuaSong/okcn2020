Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אליסף
Eliasaph = "God has added"
1. a Gadite chief in the wilderness census
2. a head of the Gershonites
Origin: from H410 and H3254
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
