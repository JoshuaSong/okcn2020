παραπίπτω
to fall beside
παραπίπτω to fall aside, i.e. (figuratively) to apostatize
Derivation: from G3844 and G4098;
KJV Usage: fall away. G3844 G4098
Thayer:
1) to fall beside a person or thing
2) to slip aside
2a) to deviate from the right path, turn aside, wander
2b) to error
2c) to fall away (from the true faith): from worship of Jehovah
