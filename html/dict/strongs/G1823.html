ἐξαστράπτω
to flash as with lightning
ἐξαστράπτω to lighten forth, i.e. (figuratively) to be radiant (of very white garments)
Derivation: from G1537 and G797;
KJV Usage: glistening. G1537 G797
Thayer:
1) to send forth lightning, to lighten
2) to flash out like lightning, to shine, be radiant
2a) of garments
