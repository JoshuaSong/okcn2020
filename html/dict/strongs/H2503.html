Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חלץ חלץ
Helez = "he has saved"
1. an Ephraimite, one of the 30 of David's mighty warriors, leader of the 7th monthly course
2. a man of Judah, son of Azariah
Origin: from H2502
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
