מגר מגוּר
mâgûr mâgûr
maw-goor', maw-goor'
From H1481 in the sense of lodging; a temporary abode; by extension a permanent residence
KJV Usage: dwelling, pilgrimage, where sojourn, be a stranger. Compare H4032.
