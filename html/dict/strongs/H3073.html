יהוה שׁלום
ye hôvâh shâlôm
yeh-ho-vaw' shaw-lome'
From H3068 and H7965; Jehovah (is) peace; Jehovah-Shalom, a symbolical name of an altar in Palestine
KJV Usage: Jehovah-shalom.
