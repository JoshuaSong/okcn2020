εὐσχήμων
elegant in figure, mien and bearing, graceful
εὐσχήμων well-formed, i.e. (figuratively) decorous, noble (in rank)
Derivation: from G2095 and G4976;
KJV Usage: comely, honourable. G2095 G4976
Thayer:
1) of elegant figure
1a) shapely, graceful, comely, bearing one's self becomingly in speech or behaviour
2) of good standing
2a) honourable, influential, wealthy, respectable
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Comeliness, Comely
  • Estate, State
  • Honorable, without Honor
