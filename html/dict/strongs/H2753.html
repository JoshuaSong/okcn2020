Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חורי חרי
Hori = "cave dweller"
1. a Horite, son of Lotan, the son of Seir
2. a Simeonite, father of Shaphat
Origin: the same as H2752
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
