ἐπιστομίζω
to curb in
ἐπιστομίζω to put something over the mouth, i.e. (figuratively) to silence
Derivation: from G1909 and G4750;
KJV Usage: stop mouths. G1909 G4750
Thayer:
1) to bridle or stop up the mouth
2) metaph. to stop the mouth, reduce to silence
