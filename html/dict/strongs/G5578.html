ψευδοπροφήτης a spurious prophet, i.e. pretended foreteller or religious impostor
Derivation: from G5571 and G4396;
KJV Usage: false prophet. G5571 G4396
Thayer:
1) one who, acting the part of a divinely inspired prophet, utters falsehoods under the name of divine prophecies
2) a false prophet
