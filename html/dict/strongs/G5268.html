ὑποζύγιον
a beast for the yoke, a beast of burden
ὑποζύγιον an animal under the yoke (draught-beast), i.e. (specially), a donkey
Derivation: neuter of a compound of G5259 and G2218;
KJV Usage: ass. G5259 G2218
Thayer:
1) under the yoke
2) a beast of burden, an ass
2a) the ass was the common animal used by the Orientals on journey and for carrying burdens
