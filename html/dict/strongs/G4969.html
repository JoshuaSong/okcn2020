σφάζω
to slay, slaughter
σφάζω to butcher (especially an animal for food or in sacrifice) or (generally) to slaughter, or (specially), to maim (violently)
Derivation: a primary verb;
KJV Usage: kill, slay, wound.
Thayer:
1) to slay, slaughter, butcher
2) to put to death by violence
3) mortally wounded
