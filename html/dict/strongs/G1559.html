ἐκδιώκω
to chase away, banish
ἐκδιώκω to pursue out, i.e. expel or persecute implacably
Derivation: from G1537 and G1377;
KJV Usage: persecute. G1537 G1377
Thayer:
1) to drive out, banish
2) to pursue
2a) to persecute, oppress with calamities
