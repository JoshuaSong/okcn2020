Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נקיא נקי

1. clean, free from, exempt, clear, innocent
a. free from guilt, clean, innocent
b. free from punishment
c. free or exempt from obligations
2. innocent
Origin: from H5352
TWOT: 1412a,1412b
Parts of Speech: Adjective
