לבט
lâbaṭ
law-bat'
A primitive root; to overthrow; intransitively to fall
KJV Usage: fall.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

לבט

1. to throw down, thrust down, thrust out, thrust away
a. (Niphal) to be thrust down, be thrust away, be thrown down, be cast aside
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1072
