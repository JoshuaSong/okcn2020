ἀτακτέω
to be undisciplined, disorderly
ἀτακτέω to be (i.e. act) irregular
Derivation: from G813;
KJV Usage: behave self disorderly. G813
Thayer:
1) to be disorderly
1a) of soldiers marching out of order or quitting ranks
1b) to be neglectful of duty, to be lawless
1c) to lead a disorderly life
