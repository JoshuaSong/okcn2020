μερίζω
to divide, distribute
μερίζω to part, i.e. (literally) to apportion, bestow, share, or (figuratively) to disunite, differ
Derivation: from G3313;
KJV Usage: deal, be difference between, distribute, divide, give participle G3313
Thayer:
1) to divide
1a) to separate into parts, cut into pieces
1a1) to divide into parties, i.e. be split into factions
1b) to distribute
1b1) a thing among people
1b2) bestow, impart
