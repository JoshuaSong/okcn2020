συμβαίνω
to stand with the feet together
συμβαίνω to walk (figuratively, transpire) together, i.e. concur (take place)
Derivation: from G4862 and the base of G939;
KJV Usage: be(-fall), happen (unto). G4862 G939
Thayer:
1) to walk with the feet near together
2) to come together, meet with one
3) of things which fall out at the same time, to happen, turn out, come to pass
