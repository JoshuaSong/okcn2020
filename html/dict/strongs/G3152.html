μάταιος
vain, empty, idle, trifling, frivolous
μάταιος empty, i.e. (literally) profitless, or (specially), an idol
Derivation: from the base of G3155;
KJV Usage: vain, vanity. G3155
Thayer:
1) devoid of force, truth, success, result
2) useless, of no purpose
