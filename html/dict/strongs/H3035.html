Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ידּו
Iddo = "timely" or "lovely"
1. son of Zechariah, ruler of the tribe of Manasseh east of the Jordan in the time of David
2. a man who took a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
Origin: from H3034
TWOT: None
