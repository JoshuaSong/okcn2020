διασπορά
dispersion
διασπορά dispersion, i.e. (specially and concretely) the (converted) Israelite resident in Gentile countries
Derivation: from G1289;
KJV Usage: (which are) scattered (abroad). G1289
Thayer:
1) a scattering, dispersion
1a) of Israelites dispersed among foreign nations
1b) of the Christians scattered abroad among the Gentiles
