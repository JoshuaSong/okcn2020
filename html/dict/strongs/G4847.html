συμπολίτης
a fellow-citizen
συμπολίτης a native of the same town, i.e. (figuratively) co-religionist (fellow-Christian)
Derivation: from G4862 and G4177;
KJV Usage: fellow- citizen. G4862 G4177
Thayer:
1) possessing the same citizenship with others, a fellow citizen
1a) of Gentiles as received into communion of the saints
1b) of the people consecrated to God
