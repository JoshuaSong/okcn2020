after-thought, repentance
μετάνοια (subjectively) compunction (for guilt, including reformation); by implication, reversal (of (another's) decision)
Derivation: from G3340;
KJV Usage: repentance. G3340
Thayer:
1) a change of mind, as it appears to one who repents, of a purpose he has formed or of something he has done
