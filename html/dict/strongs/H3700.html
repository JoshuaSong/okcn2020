כּסף
kâsaph
kaw-saf'
A primitive root; properly to become pale, that is, (by implication) to pine after; also to fear
KJV Usage: [have] desire, be greedy, long, sore.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּסף

1. to long for, yearn for, long after
a. (Qal) to long for
b. (Niphal)
1. to long for (deeply)
2. being longed for (participle)
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1015
Parts of Speech: Verb
