ἀμίαντος
undefiled
ἀμίαντος unsoiled, i.e. (figuratively) pure
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3392;
KJV Usage: undefiled. G1 G3392
Thayer:
1) not defiled, unsoiled
1a) free from that by which the nature of a thing is deformed and debased, or its force and vigour impaired
