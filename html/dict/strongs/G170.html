ἀκαιρέομαι to be inopportune (for oneself), i.e. to fail of a proper occasion
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a negative particle) and G2540 (meaning unseasonable);
KJV Usage: lack opportunity. G1 G2540
Thayer:
1) to lack opportunity
