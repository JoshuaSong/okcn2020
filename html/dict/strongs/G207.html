Ἀκύλας Akulas, an Israelite
Derivation: probably for Latin aquila (an eagle);
KJV Usage: Aquila.
Thayer:
1) a Jew of Pontus, a tent maker convert to Christ, companion and ally of Paul in propagating Christianity
Literal: Aquila = "an eagle"
