ἄτιμος
unhonoured, dishonoured
ἄτιμος (negatively) unhonoured or (positively) dishonoured
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G5092;
KJV Usage: despised, without honour, less honourable (comparative degree). G1 G5092
Thayer:
1) without honour, unhonoured, dishonoured
2) base, of less esteem
