μεταμέλλομαι to care afterwards, i.e. regret
Derivation: from G3326 and the middle voice of G3199;
KJV Usage: repent (self). G3326 G3199
Thayer:
1) it is a care to one afterwards
1a) it repents one, to repent one's self
