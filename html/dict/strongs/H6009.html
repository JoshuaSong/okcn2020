עמק
‛âmaq
aw-mak'
A primitive root; to be (causatively make) deep (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: (be, have, make, seek) deep (-ly), depth, be profound.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עמק

1. to be deep, be profound, make deep
a. (Qal) to be deep
b. (Hiphil) to make deep, make profound
