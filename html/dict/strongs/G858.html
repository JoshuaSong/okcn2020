ἀφελότης
simplicity
ἀφελότης smoothness, i.e. (figuratively) simplicity
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a negative particle) and φέλλος (in the sense of a stone as stubbing the foot);
KJV Usage: singleness. G1
Thayer:
1) simplicity, singleness
