ἀπιστέω
to be
ἀπιστέω to be unbelieving, i.e. (transitively) disbelieve, or (by implication) disobey
Derivation: from G571;
KJV Usage: believe not. G571
Thayer:
1) to betray a trust, be unfaithful
2) to have no belief, disbelieve
