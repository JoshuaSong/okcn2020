προσαιτέω
to ask besides
προσαιτέω to ask repeatedly (importune), i.e. solicit
Derivation: from G4314 and G154;
KJV Usage: beg. G4314 G154
Thayer:
1) to ask for in addition
2) to approach one with supplications
3) to ask alms
