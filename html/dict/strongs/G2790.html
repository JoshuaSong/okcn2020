κιθαρῳδός
one who plays and sings to the cithara, a harper
κιθαρῳδός a lyre-singer(-player), i.e. harpist
Derivation: from G2788 and a derivative of the same as G5603;
KJV Usage: harper. G2788 G5603
Thayer:
1) a harper, one who plays the harp and accompanies it with his voice
