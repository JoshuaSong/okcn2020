τόκος
a bringing forth, childbirth, parturition
τόκος interest on money loaned (as a produce)
Derivation: from the base of G5088;
KJV Usage: usury. G5088
Thayer:
1) birth
1a) the act of bringing forth
1b) that which has been brought forth, offspring
2) interest of money, usury (because it multiplies money, and as it were "breeds")
