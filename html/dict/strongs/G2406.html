ἱεράτευμα
a priesthood
ἱεράτευμα the priestly fraternity, i.e. sacerdotal order (figuratively)
Derivation: from G2407;
KJV Usage: priesthood. G2407
Thayer:
1) the office of a priest
2) the order or body of priests
