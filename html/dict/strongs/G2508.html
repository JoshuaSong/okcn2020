καθαίρω
to make pure
καθαίρω to cleanse, i.e. (specially) to prune; figuratively, to expiate
Derivation: from G2513;
KJV Usage: purge. G2513
Thayer:
1) to cleanse, of filth impurity, etc
1a) to prune trees and vines from useless shoots
1b) metaph. from guilt, to expiate
