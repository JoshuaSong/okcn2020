ζεῦγος
a yoke of beasts, a pair of mules, oxen
ζεῦγος a couple, i.e. a team (of oxen yoked together) or brace (of birds tied together)
Derivation: from the same as G2218;
KJV Usage: yoke, pair. G2218
Thayer:
1) two draught cattle (horses or mules or oxen) yoked together, a pair or yoke of beasts
2) a pair
