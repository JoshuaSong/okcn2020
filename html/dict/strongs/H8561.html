תּמּרה תּמּר
timmôr timmôrâh
tim-more', tim-mo-raw'
(The first is plural only, while the second is feminine, singular and plural); from the same root as H8558; (architecturally) a palm like pilaster (that is, umbellate)
KJV Usage: palm tree.
