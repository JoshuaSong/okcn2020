Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּית חורון
Beth-horon = "house of hollowness"
1. two towns in Ephraim
a. Upper Beth-horon - town on the mountains of Ephraim
b. Lower Beth-horon - town located 800 feet (245 m) lower than Upper Beth-horon
Origin: from H1004 and H2356
TWOT: None
