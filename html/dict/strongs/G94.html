ἄδικος
wrong-doing, unrighteous, unjust
ἄδικος unjust; by extension wicked; by implication, treacherous; specially, heathen
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G1349;
KJV Usage: unjust, unrighteous. G1 G1349
Thayer:
1) descriptive of one who violates or has violated justice
1a) unjust
1b) unrighteous, sinful
1c) of one who deals fraudulently with others, deceitful
