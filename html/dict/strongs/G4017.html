περιβλέπω
to look round about, gaze around
περιβλέπω to look all around
Derivation: from G4012 and G991;
KJV Usage: look (round) about (on). G4012 G991
Thayer:
1) to look around
2) to look around about one's self
3) to look round on one (i.e. to look for one's self at one near by)
