צחר
tsôchar
tso'-khar
From the same as H6713; whiteness; Tsochar, the name of a Hittite and of an Israelite
KJV Usage: Zohar. Compare H3328.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

צחר
Zohar = "tawny"
1. a Judaite, son of Ashur by wife Helah
2. father of Ephron the Hittite
3. one of the sons of Simeon; also 'Zerah'
Origin: from the same as H6713
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
