ἐμφανίζω
to make manifest, exhibit
ἐμφανίζω to exhibit (in person) or disclose (by words)
Derivation: from G1717;
KJV Usage: appear, declare (plainly), inform, (will) manifest, shew, signify. G1717
Thayer:
1) to manifest, exhibit to view
2) to show one's self, come to view, appear, be manifest
3) to indicate, disclose, declare, make known
For Synonyms see entry G5831
