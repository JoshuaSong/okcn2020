κεράννυμι
to mix, mingle
κεράννυμι to mingle, i.e. (by implication) to pour out (for drinking)
Derivation: prolonged form of a more primary κεράω (which is used in certain tenses);
KJV Usage: fill, pour out. Compare G3396. G3396
Thayer:
1) to mix, mingle
2) to mix wine, water
3) to pour out for drinking.
