κυβεία
dice-playing, dicing
κυβεία gambling, i.e. (figuratively) artifice or fraud
Derivation: from κύβος (a "cube", i.e. die for playing);
KJV Usage: sleight.
Thayer:
1) dice playing
2) metaph. the deception of men, because dice players sometimes cheated and defrauded their fellow players
