πορφύρα
the purple-fish
πορφύρα the "purple" mussel, i.e. (by implication) the red-blue color itself, and finally a garment dyed with it
Derivation: of Latin origin;
KJV Usage: purple.
Thayer:
1) the purple fish, a species of shell fish or mussel
2) a fabric coloured with purple dye, a garment made from purple cloth
