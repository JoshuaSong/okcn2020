ἰῶτα
the letter iota
adscriptum
ἰῶτα "iota", the name of the eighth letter of the Greek alphabet, put (figuratively) for a very small part of anything
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (the tenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet);
KJV Usage: jot.
Thayer:
1) the Hebrew letter `, the smallest of them all
1a) hence equivalent to the minutest part
