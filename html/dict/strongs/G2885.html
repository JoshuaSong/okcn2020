κοσμέω
to order, arrange
κοσμέω to put in proper order, i.e. decorate (literally or figuratively); specially, to snuff (a wick)
Derivation: from G2889;
KJV Usage: adorn, garnish, trim. G2889
Thayer:
1) to put in order, arrange, make ready, prepare
2) to ornament, adore
3) metaph. to embellish with honour, gain honour
