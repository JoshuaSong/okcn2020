ye hallel'êl
yeh-hal-lel-ale'
From H1984 and H410; praising God; Jehallelel, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Jehaleleel, Jehalelel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יהלּלאל
Jehaleleel or Jehalelel = "God is praised"
1. a Calebite descendant of Judah
2. father of Azariah and a Merarite Levite in the time of Hezekiah
Origin: from H1984 and H410
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
