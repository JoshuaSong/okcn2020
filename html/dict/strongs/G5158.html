a turn, direction, course, way
τρόπος a turn, i.e. (by implication) mode or style (especially with preposition or relative prefix as adverb, like); figuratively, deportment or character
Derivation: from the same as G5157;
KJV Usage: (even) as, conversation, (+ like) manner, (+ by any) means, way. G5157
Thayer:
1) a manner, way, fashion
1a) as, even as, like as
2) manner of life, character, deportment
