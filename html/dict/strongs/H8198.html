שׁפחה
shiphchâh
shif-khaw'
Feminine from an unused root meaning to spread out (as a family; see H4940); a female slave (as a member of the household)
KJV Usage: (bond-, hand-) maid (-en, -servant), wench, bondwoman, womanservant.
