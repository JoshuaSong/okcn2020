ἀποβλέπω
to look away from
ἀποβλέπω to look away from everything else, i.e. (figuratively) intently regard
Derivation: from G575 and G991;
KJV Usage: have respect. G575 G991
Thayer:
1) to turn the eyes away from other things and fix them on some one thing
1a) to look at attentively
2) to look with steadfast mental gaze
