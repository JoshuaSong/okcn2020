δέομαι to beg (as binding oneself), i.e. petition
Derivation: middle voice of G1210;
KJV Usage: beseech, pray (to), make request. Compare G4441. G1210 G4441
Thayer:
1) to want, lack
2) to desire, long for
3) to ask, beg
3a) the thing asked for
3b) to pray, make supplications
For Synonyms see entry G5802
