Of foreign derivation; Nebukadnetstsar (or retstsar, or retstsor), king of Babylon
KJV Usage: Nebuchadnezzar, Nebuchadrezzar.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נבוּכדנאצּר
Nebuchadnezzar or Nebuchadrezzar = "may Nebo protect the crown"
1. the great king of Babylon who captured Jerusalem and carried Judah captive
Origin: of foreign derivation
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
