Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּלט
Pelet = "deliverance"
1. son of Jahdai and a descendant of Caleb
2. son of Azmaveth and one of David's mighty warriors who joined him at Ziklag
Origin: from H6403
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
