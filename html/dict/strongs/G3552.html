νοσέω
to be sick, ill, to ail
νοσέω to be sick, i.e. (by implication, of a diseased appetite) to hanker after (figuratively, to harp upon)
Derivation: from G3554;
KJV Usage: dote. G3554
Thayer:
1) to be sick
2) metaph. of any ailment of the mind
2a) to be taken with such an interest in a thing as amounts to a disease, to have a morbid fondness for
