ἐπιτελέω
to complete, finish, accomplish
ἐπιτελέω to fulfill further (or completely), i.e. execute; by implication, to terminate, undergo
Derivation: from G1909 and G5055;
KJV Usage: accomplish, do, finish, (make) (perfect), perform(X -ance). G1909 G5055
Thayer:
1) to bring to an end, accomplish, perfect, execute, complete
1a) to take upon one's self
1b) to make an end for one's self
1b1) to leave off
2) to appoint to, impose upon
