κατάλυμα
an inn, lodging
κατάλυμα properly, a dissolution (breaking up of a journey), i.e. (by implication) a lodging-place
Derivation: from G2647;
KJV Usage: guestchamber, inn. G2647
Thayer:
1) an inn, lodging place
2) an eating room, dining room
