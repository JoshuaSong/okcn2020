a way, path, track, road, highway
ὁδός a road; by implication, a progress (the route, act or distance); figuratively, a mode or means
Derivation: apparently a primary word;
KJV Usage: journey, (high-)way.
Thayer:
1) properly
1a) a way
1a1) a travelled way, road
1b) a travellers way, journey, travelling
2) metaph.
2a) a course of conduct
2b) a way (i.e. manner) of thinking, feeling, deciding
