διαλογίζομαι
to balance accounts
διαλογίζομαι to reckon thoroughly, i.e. (genitive case) to deliberate (by reflection or discussion)
Derivation: from G1223 and G3049;
KJV Usage: cast in mind, consider, dispute, muse, reason, think. G1223 G3049
Thayer:
1) to bring together different reasons, to reckon up the reasons, to reason, revolve in one's mind, deliberate
