אליפז
'ĕlı̂yphaz
el-ee-faz'
From H410 and H6337; God of gold; Eliphaz, the name of one of Job’s friends, and of a son of Esau
KJV Usage: Eliphaz.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אליפז
Eliphaz = "my God is (fine) gold"
1. Esau's son, father of Teman
2. the Temanite friend of Job
Origin: from H410 and H6337
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
