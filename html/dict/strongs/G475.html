ἀντιδιατίθεμαι
to offer resistance
ἀντιδιατίθεμαι to set oneself opposite, i.e. be disputatious
Derivation: from G473 and G1303;
KJV Usage: that oppose themselves. G473 G1303
Thayer:
1) to place one's self in opposition, to oppose, to dispose in turn, to take in hand in turn, to retaliate
