ἀναμάρτητος
without missing, unfailing, unerring
ἀναμάρτητος sinless
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G264;
KJV Usage: that is without sin. G1 G264
Thayer:
1) sinless
1a) of one who has not sinned
1b) of one who cannot sin
