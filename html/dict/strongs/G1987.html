ἐπίσταμαι
to know
ἐπίσταμαι to put the mind upon, i.e. comprehend, or be acquainted with
Derivation: apparently a middle voice of G2186 (with G3563 implied);
KJV Usage: know, understand. G2186 G3563
Thayer:
1) to put one's attention on, fix one's thoughts on, to turn one's self or one's mind to, put one's thought upon a thing
1a) to be acquainted with, to understand
1b) to know
For Synonyms see entry G5825
