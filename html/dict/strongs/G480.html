ἀντίκειμαι
to be set over against, lie opposite
ἀντίκειμαι to lie opposite, i.e. be adverse (figuratively, repugnant) to
Derivation: from G473 and G2749;
KJV Usage: adversary, be contrary, oppose. G473 G2749
Thayer:
1) to be set over against, opposite to
2) to oppose, be adverse to, withstand
