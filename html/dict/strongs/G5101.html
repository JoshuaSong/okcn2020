τίς

τίς an interrogative pronoun, who, which or what (in direct or indirect questions)
Derivation: probably emphatic of G5100;
KJV Usage: every man, how (much), + no(-ne, thing), what (manner, thing), where (-by, -fore, -of, -unto, - with, -withal), whether, which, who(-m, -se), why. G5100
Thayer:
1) who, which, what
