סרס סריס
sârı̂ys sâris
saw-reece', saw-reece'
From an unused root meaning to castrate; a eunuch; by implication valet (especially of the female apartments), and thus a minister of state
KJV Usage: chamberlain, eunuch, officer. Compare H7249.
