יהוחנן
ye hôchânân
yeh-ho-khaw-nawn'
From H3068 and H2603; Jehovah-favored; Jehochanan, the name of eight Israelites
KJV Usage: Jehohanan, Johanan. Compare H3110.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יהוחנן
Jehohanan = "Jehovah has graced"
1. a Korhite Levite and one of the doorkeepers to the tabernacle in the time of David
2. a captain of Judah under king Jehoshaphat
Origin: from H3068 and H2603
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
