נטע
nâṭa‛
naw-tah'
A primitive root; properly to strike in, that is, fix; specifically to plant (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: fastened, plant (-er).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נטע

1. to plant, fasten, fix, establish
a. (Qal)
1. to plant
2. to plant, establish (fig.)
b. (Niphal)
1. to be planted
2. to be established (fig.)
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1354
Parts of Speech: Verb
