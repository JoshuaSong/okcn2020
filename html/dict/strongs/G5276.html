ὑπολήνιον
the vessel under a press
ὑπολήνιον vessel or receptacle under the press, i.e. lower wine-vat
Derivation: neuter of a presumed compound of G5259 and G3025;
KJV Usage: winefat. G5259 G3025
Thayer:
1) a vessel placed under a press (and in the Orient usually sunk in the earth) to receive the expressed juice of the grapes, a pit
