ἀπέναντι
opposite, against
ἀπέναντι from in front, i.e. opposite, before or against
Derivation: from G575 and G1725;
KJV Usage: before, contrary, over against, in the presence of. G575 G1725
Thayer:
1) over against, opposite
2) in sight of, before
3) in opposition to, against
