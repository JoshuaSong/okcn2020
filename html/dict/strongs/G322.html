ἀναδείκνυμι
to lift up and shew
ἀναδείκνυμι to exhibit, i.e. (by implication) to indicate, appoint
Derivation: from G303 and G1166;
KJV Usage: appoint, shew. G303 G1166
Thayer:
1) to proclaim any one as elected to office
2) to announce as appointed a king, general, etc.
3) to lift up anything on high and exhibit it for all to behold

  • Shew (show)
