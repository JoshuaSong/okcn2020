ὑδρωπικός
dropsical
ὑδρωπικός to be "dropsical"
Derivation: from a compound of G5204 and a derivative of G3700 (as if looking watery);
KJV Usage: have the dropsy. G5204 G3700
Thayer:
1) dropsical, suffering from dropsy
