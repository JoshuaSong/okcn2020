διϊσχυρίζομαι to stout it through, i.e. asservate
Derivation: from G1223 and a derivative of G2478;
KJV Usage: confidently (constantly) affirm. G1223 G2478
Thayer:
1) to lean upon
2) to affirm stoutly, assert confidently
