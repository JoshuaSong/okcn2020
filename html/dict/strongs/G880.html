ἄφωνος
voiceless, speechless, dumb, silent
ἄφωνος voiceless, i.e. mute (by nature or choice); figuratively, unmeaning
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G5456;
KJV Usage: dumb, without signification. G1 G5456
Thayer:
1) voiceless, dumb
2) without faculty of speech
2a) of idols
