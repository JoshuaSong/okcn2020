ὑποφέρω
to carry away under
ὑποφέρω to bear from underneath, i.e. (figuratively) to undergo hardship
Derivation: from G5259 and G5342;
KJV Usage: bear, endure. G5259 G5342
Thayer:
1) to bear by being under, bear up (a thing placed on one's shoulders)
2) to bear patiently, to endure
