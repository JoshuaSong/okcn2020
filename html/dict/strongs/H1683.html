Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

דּברה דּבורה
Deborah = "bee"
1. the nurse of Rebekah who accompanied her from the house of Bethuel
2. a prophetess who judged Israel
Origin: the same as H1682
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
