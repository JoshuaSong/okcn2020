פּוּשׁ
pûsh
poosh
A primitive root; to spread; figuratively act proudly
KJV Usage: grow up, be grown fat, spread selves, be scattered.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּוּשׁ

1. to spring about
a. (Qal) to frisk, act proudly (fig.)
2. (Niphal) to be scattered, be spread
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1751,1752
Parts of Speech: Verb
