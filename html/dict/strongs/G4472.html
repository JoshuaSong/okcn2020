ῥαντίζω
to purify
ῥαντίζω to render besprinkled, i.e. asperse (ceremonially or figuratively)
Derivation: from a derivative of ῥαίνω (to sprinkle);
KJV Usage: sprinkle.
Thayer:
1) to sprinkle
2) to cleanse by sprinkling, hence to purify, cleanse
