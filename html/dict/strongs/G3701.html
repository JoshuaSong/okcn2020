ὀπτασία
a vision
ὀπτασία visuality, i.e. (concretely) an apparition
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G3700;
KJV Usage: vision. G3700
Thayer:
1) the act of exhibiting one's self to view
2) a sight, a vision, an appearance presented to one whether asleep or awake
