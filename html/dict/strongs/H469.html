אלצפן אליצפן
'ĕlı̂ytsâphân 'eltsâphân
el-ee-tsaw-fawn', el-tsaw-fawn'
From H410 and H6845; God of treasure; Elitsaphan or Eltsaphan, an Israelite
KJV Usage: Elizaphan, Elzaphan.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אלצפן אליצפן
Elizaphan or Elzaphan = "my God has protected"
1. a Kohathite chief in the wilderness
2. a Zebulunite chief
3. Uzziel's son, Mishael's brother in time of Moses
Origin: from H410 and H6845
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
