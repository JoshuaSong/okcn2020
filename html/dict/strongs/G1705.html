ἐμπίπλημι
to fill quite full
ἐμπίπλημι or ἐμπλήθω to fill in (up), i.e. (by implication) to satisfy (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G1722 and the base of G4118;
KJV Usage: fill. G1722 G4118
Thayer:
1) to fill up, fill full
2) to take one's fill of, glut one's desire for, satisfy, satiate
