ἀντιλέγω
to speak against, gainsay, contradict
ἀντιλέγω to dispute, refuse
Derivation: from G473 and G3004;
KJV Usage: answer again, contradict, deny, gainsay(-er), speak against. G473 G3004
Thayer:
1) to speak against, gainsay, contradict
2) to oppose one's self to one, decline to obey him, declare one's self against him, refuse to have anything you do with him
