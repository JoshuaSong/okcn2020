אביגל אביגיל
'ăbı̂ygayil 'ăbı̂ygal
ab-ee-gah'yil, ab-ee-gal'
From H1 and H1524; father (that is source) of joy; Abigail or Abigal, the name of two Israelitesses
KJV Usage: Abigal.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אביגל אביגיל
Abigail = "my father is joy"
1. wife of Nabal, then of David
2. sister of David
Origin: from H1 and H1524
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
