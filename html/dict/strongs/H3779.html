כּשׂדּי
kaśday
kas-dah'ee
(Chaldee); corresponding to H3778; a Chaldaean or inhabitant of Chaldaea; by implication a Magian or professional astrologer
KJV Usage: Chaldean.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּשׂדּי
Chaldean = "clod breakers"
1. the inhabitants of Chaldea, living on the lower Euphrates and Tigris
2. those persons considered the wisest in the land (by extension)
Origin: corresponding to H3778
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
