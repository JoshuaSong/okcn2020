יבנאל
yabne 'êl
yab-neh-ale'
From H1129 and H410; built of God; Jabneel, the name of two places in Palestine
KJV Usage: Jabneel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יבנאל
Jabneel = "God causes to build"
1. a town in Judah on the northern boundary near the sea
2. a town in Naphtali on the boundary in upper Galilee
Origin: from H1129 and H410
TWOT: None
