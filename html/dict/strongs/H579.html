אנה
'ânâh
aw-naw'
A primitive root (perhaps rather identical with H578 through the idea of contraction in anguish); to approach; hence to meet in various senses
KJV Usage: befall, deliver, happen, seek a quarrel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אנה

1. to meet, encounter, approach, be opportune
a. (Piel) to allow to meet, cause to meet
b. (Pual) to be sent, be allowed to meet
c. (Hithpael) to seek occasion (quarrel), cause oneself to meet
Origin: a primitive root [perhaps rather identical with H578 through the idea of contraction in anguish]
TWOT: 126
Parts of Speech: Verb
