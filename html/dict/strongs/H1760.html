דּחח דּחה
dâchâh dâchach
daw-khaw', daw-khakh'
A primitive root; to push down
KJV Usage: chase, drive away (on), overthrow, outcast, X sore, thrust, totter.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

דּחח דּחה

1. to push, thrust, chase, overflow, totter, sore, drive away or out, be outcast, be cast down
a. (Qal) to push, push violently
b. (Niphal) to be thrust down, be cast down
c. (Pual) to be thrust down
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 420
Parts of Speech: Verb
