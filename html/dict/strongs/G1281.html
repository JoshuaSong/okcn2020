διαπραγματεύομαι
to examine thoroughly
διαπραγματεύομαι to thoroughly occupy oneself, i.e. (transitively and by implication) to earn in business
Derivation: from G1223 and G4231;
KJV Usage: gain by trading. G1223 G4231
Thayer:
1) thoroughly, earnestly to undertake a business
2) to undertake a business for sake of gain
