ἀνθομολογέομαι
to make a mutual agreement
ἀνθομολογέομαι to confess in turn, i.e. respond in praise
Derivation: from G473 and the middle voice of G3670;
KJV Usage: give thanks. G473 G3670
Thayer:
1) to reply by professing or by confessing
2) to agree mutually (in turn), to make a compact
3) to acknowledge in the presence of
3a) to give thanks
