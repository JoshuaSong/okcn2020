סיני
sı̂ynı̂y
see-nee'
From an otherwise unknown name of a man; a Sinite, or descendant of one of the sons of Canaan
KJV Usage: Sinite.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

סיני
Sinite = see Sin "thorn" or "clay"
1. a tribe of the Canaanites descended from Canaan inhabiting the northern part of the Lebanon district
Origin: from an otherwise unknown name of a man
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Adjective
