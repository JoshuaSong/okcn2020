συνδέω
to bind
συνδέω to bind with, i.e. (passively) be a fellow-prisoner (figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and G1210;
KJV Usage: be bound with. G4862 G1210
Thayer:
1) to tie together, to bind together
2) to bind or fasten on all sides
3) to bind just as (i.e. jointly with) another
