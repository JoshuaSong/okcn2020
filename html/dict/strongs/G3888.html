παραμυθέομαι
to encourage
παραμυθέομαι to relate near, i.e. (by implication) encourage, console
Derivation: from G3844 and the middle voice of a derivative of G3454;
KJV Usage: comfort. G3844 G3454
Thayer:
1) to speak to, address one, whether by way of admonition and incentive, or to calm and console
1a) to encourage, console
