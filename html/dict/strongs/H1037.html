Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּית מלּא בּית מלּוא
Beth-millo = "house of Millo" or "house of the rampart"
1. a place near Shechem, site unknown
2. a citadel in Jerusalem
Origin: from H1004 and H4407
TWOT: None
