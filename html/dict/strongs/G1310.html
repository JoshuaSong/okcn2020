διαφημίζω
to spread abroad
διαφημίζω to report thoroughly, i.e. divulgate
Derivation: from G1223 and a derivative of G5345;
KJV Usage: blaze abroad, commonly report, spread abroad, fame. G1223 G5345
Thayer:
1) to spread abroad, blaze abroad
2) to spread abroad his fame or renown
