ἀντάλλαγμα
that which is given
ἀντάλλαγμα an equivalent or ransom
Derivation: from a compound of G473 and G236;
KJV Usage: in exchange. G473 G236
Thayer:
1) that which is given in place of another thing by way of exchange
2) what is given either in order to keep or to acquire anything
