כּסה
kâsâh
kaw-saw'
A primitive root; properly to plump, that is, fill up hollows; by implication to cover (for clothing or secrecy)
KJV Usage: clad self, close, clothe, conceal, cover (self), (flee to) hide, overwhelm. Compare H3780.
