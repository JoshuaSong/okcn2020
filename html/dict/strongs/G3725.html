ὅριον
a boundary, limit
ὅριον a boundary-line, i.e. (by implication) a frontier (region)
Derivation: neuter of a derivative of an apparently primary ὅρος (a bound or limit);
KJV Usage: border, coast.
Thayer:
1) boundaries
1a) for a region, district, land, territory
