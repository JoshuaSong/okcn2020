διάνοια deep thought, properly, the faculty (mind or its disposition), by implication, its exercise
Derivation: from G1223 and G3563;
KJV Usage: imagination, mind, understanding. G1223 G3563
Thayer:
1) the mind as a faculty of understanding, feeling, desiring
2) understanding
3) mind, i.e. spirit, way of thinking and feeling
4) thoughts, either good or bad
