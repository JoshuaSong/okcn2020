μεταλαμβάνω
to have
μεταλαμβάνω to participate; genitive case, to accept (and use)
Derivation: from G3326 and G2983;
KJV Usage: eat, have, be partaker, receive, take. G3326 G2983
Thayer:
1) to be or to be made a partner
2) to partake of, take [some] food
3) of a thing, to get, find (a whole)
