παραλογίζομαι
to misreckon, miscalculate
παραλογίζομαι to misreckon, i.e. delude
Derivation: from G3844 and G3049;
KJV Usage: beguile, deceive. G3844 G3049
Thayer:
1) to reckon wrong, miscount
2) to cheat by false reckoning
2a) to deceive by false reasoning
3) to deceive, delude, circumvent
