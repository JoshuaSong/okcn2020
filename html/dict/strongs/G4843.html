συμπεριλαμβάνω
to comprehend
συμπεριλαμβάνω to take by enclosing altogether, i.e. earnestly throw the arms about one
Derivation: from G4862 and a compound of G4012 and G2983;
KJV Usage: embrace. G4862 G4012 G2983
Thayer:
1) to comprehend at once
2) to embrace completely
