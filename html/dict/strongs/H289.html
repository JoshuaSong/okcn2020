אחימן אחימן
'ăchı̂yman 'ăchı̂ymân
akh-ee-man', akh-ee-mawn'
From H251 and H4480; brother of a portion (that is, gift); Achiman, the name of an Anakite and of an Israelite
KJV Usage: Ahiman.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אחימן אחימן
Ahiman = "my brother is a gift"
1. a son of Anak
2. a Levite servant in the temple
Origin: from H251 and H4480
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
