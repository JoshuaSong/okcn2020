אח
'âch
awkh
A primitive word; a brother (used in the widest sense of literal relationship and metaphorical affinity or resemblance (like H1))
KJV Usage: another, brother (-ly), kindred, like, other. Compare also the proper names beginning with “Ah-” or “Ahi-” .

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אח

1. brother
a. brother of same parents
b. half-brother (same father)
c. relative, kinship, same tribe
d. each to the other (reciprocal relationship)
e. (fig.) of resemblance
Origin: a primitive word
TWOT: 62a
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
