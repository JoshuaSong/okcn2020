μίλιον
a Roman mile, milliarium
μίλιον a thousand paces, i.e. a "mile"
Derivation: of Latin origin;
KJV Usage: mile.
Thayer:
1) a mile, among the Romans the distance of a thousand paces or eight stadia, about 1.5 km [somewhat less than our mile]
