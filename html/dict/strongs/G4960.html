συστοιχέω
to correspond to
συστοιχέω to file together (as soldiers in ranks), i.e. (figuratively) to correspond to
Derivation: from G4862 and G4748;
KJV Usage: answer to. G4862 G4748
Thayer:
1) to stand or march in the same row (file) with
1a) of soldiers
1b) to stand over against, be parallel with
1c) to answer to, resemble
1c1) so once of a type in the OT which answers to the antitype in the NT
