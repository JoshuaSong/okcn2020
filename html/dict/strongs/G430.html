ἀνέχομαι to hold oneself up against, i.e. (figuratively) put up with
Derivation: middle voice from G303 and G2192;
KJV Usage: bear with, endure, forbear, suffer. G303 G2192
Thayer:
1) to hold up
2) to hold one's self erect and firm
3) to sustain, to bear, to endure
