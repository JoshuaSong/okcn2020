χολή
gall, bile
χολή "gall" or bile, i.e. (by analogy) poison or an anodyne (wormwood, poppy, etc.)
Derivation: feminine of an equivalent perhaps akin to the same as 5514 (from the greenish hue);
KJV Usage: gall.
Thayer:
1) bile, gall
2) in the OT used of other bitter things
2a) wormwood
2b) possibly myrrh
