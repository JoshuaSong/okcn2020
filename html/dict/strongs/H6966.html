Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קוּם

1. to arise, stand
a. (P'al)
1. to arise from
2. to come on the scene (fig)
3. to arise (out of inaction)
4. to stand
5. to endure
b. (Pael) to set up, establish
c. (Aphel)
1. to set up
2. to lift up
3. to establish
4. to appoint
d. (Hophal) to be made to stand
Origin: corresponding to H6965
TWOT: 2968
Parts of Speech: Verb
