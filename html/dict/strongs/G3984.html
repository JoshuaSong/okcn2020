πεῖρα
a trial, attempt, essay, experiment
πεῖρα a test, i.e. attempt, experience
Derivation: from the base of G4008 (through the idea of piercing);
KJV Usage: assaying, trial. G4008
Thayer:
1) a trial, experience, attempt
2) to attempt a thing, to make trial of a thing or of a person
3) to have a trial of a thing
4) to experience, learn to know by experience
