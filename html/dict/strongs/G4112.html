πλαστός
formed, moulded
πλαστός moulded, i.e. (by implication) artificial or (figuratively) fictitious (false)
Derivation: from G4111;
KJV Usage: feigned. G4111
Thayer:
1) moulded, formed, as from clay, wax, or stone
2) feigned
