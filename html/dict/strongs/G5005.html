ταλαίπωρος
suffering, miserable
ταλαίπωρος enduring trial, i.e. miserable
Derivation: from the base of G5007 and a derivative of the base of G3984;
KJV Usage: wretched. G5007 G3984
Thayer:
1) enduring toils and troubles
2) afflicted, wretched
