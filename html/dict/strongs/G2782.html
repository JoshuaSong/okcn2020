κήρυγμα
that which is cried by a herald, a proclamation, public notice
κήρυγμα a proclamation (especially of the gospel; by implication, the gospel itself)
Derivation: from G2784;
KJV Usage: preaching. G2784
Thayer:
1) that which is proclaimed by a herald or public crier, a proclamation by herald
2) in the NT the message or proclamation of the heralds of God or Christ
