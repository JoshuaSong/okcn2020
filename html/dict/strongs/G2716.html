κατεργάζομαι
to effect by labour, to achieve, accomplish
κατεργάζομαι to work fully, i.e. accomplish; by implication, to finish, fashion
Derivation: from G2596 and G2038;
KJV Usage: cause, to (deed), perform, work (out). G2596 G2038
Thayer:
1) to perform, accomplish, achieve
2) to work out i.e. to do that from which something results
2a) of things: bring about, result in
3) to fashion i.e. render one fit for a thing
