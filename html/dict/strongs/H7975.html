שׁלח שׁלּח
shillôach shelach
shee-lo'-akh, sheh'-lakh
The second form is in imitation of H7974, used in Nehemiah 3:15; from H7971; rill; Shiloach, a fountain of Jerusalem
KJV Usage: Shiloah, Siloah.
