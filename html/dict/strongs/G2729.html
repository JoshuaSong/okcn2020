κατισχύω
to have power over, overpower, prevail against
κατισχύω to overpower
Derivation: from G2596 and G2480;
KJV Usage: prevail (against). G2596 G2480
Thayer:
1) to be strong to another's detriment, to prevail against
2) to be superior in strength
3) to overcome
4) to prevail
