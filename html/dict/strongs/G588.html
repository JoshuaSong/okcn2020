ἀποδέχομαι
to accept from
ἀποδέχομαι to take fully, i.e. welcome (persons), approve (things)
Derivation: from G575 and G1209;
KJV Usage: accept, receive (gladly). G575 G1209
Thayer:
1) to accept from, receive
2) to accept what is offered from without
