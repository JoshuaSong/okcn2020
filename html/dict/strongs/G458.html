ἀνομία
lawlessness
ἀνομία illegality, i.e. violation of law or (genitive case) wickedness
Derivation: from G459;
KJV Usage: iniquity, X transgress(-ion of) the law, unrighteousness. G459
Thayer:
1) the condition of without law
1a) because ignorant of it
1b) because of violating it
2) contempt and violation of law, iniquity, wickedness
