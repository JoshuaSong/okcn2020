ἐπιχειρέω
to put one's hand on
ἐπιχειρέω to put the hand upon, i.e. undertake
Derivation: from G1909 and G5495;
KJV Usage: go about, take in hand (upon). G1909 G5495
Thayer:
1) to put the hand to
2) to take in hand, undertake, attempt
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Go (went), Go onward, etc.
  • Hand (take in)
  • Take
