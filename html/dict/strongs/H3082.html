יהונדב
ye hônâdâb
yeh-ho-naw-dawb'
From H3068 and H5068; Jehovah-largessed; Jehonadab, the name of an Israelite and of an Arab
KJV Usage: Jehonadab, Jonadab. Compare H3122.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יהונדב
Jehonadab = "Jehovah is willing"
1. a son of Rechab, chief of the Rechabites, in the time of Jehu and Ahab
2. a nephew of David
Origin: from H3068 and H5068
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
