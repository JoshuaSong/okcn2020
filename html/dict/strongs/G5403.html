Φοινίκη palm-country; Phœnice (or Phœnicia), a region of Palestine
Derivation: from G5404;
KJV Usage: Phenice, Phenicia. G5404
Thayer:
1) a territory of the province of Syria, situated on the coast of the Mediterranean between the river Eleutherus and the promontory of Carmel, some
30 miles (50 km) long and
3 (5 km) broad
Literal: Phenicia = "land of palm trees"
