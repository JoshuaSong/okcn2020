משׁמן
mashmân
mash-mawn'
From H8080; fat, that is, (literally and abstractly) fatness; but usually (figuratively and concretely) a rich dish, a fertile field, a robust man
KJV Usage: fat (one, -ness, -test, -test place).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

משׁמן

1. fatness, fat piece, fertile place, richly prepared food
a. fat, fatness, fat pieces
b. oil, olive oil
2. fatness
a. stout, vigorous
b. fertile spots or places
Origin: from H8080
