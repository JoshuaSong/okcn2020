ἄφρων
without sense
ἄφρων properly, mindless, i.e. stupid, (by implication) ignorant, (specially) egotistic, (practically) rash, or (morally) unbelieving
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G5424;
KJV Usage: fool(-ish), unwise. G1 G5424
Thayer:
1) without reason
2) senseless, foolish, stupid
3) without reflection or intelligence, acting rashly
