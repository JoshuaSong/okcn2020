συνέρχομαι
ibo
συνέρχομαι to convene, depart in company with, associate with, or (specially), cohabit (conjugally)
Derivation: from G4862 and G2064;
KJV Usage: accompany, assemble (with), come (together), come (company, go) with, resort. G4862 G2064
Thayer:
1) to come together
1a) to assemble
1b) of conjugal cohabitation
2) to go (depart) or come with one, to accompany one
