χλιαρός
warm, lukewarm
χλιαρός tepid
Derivation: from χλίω (to warm);
KJV Usage: lukewarm.
Thayer:
1) tepid, lukewarm
2) metaph. of the condition of the soul wretchedly fluctuating between a torpor and a fervour of love
