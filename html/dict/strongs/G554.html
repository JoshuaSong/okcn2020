ἀπεκδύομαι to divest wholly oneself, or (for oneself) despoil
Derivation: middle voice from G575 and G1562;
KJV Usage: put off, spoil. G575 G1562
Thayer:
1) wholly put off from one's self
1a) denoting separation from what is put off
2) wholly to strip off for one's self (for one's own advantage)
3) despoil, disarm
