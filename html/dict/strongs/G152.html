αἰσχύνη
shame done one, disgrace, dishonour
αἰσχύνη shame or disgrace (abstractly or concretely)
Derivation: from G153;
KJV Usage: dishonesty, shame. G153
Thayer:
1) the confusion of one who is ashamed of anything, sense of shame
2) ignominy, disgrace, dishonour
3) a thing to be ashamed of
