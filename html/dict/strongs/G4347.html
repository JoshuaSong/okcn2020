προσκολλάω
to glue on
προσκολλάω to glue to, i.e. (figuratively) to adhere
Derivation: from G4314 and G2853;
KJV Usage: cleave, join (self). G4314 G2853
Thayer:
1) to glue upon, glue to
2) to join one's self to closely, cleave to, stick to
