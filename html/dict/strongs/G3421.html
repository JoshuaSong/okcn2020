μνημονεύω
to call to mind, remember
μνημονεύω to exercise memory, i.e. recollect; by implication, to punish; also to rehearse
Derivation: from a derivative of G3420;
KJV Usage: make mention; be mindful, remember. G3420
Thayer:
1) to be mindful of, to remember, to call to mind
1a) to think of and feel for a person or thing
1b) to hold in memory, keep in mind
2) to make mention of
