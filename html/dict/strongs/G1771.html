ἔννοια
a thought in the mind, notion, conception
ἔννοια thoughtfulness, i.e. moral understanding
Derivation: from a compound of G1722 and G3563;
KJV Usage: intent, mind. G1722 G3563
Thayer:
1) the act of thinking, consideration, meditation
2) a thought, notion, conception
3) mind, understanding, will, manner of feeling, and thinking
