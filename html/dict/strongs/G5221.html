ὑπαντάω
to come
ὑπαντάω to go opposite (meet) under (quietly), i.e. to encounter, fall in with
Derivation: from G5259 and a derivative of G473;
KJV Usage: (go to) meet. G5259 G473
Thayer:
1) to go to meet, to meet
2) in military reference
2a) of a hostile meeting
