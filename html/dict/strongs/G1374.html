δίψυχος
double-minded
δίψυχος two-spirited, i.e. vacillating (in opinion or purpose)
Derivation: from G1364 and G5590;
KJV Usage: double minded. G1364 G5590
Thayer:
1) double minded
1a) wavering, uncertain, doubting
1b) divided in interest
