παρατείνω
to stretch out along
παρατείνω to extend along, i.e. prolong (in point of time)
Derivation: from G3844 and teino (to stretch);
KJV Usage: continue. G3844
Thayer:
1) to extend beside, to stretch out lengthwise, to extend
2) to prolong
