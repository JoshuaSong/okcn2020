βουλεύω
to take counsel, deliberate, concert measures
βουλεύω to advise, i.e. (reflexively) deliberate, or (by implication) resolve
Derivation: from G1012;
KJV Usage: consult, take counsel, determine, be minded, purpose. G1012
Thayer:
1) to deliberate with one's self, consider
2) to take counsel, resolve
