ἐπιδημέω
to be at home, live at home
ἐπιδημέω to make oneself at home, i.e. (by extension) to reside (in a foreign country)
Derivation: from a compound of G1909 and G1218;
KJV Usage: (be) dwelling (which were) there, stranger. G1909 G1218
Thayer:
1) to be present among one's people, in one's city or one's native land
2) to be a sojourner
2a) of a foreign resident, among any people, in any country
