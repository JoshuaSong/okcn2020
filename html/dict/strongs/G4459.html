πῶς
in a certain way
how? in what way
πῶς an interrogative particle of manner; in what way? (sometimes the question is indirect, how?); also as exclamation, how much!
Derivation: adverb from the base of G4226;
KJV Usage: how, after (by) what manner (means), that. (Occasionally unexpressed in English). G4226
Thayer:
1) how, in what way
