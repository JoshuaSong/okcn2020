רחק רחוק
râchôq râchôq
raw-khoke', raw-khoke'
From H7368; remote, literally of figuratively, of place or time; specifically precious; often used adverbially (with preposition)
KJV Usage: (a-) far (abroad, off), long ago, of old, space, great while to come.
