διαδέχομαι
to receive one from another
διαδέχομαι to receive in turn, i.e. (figuratively) succeed to
Derivation: from G1223 and G1209;
KJV Usage: come after. G1223 G1209
Thayer:
1) to receive through another, anything left or bequeathed by him, to receive in succession, receive in turn, succeed to
