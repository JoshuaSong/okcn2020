נקף
nâqaph
naw-kaf'
A primitive root; to strike with more or less violence (beat, fell, corrode); by implication (of attack) to knock together, that is, surround or circulate
KJV Usage: compass (about, -ing), cut down, destroy, go round (about), inclose, round.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נקף

1. to strike, strike off
a. (Piel) to strike off skin
2. to go around, compass, round
a. (Qal) to go around
b. (Hiphil)
1. to go around, surround, encompass, enclose
2. to make the round, complete the circuit
3. to make round, round off
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1415,1416
Parts of Speech: Verb
