סכן
sâkan
saw-kan'
Probably a denominative from H7915; properly to cut, that is, damage; also to grow (causatively make) poor
KJV Usage: endanger, impoverish.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

סכן

1. to incur danger
a. (Niphal) to endanger oneself
2. to be poor
a. (Pual) to be impoverished
Origin: probably a denominative from H7915
TWOT: 1495,1496
Parts of Speech: Verb
