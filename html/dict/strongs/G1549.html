ἔκγονον a descendant, i.e. (specially) grandchild
Derivation: neuter of a derivative of a compound of G1537 and G1096;
KJV Usage: nephew. G1537 G1096
Thayer:
1) sprung from one, born, begotten
2) a son, daughter, offspring, children, descendants
3) grandchildren
