בּית לעפרה
bêyth le ‛aphrâh
bayth le-af-raw'
From H1004 and the feminine of H6083 (with preposition interposed); house to (that is, of) dust; Beth-le-Aphrah, a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: house of Aphrah.
