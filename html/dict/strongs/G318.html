ἀνάγκη
force, constraint, necessity
ἀνάγκη constraint (literally or figuratively); by implication, distress
Derivation: from G303 and the base of G43;
KJV Usage: distress, must needs, (of) necessity(-sary), needeth, needful. G303 G43
Thayer:
1) necessity, imposed either by the circumstances, or by law of duty regarding to one's advantage, custom, argument
2) calamity, distress, straits
