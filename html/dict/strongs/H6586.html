פּשׁע
pâsha‛
paw-shah'
A primitive root (rather identical with H6585 through the idea of expansion); to break away (from just authority), that is, trespass, apostatize, quarrel
KJV Usage: offend, rebel, revolt, transgress (-ion, -or).
