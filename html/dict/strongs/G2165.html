εὐφραίνω
to cheer, delight, gladden
εὐφραίνω to put (middle voice or passively, be) in a good frame of mind, i.e. rejoice
Derivation: from G2095 and G5424;
KJV Usage: fare, make glad, be (make) merry, rejoice. G2095 G5424
Thayer:
1) to gladden, make joyful
1a) to be glad, to be merry, to rejoice
1b) to rejoice in, be delighted with a thing
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Fare, Farewell
  • Glad (be, make), Gladly
  • Merry (to be, to make)
  • Rejoice
