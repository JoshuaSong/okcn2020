ἐξαρτίζω
to complete, finish
ἐξαρτίζω to finish out (time); figuratively, to equip fully (a teacher)
Derivation: from G1537 and a derivative of G739;
KJV Usage: accomplish, thoroughly furnish. G1537 G739
Thayer:
1) to complete, finish
1a) to furnish perfectly
1b) to finish, accomplish, (as it were, to render the days complete)
