ἐγγίζω to make near, i.e. (reflexively) approach
Derivation: from G1451;
KJV Usage: approach, be at hand, come (draw) near, be (come, draw) nigh. G1451
Thayer:
1) to bring near, to join one thing to another
2) to draw or come near to, to approach
