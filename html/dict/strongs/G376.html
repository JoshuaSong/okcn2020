ἀνάπηρος
much maimed, crippled
ἀνάπηρος crippled
Derivation: from G303 (in the sense of intensity) and πῆρος (maimed);
KJV Usage: maimed. G303
Thayer:
1) disabled in the limbs, maimed, crippled
2) injured in, or bereft of, some member of the body
