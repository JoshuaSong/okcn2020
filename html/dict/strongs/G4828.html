συμμαρτυρέω
to bear witness with
συμμαρτυρέω to testify jointly, i.e. corroborate by (concurrent) evidence
Derivation: from G4862 and G3140;
KJV Usage: testify unto, (also) bear witness (with). G4862 G3140
Thayer:
1) to bear witness with, bear joint witness
