אהליבהּ אהליבה
'ohŏlı̂ybâh 'ohŏlı̂ybâhh
o''-hol-ee-baw', o''-hol-e-baw'
(As with H170 the first form is in form a feminine of H168, but is in fact for the second form); from H168; my tent (is) in her; Oholibah, a symbolic name for Judah
KJV Usage: Aholibah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אהליבהּ אהליבה
Aholibah = "woman of the tent" or "the tent is in her"
1. (metaph) Jerusalem as adulterous wife of Jehovah
Origin: (similarly with H170) for 'Oholiybahh {o''-hol-e-baw'}, from H168
TWOT: 32c
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
