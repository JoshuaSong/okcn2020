ἀτενίζω
to look intently, gaze earnestly
ἀτενίζω to gaze intently
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a particle of union) and τείνω (to stretch);
KJV Usage: behold earnestly (stedfastly), fasten (eyes), look (earnestly, stedfastly, up stedfastly), set eyes. G1
Thayer:
1) to fix the eyes on, gaze upon
2) to look into anything
3) metaph. to fix one's mind on one as an example
