κατασείω
to shake down, throw down
κατασείω to sway downward, i.e. make a signal
Derivation: from G2596 and G4579;
KJV Usage: beckon. G2596 G4579
Thayer:
1) to shake down, throw down
2) to shake
2a) to make a sign, to signal with the hand to one
