ἀκροθίνιον
the topmost part of a heap, the choice part, first-fruits
ἀκροθίνιον properly (in the plural) the top of the heap, i.e. (by implication) best of the booty
Derivation: from G206 and θίς (a heap);
KJV Usage: spoils. G206
Thayer:
1) top of the heap, the first fruits
1a) best of the spoils or crops
2) the Greeks customarily selected from the topmost part of the heaps and offered this to the gods
