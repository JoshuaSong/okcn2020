ענניה
‛ănanyâh
an-an-yaw'
From H6049 and H3050; Jah has covered; Ananjah, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Ananiah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ענניה
Ananiah = "Jehovah clouds" n pr m
1. ancestor of Azariah who assisted in rebuilding the city wall in the time of Nehemiah n pr loc
2. a city northwest of Jerusalem to which some Benjamite exiles returned
Origin: from H6049 and H3050
TWOT: None
