Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ענּי
Unni = "afflicted"
1. a Levite doorkeeper in the time of David
2. a Levite concerned in the sacred office after the return from exile in Babylon
Origin: from H6031
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
