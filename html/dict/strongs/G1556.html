ἐκδικέω
to avenge, punish
ἐκδικέω to vindicate, retaliate, punish
Derivation: from G1558;
KJV Usage: a (re-)venge. G1558
Thayer:
1) to vindicate one's right, do one justice
1a) to protect, defend, one person from another
2) to avenge a thing
2a) to punish a person for a thing
