ἐπηρεάζω
to threaten abusively
ἐπηρεάζω to insult, slander
Derivation: from a comparative of G1909 and (probably) ἀρειά (threats);
KJV Usage: use despitefully, falsely accuse. G1909
Thayer:
1) to insult
2) to treat abusively, use despitefully
3) to revile
4) in a forensic sense, to accuse falsely
5) to threaten
