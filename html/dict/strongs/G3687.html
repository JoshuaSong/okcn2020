ὀνομάζω
to name
ὀνομάζω to name, i.e. assign an appellation; by extension, to utter, mention, profess
Derivation: from G3686;
KJV Usage: call, name. G3686
Thayer:
1) to name
1a) to name, to utter, to make mention of the name
1b) to name
1b1) give name to, one
1b2) be named
1b2a) to bear the name of a person or thing
1c) to utter the name of a person or thing
