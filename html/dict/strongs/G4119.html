πλείων
more, larger
πλείων , or neuter πλεῖον , or πλέον more in quantity, number, or quality; also (in plural) the major portion
Derivation: comparative of G4183;
KJV Usage: X above, + exceed, more excellent, further, (very) great(-er), long(-er), (very) many, greater (more) part, + yet but. G4183
Thayer:
1) greater in quantity
1a) the more part, very many
2) greater in quality, superior, more excellent
