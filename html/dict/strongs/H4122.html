מהר שׁלל חשׁ בּז
mahêr shâlâl châsh baz
mah-hare' shaw-lawl' khawsh baz
From H4118 and H7998 and H2363 and H957; hasting (as he (the enemy) to the) booty, swift (to the) prey; Maher-Shalal Chash-Baz; the symbolical name of the son of Isaiah
KJV Usage: Maher-shalal-hash-baz.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מהר שׁלל חשׁ בּז
= Maher-shalal-hash-baz "swift is booty, speedy is prey"
1. symbolic name given by Isaiah by the Lord's direction to Isaiah's son; prophetic indication that Damascus and Samaria were soon to be plundered by the king of Assyria
Origin: from H4118 and H7998 and H2363 and H957
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
