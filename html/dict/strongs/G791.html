ἀστεῖος
of the town
ἀστεῖος urbane, i.e. (by implication) handsome
Derivation: from ἄστυ (a city);
KJV Usage: fair.
Thayer:
1) of the city
2) of polished manners
3) elegant (of body), comely, fair
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Beautiful, graceful
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Fair
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Beautiful
  • Fair
  • Proper
