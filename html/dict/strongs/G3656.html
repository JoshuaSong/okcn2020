ὁμιλέω
to be in company with, consort with
ὁμιλέω to be in company with, i.e. (by implication) to converse
Derivation: from G3658;
KJV Usage: commune, talk. G3658
Thayer:
1) to be in company with
2) to associate with
3) to stay with
4) to converse with, talk about: with one
