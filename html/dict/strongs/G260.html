ἅμα
at once, at the same time
ἅμα properly, at the "same" time, but freely used as a preposition or adverb denoting close association
Derivation: a primary particle;
KJV Usage: also, and, together, with(-al).
Thayer:
1) at the same time, at once, together prep.
2) together with
For Synonyms see entry G5807
