יזרעאל
yizre ‛ê'l
yiz-reh-ale'
From H2232 and H410; God will sow; Jizreel, the name of two places in Palestine and of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Jezreel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יזרעאל
Jezreel = "God sows" n pr m
1. a descendant of the father or founder of Etam of Judah
2. first son of Hosea the prophet n pr loc
3. a city in the Nekeb of Judah
4. a city in Issachar on the northwest spur of Mount Gilboa
Origin: from H2232 and H410
TWOT: None
