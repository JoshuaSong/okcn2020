προσαναβαίνω
to go up
προσαναβαίνω to ascend farther, i.e. be promoted (take an upper (more honorable) seat)
Derivation: from G4314 and G305;
KJV Usage: go up. G4314 G305
Thayer:
1) to go up further
2) go up higher
