not in battle-order
ἄτακτος unarranged, i.e. (by implication) insubordinate (religiously)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5021;
KJV Usage: unruly. G1 G5021
Thayer:
1) disorderly, out of ranks (often so of soldiers)
2) irregular, inordinate, immoderate pleasures
3) deviating from the prescribed order or rule
