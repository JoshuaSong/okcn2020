καταπίνω
to gulp
καταπίνω to drink down, i.e. gulp entire (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G2596 and G4095;
KJV Usage: devour, drown, swallow (up). G2596 G4095
Thayer:
1) to drink down, swallow down
2) to devour
3) to swallow up, destroy
