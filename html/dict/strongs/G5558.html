χρυσόω
to make golden, gild
χρυσόω to gild, i.e. bespangle with golden ornaments
Derivation: from G5557;
KJV Usage: deck. G5557
Thayer:
1) to adorn with gold, to gild
1a) of a woman ornamented with gold so profusely that she seems to be gilded
