μῦθος
anything, delivered by word of mouth, word, speech
μῦθος a tale, i.e. fiction ("myth")
Derivation: perhaps from the same as G3453 (through the idea of tuition);
KJV Usage: fable. G3453
Thayer:
1) a speech, word, saying
2) a narrative, story
2a) a true narrative
2b) a fiction, a fable
2b1) an invention, a falsehood
