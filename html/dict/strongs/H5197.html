נטף
nâṭaph
naw-taf'
A primitive root; to ooze, that is, distil gradually; by implication to fall in drops; figuratively to speak by inspiration
KJV Usage: drop (-ping), prophesy (-et).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
