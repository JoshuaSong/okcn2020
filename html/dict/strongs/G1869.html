ἐπαίρω
to lift up and set on
ἐπαίρω to raise up (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G1909 and G142;
KJV Usage: exalt self, poise (lift, take) up. G1909 G142
Thayer:
1) to lift up, raise up, raise on high
2) metaph. to be lifted up with pride, to exalt one's self
