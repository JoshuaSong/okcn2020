בּרכה
be râkâh
ber-aw-kaw'
The same as H1293; Berakah, the name of an Israelite, and also of a valley in Palestine
KJV Usage: Berachah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּרכה
n pr m Berachah = "blessing"
1. a Benjamite, one of David's warriors n pr loc
2. a valley in the wilderness near Tekoa where Jehoshaphat and his people assembled to bless Jehovah after the overthrow of the hosts of the Moabites
Origin: the same as H1293
TWOT: None
