συναλίζω
to bring together, collect
συναλίζω to accumulate, i.e. convene
Derivation: from G4862 and ἁλίζω (to throng);
KJV Usage: assemble together. G4862
Thayer:
1) to gather together, assemble
2) to be assembled, meet with
