παθητός
one who has suffered: subject to passion
παθητός liable (i.e. doomed) to experience pain
Derivation: from the same as G3804;
KJV Usage: suffer. G3804
Thayer:
1) passible, endued with the capacity of suffering, capable of feeling
2) subject to the necessity of suffering, destined to suffer
