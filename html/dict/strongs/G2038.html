ἐργάζομαι
to work, labour
ἐργάζομαι to toil (as a task, occupation, etc.), (by implication) effect, be engaged in or with, etc.
Derivation: middle voice from G2041;
KJV Usage: commit, do, labor for, minister about, trade (by), work. G2041
Thayer:
1) to work, labour, do work
2) to trade, to make gains by trading, "do business"
3) to do, work out
3a) exercise, perform, commit
3b) to cause to exist, produce
4) to work for, earn by working, to acquire
