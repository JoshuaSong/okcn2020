ἀνταποδίδωμι
to give back, repay, tender in repayment
ἀνταποδίδωμι to requite (good or evil)
Derivation: from G473 and G591;
KJV Usage: recompense, render, repay. G473 G591
Thayer:
1) in a good sense, to repay, requite
2) in a bad sense, penalty and vengeance
