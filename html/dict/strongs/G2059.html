ἑρμηνεύω
to interpret
ἑρμηνεύω to translate
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G2060 (as the god of language);
KJV Usage: interpret. G2060
Thayer:
1) to explain in words, expound
2) to interpret
2a) to translate what has been spoken or written in a foreign tongue into the vernacular
