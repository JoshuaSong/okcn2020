στάσις a standing (properly, the act), i.e. (by analogy) position (existence); by implication, a popular uprising; figuratively, controversy
Derivation: from the base of G2476;
KJV Usage: dissension, insurrection, X standing, uproar. G2476
Thayer:
1) a standing, station, state
2) an insurrection
3) strife, insurrection
