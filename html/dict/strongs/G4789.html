συγκληρονόμος
a joint-heir with
συγκληρονόμος a co-heir, i.e. (by analogy) participant in common
Derivation: from G4862 and G2818;
KJV Usage: fellow (joint)-heir, heir together, heir with. G4862 G2818
Thayer:
1) a fellow heir, a joint heir
2) one who obtains something assigned to himself with others, a joint participant
