משׁיזבאל
me shêyzab'êl
mesh-ay-zab-ale'
From an equivalent to H7804 and H410; delivered of God; Meshezabel, an Israelite
KJV Usage: Meshezabeel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

משׁיזבאל
Meshezabeel = "God delivers"
1. ancestor of Meshullam who assisted Nehemiah in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem
a. perhaps the same as 2 and 3
2. a head of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
a. perhaps the same as 1 and 3
3. father of Pethahiah and descendant of Zerah the son of Judah
a. perhaps the same as 1 and 2
Origin: from an equiv. to H7804 and H410
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
