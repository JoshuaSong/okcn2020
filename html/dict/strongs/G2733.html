κατοικία
a settlement, colony: the foundation of a colony
κατοικία residence (properly, the condition; but by implication, the abode itself)
KJV Usage: habitation.
Thayer:
1) dwelling, habitation
2) to cause to dwell, to send or bring into an abode
3) to give a dwelling to
