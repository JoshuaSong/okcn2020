προσφορά
a bringing to, applying, application
προσφορά presentation; concretely, an oblation (bloodless) or sacrifice
Derivation: from G4374;
KJV Usage: offering (up). G4374
Thayer:
1) the act of offering, a bringing to
2) that which is offered, a gift, a present. In the NT a sacrifice, whether bloody or not: offering for sin, expiatory offering
