δυνατός
strong, mighty, able
δυνατός powerful or capable (literally or figuratively); neuter possible
Derivation: from G1410;
KJV Usage: able, could, (that is) mighty (man), possible, power, strong. G1410
Thayer:
1) able, powerful, mighty, strong
1a) mighty in wealth and influence
1b) strong in soul
1b1) to bear calamities and trials with fortitude and patience
1b2) strong in Christian virtue
2) to be able (to do something)
2a) mighty, excelling in something
2b) having power for something
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Ability, Able
  • Might (Noun), Mighty, Mightily, Mightier
  • Possible
  • Power (Noun, and Verb, to have, bring under)
  • Strong, Stronger
