σαγήνη
a large drag-net
σαγήνη a "seine" for fishing
Derivation: from a derivative of σάττω (to equip) meaning furniture, especially a pack-saddle (which in the East is merely a bag of netted rope);
KJV Usage: net.
Thayer:
1) a large fishing net, a drag net
