σκοτίζω
to make dark
σκοτίζω to obscure (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4655;
KJV Usage: darken. G4655
Thayer:
1) to cover with darkness, to darken
2) to be covered with darkness, be darkened
2a) of heavenly bodies as deprived of light
2b) metaph.
2b1) of the eyes
2b2) of the understanding
2b3) of the mind
