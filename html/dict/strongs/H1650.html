גּשׁוּר
ge shûr
ghesh-oor'
From an unused root (meaning to join); bridge; Geshur, a district of Syria
KJV Usage: Geshur, Geshurite.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּשׁוּר
Geshur or Geshurites = "proud beholder"
1. a people
2. a land in north Transjordania
Origin: from an unused root (meaning to join)
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
