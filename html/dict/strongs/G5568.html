ψαλμός
a twitching
ψαλμός a set piece of music, i.e. a sacred ode (accompanied with the voice, harp or other instrument; a "psalm"); collectively, the book of the Psalms
Derivation: from G5567;
KJV Usage: psalm. Compare G5603. G5567 G5603
Thayer:
1) a striking, twanging
1a) of a striking the chords of a musical instrument
1b) of a pious song, a psalm
For Synonyms see entry G5876
