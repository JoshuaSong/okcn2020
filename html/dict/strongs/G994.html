βοάω
to cry aloud, to shout
βοάω to halloo, i.e. shout (for help or in a tumultuous way)
Derivation: apparently a prolonged form of a primary verb;
KJV Usage: cry.
Thayer:
1) to raise a cry, of joy pain etc.
2) to cry, speak with a high, strong voice
3) to cry to one for help, to implore his aid
For Synonyms see entry G5823
