Ταβιθά the gazelle; Tabitha (i.e. Tabjetha), a Christian female
Derivation: of Chaldee origin (compare H6646);
KJV Usage: Tabitha. H6646
Thayer:
1) the name of the woman that Peter raised from the dead
Literal: Tabitha = "female gazelle"
