ἀσάλευτος
not agitated, tranquil
ἀσάλευτος unshaken, i.e. (by implication) immovable (figuratively)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4531;
KJV Usage: which cannot be moved, unmovable. G1 G4531
Thayer:
1) unshaken, unmoved
2) metaph. not liable to overthrow and disorder, firm stable
