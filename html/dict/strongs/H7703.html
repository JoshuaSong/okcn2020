שׁדד
shâdad
shaw-dad'
A primitive root; properly to be burly, that is, (figuratively) powerful (passively impregnable); by implication to ravage
KJV Usage: dead, destroy (-er), oppress, robber, spoil (-er), X utterly, (lay) waste.
