οἰκονομία
the management of a household
οἰκονομία administration (of a household or estate); specially, a (religious) "economy"
Derivation: from G3623;
KJV Usage: dispensation, stewardship. G3623
Thayer:
1) the management of a household or of household affairs
1a) specifically, the management, oversight, administration, of other's property
1b) the office of a manager or overseer, stewardship
1c) administration, dispensation
