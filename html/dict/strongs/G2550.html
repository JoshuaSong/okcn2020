κακοήθεια
badness of disposition, malignity
κακοήθεια bad character, i.e. (specially) mischievousness
Derivation: from a compound of G2556 and G2239;
KJV Usage: malignity. G2556 G2239
Thayer:
1) bad character, depravity of heart and life
2) malignant subtlety, malicious craftiness
