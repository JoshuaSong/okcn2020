καταφθείρω
to destroy
καταφθείρω to spoil entirely, i.e. (literally) to destroy; or (figuratively) to deprave;
Derivation: from G2596 and G5351;
KJV Usage: corrupt, utterly perish. G2596 G5351
Thayer:
1) to corrupt, deprave
1a) corrupted in mind
2) to destroy
2a) to be destroyed, to perish
