ἐπακούω
to listen
ἐπακούω to hearken (favorably) to
Derivation: from G1909 and G191;
KJV Usage: hear. G1909 G191
Thayer:
1) to give ear to, listen to
1a) to perceive by the ear
2) to listen to
2a) to hear with favour, grant one's request
