one who watches over, an overseer, guardian
hitting the mark
ἐπίσκοπος a superintendent, i.e. Christian officer in genitive case charge of a (or the) church (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G1909 and G4649 (in the sense of G1983);
KJV Usage: bishop, overseer. G1909 G4649 G1983
Thayer:
1) an overseer
1a) a man charged with the duty of seeing that things to be done by others are done rightly, any curator, guardian or superintendent
1b) the superintendent, elder, or overseer of a Christian church
