עטה
‛âṭâh
aw-taw'
A primitive root; to wrap, that is, cover, veil, clothe or roll
KJV Usage: array, self, be clad, (put a) cover (-ing, self), fill, put on, X surely, turn aside.

1. to cover, enwrap, wrap oneself, envelop oneself

1. to wrap, envelop oneself

3. mourner, one who covers (participle)
b. (Hiphil) to cover, enwrap, envelop
2. to grasp
