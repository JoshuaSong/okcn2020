ἀποθησαυρίζω
to store, hoard up
ἀποθησαυρίζω to treasure away
Derivation: from G575 and G2343;
KJV Usage: lay up in store. G575 G2343
Thayer:
1) to put away, lay by in store, to treasure away
2) to store up abundance for future use
