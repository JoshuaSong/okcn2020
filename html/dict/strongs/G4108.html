πλάνος
leading astray, cheating, deceiving
πλάνος roving (as a tramp), i.e. (by implication) an impostor or misleader;
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: deceiver, seducing.
Thayer:
1) wandering, roving
2) misleading, leading into error
2a) a vagabond, "tramp", imposter
2b) corrupter, deceiver
