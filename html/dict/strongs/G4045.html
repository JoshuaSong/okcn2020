περιπίπτω
to fall around, so as to embrace
περιπίπτω to fall into something that is all around, i.e. light among or upon, be surrounded with
Derivation: from G4012 and G4098;
KJV Usage: fall among (into). G4012 G4098
Thayer:
1) so to fall into as to be encompassed
