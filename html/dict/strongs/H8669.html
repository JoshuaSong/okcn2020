תּשׁוּקה
te shûqâh
tesh-oo-kaw'
From H7783 in the original sense of stretching out after; a longing
KJV Usage: desire.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּשׁוּקה

1. desire, longing, craving
a. of man for woman
b. of woman for man
c. of beast to devour
Origin: from H7783 in the original sense of stretching out after
