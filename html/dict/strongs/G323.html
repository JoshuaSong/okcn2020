ἀνάδειξις
a proclamation
ἀνάδειξις (the act of) exhibition
Derivation: from G322;
KJV Usage: shewing. G322
Thayer:
1) a pointing out, a public showing forth
2) a proclaiming, announcing, inaugurating of such as are elected to office
