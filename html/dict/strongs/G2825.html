κλίνη
that on which one lies, a couch
κλίνη a couch (for sleep, sickness, sitting or eating)
Derivation: from G2827;
KJV Usage: bed, table. G2827
Thayer:
1) a small bed, a couch
2) a couch to recline on at meals
3) a couch on which a sick man is carried
