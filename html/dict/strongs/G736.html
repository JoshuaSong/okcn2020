ἀρτέμων
a foresail
ἀρτέμων properly, something ready (or else more remotely from G142 (compare G740); something hung up), i.e. (specially) the topsail (rather foresail or jib) of a vessel
Derivation: from a derivative of G737;
KJV Usage: mainsail. G737 G142 G740
Thayer:
1) a top-sail or foresail of a ship
