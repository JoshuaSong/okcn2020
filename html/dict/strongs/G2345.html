θιγγάνω
to touch, handle
θιγγάνω to manipulate, i.e. have to do with; by implication, to injure
Derivation: a prolonged form of an obsolete primary θίγω (to finger);
KJV Usage: handle, touch.
Thayer:
1) to touch, handle
1a) to do violence to, injury
