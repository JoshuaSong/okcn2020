sheba‛
sheh'-bah

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁבע
n pr m Sheba = "seven"
1. Benjamite, son of Bichri and one who led a rebellion against David
2. Gadite, brother of Michael, Meshullam, Jorai, Jachan, Zia, and Heber n pr loc
3. a town in Simeon
Origin: the same as H7651
