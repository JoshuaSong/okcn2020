ἀμετάθετος unchangeable, or (neuter as abstract) unchangeability
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3346;
KJV Usage: immutable(-ility). G1 G3346
Thayer:
1) not transposed, not to be transferred, fixed, unalterable
