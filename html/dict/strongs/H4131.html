מוט
môṭ
mote'
A primitive root; to waver; by implication to slip, shake, fall
KJV Usage: be carried, cast, be out of course, be fallen in decay, X exceedingly, fall (-ing down), be (re-) moved, be ready shake, slide, slip.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מוט

1. to totter, shake, slip
a. (Qal) to totter, shake, slip
b. (Niphal) to be shaken, be moved, be overthrown
c. (Hiphil) to dislodge, let fall, drop
d. (Hithpael) to be greatly shaken
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1158
Parts of Speech: Verb
