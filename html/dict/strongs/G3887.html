παραμένω to stay near, i.e. remain (literally, tarry; or figuratively, be permanent, persevere)
Derivation: from G3844 and G3306;
KJV Usage: abide, continue. G3844 G3306 G3306 G3844
Thayer:
1) to remain beside, continue always near
2) to survive, remain alive
