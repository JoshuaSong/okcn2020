χαριτόω
to shew grace to
χαριτόω to grace, i.e. indue with special honor
Derivation: from G5485;
KJV Usage: make accepted, be highly favoured. G5485
Thayer:
1) to make graceful
1a) charming, lovely, agreeable
2) to peruse with grace, compass with favour
3) to honour with blessings
