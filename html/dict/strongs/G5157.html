τροπή
a turn, turning
τροπή a turn ("trope"), i.e. revolution (figuratively, variation)
Derivation: from an apparently primary τρέπω to turn;
KJV Usage: turning.
Thayer:
1) a turning
1a) of the heavenly bodies
