ἀναδίδωμι
to hold up and give
ἀναδίδωμι to hand over
Derivation: from G303 and G1325;
KJV Usage: deliver. G303 G1325
Thayer:
1) to give forth, send up, so the earth producing plants, yielding fruit etc.
2) to deliver up, hand over
