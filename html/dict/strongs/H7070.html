קנה
qâneh
kaw-neh'
From H7069; a reed (as erect); by resemblance a rod (especially for measuring), shaft, tube, stem, the radius (of the arm), beam (of a steelyard)
KJV Usage: balance, bone, branch, calamus, cane, reed, X spearman, stalk.
