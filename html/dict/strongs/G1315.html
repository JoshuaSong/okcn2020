διαχειρίζομαι to handle thoroughly, i.e. lay violent hands upon
Derivation: from G1223 and a derivative of G5495;
KJV Usage: kill, slay. G1223 G5495
Thayer:
1) to move by the use of the hands, take in hand, manage, administer, govern
2) to lay hands on, slay, kill [with one's own hand]
