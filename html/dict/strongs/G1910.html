ἐπιβαίνω
to go upon
ἐπιβαίνω to walk upon, i.e. mount, ascend, embark, arrive
Derivation: from G1909 and the base of G939;
KJV Usage: come (into), enter into, go abroad, sit upon, take ship. G1909 G939
Thayer:
1) to get upon, mount
1a) to embark in
1b) to go aboard (a ship)
2) to set foot in, enter
