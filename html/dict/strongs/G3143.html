μαρτύρομαι
to call to witness, attest, invoke
μαρτύρομαι to be adduced as a witness, i.e. (figuratively) to obtest (in affirmation or exhortation)
Derivation: middle voice from G3144;
KJV Usage: take to record, testify. G3144
Thayer:
1) to cite a witness, bring forward a witness, call to witness
1a) to affirm be appeal to God, to declare solemnly, protest
2) to conjure, beseech as in God's name, exhort solemnly
