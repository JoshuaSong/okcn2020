συμπάσχω
to suffer together, be affected by the same thing
συμπάσχω to experience pain jointly or of the same kind (specially, persecution; to "sympathize")
Derivation: from G4862 and G3958 (including its alternate);
KJV Usage: suffer with. G4862 G3958
Thayer:
1) to suffer or feel pain together
2) to suffer evils (troubles, persecutions) in the like manner with another
