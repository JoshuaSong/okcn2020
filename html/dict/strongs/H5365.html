נקר
nâqar
naw-kar'
A primitive root; to bore (penetrate, quarry)
KJV Usage: dig, pick out, pierce, put (thrust) out.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נקר

1. to bore, pick, dig, pick out
a. (Qal) to bore, pick, dig
b. (Piel) to bore out
c. (Pual) to be dug out
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1418
