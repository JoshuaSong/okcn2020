chelqath hatstsûrı̂ym

From H2520 and the plural of H6697; with the article inserted; smoothness of the rocks; Chelkath Hatstsurim, a place in Palestine

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חלקת הצּרים
Helkath-hazzurim = "field of swords"
1. a place near the pool of Gibeon where Ishbosheth's men were killed by David's men under Joab
Origin: from H2520 and the pl. of H6697, with the article inserted
