θηλάζω
to suckle
θηλάζω to suckle, (by implication) to suck
Derivation: from θηλή (the nipple);
KJV Usage: (give) suck(-ling).
Thayer:
1) to give the breast, give suck, to suckle
2) to suck
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Suck (give suck), Suckling
