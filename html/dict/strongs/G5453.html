φύω
to bring forth, produce, put forth
φύω probably originally, to "puff" or blow, i.e. to swell up; but only used in the implied sense, to germinate or grow (sprout, produce), literally or figuratively
Derivation: a primary verb;
KJV Usage: spring (up).
Thayer:
1) to beget, bring forth, produce
2) to be born, to spring up, to grow
3) to shoot forth, spring up
