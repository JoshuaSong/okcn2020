κάμνω
to work
κάμνω properly, to toil, i.e. (by implication) to tire (figuratively, faint, sicken)
Derivation: apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: faint, sick, be wearied.
Thayer:
1) to grow weary, be weary
2) to be sick
