ὑπερέχω
to hold
ὑπερέχω to hold oneself above, i.e. (figuratively) to excel; participle (as adjective, or neuter as noun) superior, superiority
Derivation: from G5228 and G2192;
KJV Usage: better, excellency, higher, pass, supreme. G5228 G2192
Thayer:
1) to have or hold over one
2) to stand out, rise above, overtop
2a) to be above, be superior in rank, authority, power
2a1) the prominent men, rulers
2b) to excel, to be superior, better than, to surpass
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Better (be)
  • Excel, Excellency, Excellent
  • Higher
  • Pass, come to Pass
  • Supreme
