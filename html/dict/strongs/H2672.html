חצב חצב
châtsab châtsêb
khaw-tsab', khaw-tsabe'
A primitive root; to cut or carve (wood, stone or other material); by implication to hew, split, square, quarry, engrave
KJV Usage: cut, dig, divide, grave, hew (out, -er), make, mason.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חצב חצב

1. to dig, cleave, divide, hew, make, cut out, dig out, cut down, quarry, hewer, mason
a. (Qal)
1. to hew out, dig
2. to hew 1a
b. stone 1a
c. wood
1. to hew in pieces (metaph.)
2. to divide, cleave
d. (Niphal) to be cut, be hewn, be engraved
e. (Hiphil) to hew into pieces, cut up into pieces
f. (Pual) to be cut from, be hewn from
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 718
Parts of Speech: Verb
