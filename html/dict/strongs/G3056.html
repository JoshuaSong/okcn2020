λόγος
the word
λόγος something said (including the thought); by implication, a topic (subject of discourse), also reasoning (the mental faculty) or motive; by extension, a computation; specially, (with the article in John) the Divine Expression (i.e. Christ)
Derivation: from G3004;
KJV Usage: account, cause, communication, X concerning, doctrine, fame, X have to do, intent, matter, mouth, preaching, question, reason, + reckon, remove, say(-ing), shew, X speaker, speech, talk, thing, + none of these things move me, tidings, treatise, utterance, word, work. G3004
Thayer:
1) of speech

1b) what someone has said
1b1) a word

1b3) decree, mandate or order
1b4) of the moral precepts given by God
