ἀποσκευάζω
to pull off
ἀποσκευάζω to pack up (one's) baggage
Derivation: from G575 and a derivative of G4632;
KJV Usage: take up… carriages. G575 G4632
Thayer:
1) to carry off goods and chattels
2) to pack up and carry off
3) to carry off one's personal property or provide for its carrying away
