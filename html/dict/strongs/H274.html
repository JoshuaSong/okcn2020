אחזיהוּ אחזיה
'ăchazyâh 'ăchazyâhû
akh-az-yaw', akh-az-yaw'-hoo
From H270 and H3050; Jah has seized; Achazjah, the name of a Jewish and an Israelitish king
KJV Usage: Ahaziah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אחזיהוּ אחזיה
Ahaziah = "Jehovah (Yahu) holds (possesses)"
1. ruler of Israel, son of Ahab
2. ruler of Judah, son of Jehoram (Joram)
Origin: from H270 and H3050
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
