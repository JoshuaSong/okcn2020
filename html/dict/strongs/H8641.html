תּרמה תּרוּמה
te rûmâh te rûmâh
ter-oo-maw', ter-oo-maw'
(The second form used in Deuteronomy 12:11); from H7311; a present (as offered up), especially in sacrifice or as tribute
KJV Usage: gift, heave offering ([shoulder]), oblation, offered (-ing).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּרמה תּרוּמה

1. contribution, offering
a. a heave offering
b. any offering
c. an offering to God
d. an offering (of grain, money, etc)
e. contribution
Origin: from H7311
TWOT: 2133i
