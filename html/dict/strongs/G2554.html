κακοποιέω
to do ill, play the knave
κακοποιέω to be a bad-doer, i.e. (objectively) to injure, or (genitive) to sin
Derivation: from G2555;
KJV Usage: do(ing) evil. G2555
Thayer:
1) to do harm
2) to do evil, do wrong
