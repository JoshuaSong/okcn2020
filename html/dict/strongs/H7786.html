שׂוּר
śûr
soor
A primitive root; properly to vanquish; by implication to rule (causatively crown)
KJV Usage: make princes, have power, reign. See H5493.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׂוּר

1. to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over
a. (Qal) to rule over, govern
b. (Hiphil) to make rulers, play the prince
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2287
Parts of Speech: Verb
