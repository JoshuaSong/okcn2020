καταστρέφω
to turn down, trample on
καταστρέφω to turn upside down, i.e. upset
Derivation: from G2596 and G4762;
KJV Usage: overthrow. G2596 G4762
Thayer:
1) to turn over, turn under
1a) the soil with a plough
2) to overturn, overthrow, throw down
