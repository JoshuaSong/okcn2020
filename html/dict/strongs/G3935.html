παρίημι
to let drop beside
παρίημι to let by, i.e. relax
Derivation: from G3844 and hiemi (to send);
KJV Usage: hang down. G3844
Thayer:
1) to let pass
1a) to pass by, neglect
1b) to disregard, omit
2) to relax, loose, let go
3) relaxed, unstrung, weakened, exhausted
