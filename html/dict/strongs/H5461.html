סגן
sâgân
saw-gawn'
From an unused root meaning to superintend; a proefect of a province
KJV Usage: prince, ruler.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

סגן

1. ruler, prefect, governor, a subordinate ruler
a. prefects (of Assyria and Babylon)
b. petty rulers or officials
Origin: from an unused root meaning to superintend
TWOT: 1461
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
