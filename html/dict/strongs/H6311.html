פּו פּא פּה
pôh pô' pô
po, po, po
Probably from a primitive inseparable particle פּ p (the second form; of demonstrative force) and H1931; this place (French, icil), that is, here or hence
KJV Usage: here, hither, the one (other, this, that) side.
