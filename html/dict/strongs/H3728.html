כּפשׁ
kâphash
kaw-fash'
A primitive root; to tread down; figuratively to humiliate
KJV Usage: cover.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּפשׁ

1. (Hiphil) to make bent, press or bend together
2. (CLBL) to bend down
a. (Hiphil) to cause to bend down, trample
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1027
Parts of Speech: Verb
