λειτουργός
one who performed a
λειτουργός a public servant, i.e. a functionary in the Temple or Gospel, or (genitive case) a worshipper (of God) or benefactor (of man)
Derivation: from a derivative of G2992 and G2041;
KJV Usage: minister(-ed). G2992 G2041
Thayer:
1) a public minister, a servant of the state
2) a minister, servant
2a) so of military labourers
2b) of the temple
2b1) of one busied with holy things
2b2) of a priest
2c) of the servants of a king
