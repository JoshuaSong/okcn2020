ἐκτρέφω
to bring up from childhood, rear up
ἐκτρέφω to rear up to maturity, i.e. (genitive case) to cherish or train
Derivation: from G1537 and G5142;
KJV Usage: bring up, nourish. G1537 G5142
Thayer:
1) to nourish up to maturity, to nourish
2) to nurture, bring up
