ἄγρα
a catching, hunting
ἄγρα (abstractly) a catching (of fish); also (concretely) a haul (of fish)
Derivation: from G71;
KJV Usage: draught. G71
Thayer:
1) a catching, hunting
2) that which is taken in hunting, the booty, prey
2a) of birds or beasts, game
2b) of fish, a draught or haul
