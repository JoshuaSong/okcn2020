ἀναλίσκω
to use up, to spend, lavish
ἀναλίσκω properly, to use up, i.e. destroy
Derivation: from G303 and a form of the alternate of G138;
KJV Usage: consume. G303 G138
Thayer:
1) to expend
1a) to consume, e.g. spend money
2) to consume, use up, destroy
