'ăchı̂yṭûb

From H251 and H2898; brother of goodness; Achitub, the name of several priests

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אחיטוּב
Ahitub = "my brother is good (goodness)"
1. a grandson of Eli
2. the father of Zadok the priest
Origin: from H251 and H2898
TWOT: None
