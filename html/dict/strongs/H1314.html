בּשׂם בּשׂם
beśem bôśem
beh'-sem, bo'-sem
From the same as H1313; fragrance; by implication spicery; also the balsam plant
KJV Usage: smell, spice, sweet (odour).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּשׂם בּשׂם

1. spice, balsam, balsam tree, perfume
2. (TWOT) sweet, sweet smell, sweet odour
Origin: from the same as H1313
TWOT: 290a
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
