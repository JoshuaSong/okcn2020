ἐπιτήδειος
made for an end
ἐπιτήδειος serviceable, i.e. (by implication) requisite
Derivation: from ἐπιτηδές (enough);
KJV Usage: things which are needful.
Thayer:
1) fit, suitable, convenient, advantageous
2) needful, esp. of the necessities of life
