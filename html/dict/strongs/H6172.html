ערוה
‛ervâh
er-vaw'
From H6168; nudity, literally (especially the pudenda) or figuratively (disgrace, blemish)
KJV Usage: nakedness, shame, unclean (-ness).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ערוה

1. nakedness, nudity, shame, pudenda
a. pudenda (implying shameful exposure)
b. nakedness of a thing, indecency, improper behaviour
c. exposed, undefended (fig.)
