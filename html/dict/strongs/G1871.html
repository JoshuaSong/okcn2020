ἐπαιτέω
to ask besides
ἐπαιτέω to ask for
Derivation: from G1909 and G154;
KJV Usage: beg. G1909 G154
Thayer:
1) to ask besides, ask for more
2) to ask again and again, importunately
3) to beg, to ask alms
