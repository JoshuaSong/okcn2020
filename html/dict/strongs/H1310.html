בּשׁל
bâshal
baw-shal'
A primitive root; properly to boil up; hence to be done in cooking; figuratively to ripen
KJV Usage: bake, boil, bring forth, is ripe, roast, seethe, sod (be sodden).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

1. to boil, cook, bake, roast, ripen, grow ripe
a. (Qal)

2. to grow ripe, ripen
b. (Piel)
1. to boil
2. to cook
c. (Pual)
1. to be boiled
2. to be sodden
d. (Hiphil)
1. to ripen
