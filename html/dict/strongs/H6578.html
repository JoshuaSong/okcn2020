פּרת
pe râth
per-awth'
From an unused root meaning to break forth; rushing; Perath (that is, Euphrates), a river of the East
KJV Usage: Euphrates.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּרת
Euphrates = "fruitfulness"
1. the largest and longest river of western Asia; rises from two chief sources in the Armenian mountains and flows into the Persian Gulf
Origin: from an unused root meaning to break forth
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
