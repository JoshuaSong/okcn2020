Μελχί Melchi (i.e. Malki), the name of two Israelites
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H4428 with pronominal suffix, my king);
KJV Usage: Melchi. H4428
Thayer:
1) the son of Janna in the genealogy of Christ
2) Melchi was the son of Addi in the genealogy of Christ
Literal: Melchi = "my king, my counsel"
