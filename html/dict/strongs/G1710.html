ἐμπορεύομαι
to travel
ἐμπορεύομαι to travel in (a country as a pedlar), i.e. (by implication) to trade
Derivation: from G1722 and G4198;
KJV Usage: buy and sell, make merchandise. G1722 G4198
Thayer:
1) to go a trading, to travel for business, to traffic, trade
2) of a thing, to import for sale
3) to deal in
4) to use a person or a thing for gain
