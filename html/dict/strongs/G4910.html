συνευωχέω to entertain sumptuously in company with, i.e. (middle voice or passive) to revel together
Derivation: from G4862 and a derivative of a presumed compound of G2095 and a derivative of G2192 (meaning to be in good condition, i.e. (by implication) to fare well, or feast);
KJV Usage: feast with. G4862 G2095 G2192
Thayer:
1) to entertain together
2) to feast sumptuously with
