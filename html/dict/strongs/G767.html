ἄσημος
without mark
ἄσημος unmarked, i.e. (figuratively) ignoble
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and the base of G4591;
KJV Usage: mean. G1 G4591
Thayer:
1) unmarked or unstamped
1a) of money
2) unknown, of no mark, insignificant, ignoble
