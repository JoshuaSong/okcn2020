ἄχυρον
husks, chaff, bran
ἄχυρον chaff (as diffusive)
Derivation: perhaps remotely from χέω (to shed forth);
KJV Usage: chaff.
Thayer:
1) a stalk of grain from which the kernels have been beaten out
2) straw broken up by a threshing machine, chaff
