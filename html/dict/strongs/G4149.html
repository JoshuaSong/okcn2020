πλοῦτος
wealth, riches
πλοῦτος wealth (as fulness), i.e. (literally) money, possessions, or (figuratively) abundance, richness, (specially), valuable bestowment
Derivation: from the base of G4130;
KJV Usage: riches. G4130
Thayer:
1) riches, wealth
1a) abundance of external possessions
1b) fulness, abundance, plenitude
1c) a good i.e. that with which one is enriched
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
