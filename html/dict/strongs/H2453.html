Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חכמוני
Hachmoni or Hachmonite = "wise" n pr m
1. father of one of David's mighty warriors
2. father of the tutor of David's sons n patr m
3. the family of one of David's servants
Origin: from H2449
TWOT: None
