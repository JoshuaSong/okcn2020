συστροφή a twisting together, i.e. (figuratively) a secret coalition, riotous crowd
Derivation: from G4962;
KJV Usage: + band together, concourse. G4962
Thayer:
1) a twisting up together, a binding together
2) a secret combination, a coalition, conspiracy
3) a concourse of disorderly persons, a riot
