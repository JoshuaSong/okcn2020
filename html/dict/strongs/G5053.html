τελευτάω
to complete, finish, accomplish
τελευτάω to finish life (by implication, of G979), i.e. expire (demise)
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G5055;
KJV Usage: be dead, decease, die. G5055 G979
Thayer:
1) to finish, bring to and end, close
2) to have an end or close, come to an end
