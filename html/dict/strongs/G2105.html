εὐδία
fair weather
εὐδία a clear sky, i.e. fine weather
Derivation: feminine from G2095 and the alternate of G2203 (as the god of the weather);
KJV Usage: fair weather. G2095 G2203
Thayer:
1) a serene sky, fair weather
