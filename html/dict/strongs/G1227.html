διαβλέπω
to look straight before one
διαβλέπω to look through, i.e. recover full vision
Derivation: from G1223 and G991;
KJV Usage: see clearly. G1223 G991
Thayer:
1) to look through, penetrate by vision
1a) to look fixedly, stare straight before one
1b) to see clearly
