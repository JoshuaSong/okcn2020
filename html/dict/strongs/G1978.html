ἐπίσημος
having a mark on
ἐπίσημος remarkable, i.e. (figuratively) eminent
Derivation: from G1909 and some form of the base of G4591;
KJV Usage: notable, of note. G1909 G4591
Thayer:
1) having a mark on it, marked, stamped, coined
2) marked
2a) in a good sense
2a1) of note, illustrious
2b) in a bad sense
2b1) notorious, infamous
