διαδίδωμι
to give from hand to hand, to pass on, hand over
διαδίδωμι to give throughout a crowd, i.e. deal out; also to deliver over (as to a successor)
Derivation: from G1223 and G1325;
KJV Usage: (make) distribute(-ion), divide, give. G1223 G1325
Thayer:
1) to distribute, divide among several
2) to give over, deliver
