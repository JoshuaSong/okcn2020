στηρίζω
to make fast, prop, fix, set
στηρίζω to set fast, i.e. (literally) to turn resolutely in a certain direction, or (figuratively) to confirm
Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G2476 (like G4731);
KJV Usage: fix, (e-)stablish, stedfastly set, strengthen. G2476 G4731
Thayer:
1) to make stable, place firmly, set fast, fix
2) to strengthen, make firm
3) to render constant, confirm, one's mind
