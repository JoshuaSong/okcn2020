ψευδόχριστος
a false Christ
ψευδόχριστος a spurious Messiah
Derivation: from G5571 and G5547;
KJV Usage: false Christ. G5571 G5547
Thayer:
1) a false Christ (or Messiah)
2) one who falsely lays claim to the name and office of the Messiah
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Anti-Christ, false Christ
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Antichrist
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Christs (false)
