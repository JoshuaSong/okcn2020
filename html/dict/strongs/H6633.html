צבא
tsâbâ'
tsaw-baw'
A primitive root; to mass (an army or servants)
KJV Usage: assemble, fight, perform, muster, wait upon, war.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

צבא

1. to go forth, wage war, fight, serve
a. (Qal)
1. to wage war, fight, go forth to war
2. to serve (at sacred tent)
b. (Hiphil) to cause to go forth, muster
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1865
Parts of Speech: Verb
