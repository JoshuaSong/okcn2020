κυριεύω
to be lord
κυριεύω to rule
Derivation: from G2962;
KJV Usage: have dominion over, lord, be lord of, exercise lordship over. G2962
Thayer:
1) to be lord of, to rule, have dominion over
2) of things and forces
2a) to exercise influence upon, to have power over
