Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אשׁכּל
Eshcol = "cluster"
1. an area of Hebron, the valley of Eshcol
2. an Amorite, the brother of Mamre, dwelling in Hebron
Origin: the same as H811
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name
