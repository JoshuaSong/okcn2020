ἀπελπίζω
to give up in despair, to despair
ἀπελπίζω to hope out, i.e. fully expect
Derivation: from G575 and G1679;
KJV Usage: hope for again. G575 G1679
Thayer:
1) nothing despairing
2) despairing of no one
3) causing no one to despair
