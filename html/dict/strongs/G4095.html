πίνω
to drink
πίνω ; to imbibe (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: a prolonged form of πίω ; which (together with another form) πόω ; occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses;
KJV Usage: drink.
Thayer:
1) to drink
2) figuratively, to receive into the soul what serves to refresh strengthen, nourish it unto life eternal
