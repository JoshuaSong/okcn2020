μαρτυρέω
to be a witness, to bear witness, give evidence, bear testimony
μαρτυρέω to be a witness, i.e. testify (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G3144;
KJV Usage: charge, give (evidence), bear record, have (obtain, of) good (honest) report, be well reported of, testify, give (have) testimony, (be, bear, give, obtain) witness. G3144
Thayer:
1) to be a witness, to bear witness, i.e. to affirm that one has seen or heard or experienced something, or that he knows it because taught by divine revelation or inspiration
1a) to give (not to keep back) testimony
1b) to utter honourable testimony, give a good report
1c) conjure, implore
