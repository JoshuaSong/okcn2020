ye hôyârı̂yb
yeh-ho-yaw-reeb'
From H3068 and H7378; Jehovah will contend; Jehojarib, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Jehoiarib. Compare H3114.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

יהויריב
Jehoiarib = "Jehovah contends"
1. A priest in Jerusalem
2. the head of the 1st of the 24 courses of priests in David's time
Origin: from H3068 and H7378
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
