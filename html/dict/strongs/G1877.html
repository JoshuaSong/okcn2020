ἐπανάγω
to bring up: to stir up, excite
ἐπανάγω to lead up on, i.e. (technical) to put out (to sea); (intransitively) to return
Derivation: from G1909 and G321;
KJV Usage: launch (thrust) out, return. G1909 G321
Thayer:
1) to lead up upon
1a) a ship upon the deep, to put out
2) to lead back
3) to return
