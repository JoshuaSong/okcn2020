ὑπακοή
obedience
ὑπακοή attentive hearkening, i.e. (by implication) compliance or submission
Derivation: from G5219;
KJV Usage: obedience, (make) obedient, obey(-ing). G5219
Thayer:
1) obedience, compliance, submission
2) obedience rendered to anyone's counsels, an obedience shown in observing the requirements of Christianity
