ἄβυσσος
with no bottom, bottomless, unfathomed
ἄβυσσος depthless, i.e. (specially) (infernal) "abyss"
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a variation of G1037;
KJV Usage: deep, (bottomless) pit. G1 G1037
Thayer:
1) bottomless
2) unbounded
3) the abyss
3a) the pit
3b) the immeasurable depth
3c) of Orcus, a very deep gulf or chasm in the lowest parts of the earth used as the common receptacle of the dead and especially as the abode of demons
