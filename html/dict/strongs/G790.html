ἀστατέω
to be unstable
ἀστατέω to be non-stationary, i.e. (figuratively) homeless
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2476;
KJV Usage: have no certain dwelling-place. G1 G2476
Thayer:
1) to wander about, to rove without a settled abode
