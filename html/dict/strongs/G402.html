ἀναχωρέω
to go back
ἀναχωρέω to retire
Derivation: from G303 and G5562;
KJV Usage: depart, give place, go (turn) aside, withdraw self. G303 G5562
Thayer:
1) to go back, return
2) to withdraw
2a) so as to leave a room
2b) of those who through fear seek some other place, or shun sight
