ἐπιγράφω
to mark the surface, just pierce, graze
ἐπιγράφω to inscribe (physically or mentally)
Derivation: from G1909 and G1125;
KJV Usage: inscription, write in (over, thereon). G1909 G1125
Thayer:
1) to write upon, inscribe
2) metaph. to write upon the mind
