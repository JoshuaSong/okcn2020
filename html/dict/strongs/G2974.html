κωφός
blunt, dull, obtuse
κωφός blunted, i.e. (figuratively) of hearing (deaf) or speech (dumb)
Derivation: from G2875;
KJV Usage: deaf, dumb, speechless. G2875
Thayer:
1) blunted, dull
1a) blunted (or lamed) in tongue, dumb
1b) blunted, dull in hearing
1c) deaf
