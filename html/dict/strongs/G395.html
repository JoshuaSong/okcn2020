ἀνατολή
a rising, rise
ἀνατολή a rising of light, i.e. dawn (figuratively); by implication, the east (also in plural)
Derivation: from G393;
KJV Usage: dayspring, east, rising. G393
Thayer:
1) a rising (of the sun and stars)
2) the east (the direction of the sun's rising)
