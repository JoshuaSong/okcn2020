προκαλέομαι to call forth to oneself (challenge), i.e. (by implication) to irritate
Derivation: middle voice from G4253 and G2564;
KJV Usage: provoke. G4253 G2564
Thayer:
1) to call forth
2) to call forth to one's self
2a) esp. to challenge to a combat or contest with one
3) to provoke, to irritate
