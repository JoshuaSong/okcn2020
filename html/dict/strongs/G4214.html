πόσος
bulk)
πόσος interrogative pronoun (of amount) how much (large, long or (plural) many)
Derivation: from an absolute πός (who, what) and G3739;
KJV Usage: how great (long, many), what. G3739
Thayer:
1) how great
2) how much
3) how many
