בּארת בּני־יעקן
be 'êrôth be nêy - ya‛ăqan
be-ay-roth' be-nay' yah-a-can'
From the feminine plural of H875, and the plural contraction of H1121, and H3292; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan; Beeroth Bene-Jaakan, a place in the Desert
KJV Usage: Beeroth of the children of Jaakan.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּארת בּני־יעקן
Beeroth Bene-Jaakan or Beeroth = "wells" or "wells of the sons of Jaakan"
1. an encampment for Israel in the wilderness probably in the country of the Horites
Origin: from the fem. pl. of H875, and the pl. contraction of H1121, and H3292
TWOT: None
