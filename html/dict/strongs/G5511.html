χλαμύς a military cloak
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: robe.
Thayer:
1) a chalamys, an outer garment usually worn over the tunic
2) a kind of short cloak worn by soldiers, military officers, magistrates, kings, emperors
