σκηνοποιός
tent-making
σκηνοποιός a manufacturer of tents
Derivation: from G4633 and G4160;
KJV Usage: tent-maker. G4633 G4160
Thayer:
1) a tentmaker
2) one that makes small portable tents, of leather or cloth of goat's hair or linen, for the use of travellers
