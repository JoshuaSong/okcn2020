ἑρπετόν
a walking animal, quadruped
ἑρπετόν a reptile, i.e. (by Hebraism (compare H7431)) a small animal
Derivation: neuter of a derivative of ἕρπω (to creep);
KJV Usage: creeping thing, serpent. H7431
Thayer:
1) a creeping animal, reptile
1a) used chiefly of snakes
2) an animal of any sort
2a) four-legged animals and birds
2b) marine animals
