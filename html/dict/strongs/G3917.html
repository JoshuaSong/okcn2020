πάρδαλις
the pard
πάρδαλις a leopard
Derivation: feminine of πάρδος (a panther);
KJV Usage: leopard.
Thayer:
1) a pard, panther, leopard
2) a very fierce Asiatic and African animal, having a tawny skin marked with large black spots
