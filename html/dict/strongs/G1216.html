Δημήτριος Demetrius, the name of an Ephesian and of a Christian
Derivation: from Δημήτηρ (Ceres);
KJV Usage: Demetrius.
Thayer:
1) a heathen silversmith at Ephesus
2) a certain Christian, 3Jo 12
Literal: Demetrius = "belonging to Ceres"
