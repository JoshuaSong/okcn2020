Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׂוכו שׂכה שׂוכה
Shocho or Shochoh or Sochoh or Shoco or Socoh = "bushy"
1. a town in the lowlands of Judah
2. a town in the mountain district of Judah
Origin: from H7753
TWOT: None
