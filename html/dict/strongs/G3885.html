παραλυτικός
paralytic
παραλυτικός as if dissolved, i.e. "paralytic"
Derivation: from a derivative of G3886;
KJV Usage: that had (sick of) the palsy. G3886
Thayer:
1) paralytic
1a) suffering from the relaxing of the nerves of one's side
1b) disabled, weak of limb
