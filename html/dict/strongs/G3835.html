πανοῦργος
ready to do anything wicked, knavish, villanous
πανοῦργος all-working, i.e. adroit (shrewd)
Derivation: from G3956 and G2041;
KJV Usage: crafty. G3956 G2041
Thayer:
1) skilful, clever
1a) in a good sense, fit to undertake and accomplish anything, dexterous, wise, sagacious, skilful
1b) in a bad sense, crafty, cunning, knavish, treacherous, deceitful
