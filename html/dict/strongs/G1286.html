διασείω
to shake violently
διασείω to shake thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) to intimidate
Derivation: from G1223 and G4579;
KJV Usage: do violence to. G1223 G4579
Thayer:
1) to shake thoroughly
2) to make to tremble
3) to terrify
4) to agitate
5) to extort from one by intimidation money or other property
