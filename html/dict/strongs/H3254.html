יסף
yâsaph
yaw-saf'
A primitive root; to add or augment (often adverbially to continue to do a thing)
KJV Usage: add, X again, X any more, X cease, X come more, + conceive again, continue, exceed, X further, X gather together, get more, give moreover, X henceforth, increase (more and more), join, X longer (bring, do, make, much, put), X (the, much, yet) more (and more), proceed (further), prolong, put, be [strong-] er, X yet, yield.

יסף

1. to add, increase, do again
a. (Qal) to add, increase, do again
b. (Niphal)
1. to join, join oneself to
2. to be joined, be added to
c. (Hiphil)
1. to cause to add, increase
2. to do more, do again
