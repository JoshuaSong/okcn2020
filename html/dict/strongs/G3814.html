παιδίσκη a girl, i.e. (specially), a female slave or servant
Derivation: feminine diminutive of G3816;
KJV Usage: bondmaid(-woman), damsel, maid(-en). G3816
Thayer:
1) a young girl, damsel
2) a maid-servant, a young female slave
2a) a maid servant who has charge of the door
