גּדישׁ
gâdı̂ysh
gaw-deesh'
From an unused root (meaning to heap up); a stack of sheaves; by analogy a tomb
KJV Usage: shock (stack) (of corn), tomb.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּדישׁ

1. heap, stack, pile
2. tomb
