ἀσωτία
prodigality, wastefulness
ἀσωτία properly, unsavedness, i.e. (by implication) profligacy
Derivation: from a compound of G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4982;
KJV Usage: excess, riot. G1 G4982
Thayer:
1) an abandoned, dissolute life
2) profligacy, prodigality
