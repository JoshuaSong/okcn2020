διαπρίω
to saw quite through, saw asunder
διαπρίω to saw asunder, i.e. (figuratively) to exasperate
Derivation: from G1223 and the base of G4249;
KJV Usage: cut (to the heart). G1223 G4249
Thayer:
1) to saw asunder or in two, to divide by a saw
2) to be sawn through mentally, i.e. to be rent with vexation
