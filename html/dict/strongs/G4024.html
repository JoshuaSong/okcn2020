περιζώννυμι to gird all around, i.e. (middle voice or passive) to fasten on one's belt (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from G4012 and G2224;
KJV Usage: gird (about, self). G4012 G2224
Thayer:
1) to fasten garments with a girdle or belt
2) to gird one's self
3) metaph. with truth as a girdle
3a) to equip one's self with knowledge of the truth
