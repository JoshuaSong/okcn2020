μετριοπαθέω
to bear reasonably with
μετριοπαθέω to be moderate in passion, i.e. gentle (to treat indulgently)
Derivation: from a compound of the base of G3357 and G3806;
KJV Usage: have compassion. G3357 G3806
Thayer:
1) to be affected moderately or in due measure
2) to preserve moderation in the passions, esp. anger or grief
2a) of one who is not unduly disturbed by the errors, faults, sins of others, but bears them gently
