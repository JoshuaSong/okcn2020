פּוּץ
pûts
poots
A primitive root; to dash in pieces, literally or figuratively (especially to disperse)
KJV Usage: break (dash, shake) in (to) pieces, cast (abroad), disperse (selves), drive, retire, scatter (abroad), spread abroad.
