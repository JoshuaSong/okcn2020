ἐρωτάω
to ask
ἐρωτάω to interrogate; by implication, to request
Derivation: apparently from G2046 (compare G2045);
KJV Usage: ask, beseech, desire, intreat, pray. Compare G4441. G2046 G2045 G4441
Thayer:
1) to question
2) to ask
2a) to request, entreat, beg, beseech
