טוּל
ṭûl
tool
A primitive root; to pitch over or reel; hence (transitively) to cast down or out
KJV Usage: carry away, (utterly) cast (down, forth, out), send out.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

טוּל

1. to hurl, cast
a. (Pilpel) to throw away, carry away, hurl
b. (Hiphil) to throw, cast, cast out
c. (Hophal)
1. to be hurled, hurled down
2. to be cast, be thrown, be cast out, be thrown down
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 797
Parts of Speech: Verb
