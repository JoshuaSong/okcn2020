Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אלה
Elah = "An oak"
1. an Edomite chief
2. a king of Israel for two years, son of Baasha
3. the father of king Hoshea of Israel
4. a son of Caleb
5. son of Uzzi
Origin: the same as H424
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name
