τρυγάω
to gather in
τρυγάω to collect the vintage
Derivation: from a derivative of τρύγω (to dry) meaning ripe fruit (as if dry);
KJV Usage: gather.
Thayer:
1) to gather in ripe fruits
2) to gather the harvest or vintage
2a) of fruit gathered
