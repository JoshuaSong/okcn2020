μήποτε

μήποτε or μή ποτε not ever; also if (or lest) ever (or perhaps)
Derivation: from G3361 and G4218;
KJV Usage: if peradventure, lest (at any time, haply), not at all, whether or not. G3361 G4218
Thayer:
