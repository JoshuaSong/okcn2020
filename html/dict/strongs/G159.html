αἴτιος
to blame, blameworthy, culpable
αἴτιος causative, i.e. (concretely) a causer
Derivation: from the same as G154;
KJV Usage: author. G154
Thayer:
1) that which is the cause of anything resides, causative, causing
1a) the author
1a1) of a cause
1a2) of crime or offence
