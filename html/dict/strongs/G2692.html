καταστροφή an overturn ("catastrophe"), i.e. demolition; figuratively, apostasy
Derivation: from G2690;
KJV Usage: overthrow, subverting. G2690
Thayer:
1) overthrow, destruction
1a) of cities
2) metaph. of the extinction of a spirit of consecration
