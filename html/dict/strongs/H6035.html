עניו ענו
‛ânâv ‛ânâyv
aw-nawv', aw-nawv'
The second form is by intermixture with H6041; from H6031; depressed (figuratively), in mind (gentle) or circumstances (needy, especially saintly)
KJV Usage: humble, lowly, meek, poor`. Compare H6041.
