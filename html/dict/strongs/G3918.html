πάρειμι to be near, i.e. at hand; neuter present participle (singular) time being, or (plural) property
Derivation: from G3844 and G1510 (including its various forms);
KJV Usage: come, X have, be here, + lack, (be here) present. G3844 G1510
Thayer:
1) to be by, be at hand, to have arrived, to be present
2) to be ready, in store, at command
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words

  • Present (to be)
