מהר
mâhar
maw-har'
A primitive root (perhaps rather the same as H4116 through the idea of readiness in assent); to bargain (for a wife), that is, to wed
KJV Usage: endow, X surely.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מהר

1. to obtain or acquire by paying purchase price, give a dowry
a. (Qal) to obtain in exchange
