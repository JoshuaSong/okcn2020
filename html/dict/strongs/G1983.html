ἐπισκοπέω
to look upon
ἐπισκοπέω to oversee; by implication, to beware
Derivation: from G1909 and G4648;
KJV Usage: look diligently, take the oversight. G1909 G4648
Thayer:
1) to look upon, inspect, oversee, look after, care for
1a) of the care of the church which rested upon the elders
1b) to look carefully, beware
