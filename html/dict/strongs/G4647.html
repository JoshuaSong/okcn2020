σκόλοψ withered at the front, i.e. a point or prickle (figuratively, a bodily annoyance or disability)
Derivation: perhaps from the base of G4628 and G3700;
KJV Usage: thorn. G4628 G3700
Thayer:
1) a pointed piece of wood, a pale, a stake
2) a sharp stake, splinter
