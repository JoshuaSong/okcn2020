ἔπω
to say
ἔπω to speak or say (by word or writing)
Derivation: a primary verb (used only in the definite past tense, the others being borrowed from G2046, G4483, and G5346);
KJV Usage: answer, bid, bring word, call, command, grant, say (on), speak, tell. Compare G3004. G2046 G4483 G5346 G3004
Thayer:
1) to speak, say
