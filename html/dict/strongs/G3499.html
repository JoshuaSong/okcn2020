νεκρόω
to make dead
νεκρόω to deaden, i.e. (figuratively) to subdue
Derivation: from G3498;
KJV Usage: be dead, mortify. G3498
Thayer:
1) to make dead, to put to death, slay
2) worn out
2a) of an impotent old man
3) to deprive of power, destroy the strength of
