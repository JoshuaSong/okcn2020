ἐξετάζω
to examine well
ἐξετάζω to test thoroughly (by questions), i.e. ascertain or interrogate
Derivation: from G1537 and ἐτάζω (to examine);
KJV Usage: ask, enquire, search. G1537
Thayer:
1) to search out
1a) to examine strictly, enquire
1b) enquire of someone
