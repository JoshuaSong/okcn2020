ὑμνέω
to sing, laud, sing of
ὑμνέω to hymn, i.e. sing a religious ode; by implication, to celebrate (God) in song
Derivation: from G5215;
KJV Usage: sing a hymn (praise unto). G5215
Thayer:
1) to sing the praise of, sing hymns to
2) to sing a hymn, to sing
2a) singing of paschal hymns these were Psalms 113 - 118 and 136, which the Jews called the "great Hallel"
