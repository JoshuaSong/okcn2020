ἄπιστος
not to be trusted
ἄπιστος (actively) disbelieving, i.e. without Christian faith (specially, a heathen); (passively) untrustworthy (person), or incredible (thing)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G4103;
KJV Usage: that believeth not, faithless, incredible thing, infidel, unbeliever(-ing). G1 G4103
Thayer:
1) unfaithful, faithless, (not to be trusted, perfidious)
2) incredible
2a) of things
3) unbelieving, incredulous
3a) without trust (in God)
