Κυρήνη Cyrene, a region of Africa
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: Cyrene.
Thayer:
1) a large and very flourishing city of Libya Cyrenaica or Pentapolitana, about
11 miles (17 km) from the sea. Among its inhabitants were a great number of Jews, whom Ptolemy I. had brought there, and invested with the right of citizens
Literal: Cyrene = "supremacy of the bridle"
