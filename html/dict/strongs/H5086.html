נדף
nâdaph
naw-daf'
A primitive root; to shove asunder, that is, disperse
KJV Usage: drive (away, to and fro), thrust down, shaken, tossed to and fro.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נדף

1. to drive, drive away, drive asunder
a. (Qal) to drive about
b. (Niphal) to be driven, be driven about
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1307
Parts of Speech: Verb
