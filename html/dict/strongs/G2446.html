Ἰορδάνης the Jordanes (i.e. Jarden), a river of Palestine
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H3383);
KJV Usage: Jordan. H3383
Thayer:
1) the one river of Palestine, has its course of little more than
200 miles (300 km), from the roots of Anti-Lebanon to the head of the Dead Sea.
Literal: Jordan = "the descender"
