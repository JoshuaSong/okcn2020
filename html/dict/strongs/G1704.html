ἐμπεριπατέω
to walk about in
ἐμπεριπατέω to perambulate on a place, i.e. (figuratively) to be occupied among persons
Derivation: from G1722 and G4043;
KJV Usage: walk in. G1722 G4043
Thayer:
1) to go about in, walk in
