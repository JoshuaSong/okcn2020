בּדל
bâdal
baw-dal'
A primitive root; to divide (in various senses literally or figuratively, separate, distinguish, differ, select, etc.)
KJV Usage: (make, put) difference, divide (asunder), (make) separate (self, -ation), sever (out), X utterly.
