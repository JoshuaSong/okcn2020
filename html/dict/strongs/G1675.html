Ἑλληνιστής
one who uses the Greek language;
Ἑλληνιστής a Hellenist or Greek-speaking Jew
Derivation: from a derivative of G1672;
KJV Usage: Grecian. G1672
Thayer:
1) a Hellenist
1a) one who imitates the manners and customs or the worship of the Greeks, and use the Greek tongue
1b) used in the NT of Jews born in foreign lands and speaking Greek
