κλύδων
a wave, billow
κλύδων a surge of the sea (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from κλύζω (to billow or dash over);
KJV Usage: raging, wave.
Thayer:
1) a dashing or surging wave, a surge, a violent agitation of the sea
