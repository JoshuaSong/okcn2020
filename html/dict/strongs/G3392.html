μιαίνω
to stain, dye
μιαίνω to sully or taint, i.e. contaminate (ceremonially or morally)
Derivation: perhaps a primary verb;
KJV Usage: defile.
Thayer:
1) to dye with another colour, to stain
2) to defile, pollute, sully, contaminate, soil
2a) to defile with sins
