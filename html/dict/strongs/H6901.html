קבל
qâbal
kaw-bal'
A primitive root; to admit, that is, take (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: choose, (take) hold, receive, (under-) take.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

קבל

1. to take, receive, be before
a. (Piel)
1. to take, choose, receive
2. to accept
3. to accept, assume
b. (Hiphil)
1. to show oppositeness
2. to correspond, receive one to the other
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1980
Parts of Speech: Verb
