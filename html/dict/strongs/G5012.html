ταπεινοφροσύνη
lowliness, humility
ταπεινοφροσύνη humiliation of mind, i.e. modesty
Derivation: from a compound of G5011 and the base of G5424;
KJV Usage: humbleness of mind, humility (of mind, loneliness (of mind). G5011 G5424
Thayer:
1) the having a humble opinion of one's self
2) a deep sense of one's (moral) littleness
3) modesty, humility, lowliness of mind
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Humility, gentleness
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Humility
  • Meekness
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Humbleness of mind, Humility
  • Lowliness, Lowly
