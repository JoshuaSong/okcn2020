ἑδραῖος
sitting, sedentary
ἑδραῖος sedentary, i.e. (by implication) immovable
Derivation: from a derivative of ἕζομαι (to sit);
KJV Usage: settled, stedfast.
Thayer:
1) sitting, sedentary
2) firm, immovable, steadfast
