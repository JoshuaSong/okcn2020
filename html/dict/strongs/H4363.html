Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מכמשׁ מכמשׁ מכמס
Michmas or Michmash = "hidden"
1. a city in Benjamin lying near Ramah and approximately 10 miles (16 km) north of Jerusalem; probably modern 'Mukhmas'
