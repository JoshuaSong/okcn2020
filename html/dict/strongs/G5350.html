φθέγγομαι
to utter a sound
φθέγγομαι to utter a clear sound, i.e. (generally) to proclaim
Derivation: probably akin to G5338 and thus to G5346;
KJV Usage: speak. G5346 G5338
Thayer:
1) to give out a sound, noise or cry
1a) of any sort of sound or voice, whether of man or of animal or inanimate object as thunder, musical instruments, etc.
