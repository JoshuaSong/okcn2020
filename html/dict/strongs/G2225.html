ζωογονέω to engender alive, i.e. (by analogy) to rescue (passively, be saved) from death
Derivation: from the same as G2226 and a derivative of G1096;
KJV Usage: live, preserve. G2226 G1096
Thayer:
1) to bring forth alive
2) to give life
3) to preserve alive
