μηδείς
and not one
μηδείς , including the irregular feminine μηδεμία , and the neuter μηδέν not even one (man, woman, thing)
Derivation: from G3361 and G1520;
KJV Usage: any (man, thing), no (man), none, not (at all, any man, a whit), nothing, + without delay. G3361 G1520
