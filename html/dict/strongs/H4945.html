משׁקה
mashqeh
mash-keh'
From H8248; properly causing to drink, that is, a butler; by implication (intransitively) drink (itself); figuratively a well watered region
KJV Usage: butler (-ship), cupbearer, drink (-ing), fat pasture, watered.
