συμβιβάζω
to bring together
συμβιβάζω causative (by reduplication) of the base of G939); to drive together, i.e. unite (in association or affection), (mentally) to infer, show, teach
Derivation: from G4862 and βιβάζω (to force;
KJV Usage: compact, assuredly gather, intrust, knit together, prove. G4862 G939
Thayer:
1) to cause to coalesce, to join together, put together
1a) to unite or knit together: in affection
2) to put together in one's mind
2a) to compare
2b) to gather, conclude, consider
3) to cause a person to unite with one in a conclusion or come to the same opinion, to prove, demonstrate
3a) to teach, instruct, one
