ἀπερίτμητος
uncircumcised
ἀπερίτμητος uncircumcised (figuratively)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4059;
KJV Usage: uncircumcised. G1 G4059
Thayer:
1) uncircumcised
1a) metaph. those whose heart and ears are covered, i.e. whose soul and senses are closed to divine admonition
