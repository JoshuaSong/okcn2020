ἀναγεννάω
to beget anew, regenerate
ἀναγεννάω to beget or (by extension) bear (again)
Derivation: from G303 and G1080;
KJV Usage: beget, (bear) X (again). G303 G1080
Thayer:
1) to produce again, be born again, born anew
2) metaph. to have one's mind changed so that he lives a new life and one conformed to the will of God
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Beget, Bear (of begetting), Born
