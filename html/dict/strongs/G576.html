ἀποβαίνω
to step off from
ἀποβαίνω literally, to disembark; figuratively, to eventuate
Derivation: from G575 and the base of G939;
KJV Usage: become, go out, turn. G575 G939
Thayer:
1) to come down from, i.e. a ship
2) to turn out, result, to be the outcome
