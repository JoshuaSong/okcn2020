καταδικάζω
to give judgment against
καταδικάζω to adjudge against, i.e. pronounce guilty
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of G1349;
KJV Usage: condemn. G2596 G1349
Thayer:
1) to give judgment against (one), to pronounce guilty
2) to condemn
