ἀνήκω
to have come up to
ἀνήκω to attain to, i.e. (figuratively) be proper
Derivation: from G303 and G2240;
KJV Usage: convenient, be fit. G303 G2240
Thayer:
1) to have come up to, arrived at, to reach to
2) to pertain to what is due, duty, as was fitting

  • Befit, Befitting
  • Convenient, Conveniently
  • Fit (Adjective and Verb), Fitly, Fitting
