משּׂא
maśśâ'
mas-saw'
From H5375; a burden; specifically tribute, or (abstractly) porterage; figuratively an utterance, chiefly a doom, especially singing; mental, desire
KJV Usage: burden, carry away, prophecy, X they set, song, tribute.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

משּׂא
n m
1. load, bearing, tribute, burden, lifting
a. load, burden
b. lifting, uplifting, that to which the soul lifts itself up
c. bearing, carrying
d. tribute, that which is carried or brought or borne
2. utterance, oracle, burden n pr m (BDB) Massa = "burden"
3. a son of Ishmael
