περίεργος
careful overmuch
περίεργος working all around, i.e. officious (meddlesome, neuter plural magic)
Derivation: from G4012 and G2041;
KJV Usage: busybody, curious arts. G4012 G2041
Thayer:
1) busy about trifles and neglectful of important matters
1a) esp. busy about other folks' affairs, a busybody
1b) of things: impertinent and superfluous
1b1) of magic arts
