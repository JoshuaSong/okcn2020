συντάσσω
to put in order together
συντάσσω to arrange jointly, i.e. (figuratively) to direct
Derivation: from G4862 and G5021;
KJV Usage: appoint. G4862 G5021
Thayer:
1) to put in order with or together, to arrange
2) to (put together), constitute
2a) to prescribe, appoint
