ἀπόκειμαι
to be laid away
ἀπόκειμαι to be reserved; figuratively, to await
Derivation: from G575 and G2749;
KJV Usage: be appointed, (be) laid up. G575 G2749
Thayer:
1) to be laid away, laid by, reserved
2) reserved for one, awaiting him
