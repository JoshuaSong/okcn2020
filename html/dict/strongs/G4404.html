πρωΐ at dawn; by implication, the day-break watch
Derivation: adverb from G4253;
KJV Usage: early (in the morning), (in the) morning. G4253
Thayer:
1) in the morning, early
2) the fourth watch of the night, from 3 o'clock in the morning until 6 o'clock approximately
