ἀντικαθίστημι
to lay down
ἀντικαθίστημι to set down (troops) against, i.e. withstand
Derivation: from G473 and G2525;
KJV Usage: resist. G473 G2525
Thayer:
1) to put in place of another
2) to place in opposition
2a) to dispose troops, set an army in line of battle
2b) to stand against, resist
