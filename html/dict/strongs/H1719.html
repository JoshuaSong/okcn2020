דּדנה דּדן
de dân dedâneh
ded-awn', deh-daw'-neh
Of uncertain derivation; Dedan, the name of two Cushites and of their territory. The second form used in Ezekiel 25:13
KJV Usage: Dedan.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

דּדנה דּדן
Dedan = "low country" n pr m
1. the son of Raamah and grandson of Cush. A son of Jokshan and grandson of Keturah n pr loc
2. a place in south Arabia
Origin: of uncertain derivation
TWOT: None
