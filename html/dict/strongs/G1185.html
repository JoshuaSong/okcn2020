δελεάζω
to entice
δελεάζω to entrap, i.e. (figuratively) delude
Derivation: from the base of G1388;
KJV Usage: allure, beguile, entice. G1388
Thayer:
1) to bait, catch by a bait
2) metaph. to beguile by blanishments, allure, entice, deceive
