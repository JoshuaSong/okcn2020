αἰδώς
a sense of shame, shame, modesty, self-respect
αἰδώς bashfulness, i.e. (towards men), modesty or (towards God) awe
Derivation: perhaps from G1 (as a negative particle) and G1492 (through the idea of downcast eyes);
KJV Usage: reverence, shamefacedness. G1 G1492
Thayer:
1) a sense of shame or honour, modesty, bashfulness, reverence, regard for others, respect
