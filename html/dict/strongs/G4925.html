συνοικοδομέω
to build together
συνοικοδομέω to construct, i.e. (passively) to compose (in company with other Christians, figuratively)
Derivation: from G4862 and G3618;
KJV Usage: build together. G4862 G3618
Thayer:
1) to build together
1a) to build together or with others
1b) to put together or construct by building, out of several things to build up one whole
1b1) of the human body
