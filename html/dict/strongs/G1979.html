ἐπισιτισμός
a furnishing oneself with provisions, foraging
ἐπισιτισμός a provisioning, i.e. (concretely) food
Derivation: from a compound of G1909 and a derivative of G4621;
KJV Usage: victuals. G1909 G4621
Thayer:
1) foraging, providing food
2) supplies, provisions, food
