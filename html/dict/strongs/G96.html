ἀδόκιμος
not standing the test, spurious
ἀδόκιμος unapproved, i.e. rejected; by implication, worthless (literally or morally)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G1384;
KJV Usage: castaway, rejected, reprobate. G1 G1384
Thayer:
1) not standing the test, not approved
1a) properly used of metals and coins
2) that which does not prove itself such as it ought
2a) unfit for, unproved, spurious, reprobate
