ἐκβολή
a throwing out
ἐκβολή ejection, i.e. (specially) a throwing overboard of the cargo
Derivation: from G1544;
KJV Usage: + lighten the ship. G1544
Thayer:
1) a casting out
2) the throwing overboard of goods and lading whereby sailors lighten a ship in a storm to keep her from sinking
