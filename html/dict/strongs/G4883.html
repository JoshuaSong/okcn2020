συναρμολογέω to render close-jointed together, i.e. organize compactly
Derivation: from G4862 and a derivative of a compound of G719 and G3004 (in its original sense of laying);
KJV Usage: be fitly framed (joined) together. G4862 G719 G3004
Thayer:
1) to join closely together
2) to frame together
2a) parts of a building
2b) the members of the body
