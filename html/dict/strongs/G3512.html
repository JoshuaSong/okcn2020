νεωτερικός appertaining to younger persons, i.e. juvenile
Derivation: from the comparative of G3501;
KJV Usage: youthful. G3501
Thayer:
1) peculiar to an age, of youth, youthful, younger
1a) younger (than now)
1b) young, youthful
1c) younger (by birth)
1d) an attendant, servant, inferiority in rank
