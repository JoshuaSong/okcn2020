ὀφθαλμός
the eye
ὀφθαλμός the eye (literally or figuratively); by implication, vision; figuratively, envy (from the jealous side-glance)
Derivation: from G3700;
KJV Usage: eye, sight. G3700
Thayer:
1) the eye
2) metaph. the eyes of the mind, the faculty of knowing
