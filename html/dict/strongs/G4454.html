πῶλος
a foal, young horse
πῶλος a "foal" or "filly", i.e. (specially), a young ass
Derivation: apparently a primary word;
KJV Usage: colt.
Thayer:
1) a colt, the young of a horse
2) a young creature
2a) of a young ass
2b) an ass's colt
