εὐδοκία
satisfaction, approval
εὐδοκία satisfaction, i.e. (subjectively) delight, or (objectively) kindness, wish, purpose
Derivation: from a presumed compound of G2095 and the base of G1380;
KJV Usage: desire, good pleasure (will), X seem good. G2095 G1380
Thayer:
1) will, choice
1a) good will, kindly intent, benevolence
2) delight, pleasure, satisfaction
3) desire
3a) for delight in any absent thing easily produces longing for it
