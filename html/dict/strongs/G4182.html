πολυποίκιλος
much-variegated
πολυποίκιλος much variegated, i.e. multifarious
Derivation: from G4183 and G4164;
KJV Usage: manifold. G4183 G4164
Thayer:
1) much variegated, marked with a great variety of colours
1a1) of cloth or a painting
2) much varied, manifold
