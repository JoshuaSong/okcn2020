ירט
yâraṭ
yaw-rat'
A primitive root; to precipitate or hurl (rush) headlong; (intransitively) to be rash
KJV Usage: be perverse, turn over.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

ירט

1. to precipitate, be precipitate, push headlong, drive recklessly
a. (Qal) to precipitate, wring out, be precipitate
Origin: a primitive root
