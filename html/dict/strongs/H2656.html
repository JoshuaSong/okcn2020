חפץ
chêphets
khay'-fets
From H2654; pleasure; hence (abstractly) desire; concretely a valuable thing; hence (by extension) a matter (as something in mind)
KJV Usage: acceptable, delight (-some), desire, things desired, matter, pleasant (-ure), purpose, willingly.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חפץ

1. delight, pleasure
a. delight
b. desire, longing
c. the good pleasure
d. that in which one takes delight
