θηρεύω
to hunt, go hunting
θηρεύω to hunt (an animal), i.e. (figuratively) to carp at
Derivation: from G2339;
KJV Usage: catch. G2339
Thayer:
1) to go a hunting, to hunt, to catch in hunting
2) metaph. to lay wait for, strive to ensnare, catch artfully
