βραβεύω
to act as a judge
βραβεύω to arbitrate, i.e. (genitive case) to govern (figuratively, prevail)
Derivation: from the same as G1017;
KJV Usage: rule. G1017
Thayer:
1) to be an umpire
2) to decide, determine
3) to direct, control, rule
