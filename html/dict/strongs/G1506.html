εἰλικρινής
unmixed, without alloy, pure
εἰλικρινής judged by sunlight, i.e. tested as genuine (figuratively)
Derivation: from εἵλη (the sun's ray) and G2919;
KJV Usage: pure, sincere. G2919
Thayer:
1) pure, sincere, unsullied
2) found pure when unfolded and examined by the sun's light
For Synonyms see entry G5840
