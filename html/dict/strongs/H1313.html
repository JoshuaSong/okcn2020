בּשׂם
bâśâm
baw-sawm'
From an unused root meaning to be fragrant; (compare H5561) the balsam plant
KJV Usage: spice.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּשׂם

1. spice, balsam
2. (TWOT) sweet, sweet smell, sweet odour
Origin: from an unused root meaning to be fragrant
TWOT: 290a
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
