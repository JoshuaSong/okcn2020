Βηθαβαρά ferry-house; Bethabara (i.e. Bethabarah), a place on the Jordan
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H1004 and H5679);
KJV Usage: Bethabara. H1004 H5679
Thayer:
1) a place beyond Jordan, where John was baptising. This may correspond to Bethbarah (fords of Abarah), the ancient ford of the Jordan on the road to Gilead
Literal: Bethabara = "house of the ford"
