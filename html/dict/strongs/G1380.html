δοκέω compare the base of G1166) of the same meaning; to think; by implication, to seem (truthfully or uncertainly)
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary verb, δόκω (used only in an alternate in certain tenses;
KJV Usage: be accounted, (of own) please(-ure), be of reputation, seem (good), suppose, think, trow. G1166
Thayer:
1) to be of opinion, think, suppose
2) to seem, to be accounted, reputed
3) it seems to me
3a) I think, judge: thus in question
3b) it seems good to, pleased me, I determined
