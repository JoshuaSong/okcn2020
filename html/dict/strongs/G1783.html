ἔντευξις
a lighting upon, meeting with, converse, intercourse
ἔντευξις an interview, i.e. (specially) supplication
Derivation: from G1793;
KJV Usage: intercession, prayer. G1793
Thayer:
1) a falling in with, meeting with
1a) an interview
1a1) a coming together
1a2) to visit
1a3) converse or for any other cause
1b) that for which an interview is held
1b1) a conference or conversation
1b2) a petition, supplication
