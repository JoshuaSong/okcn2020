κατηγορέω
to speak against, to accuse
κατηγορέω to be a plaintiff, i.e. to charge with some offence
Derivation: from G2725;
KJV Usage: accuse, object. G2725
Thayer:
1) to accuse
1a) before a judge: to make an accusation
1b) of an extra-judicial accusation
For Synonyms see entry G5803
