ἐπακολουθέω
to follow close upon, follow after
ἐπακολουθέω to accompany
Derivation: from G1909 and G190;
KJV Usage: follow (after). G1909 G190
Thayer:
1) to follow (close) upon, follow after
2) to tread in one's footsteps i.e. to imitate his example
