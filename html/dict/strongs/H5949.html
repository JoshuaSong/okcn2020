עללה עלילה
‛ălı̂ylâh ‛ălilâh
al-ee-law', al-ee-law'
From H5953 in the sense of effecting; an exploit (of God), or a performance (of man, often in a bad sense); by implication an opportunity
KJV Usage: act (-ion), deed, doing, invention, occasion, work.
