אביעזר
'ăbı̂y‛ezer
ab-ee-ay'-zer
From H1 and H5829; father of help (that is, helpful); Abiezer, the name of two Israelites
KJV Usage: Abiezer.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אביעזר
Abiezer = "my father is help"
1. a Manassite, called "son" of Gilead, also son of Gilead's sister
2. a Benjamite, warrior of David
Origin: from H1 and H5829
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
