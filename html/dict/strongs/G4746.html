στοιβάς a spread (as if tramped flat) of loose materials for a couch, i.e. (by implication) a bough of a tree so employed
Derivation: from a primary στείβω (to "step" or "stamp");
KJV Usage: branch.
Thayer:
1) branch, leafy limb
