κλυδωνίζομαι
to be tossed like waves
κλυδωνίζομαι to surge, i.e. (figuratively) to fluctuate
Derivation: middle voice from G2830;
KJV Usage: toss to and fro. G2830
Thayer:
1) to be tossed by the waves
2) metaph. to be agitated (like the waves) mentally
