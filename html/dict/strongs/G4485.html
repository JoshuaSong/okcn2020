ῥῆγμα
a breakage, fracture
ῥῆγμα something torn, i.e. a fragment (by implication and abstractly, a fall)
Derivation: from G4486;
KJV Usage: ruin. G4486
Thayer:
1) that which has been broken or rent asunder
1a) a fracture, breach, cleft
1b) rent clothes
1c) fall, ruin
