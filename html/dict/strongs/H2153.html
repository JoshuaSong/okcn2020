KJV Usage: Zilpah.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

זלפּה
Zilpah = "a trickling"
1. the Syrian given by Laban to Leah as a handmaid, a concubine of Jacob, mother of Asher and Gad
Origin: from an unused root apparently mean to trickle, as myrrh
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Feminine
