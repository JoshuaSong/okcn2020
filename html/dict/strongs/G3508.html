Νεφθαλείμ Nephthaleim (i.e. Naphthali), a tribe in Palestine
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H5321);
KJV Usage: Nephthalim. H5321
Thayer:
1) was the sixth son of Jacob, the second child borne to him by Bilhah, Rachel's slave. His posterity form the tribe of Naphtali.
Literal: Naphtali = "wrestling"
