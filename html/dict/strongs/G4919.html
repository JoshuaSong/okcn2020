συνθρύπτω
to break in pieces: to crush
συνθρύπτω to crush together, i.e. (figuratively) to dispirit
Derivation: from G4862 and θρύπτω (to crumble);
KJV Usage: break. G4862
Thayer:
1) break in pieces, to crush
2) metaph.
2a) to break one's heart
2b) to deprive of strength and courage, dispirit, incapacitate for enduring trials
