ἔνδοξος in glory, i.e. splendid, (figuratively) noble
Derivation: from G1722 and G1391;
KJV Usage: glorious, gorgeous(-ly), honourable. G1722 G1391
Thayer:

1a) illustrious, honourable, esteemed
1b) notable, glorious
1c) splendid
1c1) of clothing
1c2) fig., free from sins
