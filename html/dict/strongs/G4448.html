πυρόω
to burn with fire, burn up
πυρόω to kindle, i.e. (passively) to be ignited, glow (literally), be refined (by implication), or (figuratively) to be inflamed (with anger, grief, lust)
Derivation: from G4442;
KJV Usage: burn, fiery, be on fire, try. G4442
Thayer:
1) to burn with fire, to set on fire, kindle
1a) to be on fire, to burn
1a1) to be incensed, indignant
1b) make to glow
1b1) full of fire, fiery, ignited
1b1a) of darts filled with inflammable substances and set on fire
1b2) melted by fire and purged of dross
