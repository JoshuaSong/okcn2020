Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׁמיר
Shamir = "a point" or "thorn" n pr loc
1. a town in the mountain district of Judah
2. a town in Ephraim where the judge Tola lived and was buried
Origin: the same as H8068
TWOT: pr n loc
