συμβουλεύω
to advise, counsel
συμβουλεύω to give (or take) advice jointly, i.e. recommend, deliberate or determine
Derivation: from G4862 and G1011;
KJV Usage: consult, (give, take) counsel (together). G4862 G1011
Thayer:
1) to give counsel
2) to take counsel with others, take counsel together, to consult, deliberate
