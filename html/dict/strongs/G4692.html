σπεύδω
to set a-going, to urge on, hasten, quicken
σπεύδω to "speed" ("study"), i.e. urge on (diligently or earnestly); by implication, to await eagerly
Derivation: probably strengthened from G4228;
KJV Usage: (make, with) haste unto. G4228
Thayer:
1) to haste, make haste
2) to desire earnestly
