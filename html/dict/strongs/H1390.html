Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּבעה
Gibeah = "hill"
1. a city in the mountain district of Judah
2. a city of Benjamin, birthplace of king Saul
3. a city in Kirjath-jearim of Ephraim
Origin: the same as H1389
