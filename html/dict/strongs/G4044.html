περιπείρω
to pierce as with a spit
περιπείρω to penetrate entirely, i.e. transfix (figuratively)
Derivation: from G4012 and the base of G4008;
KJV Usage: pierce through. G4012 G4008
Thayer:
1) to pierce through
2) metaph. to torture one's soul with sorrows
