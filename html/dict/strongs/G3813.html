παιδίον
a little
παιδίον a childling (of either sex), i.e. (properly), an infant, or (by extension) a half-grown boy or girl; figuratively, an immature Christian
Derivation: neuter diminutive of G3816;
KJV Usage: (little, young) child, damsel. G3816
Thayer:
1) a young child, a little boy, a little girl
1a) infants
1b) children, little ones
1c) an infant
1c1) of a (male) child just recently born
1d) of a more advanced child; of a mature child;
1e) metaph. children (like children) in intellect
For Synonyms see entry G5868
