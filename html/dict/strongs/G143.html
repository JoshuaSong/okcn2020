αἰσθάνομαι
to perceive, apprehend by the senses, to see, hear, feel
αἰσθάνομαι to apprehend (properly, by the senses)
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: perceive.
Thayer:
1) to perceive
1a) by the bodily senses
1b) with the mind, understand
