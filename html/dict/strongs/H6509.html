פּרה
pârâh
paw-raw'
A primitive root; to bear fruit (literally or figuratively)
KJV Usage: bear, bring forth (fruit), (be, cause to be, make) fruitful, grow, increase.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּרה

1. to bear fruit, be fruitful, branch off
a. (Qal) to bear fruit, be fruitful
b. (Hiphil)
1. to cause to bear fruit
2. to make fruitful
3. to show fruitfulness, bear fruit
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1809
Parts of Speech: Verb
