אשׁתּמה אשׁתּמוע אשׁתּמע
'eshte môa‛ 'eshte môa‛ 'eshte môh
esh-tem-o'-ah, esh-tem-o'-ah, esh-tem-o'
From H8085 (in the sense of obedience); Eshtemoa or Eshtemoh, a place in Palestine
KJV Usage: Eshtemoa, Eshtemoh.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אשׁתּמה אשׁתּמוע אשׁתּמע
Eshtemoa = "I will make myself heard" Eshtemoh = "I shall cause my own ruin: fire of astonishment: I shall soar aloft"
1. a Levitical city in Judah
2. a man of Judah
Origin: from H8085 (in the sense of obedience)
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name
