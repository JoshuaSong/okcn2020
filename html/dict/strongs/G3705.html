ὅραμα
that which is seen, a sight, spectacle
ὅραμα something gazed at, i.e. a spectacle (especially supernatural)
Derivation: from G3708;
KJV Usage: sight, vision. G3708
Thayer:
1) that which is seen, spectacle
2) a sight divinely granted in an ecstasy or in a sleep, a vision
