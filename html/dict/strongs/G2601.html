καταβιβάζω
to make to go down, bring down, bring down
καταβιβάζω to cause to go down, i.e. precipitate
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of the base of G939;
KJV Usage: bring (thrust) down. G2596 G939
Thayer:
1) to cause to go down
2) to bring down
3) to cast down, thrust down
