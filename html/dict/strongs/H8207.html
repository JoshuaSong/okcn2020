שׁפיפן
she phı̂yphôn
shef-ee-fone'
From an unused root meaning the same as H7779; a kind of serpent (as snapping), probably the cerastes or horned adder
KJV Usage: adder.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
