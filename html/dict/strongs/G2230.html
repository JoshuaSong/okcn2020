ἡγεμονεύω
to be
ἡγεμονεύω to act as ruler
Derivation: from G2232;
KJV Usage: be governor. G2232
Thayer:
1) to be leader, to lead the way
2) to rule, command
2a) of a province, to be governor of a province
2b) said of a proconsul, of a procurator
