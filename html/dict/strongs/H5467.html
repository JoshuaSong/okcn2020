סדם
se dôm
sed-ome'
From an unused root meaning to scorch; burnt (that is, volcanic or bituminous) district; Sedom, a place near the Dead Sea
KJV Usage: Sodom.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

סדם
Sodom = "burning"
1. a Canaanite city, usually paired with Gomorrah, located in the area of the Dead Sea and the Jordan river; both cities destroyed by God in judgment
Origin: from an unused root meaning to scorch
