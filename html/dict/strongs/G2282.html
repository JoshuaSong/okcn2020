θάλπω
to heat, soften by heat
θάλπω to brood, i.e. (figuratively) to foster
Derivation: probably akin to θάλλω (to warm);
KJV Usage: cherish.
Thayer:
1) to warm, keep warm
2) to cherish with tender love, to foster with tender care
