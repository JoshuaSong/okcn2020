πανουργία
knavery, roguery, villany
πανουργία adroitness, i.e. (in a bad sense) trickery or sophistry
Derivation: from G3835;
KJV Usage: (cunning) craftiness, subtilty. G3835
Thayer:
1) craftiness, cunning
2) a specious or false wisdom
3) in a good sense, prudence, skill, in undertaking and carrying on affairs
