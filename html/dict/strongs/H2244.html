חבא
châbâ'
khaw-baw'
A primitive root (compare H2245); to secrete
KJV Usage: X held, hide (self), do secretly.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חבא

1. to withdraw, hide
a. (Niphal) to hide oneself
b. (Pual) to be forced into hiding
c. (Hiphil) to hide
d. (Hophal) to be hidden
e. (Hithpael)
1. to hide oneself, draw back
2. to draw together, thicken, harden
Origin: a primitive root [compare H2245]
