ἕτοιμος adjusted, i.e. ready
Derivation: from an old noun ἐτεός (fitness);
KJV Usage: prepared, (made) ready(-iness, to our hand).
Thayer:
1) prepare ready
1a) of things
1a1) ready at hand
1a2) opportune, seasonable
1b) of persons
1b1) ready prepared
1b1a) to do something
1b1b) to receive one coming
