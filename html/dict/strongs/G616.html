ἀποκυέω
to bear young, bring forth
ἀποκυέω to breed forth, i.e. (by transference) to generate (figuratively)
Derivation: from G575 and the base of G2949;
KJV Usage: beget, produce. G575 G2949
Thayer:
1) to bring forth
1a) from the womb
1b) give birth to
2) produce
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Beget, Bear (of begetting), Born
  • Bring, Bringing, Brought
