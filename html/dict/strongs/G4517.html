ῥώννυμι
to strengthen, make strong and mighty
ῥώννυμι probably akin to G4506); to strengthen, i.e. (impersonal passive) have health (as a parting exclamation, good-bye)
Derivation: prolongation from ῥώομαι (to dart;
KJV Usage: farewell. G4506
Thayer:
1) to make strong, strengthen
2) to be strong, to thrive, prosper
3) in the usual formula in closing of a letter, farewell
