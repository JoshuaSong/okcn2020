εὐλαβέομαι
to behave like the
εὐλαβέομαι to be circumspect, i.e. (by implication) to be apprehensive; religiously, to reverence
Derivation: middle voice from G2126;
KJV Usage: (moved with) fear. G2126
Thayer:
1) to act cautiously, circumspectly
2) to beware, fear
3) to reverence, stand in awe of
