Βαπτιστής a baptizer, as an epithet of Christ's forerunner
Derivation: from G907;
KJV Usage: Baptist. G907
Thayer:
1) a baptiser
2) one who administers the rite of baptism
3) the surname of John, the forerunner of Christ
