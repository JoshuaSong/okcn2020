פּתל
pâthal
paw-thal'
A primitive root; to twine, that is, (literally) to struggle or (figuratively) be (morally) tortuous
KJV Usage: (shew self) froward, shew self unsavoury, wrestle.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

פּתל

1. to be twisted
