χόρτος
an inclosed place, a feeding-place
χόρτος a "court" or "garden", i.e. (by implication, of pasture) herbage or vegetation
Derivation: apparently a primary word;
KJV Usage: blade, grass, hay.
Thayer:
1) the place where grass grows and animals graze
2) grass, herbage, hay, provender
2a) of green grass
2b) of growing crops
