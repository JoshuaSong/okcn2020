סנאה סנוּאה
se nû'âh se nû'âh
sen-oo-aw', sen-oo-aw'
From the same as H5570; pointed; (used with the article as a proper name) Senuah, the name of two Israelites. (Hasenuah includes the article.)
KJV Usage: Hasenuah [includ. the article, Senuah.
