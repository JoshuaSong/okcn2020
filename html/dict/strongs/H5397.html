נשׁמה
ne shâmâh
nesh-aw-maw'
From H5395; a puff, that is, wind, angry or vital breath, divine inspiration, intellect or (concretely) an animal
KJV Usage: blast, (that) breath (-eth), inspiration, soul, spirit.
