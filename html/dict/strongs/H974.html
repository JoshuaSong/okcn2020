בּחן
bâchan
baw-khan'
A primitive root; to test (especially metals); generally and figuratively to investigate
KJV Usage: examine, prove, tempt, try (trial).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

בּחן

1. to examine, try, prove
a. (Qal)
1. to examine, scrutinise
2. to test, prove, try (of gold, persons, the heart, man of God)
b. (Niphal) to be tried, proved
c. (Pual) to make a trial
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 230
Parts of Speech: Verb
