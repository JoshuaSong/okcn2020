ἱερός sacred
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: holy.
Thayer:
1) sacred, consecrated to the deity, pertaining to God
1a) sacred Scriptures, because inspired by God, treating of divine things and therefore to be devoutly revered
