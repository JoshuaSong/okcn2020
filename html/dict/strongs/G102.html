ἀδύνατος
unable
ἀδύνατος unable, i.e. weak (literally or figuratively); passively, impossible
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G1415;
KJV Usage: could not do, impossible, impotent, not possible, weak. G1 G1415
Thayer:
1) without strength, impotent, powerless, weakly, disabled
2) unable to be done, impossible
