ψεύδομαι to utter an untruth or attempt to deceive by falsehood
Derivation: middle voice of an apparently primary verb;
KJV Usage: falsely, lie.
Thayer:
1) to lie, to speak deliberate falsehoods
2) to deceive one by a lie, to lie to
