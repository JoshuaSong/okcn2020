גּלח
gâlach
gaw-lakh'
A primitive root; properly to be bald, that is, (causatively) to shave; figuratively to lay waste
KJV Usage: poll, shave (off).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּלח

1. to poll, shave, shave off, be bald
a. (Piel)
1. to shave
2. to shave off
3. (fig. of devastation)
b. (Pual) to be shaven
c. (Hithpael) to shave oneself
