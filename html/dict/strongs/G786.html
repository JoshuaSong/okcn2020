ἄσπονδος
without drink-offering
ἄσπονδος literally, without libation (which usually accompanied a treaty), i.e. (by implication) truceless
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4689;
KJV Usage: implacable, truce-breaker. G1 G4689
Thayer:
1) without a treaty or covenant
1a) of things not mutually agreed upon e.g. abstinences from hostilities
2) that cannot be persuaded to enter into a covenant, implacable
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Covenant-breaker, implacable
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Covenant Breaker
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Implacable
