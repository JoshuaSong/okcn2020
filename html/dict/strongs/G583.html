ἀπογράφω
to write off, copy: to enter in a list, register
ἀπογράφω to write off (a copy or list), i.e. enrol
Derivation: from G575 and G1125;
KJV Usage: tax, write. G575 G1125
Thayer:
1) to write off, copy (from some pattern)
2) to enter in a register or records
2a) spec. to enter in public records the names of men, their property and income
2b) to enrol
