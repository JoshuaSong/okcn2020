שׁלג
shâlag
shaw-lag'
A primitive root; properly meaning to be white; used only as denominative from H7950; to be snow white (with the linen clothing of the slain)
KJV Usage: be as snow.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions
