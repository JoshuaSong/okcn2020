שׂרט
śâraṭ
saw-rat'
A primitive root; to gash
KJV Usage: cut in pieces, make [cuttings] pieces.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׂרט

1. to incise, scratch, tattoo, cut
a. (Qal) to make incision
b. (Niphal) to be scratched or lacerated, be cut
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2289
Parts of Speech: Verb
