ἀνεπίληπτος
not open to attack, not censured, blameless
ἀνεπίληπτος not arrested, i.e. (by implication) inculpable
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G1949;
KJV Usage: blameless, unrebukeable. G1 G1949
Thayer:
1) not apprehended, that cannot be laid hold of
2) that cannot be reprehended, not open to censure, irreproachable
Berry's New Testament Synonyms
  • Faultless, unblamed
Trench's New Testament Synonyms
  • Without Blemish
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Blame, Blameless
