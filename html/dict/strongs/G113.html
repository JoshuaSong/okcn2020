ἄθεσμος lawless, i.e. (by implication) criminal
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5087 (in the sense of enacting);
KJV Usage: wicked. G1 G5087
Thayer:
1) one who breaks through the restraint of law and gratifies his lusts
