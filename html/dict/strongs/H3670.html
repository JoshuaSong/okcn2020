כּנף
kânaph
kaw-naf'
A primitive root; properly to project laterally, that is, probably (reflexively) to withdraw
KJV Usage: be removed.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּנף

1. (Niphal) to be put or thrust in or into a corner, be hidden from view, be cornered, be thrust aside
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1003
Parts of Speech: Verb
