זרע
zâra‛
zaw-rah'
A primitive root; to sow; figuratively to disseminate, plant, fructify
KJV Usage: bear, conceive seed, set with, sow (-er), yield.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

זרע

1. to sow, scatter seed
a. (Qal)
1. to sow
2. producing, yielding seed
b. (Niphal)
1. to be sown
2. to become pregnant, be made pregnant
c. (Pual) to be sown
d. (Hiphil) to produce seed, yield seed
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 582
Parts of Speech: Verb
