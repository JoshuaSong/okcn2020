κατηχέω
to sound
κατηχέω to sound down into the ears, i.e. (by implication) to indoctrinate ("catechize") or (genitive case) to apprise of
Derivation: from G2596 and G2279;
KJV Usage: inform, instruct, teach. G2596 G2279
Thayer:
1) to sound towards, sound down upon, resound
1a) to charm with resounding sound, to fascinate
2) to teach orally, to instruct
3) to inform by word of mouth
3a) to be orally informed
