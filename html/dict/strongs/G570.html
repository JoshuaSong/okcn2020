ἀπιστία
disbelief, distrust, mistrust
ἀπιστία faithlessness, i.e. (negatively) disbelief (lack of Christian faith), or (positively) unfaithfulness (disobedience)
Derivation: from G571;
KJV Usage: unbelief. G571
Thayer:
1) unfaithfulness, faithless
2) want of faith, unbelief
3) weakness of faith
