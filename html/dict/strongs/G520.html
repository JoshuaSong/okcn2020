ἀπάγω
to lead away, carry off
ἀπάγω to take off (in various senses)
Derivation: from G575 and G71;
KJV Usage: bring, carry away, lead (away), put to death, take away. G575 G71
Thayer:
1) to lead away
1a) esp. of those who are led off to trial, prison, or punishment

  • Carry
  • Death, Death-stroke (see also Die)
  • Lead, Led
