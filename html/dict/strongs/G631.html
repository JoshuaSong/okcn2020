ἀπομάσσομαι to scrape away
Derivation: middle voice from G575 and μάσσω (to squeeze, knead, smear);
KJV Usage: wipe off. G575
Thayer:
1) to wipe off
2) to wipe off one's self, to wipe of for one's self
