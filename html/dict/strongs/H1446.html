Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּדור גּדר
Gedor = "wall" n pr m
1. a son of Penuel
2. a son of Jered
3. a Benjamite of Gibeon n pr loc
4. a town in the mountainous part of Judah a few miles north of Hebron
Origin: from H1443
