εἰρηνεύω
to bring to peace, reconcile
εἰρηνεύω to be (act) peaceful
Derivation: from G1515;
KJV Usage: be at (have, live in) peace, live peaceably. G1515
Thayer:
1) to make peace
2) to cultivate or keep peace, harmony
3) to be at peace, live in peace
