ἄμαχος
without battle
ἄμαχος peaceable
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G3163;
KJV Usage: not a brawler. G1 G3163
Thayer:
1) not to be withstood, invincible
2) not contentious
3) abstaining from fighting
