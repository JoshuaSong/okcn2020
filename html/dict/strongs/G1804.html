ἐξαγγέλλω
to send out
ἐξαγγέλλω to publish, i.e. celebrate
Derivation: from G1537 and the base of G32;
KJV Usage: shew forth. G1537 G32
Thayer:
1) to tell out or forth
2) to declare abroad, divulge, publish
3) to make known by praising or proclaiming, to celebrate
