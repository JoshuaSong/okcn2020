κρυσταλλίζω
to be clear as crystal
κρυσταλλίζω to make (i.e. intransitively, resemble) ice ("crystallize")
Derivation: from G2930;
KJV Usage: be clear as crystal. G2930
Thayer:
1) to be of crystalline brightness and transparency
2) to shine like crystal
