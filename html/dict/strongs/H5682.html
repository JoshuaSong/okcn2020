Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עברים
Abarim = "regions beyond"
1. a mountain or range of mountains on the east of the Jordan, in the land of Moab, opposite to Jericho; Mount Nebo is part of this range
Origin: pl. of H5676
TWOT: None
