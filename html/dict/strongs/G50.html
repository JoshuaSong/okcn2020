ἀγνοέω
not to perceive
ἀγνοέω not to know (through lack of information or intelligence); by implication, to ignore (through disinclination)
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and G3539;
KJV Usage: (be) ignorant(-ly), not know, not understand, unknown. G1 G3539
Thayer:
1) to be ignorant, not to know
2) not to understand, unknown
3) to err or sin through mistake, to be wrong

  • Know, Known, Knowledge, Unknown
  • Understand, Understood
