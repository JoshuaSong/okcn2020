ἐπέρχομαι
ibo
ἐπέρχομαι to supervene, i.e. arrive, occur, impend, attack, (figuratively) influence
Derivation: from G1909 and G2064;
KJV Usage: come (in, upon). G1909 G2064
Thayer:
1) to come to arrive
1a) of time, come on, be at hand, be future
2) to come upon, overtake, one
2a) of sleep
2b) of disease
2c) of calamities
2d) of the Holy Spirit, descending and operating in one
2e) of an enemy attacking one
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Come, Came (see also Coming)
