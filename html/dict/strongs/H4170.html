מקשׁ מוקשׁ
môqêsh môqêsh
mo-kashe', mo-kashe'
From H3369; a noose (for catching animals), (literally or figuratively); by implication a hook (for the nose)
KJV Usage: be ensnared, gin, (is) snare (-d), trap.
