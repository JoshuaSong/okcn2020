תּמהּ
tâmahh
taw-mah'
A primitive root; to be in consternation
KJV Usage: be amazed, be astonished, marvel (-lously), wonder.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּמהּ

1. to be astounded, be stunned, be amazed, be dumbfounded
a. (Qal) to be astounded
b. (Hithpael) to astonish yourself, be astounded, be astonished at one another
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 2518
Parts of Speech: Verb
