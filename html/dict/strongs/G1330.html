διέρχομαι
to go through, pass through
διέρχομαι to traverse (literally)
Derivation: from G1223 and G2064;
KJV Usage: come, depart, go (about, abroad, everywhere, over, through, throughout), pass (by, over, through, throughout), pierce through, travel, walk through. G1223 G2064
Thayer:
1) to go through, pass through
1a) to go, walk, journey, pass through a place
1b) to travel the road which leads through a place, go, pass, travel through a region
2) to go different places
2a) of people, to go abroad
2b) of a report, to spread, go abroad

  • Depart
  • Pass, come to Pass
  • Pierce
