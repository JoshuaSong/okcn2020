στρατοπεδάρχης
a military commander
στρατοπεδάρχης a ruler of an army, i.e. (specially), a Prætorian præfect
Derivation: from G4760 and G757;
KJV Usage: captain of the guard. G4760 G757
Thayer:
1) the commander of a camp and army, a military tribune
2) Praetorian prefect, commander of the praetorian cohorts, captain of the Roman emperor's body guardThere were two praetorian prefects, to whose custody prisoners sent bound to the emperor were consigned.
