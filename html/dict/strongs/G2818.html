κληρονόμος
one who receives a portion
κληρονόμος a sharer by lot, i.e. inheritor (literally or figuratively); by implication, a possessor
Derivation: from G2819 and the base of G3551 (in its original sense of partitioning, i.e. (reflexively) getting by apportionment);
KJV Usage: heir. G2819 G3551
Thayer:
1) one who receives by lot, an heir
1a) an heir
1b) in Messianic usage, one who receives his allotted possession by right of sonship
2) one who has acquired or obtained the portion allotted to him
