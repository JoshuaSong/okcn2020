τράχηλος
the neck, throat
τράχηλος the throat (neck), i.e. (figuratively) life
Derivation: probably from G5143 (through the idea of mobility);
KJV Usage: neck. G5143
Thayer:
1) to be ready to incur the most imminent peril to life
