עלס
‛âlas
aw-las'
A primitive root; to leap for joy, that is, exult, wave joyously
KJV Usage: X peacock, rejoice, solace self.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עלס

1. to rejoice
a. (Qal) to rejoice
b. (Niphal) to exult
1. to flap joyously (of ostrich)
c. (Hithpael) to delight oneself
