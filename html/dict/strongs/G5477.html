Χαναάν Chanaan (i.e. Kenaan), the early name of Palestine
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H3667);
KJV Usage: Chanaan. H3667
Thayer:
1) the land of Canaan
2) in a narrower sense: the part of Palestine lying west of the Jordan
3) in a wider sense: all of Palestine
Literal: Canaan = "lowland"
