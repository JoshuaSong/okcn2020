שׂמאל
śâma'l
saw-mal'
A primitive root (rather denominative from H8040); to use the left hand or pass in that direction
KJV Usage: (go, turn) (on the, to the) left.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

שׂמאל

1. to take the left, go to the left
a. (Hiphil)
1. to go to the left
2. to turn (aside) to the left (fig)
3. to use the left hand
Origin: a primitive root [denom from H8040]
