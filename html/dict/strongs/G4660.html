σκύλλω
to rend, mangle
σκύλλω to flay, i.e. (figuratively) to harass
Derivation: apparently a primary verb;
KJV Usage: trouble(self).
Thayer:
1) to skin, flay
2) to rend, mangle
2a) to vex, trouble, annoy
2b) to give one's self trouble, trouble one's self
