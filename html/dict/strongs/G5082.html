τηλικοῦτος , or feminine τηλικαύτη such as this, i.e. (in (figurative) magnitude) so vast
Derivation: from a compound of G3588 with G2245 and G3778;
KJV Usage: so great, so mighty. G3588 G2245 G3778
Thayer:
1) of age
1a) of such an age
1b) of so great an age, so old
1c) so young
2) of so great a size, in bulk
3) such and so great
