ἀποσπάω
to tear
ἀποσπάω to drag forth, i.e. (literally) unsheathe (a sword), or relatively (with a degree of force implied) retire (personally or factiously)
Derivation: from G575 and G4685;
KJV Usage: (with-)draw (away), after we were gotten from. G575 G4685
Thayer:
1) to draw off, tear away
