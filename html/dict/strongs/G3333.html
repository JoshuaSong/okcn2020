μετακαλέω
to call away to another place
μετακαλέω to call elsewhere, i.e. summon
Derivation: from G3326 and G2564;
KJV Usage: call (for, hither). G3326 G2564
Thayer:
1) to call from one place to another, to summon
2) to call one's self
