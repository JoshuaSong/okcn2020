לבן
lâban
law-ban'
A primitive root; to be (or become) white; also (as denominative from H3843) to make bricks
KJV Usage: make brick, be (made, make) white (-r).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

לבן

1. to be white
a. (Hiphil)
1. to make white, become white, purify
2. to show whiteness, grow white
b. (Hithpael) to become white, be purified (ethical)
2. (Qal) to make bricks
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1074b,1074h
Parts of Speech: Verb
