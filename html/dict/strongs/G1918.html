ἐπιγαμβρεύω
to marry as the next of kin
ἐπιγαμβρεύω to form affinity with, i.e. (specially) in a levirate way
Derivation: from G1909 and a derivative of G1062;
KJV Usage: marry. G1909 G1062
Thayer:
1) to be related to by marriage, enter into affinity with
1a) become any one's father-in-law or son-in-law
1b) to marry the widow of a brother who has died childless
