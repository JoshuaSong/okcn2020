συμμερίζομαι to share jointly, i.e. participate in
Derivation: middle voice from G4862 and G3307;
KJV Usage: be partaker with. G4862 G3307
Thayer:
1) to divide at the same time, divide together
2) to assign a portion
3) to divide together with one (so that a part comes to me, a part to him)
