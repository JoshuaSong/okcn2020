ἀνάχυσις
effusion
ἀνάχυσις properly, effusion, i.e. (figuratively) license
Derivation: from a comparative of G303 and χέω (to pour);
KJV Usage: excess. G303
Thayer:
1) an overflowing, a pouring out
2) metaph. the excess (flood) of riot in which a dissolute life pours itself forth
