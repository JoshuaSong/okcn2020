Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אפרתי
Ephrathite = "ashiness: fruitfulness"
1. an inhabitant or descendant of Ephraim
2. an inhabitant of Bethlehem
Origin: patrial form H672
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Adjective
