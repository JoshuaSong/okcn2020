Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

אמינון אמנון
Amnon = "faithful"
1. oldest son of David, rapist of Tamar, slain by Absalom
2. a son of Shimon (of Caleb's clan)
Origin: from H539
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Noun Masculine
