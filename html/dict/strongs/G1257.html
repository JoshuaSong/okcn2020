διαλείπω
to leave an interval between
διαλείπω to leave off in the middle, i.e. intermit
Derivation: from G1223 and G3007;
KJV Usage: cease. G1223 G3007
Thayer:
1) to interpose a delay, to intermit, leave off for a time something already begun
