ἀπρόσκοπος
not stumbling, void of offence
ἀπρόσκοπος actively, inoffensive, i.e. not leading into sin; passively, faultless, i.e. not led into sin
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4350;
KJV Usage: none (void of, without) offence. G1 G4350
Thayer:
1) having nothing to strike against, not causing to stumble
1a) of a smooth road
1b) metaph. of not leading others to sin by one's mode of life
2) not striking against or stumbling
2a) metaph. not led into sin, blameless
3) without offense, not troubled by a consciousness of sin
