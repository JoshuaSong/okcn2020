גּמר
gâmar
gaw-mar'
A primitive root; to end (in the sense of completion or failure)
KJV Usage: cease, come to an end, fail, perfect, perform.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

גּמר

1. to end, come to an end, complete, cease
a. (Qal)
1. to come to an end, be no more
2. to bring to an end, complete
2. (TWOT) to fail, perfect, perform
