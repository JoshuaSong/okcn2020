πυκνός
close, compact
πυκνός clasped (thick), i.e. (figuratively) frequent; neuter plural (as adverb) frequently
Derivation: from the same as G4635;
KJV Usage: often(-er). G4635
Thayer:
1) thick, dense, compact
1a) in reference to time
1a1) frequently, often, recurring
1b) vigorously, diligently
1c) often, more frequently, the oftener
