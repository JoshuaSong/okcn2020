ἀναφαίνω
to make to give light, make to blaze up
ἀναφαίνω to show, i.e. (reflexively) appear, or (passively) to have pointed out
Derivation: from G303 and G5316;
KJV Usage: (should) appear, discover. G303 G5316
Thayer:
1) to bring to light, hold up to view, show
2) to appear, be made apparent
