ὀμνύω ; to swear, i.e. take (or declare on) oath
Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary, but obsolete ὄμω, for which another prolonged form ὀμόω is used in certain tenses;
KJV Usage: swear.
Thayer:
1) to swear
2) to affirm, promise, threaten, with an oath
3) in swearing to call a person or thing as witness, to invoke, swear by
