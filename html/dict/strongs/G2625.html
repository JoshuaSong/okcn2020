κατακλίνω
to lay down
κατακλίνω to recline down, i.e. (specially) to take a place at table
Derivation: from G2596 and G2827;
KJV Usage: (make) sit down (at meat). G2596 G2827
Thayer:
1) in the NT in reference to eating, to make to recline
2) to recline (at a table)
