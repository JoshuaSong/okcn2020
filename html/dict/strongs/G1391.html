δόξα
a notion
δόξα glory (as very apparent), in a wide application (literal or figurative, objective or subjective)
Derivation: from the base of G1380;
KJV Usage: dignity, glory(-ious), honour, praise, worship. G1380
Thayer:
1) opinion, judgment, view
2) opinion, estimate, whether good or bad concerning someone
2a) in the NT always a good opinion concerning one, resulting in praise, honour, and glory
3) splendour, brightness
3a) of the moon, sun, stars
3b) magnificence, excel
