ἔκτρωμα
a child untimely born, an abortion
ἔκτρωμα a miscarriage (abortion), i.e. (by analogy) untimely birth
Derivation: from a comparative of G1537 and τιτρώσκω (to wound);
KJV Usage: born out of due time. G1537
Thayer:
1) an abortion, abortive birth
2) an untimely birth
