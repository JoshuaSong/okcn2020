συστρατιώτης
a fellow-soldier
συστρατιώτης a co-campaigner, i.e. (figuratively) an associate in Christian toil
Derivation: from G4862 and G4757;
KJV Usage: fellowsoldier. G4862 G4757
Thayer:
1) a fellow soldier
2) an associate in labours and conflicts for the cause of Christ
