נכח
nôkach
no'-kakh
From the same as H5226; properly, the front part; used adverbially (especially with a preposition), opposite, in front of, forward, in behalf of
KJV Usage: (over) against, before, direct [-ly], for, right (on).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נכח

1. be in front of adv
2. in front of, opposite to, in the sight of, before, to the front, right on prep
3. towards the front of, in front of, on behalf of, as far as in front of
Origin: from the same as H5226
TWOT: 1365a
