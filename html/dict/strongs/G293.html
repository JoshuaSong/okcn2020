ἀμφίβληστρον
anything thrown round
ἀμφίβληστρον a (fishing) net (as thrown about the fish)
Derivation: from a compound of the base of G297 and G906;
KJV Usage: net. G297 G906
Thayer:
1) anything thrown around one to impede his motions, as chains, a garment, a net for fishing
