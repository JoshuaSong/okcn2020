להט
lâhaṭ
law-hat'
A primitive root; properly to lick, that is, (by implication) to blaze
KJV Usage: burn (up), set on fire, flaming, kindle.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

להט

1. to burn, blaze, scorch, kindle, blaze up, flame
a. (Qal) blazing (participle)
b. (Piel) to scorch, burn, blaze
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1081
Parts of Speech: Verb
