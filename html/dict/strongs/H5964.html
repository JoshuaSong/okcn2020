Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

עלמת
Alameth or Alemeth = "covering" n pr m
1. a Benjamite, son of Jehoadah or Jerah and descended from Saul through Jonathan
2. a town in Benjamin
Origin: from H5956
