מכך
mâkak
maw-kak'
A primitive root; to tumble (in ruins); figuratively to perish
KJV Usage: be brought low, decay.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מכך

1. to be low, be humiliated
a. (Qal) to be low, be humiliated
b. (Niphal) to sink
c. (Hophal) to be brought low
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1193
Parts of Speech: Verb
