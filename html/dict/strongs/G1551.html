ἐκδέχομαι
to take
ἐκδέχομαι to accept from some source, i.e. (by implication) to await
Derivation: from G1537 and G1209;
KJV Usage: expect, look (tarry) for, wait (for). G1537 G1209
Thayer:
1) to receive, accept
2) to look for, expect, wait for, await
