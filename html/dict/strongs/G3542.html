νομή
a pasture, pasturage
νομή pasture, i.e. (the act) feeding (figuratively, spreading of a gangrene), or (the food) pasturage
Derivation: feminine from the same as G3551;
KJV Usage: X eat, pasture. G3551
Thayer:
1) pasturage, fodder, food
1a) fig. he shall not want the needful supplies for the true life
2) growth, increase
2a) of evils spreading like a gangrene
2b) of ulcers
2c) of a conflagration
