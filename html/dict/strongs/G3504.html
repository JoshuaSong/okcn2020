νεόφυτος
newly planted
νεόφυτος newly planted, i.e. (figuratively) a young convert ("neophyte")
Derivation: from G3501 and a derivative of G5453;
KJV Usage: novice. G3501 G5453
Thayer:
1) newly planted
1a) a new convert, neophyte (one who has recently become a Christian)
