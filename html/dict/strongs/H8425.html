Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּגרמה תּוגרמה
Togarmah = "thou wilt break her" n pr m
1. son of Gomer, grandson of Japheth, and great grandson of Noah n pr terr
2. territory settled by the descendants of Togarmah
a. probably the area known as Armenia
Origin: probably of foreign derivation
