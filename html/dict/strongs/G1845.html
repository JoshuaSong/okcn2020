ἐξορκιστής
an exorcist
ἐξορκιστής one that binds by an oath (or spell), i.e. (by implication) an "exorcist" (conjurer)
Derivation: from G1844;
KJV Usage: exorcist. G1844
Thayer:
1) he who extracts an oath of one
2) an exorcist
2a) one who employs a formula of conjuration for expelling demons
