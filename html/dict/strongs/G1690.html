ἐμβριμάομαι
to snort in
ἐμβριμάομαι to have indignation on, i.e. (transitively) to blame, (intransitively) to sigh with chagrin, (specially) to sternly enjoin
Derivation: from G1722 and βριμάομαι (to snort with anger);
KJV Usage: straitly charge, groan, murmur against. G1722
Thayer:
1) to charge with earnest admonition, sternly to charge, threatened to enjoin
