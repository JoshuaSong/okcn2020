ἀποστοματίζω
to dictate by word of mouth, teach by dictation
ἀποστοματίζω to speak off-hand (properly, dictate), i.e. to catechize (in an invidious manner)
Derivation: from G575 and a (presumed) derivative of G4750;
KJV Usage: provoke to speak. G575 G4750
Thayer:
1) to recite from memory
1a) to repeat to a pupil (anything) for him to commit to memory
2) to ply with questions, to catechise, and so to entice to answers
