ἐπιπλήσσω
to strike at, strike smartly
ἐπιπλήσσω to chastise, i.e. (with words) to upbraid
Derivation: from G1909 and G4141;
KJV Usage: rebuke. G1909 G4141
Thayer:
1) to strike upon, beat upon
2) to chastise with words, to chide, upbraid, rebuke
