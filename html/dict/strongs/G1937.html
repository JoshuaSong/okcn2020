ἐπιθυμέω
to set one's heart upon
ἐπιθυμέω to set the heart upon, i.e. long for (rightfully or otherwise)
Derivation: from G1909 and G2372;
KJV Usage: covet, desire, would fain, lust (after). G1909 G2372
Thayer:
1) to turn upon a thing
2) to have a desire for, long for, to desire
3) to lust after, covet
3a) of those who seek things forbidden
