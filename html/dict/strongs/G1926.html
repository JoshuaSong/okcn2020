ἐπιδέχομαι
to admit besides
ἐπιδέχομαι to admit (as a guest or (figuratively) teacher)
Derivation: from G1909 and G1209;
KJV Usage: receive. G1909 G1209
Thayer:
1) to receive hospitality
2) to admit, i.e. not to reject
