κατασκήνωσις an encamping, i.e. (figuratively) a perch
Derivation: from G2681;
KJV Usage: nest. G2681
Thayer:
1) the pitching of tents, encamping
2) place of tarrying, encampment, abode
2a) of the nest of birds
