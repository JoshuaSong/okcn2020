προέρχομαι
to go forward, go on, advance
προέρχομαι to go onward, precede (in place or time)
Derivation: from G4253 and G2064 (including its alternate);
KJV Usage: go before (farther, forward), outgo, pass on. G4253 G2064
Thayer:
1) to go forward, go on
2) to go before
2a) to go before, precede
2b) to go before, in the advance of another
