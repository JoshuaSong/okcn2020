κατακληροδοτέω
to distribute by lot
κατακληροδοτέω to be a giver of lots to each, i.e. (by implication) to apportion an estate
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of a compound of G2819 and G1325;
KJV Usage: divide by lot. G2596 G2819 G1325
Thayer:
1) to distribute by lot
2) to distribute as an inheritance
