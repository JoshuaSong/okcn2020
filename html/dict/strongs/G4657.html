σκύβαλον
dung, filth, refuse
σκύβαλον what is thrown to the dogs, i.e. refuse (ordure)
Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G1519 and G2965 and G906;
KJV Usage: dung. G1519 G2965 G906
Thayer:
1) any refuse, as the excrement of animals, offscourings, rubbish, dregs
1a) of things worthless and detestable
