νόμισμα
anything sanctioned by usage, a custom, institution
νόμισμα what is reckoned as of value (after the Latin numisma), i.e. current coin
Derivation: from G3543;
KJV Usage: money. G3543
Thayer:
1) anything received and sanctioned by usage or law
2) money, (current) coin, legal tender
