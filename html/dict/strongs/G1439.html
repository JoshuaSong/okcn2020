ἐάω
to let, suffer, allow, permit
ἐάω to let be, i.e. permit or leave alone
Derivation: of uncertain affinity;
KJV Usage: commit, leave, let (alone), suffer. See also G1436. G1436
Thayer:
1) to allow, permit, let
2) to allow one to do as he wishes, not to restrain, to let alone
3) to give up, let go, leave
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Leave, Left
  • Let (alone, go)
  • Suffer
