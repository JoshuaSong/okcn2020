ἐναντίος
opposite
ἐναντίος opposite; figuratively, antagonistic
Derivation: from G1725;
KJV Usage: (over) against, contrary. G1725
Thayer:
1) over against, opposite
1a) of place, opposite, contrary (of the wind)
2) metaph.
2a) opposed as an adversary, hostile, antagonistic in feeling or act
2b) an opponent
