ἀκάθαρτος
uncleansed, unclean, impure
ἀκάθαρτος impure (ceremonially, morally (lewd) or specially, (demonic))
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G2508 (meaning cleansed);
KJV Usage: foul, unclean. G1 G2508
Thayer:
1) not cleansed, unclean
1a) in a ceremonial sense: that which must be abstained from according to the levitical law
1b) in a moral sense: unclean in thought and life
