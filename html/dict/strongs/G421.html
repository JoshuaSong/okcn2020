ἀνεξιχνίαστος
not to be traced, unsearchable, inscrutable
ἀνεξιχνίαστος not tracked out, i.e. (by implication) untraceable
Derivation: from G1 (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a compound of G1537 and a derivative of G2487;
KJV Usage: past finding out; unsearchable. G1 G1537 G2487
Thayer:
1) that cannot be searched out, that cannot be comprehended
