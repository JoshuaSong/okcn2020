ὑποτίθημι
to place under
ὑποτίθημι to place underneath, i.e. (figuratively) to hazard, (reflexively) to suggest
Derivation: from G5259 and G5087;
KJV Usage: lay down, put in remembrance. G5259 G5087
Thayer:
1) to place under
2) metaph. to supply, suggest (supply from one's own resources)
