κατακυριεύω
to gain dominion over
κατακυριεύω to lord against, i.e. control, subjugate
Derivation: from G2596 and G2961;
KJV Usage: exercise dominion over (lordship), be lord over, overcome. G2596 G2961
Thayer:
1) to bring under one's power, to subject one's self, to subdue, master
2) to hold in subjection, to be master of, exercise lordship over
