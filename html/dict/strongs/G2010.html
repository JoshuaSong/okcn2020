ἐπιτρέπω
to turn towards
ἐπιτρέπω to turn over (transfer), i.e. allow
Derivation: from G1909 and the base of G5157;
KJV Usage: give leave (liberty, license), let, permit, suffer. G1909 G5157
Thayer:
1) to turn to, transfer, commit, instruct
2) to permit, allow, give leave
