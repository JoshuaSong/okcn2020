παρεισέρχομαι
to come
παρεισέρχομαι to come in alongside, i.e. supervene additionally or steathily
Derivation: from G3844 and G1525;
KJV Usage: come in privily, enter. G3844 G1525
Thayer:
1) to come in secretly or by stealth, or creep or steal in
2) to enter in addition, come in besides
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Come, Came (see also Coming)
  • Enter, Entering, Entrance
