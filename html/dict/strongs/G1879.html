ἐπαναπαύομαι
to rest upon, depend upon
ἐπαναπαύομαι to settle on; literally (remain) or figuratively (rely)
Derivation: middle voice from G1909 and G373;
KJV Usage: rest in (upon). G1909 G373
Thayer:
1) to cause to rest upon anything
2) to rest upon anything
3) to settle upon, fix its abode upon
