חטּיל
chaṭṭı̂yl
khat-teel'
From an unused root apparently meaning to wave; fluctuating; Chattil, one of Solomon’s servants
KJV Usage: Hattil.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

חטּיל
