ὅρασις
seeing, the act of sight
ὅρασις the act of gazing, i.e. (externally) an aspect or (internally) an inspired appearance
Derivation: from G3708;
KJV Usage: sight, vision. G3708
Thayer:
1) the act of seeing
1a) the sense of sight, the eyes
2) appearance, visible form
3) a vision
3a) an appearance divinely granted in an ecstasy or dream
