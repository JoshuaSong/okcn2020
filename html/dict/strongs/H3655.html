כּנה
kânâh
kaw-naw'
A primitive root; to address by an additional name; hence, to eulogize
KJV Usage: give flattering titles, surname (himself).

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

כּנה

1. to title, surname, be surnamed, give an epithet or cognomen, give a flattering title
a. (Piel) to call by name, give a title, betitle
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 997
Parts of Speech: Verb
