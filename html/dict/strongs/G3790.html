ὀφρῦς the eye-"brow" or forehead, i.e. (figuratively) the brink of a precipice
Derivation: perhaps from G3700 (through the idea of the shading or proximity to the organ of vision);
KJV Usage: brow. G3700
Thayer:
1) the eyebrow
2) any prominence or projection
