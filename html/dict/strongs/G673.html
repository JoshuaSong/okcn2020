ἀποχωρίζω
to part
ἀποχωρίζω to rend apart; reflexively, to separate
Derivation: from G575 and G5563;
KJV Usage: depart (asunder). G575 G5563
Thayer:
1) to separate, sever
2) to part asunder
3) to separate one's self, depart from
