παρασκευάζω
to get ready, prepare
παρασκευάζω to furnish aside, i.e. get ready
Derivation: from G3844 and a derivative of G4632;
KJV Usage: prepare self, be (make) ready. G3844 G4632
Thayer:
1) to make one's self ready, to prepare one's self
2) have prepared one's self, to be prepared or ready
