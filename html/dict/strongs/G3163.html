μάχη
battle, fight, combat
μάχη a battle, i.e. (figuratively) controversy
Derivation: from G3164;
KJV Usage: fighting, strive, striving. G3164
Thayer:
1) a fight or combat
1a) of those in arms, a battle
1b) of persons at variance, disputants etc., strife, contention
1c) a quarrel
