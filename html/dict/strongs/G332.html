ἀναθεματίζω
to devote
ἀναθεματίζω to declare or vow under penalty of execration
Derivation: from G331;
KJV Usage: (bind under a) curse, bind with an oath. G331
Thayer:
1) to devote to destruction
2) to declare one's self liable to the severest divine penalties
