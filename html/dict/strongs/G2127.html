εὐλογέω
to speak well of, praise, honour
εὐλογέω to speak well of, i.e. (religiously) to bless (thank or invoke a benediction upon, prosper)
Derivation: from a compound of G2095 and G3056;
KJV Usage: bless, praise. G2095 G3056
Thayer:
1) to praise, celebrate with praises
2) to invoke blessings
3) to consecrate a thing with solemn prayers
3a) to ask God's blessing on a thing
3b) pray God to bless it to one's use
3c) pronounce a consecratory blessing on
4) of God
4a) to cause to prosper, to make happy, to bestow blessings on
4b) favoured of God, blessed
Vine's Expository Dictionary of New Testament Words
  • Bless, Blessed, Blessedness, Blessing
