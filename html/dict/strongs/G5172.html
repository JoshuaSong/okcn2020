τρυφή
softness, delicacy, daintiness
τρυφή effeminacy, i.e. luxury or debauchery
Derivation: from θρύπτω (to break up or (figuratively) enfeeble, especially the mind and body by indulgence);
KJV Usage: delicately, riot.
Thayer:
1) softness, effeminate, luxurious living
