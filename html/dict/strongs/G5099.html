τίνω
to pay a price
τίνω ; to pay a price, i.e. as a penalty
Derivation: strengthened for a primary τίω (which is only used as an alternate in certain tenses);
KJV Usage: be punished with.
Thayer:
1) to pay, to recompense
2) to pay penalty, suffer punishment
