τέλειος
having reached its end, finished, complete
τέλειος complete (in various applications of labor, growth, mental and moral character, etc.); neuter (as noun, with G3588) completeness
Derivation: from G5056;
KJV Usage: of full age, man, perfect. G5056 G3588
Thayer:
1) brought to its end, finished
2) wanting nothing necessary to completeness
3) perfect
4) that which is perfect
4a) consummate human integrity and virtue
4b) of men
4b1) full grown, adult, of full age, mature
