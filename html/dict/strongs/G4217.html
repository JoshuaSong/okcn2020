ποταπός interrogatively, whatever, i.e. of what possible sort
Derivation: apparently from G4219 and the base of G4226;
KJV Usage: what (manner of). G4219 G4226
Thayer:
1) from what country, nation or tribe
2) of what sort or quality (what manner of)
2a) of persons
2b) of things
