κοπιάω
to be tired, grow weary
κοπιάω to feel fatigue; by implication, to work hard
Derivation: from a derivative of G2873;
KJV Usage: (bestow) labour, toil, be wearied. G2873
Thayer:
1) to grow weary, tired, exhausted (with toil or burdens or grief)
2) to labour with wearisome effort, to toil
2a) of bodily labour
