ἐναντίον (adverbially) in the presence (view) of
Derivation: neuter of G1727;
KJV Usage: before, in the presence of. G1727
Thayer:
1) over against, opposite
1a) of place, opposite, contrary (of the wind)
2) metaph.
2a) opposed as an adversary, hostile, antagonistic in feeling or act
2b) an opponent
