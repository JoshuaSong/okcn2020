καταδυναστεύω
to exercise power over
καταδυναστεύω to exercise dominion against, i.e. oppress
Derivation: from G2596 and a derivative of G1413;
KJV Usage: oppress. G2596 G1413
Thayer:
1) to exercise harsh control over one, to use one's power against one
2) to oppress one
