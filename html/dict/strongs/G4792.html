συγκομίζω
to carry
συγκομίζω to convey together, i.e. collect or bear away in company with others
Derivation: from G4862 and G2865;
KJV Usage: carry. G4862 G2865
Thayer:
1) to carry or bring together, to collect
1a) to house crops, gather into granaries
2) to carry with others, help in carrying out, the dead to be buried or burned
2a) to bury
