ἀπαλλάσσω
to set free, release, deliver
ἀπαλλάσσω to change away, i.e. release, (reflexively) remove
Derivation: from G575 and G236;
KJV Usage: deliver, depart. G575 G236
Thayer:
1) to remove, release, to be removed, to depart
2) to set free, deliver
