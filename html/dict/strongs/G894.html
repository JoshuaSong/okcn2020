ἄψινθος
wormwood
ἄψινθος wormwood (as a type of bitterness, i.e. (figuratively) calamity)
Derivation: of uncertain derivation;
KJV Usage: wormwood.
Thayer:
1) wormwood
2) the name of a star which fell into the waters and made them bitter
