περίσσευμα

περίσσευμα a surplus, or superabundance
Derivation: from G4052;
KJV Usage: abundance, that was left, over and above. G4052
Thayer:
1) abundance, in which one delights
1a) of that which fills the heart
2) that which is left over, residue, remains
