βρῶσις
meat
βρῶσις (abstractly) eating (literally or figuratively); by extension (concretely) food (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: from the base of G977;
KJV Usage: eating, food, meat. G977
Thayer:
1) act of eating
1a) in a wider sense, corrosion
2) that which is eaten, food, ailment
2a) of the soul's food, either which refreshes the soul, or nourishes and supports it
