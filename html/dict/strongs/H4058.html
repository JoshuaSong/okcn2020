מדד
mâdad
maw-dad'
A primitive root; properly to stretch; by implication to measure (as if by stretching a line); figuratively to be extended
KJV Usage: measure, mete, stretch self.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מדד

1. to measure, stretch
a. (Qal) to measure
b. (Niphal) to be measured
c. (Piel)
1. to extend, continue
2. to measure, measure off
d. (Po) measured
e. (Hithpolel) to extend oneself, stretch oneself
Origin: a primitive root
TWOT: 1146
Parts of Speech: Verb
