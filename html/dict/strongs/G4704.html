σπουδάζω
to make haste
σπουδάζω to use speed, i.e. to make effort, be prompt or earnest
Derivation: from G4710;
KJV Usage: do (give) diligence, be diligent (forward), endeavour, labour, study. G4710
Thayer:
1) to hasten, make haste
2) to exert one's self, endeavour, give diligence
