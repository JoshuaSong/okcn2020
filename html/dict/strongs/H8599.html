Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

תּפּוּח
Tappuah = "the apple city" n pr m
1. a Judaite of the family of Caleb, son of Hebron n pr loc
2. a town in the lowland of Judah
3. a town on the border in Ephraim
4. a territory within the boundaries of Manasseh
Origin: the same as H8598
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
