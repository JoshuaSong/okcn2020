מהללאל
mahălal'êl
mah-hal-al-ale'
From H4110 and H410; praise of God; Mahalalel, the name of an antediluvian patriarch and of an Israelite
KJV Usage: Mahalaleel.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מהללאל
Mahalaleel = "praise of God"
1. son of Cainan and the 4th in descent from Adam in the line of Seth
2. a descendant of Perez, the son of Judah
Origin: from H4110 and H410
TWOT: None
Parts of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
