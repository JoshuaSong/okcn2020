Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

מפעת מיפעת מופעתo
Mephaath = "splendour" or "height"
1. a city of Reuben allotted to the Merarite Levites lying in the district of the Mishor; site uncertain
2. a city in Moab
