ὑπέχω
to hold
ὑπέχω to hold oneself under, i.e. endure with patience
Derivation: from G5259 and G2192;
KJV Usage: suffer. G5259 G2192
Thayer:
1) to hold under, to put under, place underneath
2) metaph. to sustain, undergo
2a) suffer punishment
