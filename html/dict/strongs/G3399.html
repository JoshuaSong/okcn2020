Μίλητος Miletus, a city of Asia Minor
Derivation: of uncertain origin;
KJV Usage: Miletus.
Thayer:
1) a maritime city, now nearly
10 miles (16 km), from the coast of Caria or Ionia, near the mouth of the Maeander and
35 miles (55 km) from Ephesus. It was the mother of about eighty colonies, the the birth place of Thales, Anaximander, and other famous men.
Literal: Miletus = "pure white fine wool"
