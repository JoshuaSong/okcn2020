δηλόω
to make visible
δηλόω to make plain (by words)
Derivation: from G1212;
KJV Usage: declare, shew, signify. G1212
Thayer:
1) to make manifest
2) to make known by relating, to declare
3) to give one to understand, to indicate, signify
For Synonyms see entry G5831
