פּנים
pânı̂ym
paw-neem'
Plural (but always used as a singular) of an unused noun ( פּנה pâneh , paw-neh' ; from 6437); the face (as the part that turns); used in a great variety of applications (literally and figuratively); also (with prepositional prefix) as a preposition (before, etc.)
