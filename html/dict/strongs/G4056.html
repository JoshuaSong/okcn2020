περισσοτέρως more superabundantly
Derivation: adverb from G4055;
KJV Usage: more abundant(-ly), X the more earnest, (more) exceedingly, more frequent, much more, the rather. G4055
Thayer:
1) more abundantly
2) more in a greater degree
3) more earnestly, more exceedingly
4) especially, above others
