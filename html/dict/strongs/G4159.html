πόθεν from which (as interrogative) or what (as relative) place, state, source or cause
Derivation: from the base of G4213 with enclitic adverb of origin;
KJV Usage: whence. G4213
Thayer:
1) of place: from where, from what condition
2) of origin or source: from what author or giver
3) of cause: how is that?, how can that be?
