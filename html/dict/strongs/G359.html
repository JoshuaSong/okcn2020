a loosing, releasing
ἀνάλυσις departure
Derivation: from G360;
KJV Usage: departure. G360
Thayer:
1) an unloosing (as of things woven)
2) a dissolving (into separate parts)
3) departure
3a) a metaphor drawn from loosing from moorings preparatory to setting sail
