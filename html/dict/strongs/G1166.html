δεικνύω to show (literally or figuratively)
Derivation: a prolonged form of an obsolete primary of the same meaning;
KJV Usage: shew.
Thayer:
1) to show, expose to the eyes
2) metaph.
2a) to give evidence or proof of a thing
2b) to show by words or teach
