περικεφαλαία
a covering for the head, a helmet, cap
περικεφαλαία encirclement of the head, i.e. a helmet
Derivation: feminine of a compound of G4012 and G2776;
KJV Usage: helmet. G4012 G2776
Thayer:
1) a helmet
2) metaph.
2a) the protection of the soul which consists in (the hope of) salvation
