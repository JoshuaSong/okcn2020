ὑπερφρονέω
to be over-proud, to have high thoughts
ὑπερφρονέω to esteem oneself overmuch, i.e. be vain or arrogant
Derivation: from G5228 and G5426;
KJV Usage: think more highly. G5228 G5426
Thayer:
1) to think more highly of one's self than is proper
