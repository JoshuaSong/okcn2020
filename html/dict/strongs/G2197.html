Ζαχαρίας Zacharias (i.e. Zechariah), the name of two Israelites
Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H2148);
KJV Usage: Zacharias. H2148
Thayer:
1) the father of John the Baptist
2) son of Barachias, who was slain by the Jews between the altar and the temple
Literal: Zacharias = "remembered of Jehovah"
