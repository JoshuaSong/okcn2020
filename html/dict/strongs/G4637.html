σκηνόω
to pitch tents, encamp
σκηνόω to tent or encamp, i.e. (figuratively) to occupy (as a mansion) or (specially), to reside (as God did in the Tabernacle of old, a symbol of protection and communion)
Derivation: from G4636;
KJV Usage: dwell. G4636
Thayer:
1) to fix one's tabernacle, have one's tabernacle, abide (or live) in a tabernacle (or tent), tabernacle
2) to dwell
