συζητέω
to search
συζητέω to investigate jointly, i.e. discuss, controvert, cavil
Derivation: from G4862 and G2212;
KJV Usage: dispute (with), enquire, question (with), reason (together). G4862 G2212
Thayer:
1) to seek or examine together
2) in the NT to discuss, dispute, question
