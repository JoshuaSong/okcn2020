נטל
nâṭal
naw-tal'
A primitive root; to lift; by implication to impose
KJV Usage: bear, offer, take up.

Brown-Driver-Briggs' Hebrew Definitions

נטל

1. to lift, bear, bear up
a. (Qal) to lift, lift over, lift upon, set up
b. (Piel) to bear up
Origin: a primary root
