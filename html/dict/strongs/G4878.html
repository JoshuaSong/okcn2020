συναντιλαμβάνομαι to take hold of opposite together, i.e. co-operate (assist)
Derivation: from G4862 and G482;
KJV Usage: help. G4862 G482
Thayer:
1) to lay hold along with, to strive to obtain with others, help in obtaining
2) to take hold with another
