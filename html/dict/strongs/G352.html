ἀνακύπτω
to lift up the head
ἀνακύπτω to unbend, i.e. rise; figuratively, be elated
Derivation: from G303 (in the sense of reversal) and G2955;
KJV Usage: lift up, look up. G303 G2955
Thayer:
1) to raise or lift one's self up
1a) one's body
1b) one's soul
1b1) to be elated or exalted
