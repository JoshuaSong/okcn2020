χράομαι
consulting
χράομαι to furnish what is needed; (give an oracle, "graze" (touch slightly), light upon, etc.), i.e. (by implication) to employ or (by extension) to act towards one in a given manner
Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb (perhaps rather from G5495, to handle);
KJV Usage: entreat, use. Compare G5531; G5534. G5531 G5495 G5534
Thayer:
1) to receive a loan
2) borrow
3) to take for one's use, to use
3a) to make use of a thing
