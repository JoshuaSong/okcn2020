παραγίνομαι to become near, i.e. approach (have arrived); by implication, to appear publicly
Derivation: from G3844 and G1096;
KJV Usage: come, go, be present. G3844 G1096
Thayer:
1) to be present, to come near, approach
2) to come forth, make one's public appearance
