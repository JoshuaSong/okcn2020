ὁρίζω
to divide
ὁρίζω to mark out or bound ("horizon"), i.e. (figuratively) to appoint, decree, specify
Derivation: from G3725;
KJV Usage: declare, determine, limit, ordain. G3725
Thayer:
1) to define
1a) to mark out the boundaries or limits (of any place or thing)
1b to determine, appoint 1b1) that which has been determined, acc. to appointment, decree
1b2) to ordain, determine, appoint
