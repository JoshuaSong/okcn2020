προκαταγγέλλω
to announce
προκαταγγέλλω to anounce beforehand, i.e. predict, promise
Derivation: from G4253 and G2605;
KJV Usage: foretell, have notice, (shew) before. G4253 G2605
Thayer:
1) to announce beforehand (that a thing will be)
1a) of prophecies
2) to preannounce in the sense of to promise
