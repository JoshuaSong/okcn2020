πρόφασις
that which is alleged as the cause, an allegation, plea
πρόφασις an outward showing, i.e. pretext
Derivation: from a compound of G4253 and G5316;
KJV Usage: cloke, colour, pretence, show. G4253 G5316
Thayer:
1) a pretext (alleged reason, pretended cause)
2) show
2a) under colour as though they would do something
2b) in pretence, ostensibly
