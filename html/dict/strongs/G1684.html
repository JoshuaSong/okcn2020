ἐμβαίνω
to step in
ἐμβαίνω to walk on, i.e. embark (aboard a vessel), reach (a pool)
Derivation: from G1722 and the base of G939;
KJV Usage: come (get) into, enter (into), go (up) into, step in, take ship. G1722 G939
Thayer:
1) to go into, step into

  • Enter, Entering, Entrance
  • Get, Got, Gotten
