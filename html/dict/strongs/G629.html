ἀπολύτρωσις
a ransoming
ἀπολύτρωσις (the act) ransom in full, i.e. (figuratively) riddance, or (specially) Christian salvation
Derivation: from a compound of G575 and G3083;
KJV Usage: deliverance, redemption. G575 G3083
Thayer:
1) a releasing effected by payment of ransom
1a) redemption, deliverance
1b) liberation procured by the payment of a ransom
