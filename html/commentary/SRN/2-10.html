Chapter 10

10:11  Not so: go now ye that are men, and serve the LORD; for that ye did desire. And they were driven out from Pharaoh's presence.

ye that are

The third compromise proposed by Pharaoh is, perhaps, as applied to believers, the sublest and most successful of them all. The most godly parents desire worldly prosperity and position for their children. Matthew 20:20 Matthew 20:21 .

10:24  And Pharaoh called unto Moses, and said, Go ye, serve the LORD; only let your flocks and your herds be stayed: let your little ones also go with you.

said, Go ye

i.e. "Leave your property in the world." Cf. Matthew 16:25-27 ; Luke 18:18-25 .
