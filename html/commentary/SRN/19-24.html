Chapter 24

24:3  Who shall ascend into the hill of the LORD? or who shall stand in his holy place?

Who shall

The order is:

(1) the declaration of title, "the earth is the Lord's" Psalms 24:1 Psalms 24:2 .

(2) Who shall rule the earth? ( Psalms 24:3-6 ). It is a question of worthiness, and no one is worthy but the Lamb. Cf. ; Daniel 7:13 Daniel 7:14 ; Revelation 5:3-10 ; Matthew 25:31 .

(3) The King of glory takes the throne of earth Psalms 24:7-10 .

See Psa 40., next in order of the Messianic Psalms.
