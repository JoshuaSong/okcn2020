Chapter 21

21:8  Thou shalt sanctify him therefore; for he offereth the bread of thy God: he shall be holy unto thee: for I the LORD, which sanctify you, am holy.

sanctify

Verse 8 illustrates the O.T. holiness or sanctification--a person set apart for the service of God.

sanctify

Heb. "qodesh." (See Scofield "Genesis 2:3") .
