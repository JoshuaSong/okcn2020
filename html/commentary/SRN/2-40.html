Chapter 40

40:2  On the first day of the first month shalt thou set up the tabernacle of the tent of the congregation.

first month

i.e. April.

40:34  Then a cloud covered the tent of the congregation, and the glory of the LORD filled the tabernacle.

glory

Cf. Ephesians 2:22 . What the shekinah glory was to tabernacle and temple, that the Spirit is to the "holy temple," the Church, and to the temple which is the believer's body. 1 Corinthians 6:19 .
