52:14  As many were astonied at thee; his visage was so marred more than any man, and his form more than the sons of men:

so marred

The literal rendering is terrible: "So marred from the form of man was His aspect that His appearance was not that of a son of man"--i.e. not human--the effect of the brutalities described in Matthew 26:67 Matthew 26:68 ; 27:27-30 .
