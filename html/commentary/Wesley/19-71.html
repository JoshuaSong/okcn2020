The Book of Psalms
Chapter 71

Chapter Overview:


David wrote this psalm in his old age, of which the former part is prayer, ver. 1 - 13.
The latter part, joyful praise and thanksgiving, ver. 14 - 24.
Verses:
71:7A wonder - For my many and sore calamities.
71:15The numbers - Of thy salvations and mercies vouchsafed to me.
71:16Make mention - To support and comfort myself with the remembrance of it. Righteousness - Of thy faithfulness in making good all thy promises.
71:19Very high - Most eminent.
71:20Bring me - From the grave.
