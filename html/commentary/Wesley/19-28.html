The Book of Psalms
Chapter 28

Chapter Overview:


A prayer for help, ver. 1 - 3.
The doom of the wicked, ver. 4, 5.
A thanksgiving, closed with prayer, ver. 6 - 9.
A psalm of David.
Verses:
28:2Towards - Towards the holy of holies, because there the ark was; from whence God gave oracular answers to his people.
28:3Draw not - Do not drag me; as thou dost these, to execution and destruction.
28:5Regard not - The providential works of God towards his people.
28:7I am helped - He speaks of it as past, because God assured him by his spirit, that he had heard and accepted his prayers.
