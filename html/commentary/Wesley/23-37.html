The Book of Isaiah
Chapter 37

Chapter Overview:


Hezekiah mourns and sends to Isaiah to pray for them, ver. 1 - 5.
He comforts them, ver. 6, 7.
Sennacherib called away against the king of Ethiopia, sends a blasphemous letter to Hezekiah, ver. 8 - 13.
His prayer, ver. 14 - 20.
Isaiah's prophecy, ver. 21 - 35.
An angel slays the Assyrians, ver. 36.
Sennacherib is slain at Nineveh by his own sons, ver. 37, 38.
