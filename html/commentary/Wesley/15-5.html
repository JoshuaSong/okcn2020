The Book of Ezra
Chapter 5

Chapter Overview:


Zerubbabel encouraged by Haggai and Zechariah, sets the work forward again, ver. 1, 2.
Their adversaries oppose them again, ver. 3 - 5.
Write to Darius, ver. 6 - 17.
Verses:
5:1The son - His grand - child; for he was the son of Baraciah.Prophesied - Commanding them from God to return to building the temple, with a promise of his favour and assistance.
5:2Helping - Encouraging the people to work by their presence, and assurance of success. It is supposed, the work had stopt about fifteen years. The first chapter of Haggai is the best comment on these two verses.
5:3Shethar - boznai - Not Rehum and Shimshai, &c. who were either dead, or removed from their office by Darius.
5:4We - Jews. Accordingly - According to what they asked.That made this building - That were the undertakers and encouragers of it.
5:8Great God - And indeed, thus far the greater part of the Samaritans agreed with them.
5:17Now therefore. &c. - If the case had been so fairly stated to Artaxerxes, he would hardly have hindered the work. The people of God could not be persecuted, if they were not belied.
