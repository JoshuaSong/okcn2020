The Book of Psalms
Chapter 30

Chapter Overview:


He praises God for delivering him, and exhorts others to praise him, ver. 1 - 5.
Recollects his former security, and his prayer when in trouble, ver. 6 - 10.
And stirs himself up to thankfulness, ver. 11, 12.
A Psalm and song, at the dedication of the house of David.
 
Title of the psalm. Song - A psalm to be sung with the voice to an instrument. David - At the dedication of David's house, which was built, 5:11 , and doubtless was dedicated, as God had commanded.
