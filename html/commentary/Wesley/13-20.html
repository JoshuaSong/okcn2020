The Book of 1 Chronicles
Chapter 20

Chapter Overview:


Rabbah is taken, ver. 1 - 3.
The Philistine giants are slain, ver. 4 - 8.

Verses:
20:2To weigh a talent - Or, to be worth a talent, that is, five thousand four hundred and seventy five pounds.
20:8They fell, &c - We need not fear great men against us, while we have the great God for us.
