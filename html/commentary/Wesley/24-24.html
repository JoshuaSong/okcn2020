The Book of Jeremiah
Chapter 24

Chapter Overview:


Under the type of good and bad figs, ver. 1 - 3.
He fore - shews the return of some from captivity, ver. 4 - 7.
and the ruin of Zedekiah and the rest, ver. 8 - 10.
Verses:
24:1And behold - Probably a vision.
24:5Acknowledge - I will acknowledge them for their good; I will shew them favour, being of the number of those who were not leaders to sin, but led away by the ill example of others, and who being carried away grew sensible of their sins, and so accepted of the punishment of their iniquities.
