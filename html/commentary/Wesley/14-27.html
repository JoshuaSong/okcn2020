The Book of 2 Chronicles
Chapter 27

Chapter Overview:


Jotham reigns well and prospers, ver. 1 - 6.
The conclusion of his reign, ver. 7 - 9.

Verses:
27:2He did - He did according to all that his father Uzziah did; except in his miscarriages. We must not imitate those we have the greatest esteem for, any farther than we do well; but their failings must be warnings to us, to walk more circumspectly.
27:3Built - Repaired it: for it was built before, chap. 11:5 .
