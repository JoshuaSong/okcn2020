The Book of Jeremiah
Chapter 45

Chapter Overview:


Jeremiah instructs and comforts Baruch, ver. 1 - 5.

Verses:
45:3I find no rest - Upon Baruch's reading the prophecies both he and Jeremiah were advised to hide themselves. This probably disturbed Baruch, and made him lament his condition.
45:5Seek them not - Dost thou expect what none is like to meet with who feareth God? Upon all flesh - I am bringing calamities upon the whole nation.
