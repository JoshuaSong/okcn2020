The Book of 1 Chronicles
Chapter 19

Chapter Overview:


David's friendly message to king Hanun, ver. 1, 2.
Hanun's base usage of his ambassadors, ver. 3 - 5.
The Ammonites prepare for war, ver. 6, 7.
David overthrows them and the Syrians, ver. 8 - 19.

Verses:
19:7Chariots - Thirty two thousand men, who fought partly from chariots, and partly on foot with chariots, or attending upon thechariots, as the ancient manner of fighting was.
19:19His servants - Let those who have in vain stood it out against God, be thus wise for themselves. Let them become his servants;for they are undone, if they remain his enemies.
