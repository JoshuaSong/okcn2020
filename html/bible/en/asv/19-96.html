96
1Oh sing unto Jehovah a new song: Sing unto Jehovah, all the earth. 2Sing unto Jehovah, bless his name; Show forth his salvation from day to day. 3Declare his glory among the nations, His marvellous works among all the peoples. 4For great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised: He is to be feared above all gods. 5For all the gods of the peoples are idols; But Jehovah made the heavens. 6Honor and majesty are before him: Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary. 7Ascribe unto Jehovah, ye kindreds of the peoples, Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength. 8Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name: Bring an offering, and come into his courts. 9Oh worship Jehovah in holy array: Tremble before him, all the earth. 10Say among the nations, Jehovah reigneth: The world also is established that it cannot be moved: He will judge the peoples with equity. 11Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice; Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; 12Let the field exult, and all that is therein; Then shall all the trees of the wood sing for joy 13Before Jehovah; for he cometh, For he cometh to judge the earth: He will judge the world with righteousness, And the peoples with his truth.
