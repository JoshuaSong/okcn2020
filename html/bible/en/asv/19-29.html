29
1Ascribe unto Jehovah, O ye sons of the mighty, Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength. 2Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name; Worship Jehovah in holy array.
3The voice of Jehovah is upon the waters: The God of glory thundereth, Even Jehovah upon many waters. 4The voice of Jehovah is powerful; The voice of Jehovah is full of majesty. 5The voice of Jehovah breaketh the cedars; Yea, Jehovah breaketh in pieces the cedars of Lebanon. 6He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild-ox. 7The voice of Jehovah cleaveth the flames of fire. 8The voice of Jehovah shaketh the wilderness; Jehovah shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh. 9The voice of Jehovah maketh the hinds to calve, And strippeth the forests bare: And in his temple everything saith, Glory.
10Jehovah sat as King at the Flood; Yea, Jehovah sitteth as King for ever. 11Jehovah will give strength unto his people; Jehovah will bless his people with peace.
