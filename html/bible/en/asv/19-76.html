76
1In Judah is God known: His name is great in Israel. 2In Salem also is his tabernacle, And his dwelling-place in Zion. 3There he brake the arrows of the bow; The shield, and the sword, and the battle.
4Glorious art thou and excellent, From the mountains of prey. 5The stouthearted are made a spoil, They have slept their sleep; And none of the men of might have found their hands. 6At thy rebuke, O God of Jacob, Both chariot and horse are cast into a deep sleep. 7Thou, even thou, art to be feared; And who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry? 8Thou didst cause sentence to be heard from heaven; The earth feared, and was still, 9When God arose to judgment, To save all the meek of the earth.
10Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: The residue of wrath shalt thou gird upon thee. 11Vow, and pay unto Jehovah your God: Let all that are round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared. 12He will cut off the spirit of princes: He is terrible to the kings of the earth.
