The Allotment for Judah
1The allotment for the tribe of the people of Judah according to their clans reached southward #Num. 34:3to the boundary of Edom, to #Num. 33:36the wilderness of Zin at the farthest south. 2And their south boundary ran from the end of the #[See ver. 1 above]Salt Sea, from the bay that faces southward. 3It goes out southward of #Num. 34:4Judg. 1:36the ascent of Akrabbim, passes along to Zin, and goes up south of Kadesh-barnea, along by Hezron, up to Addar, turns about to Karka, 4passes along to Azmon, goes out by #ver. 47Num. 34:5[ch. 13:3]the Brook of Egypt, and comes to its end at the sea. This shall be your south boundary. 5And the east boundary is the #[See ver. 1 above]Salt Sea, to the mouth of the Jordan. And the boundary on the north side runs from the bay of the sea at the mouth of the Jordan. 6And the boundary goes up to #ch. 18:19Beth-hoglah and passes along north of #ch. 18:18Beth-arabah. And the boundary goes up to #ch. 18:17the stone of Bohan the son of Reuben. 7And the boundary goes up to Debir from #ch. 7:26the Valley of Achor, and so northward, turning toward Gilgal, which is opposite #ch. 18:17the ascent of Adummim, which is on the south side of the valley. And the boundary passes along to the waters of #ch. 18:17En-shemesh and ends at #ch. 18:162 Sam. 17:171 Kgs. 1:9En-rogel. 8Then the boundary goes up by #ch. 18:162 Kgs. 23:10the Valley of the Son of Hinnom at the southern shoulder of the Jebusite (#ver. 63ch. 18:28[Judg. 19:101 Chr. 11:4]that is, Jerusalem). And the boundary goes up to the top of the mountain that lies over against the Valley of Hinnom, on the west, at the northern end of the Valley #ch. 18:16See ch. 12:4of Rephaim. 9Then the boundary extends from the top of the mountain #ch. 18:15to the spring of the waters of Nephtoah, and from there to the cities of Mount Ephron. Then the boundary bends around to Baalah (#ver. 601 Chr. 13:6that is, Kiriath-jearim). 10And the boundary circles west of Baalah to Mount Seir, passes along to the northern shoulder of Mount Jearim (that is, Chesalon), and goes down to #1 Sam. 6:9Beth-shemesh and passes along by #Gen. 38:12-14Judg. 14:1Timnah. 11The boundary goes out #ch. 13:3to the shoulder of the hill north of Ekron, then the boundary bends around to Shikkeron and passes along to Mount #ver. 9Baalah and goes out to Jabneel. Then the boundary comes to an end at the sea. 12And the west boundary was #ver. 47ch. 23:4Num. 34:6Ezek. 47:20the Great Sea with its coastline. This is the boundary around the people of Judah according to their clans.

And he gave him Achsah his daughter as wife. 18When she came to him, she urged him to ask her father for a field. And she got off her donkey, and Caleb said to her, "What do you want?" 19She said to him, "Give me a blessing. Since you have given me the land of the Negeb, give me also springs of water." And he gave her the upper springs and the lower springs.
