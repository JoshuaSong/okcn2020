Forgive the Sinner
5Now #[1 Cor. 5:1if anyone has caused pain, #[Gal. 4:12]he has caused it not to me, but #ch. 1:14in some measure—not to put it too severely—to all of you. 6For such a one, #1 Cor. 5:4[ch. 7:11]this punishment by the majority is enough, 7so #Gal. 6:1[Eph. 4:32]you should rather turn to forgive and comfort him, or he may be overwhelmed by excessive sorrow. 8So I beg you to reaffirm your love for him. 9For this is why I wrote, that I might #Phil. 2:22test you and know #ch. 7:15|NoBook| 10:6whether you are obedient in everything. 10Anyone whom you forgive, I also forgive. Indeed, what I have forgiven, if I have forgiven anything, has been for your sake in the presence of Christ, 11so that we would not be outwitted by Satan; for #See 1 Pet. 5:8we are not ignorant of his designs.

12When #Acts 16:8Acts 20:6I came to Troas to preach the gospel of Christ, even though #See Acts 14:27a door was opened for me in the Lord, 13my spirit #ch. 7:5was not at rest because I did not find my brother Titus there. So I took leave of them and went on to Macedonia.
14But #ch. 8:16[ch. 9:15]See Rom. 6:17thanks be to God, who in Christ always #Col. 2:15 (Gk.)leads us in triumphal procession, and through us spreads #Eph. 5:2Phil. 4:18[Song 1:3]the fragrance of the knowledge of him everywhere. 15For we are the aroma of Christ to God among #See 1 Cor. 1:18those who are being saved and among #ch. 4:3those who are perishing, 16#[Luke 2:34John 9:391 Pet. 2:78]to one a fragrance from death to death, #[Luke 2:34John 9:391 Pet. 2:78]to the other a fragrance from life to life. #ch. 3:5Who is sufficient#Or competent for these things? 17For we are not, like so many, peddlers of God's word, but as men of sincerity, as commissioned by God, in the sight of God we speak in Christ.
