12So Jeroboam and all the people came to Rehoboam the third day, as the king said, #ver. 5"Come to me again the third day." 13And the king answered the people harshly, and forsaking the counsel that the old men had given him, 14he spoke to them according to the counsel of the young men, saying, #[See ver. 4 above]"My father made your yoke heavy, but I will add to your yoke.
