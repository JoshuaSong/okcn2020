Jeremiah Taken to Egypt
1When Jeremiah finished speaking to all the people all these words of the Lord their God, with which the Lord their God had sent him to them, 2Azariah the son of #ch. 42:1Hoshaiah and #See ch. 40:8Johanan the son of Kareah and #Ps. 86:14Isa. 13:11Mal. 4:1all the insolent men said to Jeremiah, "You are telling a lie. The Lord our God did not send you to say, #ch. 42:19"Do not go to Egypt to live there," 3but #See ch. 32:12Baruch the son of Neriah #[ch. 38:22]has set you against us, #[ch. 37:13]to deliver us into the hand of the Chaldeans, that they may kill us or take us into exile in Babylon." 4So #[See ver. 2 above]Johanan the son of Kareah and all #ch. 40:13the commanders of the forces and all the people did not obey the voice of the Lord, to remain in the land of Judah. 5But Johanan the son of Kareah and all the commanders of the forces took #[ch. 40:11all the remnant of Judah who had returned to live in the land of Judah from all the nations to which they had been driven— 6#ch. 44:20the men, the women, the children, #See ch. 38:23the princesses, and every person whom #ch. 39:10|NoBook| 40:7Nebuzaradan the captain of the guard had left with #See ch. 39:14Gedaliah the son of Ahikam, son of Shaphan; also Jeremiah the prophet and Baruch the son of Neriah. 7And they came into the land of Egypt, for they did not obey the voice of the Lord.
