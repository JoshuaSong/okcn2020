The Nazirite Vow
1And the Lord spoke to Moses, saying, 2"Speak to the people of Israel and say to them, When either a man or a woman makes a special vow, the vow of #Judg. 13:5[Acts 21:23]a Nazirite,#Nazirite means one separated, or one consecrated #[Rom. 1:1]to separate himself to the Lord, 3he #Amos 2:12Luke 1:15shall separate himself from wine and strong drink. He shall drink no vinegar made from wine or strong drink and shall not drink any juice of grapes or eat grapes, fresh or dried. 4All the days of his separation#Or Naziriteship he shall eat nothing that is produced by the grapevine, not even the seeds or the skins.

21"This is the law of the Nazirite. But if he vows an offering to the Lord above his Nazirite vow, as he can afford, in exact accordance with the vow that he takes, then he shall do in addition to the law of the Nazirite."
