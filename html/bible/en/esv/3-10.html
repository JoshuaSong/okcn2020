The Death of Nadab and Abihu
1Now #ch. 16:1Ex. 6:23|NoBook| 28:1Num. 3:4|NoBook| 26:611 Chr. 24:2Nadab and Abihu, the sons of Aaron, #[Num. 16:18]each took his censer and put fire in it and laid incense on it and offered #Ex. 30:9unauthorized#Or strange fire before the Lord, which he had not commanded them. 2And fire #ch. 9:24Num. 16:35[2 Sam. 6:7]came out from before the Lord and consumed them, and they died before the Lord. 3Then Moses said to Aaron, "This is what the Lord has said, "Among #ch. 21:17those who are near me #Ezek. 28:22I will be sanctified, and before all the people I will be glorified." #[Ps. 39:9]And Aaron held his peace.
4And Moses called Mishael and Elzaphan, the sons of #Ex. 6:18Num. 3:19Uzziel the uncle of Aaron, and said to them, "Come near; carry your brothers away from the front of the sanctuary and out of the camp." 5So they came near and carried them in their coats out of the camp, as Moses had said. 6And Moses said to Aaron and to Eleazar and Ithamar his sons, #ch. 13:45|NoBook| 21:10Ezek. 24:16"Do not let the hair of your heads hang loose, and do not tear your clothes, lest you die, and #Num. 1:53|NoBook| 16:22|NoBook| 18:5Josh. 7:1|NoBook| 22:18wrath come upon all the congregation; but let your brothers, the whole house of Israel, bewail the burning that the Lord has kindled. 7#ch. 21:12And do not go outside the entrance of the tent of meeting, lest you die, #ch. 8:30for the anointing oil of the Lord is upon you." And they did according to the word of Moses.
8And the Lord spoke to Aaron, saying, 9#Ezek. 44:21[Num. 6:3Luke 1:151 Tim. 3:38]"Drink no wine or strong drink, you or your sons with you, when you go into the tent of meeting, lest you die. It shall be a statute forever throughout your generations. 10You are to #ch. 11:47|NoBook| 20:25Ezek. 22:26|NoBook| 44:23distinguish between the holy and the common, and between the unclean and the clean, 11and #ch. 14:57Deut. 24:8Neh. 8:2[Jer. 18:18Mal. 2:7]you are to teach the people of Israel all the statutes that the Lord has spoken to them by Moses."
12Moses spoke to Aaron and to Eleazar and Ithamar, his surviving sons: "Take the #ch. 6:16Num. 18:9grain offering that is left of the Lord's food offerings, and eat it unleavened beside the altar, for #See ch. 6:17it is most holy. 13You shall eat it in a holy place, because it is your due and your sons' due, from the Lord's food offerings, for #ch. 2:3|NoBook| 6:16so I am commanded. 14But the #ch. 7:31Ex. 29:24Num. 18:11breast that is waved and the thigh that is contributed you shall eat in a clean place, you and your sons and your daughters with you, for they are given as your due and your sons' due from the sacrifices of the peace offerings of the people of Israel. 15#ch. 7:31The thigh that is contributed and the breast that is waved they shall bring with the food offerings of the fat pieces to wave for a wave offering before the Lord, and it shall be yours and your sons' with you as a due forever, as the Lord has commanded."
16Now Moses diligently inquired about #ch. 9:3the goat of the sin offering, and behold, it was burned up! And he was angry with Eleazar and Ithamar, the surviving sons of Aaron, saying, 17#ch. 6:26"Why have you not eaten the sin offering in the place of the sanctuary, since #[See ver. 12 above]it is a thing most holy and has been given to you that you may bear the iniquity of the congregation, to make atonement for them before the Lord? 18Behold, #ch. 6:30its blood was not brought into the inner part of the sanctuary. You certainly ought to have eaten it in the sanctuary, #ch. 6:26as I commanded." 19And Aaron said to Moses, "Behold, #ch. 9:8today they have offered their sin offering and their burnt offering before the Lord, and yet such things as these have happened to me! If I had eaten the sin offering today, #Jer. 6:20|NoBook| 14:12Hos. 9:4Mal. 1:10|NoBook| 2:13would the Lord have approved?" 20And when Moses heard that, he approved.
