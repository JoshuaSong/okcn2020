Afterward he will let you go from here. #ch. 12:31When he lets you go, he will drive you away completely. 2Speak now in the hearing of the people, that #ch. 3:22|NoBook| 12:35they ask, every man of his neighbor and every woman of her neighbor, for silver and gold jewelry." 3#ch. 3:21|NoBook| 12:36And the Lord gave the people favor in the sight of the Egyptians. 4So Moses said, "Thus says the Lord: #ch. 12:29[Job 34:20Amos 4:10]About midnight I will go out in the midst of Egypt, 5and every firstborn in the land of Egypt shall die, from the firstborn of Pharaoh who sits on his throne, even to the firstborn of the slave girl who is #Matt. 24:41Luke 17:35behind the handmill, and all the firstborn of the cattle. 6#ch. 12:30[Amos 5:16There shall be a great cry throughout all the land of Egypt, such as there has never been, nor ever will be again. 7But not a dog shall growl #[ch. 8:22|NoBook| 9:4]against any of the people of Israel, either man or beast, that you may know that the Lord #ch. 9:4makes a distinction between Egypt and Israel. 8And #ch. 12:33all these your servants shall come down to me and bow down to me, saying, "Get out, you and all the people who follow you." And after that I will go out." And he went out from Pharaoh in hot anger. 9Then the Lord said to Moses, #ch. 3:19|NoBook| 7:4|NoBook| 10:1"Pharaoh will not listen to you, that #ch. 7:3my wonders may be multiplied in the land of Egypt."
